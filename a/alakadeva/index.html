Alakadeva untranslated

Alakadeva. A Thera who accompanied Majjhima to Himavā (Vin-a.i.68). He converted one of the five districts there and ordained 100,000 monks (Mbv.115). The Dīpavaṁsa (viii.10) gives his name as Mūlakadeva.

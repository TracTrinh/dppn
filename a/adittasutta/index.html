Ādittasutta untranslated

01. Ādittasutta 01 untranslated

Ādittasutta 01. Spoken before the Buddha at Jetavana by a Deva who visited him. Like a man who rescues what he can from his burning house, let the wise man enjoy his possessions and give them away with discernment. Thus will he attain to happiness hereafter. SN.i.31.

Chưa dịch.

02. Ādittasutta 02 untranslated

Ādittasutta 02. All the khandhas are on fire. Seeing this, the Ariyan disciple feels revulsion from them and, through knowledge, attains to freedom. SN.iii.71.

Chưa dịch.

03. Ādittasutta 03 untranslated

Ādittasutta 03. Same as the Ādittapariyāyasutta. SN.iv.19.

Chưa dịch.

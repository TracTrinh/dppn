Ākaṅkhavagga draft

Ākaṅkhavagga. The eighth chapter of the Dasakanipāta of the Aṅguttaranikāya.

It consists of ten Suttas on such subjects as the “thornless” life, the obstacles to desired things, Migasālā’s questions on the future life of individuals, the likeness of a bad monk to a crow, the qualities of the Nigaṇṭhas, etc. AN.v.131-51.

Ākaṅkhavagga. Chương thứ tám của phẩm Dasakanipāta thuộc kinh Aṅguttaranikāya.

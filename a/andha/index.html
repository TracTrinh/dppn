Andhā

Andhā. Mentioned in the Samantapāsādikā, together with the Damiḷas, as being non-Ariyan (milakkha); the name is probably the same as Andhaka(ā) (q.v.). Vin-a.i.255; see also Vibh­a.387-8, where the Andhaka-language is mentioned. In Buddhaghosa’s time the Vedas were taught in the Andha language also (MN­a.i.113).

Ārakkhadāyaka Thera

01. Ārakkhadāyaka Thera 01

Ārakkhadāyaka Thera 01. An Arahant. In a previous birth he built a railing (vedi) round the Thūpa of Siddhattha Buddha and made provision for its protection. Six kappas ago he was a king named Apassena. Ap.i.214-15.

02. Ārakkhadāyaka Thera 02

Ārakkhadāyaka Thera 02. An Arahant. He put a fence round the Thūpa of the Buddha Dhammadinna and arranged for its protection. This act resulted in his becoming an Arahant in the present age. Ap.i.253.

# Phāsusutta 02

## English source

Phāsusutta 02. The five comfortable abodes: living in amity with one’s fellows in act of deed, in act of word, in act of mind, maintaining whole and unbroken the virtues, praised by the wise and living in accordance with the Ariyan view. AN.iii.132; cf. DN.ii.88.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

# Pāsāṇatittha

## English source

Pāsāṇatittha. A ford across the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kadambanadī</span>. From this ford the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sīmā</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> started, returning to the same spot. In <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāsāṇatittha</span> was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuḍḍavāṭakapāsāṇa</span>. Mbv. 134,135.

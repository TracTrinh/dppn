# Parakkamabāhu 09

## English source

Parakkamabāhu 09. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu VIII</span>. Also called Vīra Parakkamabāhu, uncle of Parakkamabāhu <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">VII</span>. (Cv.xcii.3).

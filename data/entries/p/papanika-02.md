# Pāpaṇika 02

## English source

Pāpaṇika 02. The shopkeeper who is shrewd, supremely capable, and who inspires confidence, soon attains to greatness and increase of wealth; a monk will in like manner attain to spiritual development. AN.i.115f.

## Vietnamese translation

# Pariññāsutta 02

## English source

Pariññāsutta 02. The teaching for the comprehension of all attachment: from eye and object arises eye-consciousness, the union of these is contact; from contact comes feeling; similarly with regard to the other senses. SN.iv.32f.

## Vietnamese translation

# Pāsāṇasutta

## English source

Pāsāṇasutta. Once when the Buddha was in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span> on a dark and rainy night, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> sent many rocks crashing down in order to frighten him, but the Buddha was quite calm (SN.i.109).

# Panāda 02

## English source

Panāda 02. One of the chief <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span> to be invoked by the Buddha’s followers in time of need (DN.iii.204). He is also mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (DN.ii.258). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (DN­a.ii.688) that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Panāda</span> was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhabba</span>.

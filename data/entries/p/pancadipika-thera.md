# Pañcadīpika Thera

## English source

Pañcadīpika Thera. An *Arahant*. He was once a follower of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> and lit a lamp under his Bodhi tree. Thereby he obtained the power of being able to see through all obstacles. Thirty-four *kappas* ago he was king, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Satacakkhu</span>. Ap.i.108.

# Pācittiya

## English source

Pācittiya. One of the two main divisions of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttavibhaṅga</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayapiṭaka</span>. It contains Vinaya rules connected with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Pātimokkha</span>, the violation of which can be expiated in some way.

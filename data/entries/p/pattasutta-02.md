# Pattasutta 02

## English source

Pattasutta 02. The Buddha was once preaching a sermon to the monks on the five *upādānakkhandhā*, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>, wishing to disturb their thoughts, appeared in the shape of a bullock and wandered about among the bowls which had been placed to dry. But he was recognized and had to retire discomfited. SN.i.112f.

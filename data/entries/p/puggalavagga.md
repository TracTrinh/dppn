# Puggalavagga

## English source

Puggalavagga. Several sections of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span> are called by this name: the sixth of the *Dukanipāta* (AN.i.76-80), the third of the *Tika* (AN.i.118-31), the fourteenth of the *Catukka* (ii.133-39), the sixteenth of the *Dasaka* (v. 247-49), and the twentieth of the *Dasaka* (v. 281-2).

# Palutthagiri

## English source

Palutthagiri, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Palutthapabbata</span>. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, the scene of two fierce battles against the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Coḷas</span>, in both of which they were defeated, once in the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda V</span>., (Cv.Iv.28) and again in the twelfth year of the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. (Cv.Iviii.18).

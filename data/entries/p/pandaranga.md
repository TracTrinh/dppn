# Paṇḍaraṅga

## English source

Paṇḍaraṅga. A sect of Brahmin ascetics; they are mentioned in the time of the Buddha (e.g., Dhp­a.iv.8) and also in that of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>. Perhaps they covered their bodies with ashes, e.g., Dpv.viii.35; Vin-a.i.44.

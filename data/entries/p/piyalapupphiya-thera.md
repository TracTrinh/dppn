# Piyālapupphiya Thera

## English source

Piyālapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a hunter who, seeing the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, threw a *piyāla*-flower on the path whereon he had trodden. Ap.i.220.

# Pokkharaṇiyā

## English source

Pokkharaṇiyā. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāmagāma</span> where the Buddha is said once to have stayed. AN.iii.309; AN­a.ii.660. The translator calls it a lotus pond; the Commentary definitely calls it a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Vihāra</span> (GS.iii.220).

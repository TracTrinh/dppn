# Parosahassasutta

## English source

Parosahassasutta. Relates how once, when the Buddha was at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> with twelve hundred and fifty monks, instructing them and inciting them by means of a sermon on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nibbāna</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅgīsa</span>, who was in the assembly, after obtaining the Buddha’s permission, extolled him in a number of verses. SN.i.192.

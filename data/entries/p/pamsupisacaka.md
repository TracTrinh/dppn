# Paṁsupisācakā

## English source

Paṁsupisācakā. A class of *pisācas*, born in filth. MN­a.ii.713, 921; Ud­a.247. The word is used as a term of contempt, e.g., AN­a.i.438; MN­a.ii.610, 611.

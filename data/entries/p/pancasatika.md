# Pañcasatikā

## English source

Pañcasatikā. The name given to the First Council, which was held under the presidency of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span>. Five hundred monks took part in it, hence its name. Mhv­ṭ.151.

# Pavivekasutta

## English source

Pavivekasutta. Teachers of other schools teach three forms of aloofness: from robes, from food, from lodging. The Buddha’s teachings give three other forms of aloofness: from immorality, from wrong views, from the *āsavas*. AN.i.240f.

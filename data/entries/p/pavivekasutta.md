# Pavivekasutta

## English source

Pavivekasutta. Teachers of other schools teach three forms of aloofness: from robes, from food, from lodging. The Buddha’s teachings give three other forms of aloofness: from immorality, from wrong views, from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span>. AN.i.240f.

# Paccorohaṇīvagga

## English source

Paccorohaṇīvagga. The twelfth section of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dassakanipāta</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span> (AN.v.222-37). One of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> deals with the “spiritual coming down again” (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">paccorohaṇi</span>); hence, probably the name of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Vagga</span>.

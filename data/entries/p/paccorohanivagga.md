# Paccorohaṇīvagga

## English source

Paccorohaṇīvagga. The twelfth section of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dassakanipāta</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span> (AN.v.222-37). One of the *Suttas* deals with the “spiritual coming down again” (*paccorohaṇi*); hence, probably the name of the *Vagga*.

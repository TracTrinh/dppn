# Pañcālacaṇḍa 02

## English source

Pañcālacaṇḍa 02. A handsome Brahmin youth with whom the queen of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> king misconducted herself on various occasions. She first saw and fell in love with him when on the way to visit her son; thereafter she found various excuses for coming to the city where he lived. This is one of the stories related by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇāla</span>. Ja 536, Ja.v.425, 430f.

# Padhānasutta 02

## English source

Padhānasutta 02. Four qualities which show that their possessor has entered on the path to surety, and that he is definitely bent on the destruction of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span>: virtue, learning, ardent energy, wisdom. AN.ii.76.

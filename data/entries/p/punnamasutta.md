# Puṇṇamāsutta

## English source

Puṇṇamāsutta. Once, when the Buddha was staying in the *Migārāmātupāsāda*, a certain monk asked him a series of questions regarding the five *upādānakkhandhā* their origin and nature, their definitions, the cause of their arising, etc. The Buddha answered his questions. At the end of the *Sutta* is an explanation of *atta*. SN.iii.100 ff.

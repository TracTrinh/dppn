# Pekhuṇiya

## English source

Pekhuṇiya. Grandson of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> (*q.v.*), who is, therefore, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pekhuṇiyanattā</span> (AN.i.193). The Commentary (AN­a.i.419) calls Pekhuṇiya a *seṭṭhi*.

# Pariyāyasutta

## English source

Pariyāyasutta. Followers of other teachers might say that their teaching was the same as that of the Buddha regarding the five hindrances and the seven limbs of wisdom. But there is a method in the teaching of the Buddha whereby five become ten and seven fourteen. Other teachers cannot satisfactorily explain this method. SN.v.108f.

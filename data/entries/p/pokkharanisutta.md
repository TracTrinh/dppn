# Pokkharaṇīsutta

## English source

Pokkharaṇīsutta. The suffering which remains to an Ariyan disciple who has won insight compared to the suffering which he has destroyed, is as the water taken up by the tip of a blade of grass compared to the water left behind in a tank fifty <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yojanas</span> in length, breadth and depth. SN.ii.134; SN.v.460.

# Piṇḍolasutta

## English source

Piṇḍolasutta. The Buddha explains to some monks at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ghositārāma</span> that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piṇḍola Bhāradvāja</span> had realized truth through having cultivated three controlling faculties: mindfulness, concentration, and insight. These accomplish the destruction of birth, old age, and death. SN.v.224f.

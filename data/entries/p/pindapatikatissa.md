# Piṇḍapātikatissa

## English source

Piṇḍapātikatissa. An Elder of the kingdom of *Devaputta*. He is mentioned in a list of *Arahants*, who, having become *Arahants* by the development of *ānāpānasati*, could limit the term of their lives. Vsm.292.

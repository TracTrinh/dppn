# Piṇḍapātikatissa

## English source

Piṇḍapātikatissa. An Elder of the kingdom of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Devaputta</span>. He is mentioned in a list of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>, who, having become <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span> by the development of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ānāpānasati</span>, could limit the term of their lives. Vsm.292.

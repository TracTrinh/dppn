# Piyasutta 01

## English source

Piyasutta 01. A monk who is virtuous, learned, lovely in speech, who cultivates the four *jhānas* and possesses emancipation in mind and through insight – such a monk is pious and pleasing in *brahmacariyā*. AN.iii.262.

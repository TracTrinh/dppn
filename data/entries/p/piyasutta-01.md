# Piyasutta 01

## English source

Piyasutta 01. A monk who is virtuous, learned, lovely in speech, who cultivates the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span> and possesses emancipation in mind and through insight – such a monk is pious and pleasing in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">brahmacariyā</span>. AN.iii.262.

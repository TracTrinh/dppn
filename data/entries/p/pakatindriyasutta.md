# Pākatindriyasutta

## English source

Pākatindriyasutta (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambahulāsutta</span>). Once, a company of monks, staying in a forest track in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>, were muddled in mind, noisy and uncontrolled in their senses. The *Deva*, who haunted the forest, admonished them, which agitated them. SN.i.203f.

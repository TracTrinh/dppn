# Pānadhidāyaka Thera

## English source

Pānadhidāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave a pair of sandals (*pānadhi*?) to a forest-dwelling sage. Seventy-seven *kappas* ago he was eight times king under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suyāna</span>. Ap.i.208f.

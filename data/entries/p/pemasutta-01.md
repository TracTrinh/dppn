# Pemasutta 01

## English source

Pemasutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pemasutta</span>. Of affection can be born both affection and ill-will; likewise of ill-will. Freedom from these states is acquired by the development of the *jhānas*. AN.ii.213ff.

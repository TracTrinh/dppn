# Pallaṅkavimānavatthu

## English source

Pallaṅkavimānavatthu. The story of a woman of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> who was married to a youth of equal rank, with whom she lived a virtuous life. After death she was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> met her and learned her story. Vv.iii.3; Vv-a.128ff.

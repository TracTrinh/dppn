# Pupphacaṅgotiya Thera

## English source

Pupphacaṅgotiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he took a casket of flowers which he sprinkled over <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>. He was five times king, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devabhūti</span>. Ap.i.118.

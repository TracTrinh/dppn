# Pallavabhogga

## English source

Pallavabhogga. A country from which came <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Deva</span>, together with four hundred and sixty thousand monks, for the foundation ceremony of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span> (Mhv.xxix.38). Geiger thinks the reference is to Persia. Mhv.Trs.194, n.2.

# Pajjaraka

## English source

Pajjaraka. The name of a disease which afflicted <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayapura</span> (capital of Ceylon) in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. It was due to the influence of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇakāla</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha</span> visited the Island to dispel the disease. It is defined as an *uṇhasīsābādha*. Mhv.xv.63; Mhv­ṭ.349.

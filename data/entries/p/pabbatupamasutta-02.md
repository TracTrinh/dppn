# Pabbatūpamasutta 02

## English source

Pabbatūpamasutta 02. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span> which one Ariyan disciple who has won understanding has destroyed, placed beside the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span> which remains to be destroyed, would be like the whole <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span> beside seven grains of gravel the size of mustard seeds. SN.v.464.

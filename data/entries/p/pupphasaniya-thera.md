## English source

Pupphāsaniya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>, and, following him to his hermitage, made for him a seat of flowers. Ap.i.254f.

# Parihānisutta

## English source

Parihānisutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> tells the monks of four qualities that bring about “falling off”: abundance of lust, hatred, and delusion, and want of wisdom in profound matters. AN.ii.143f.

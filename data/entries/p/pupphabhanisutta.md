# Pupphabhāṇīsutta

## English source

Pupphabhāṇīsutta. The three kinds of people in the world: the tricky-tongued (*gūthabhāṇī*), the fair-spoken (*pupphabhāṇī*), and the honey-tongued (*madhubhāṇī*). AN.i.127.

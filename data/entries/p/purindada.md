# Purindada

## English source

Purindada. A name for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>, because, as a human being, he bestowed gifts from town to town (*pure pure dānaṁ adāsi*). SN.i.229; Dhp­a.i.264; cp. Sanskrit *Purandara* (destroyer of cities).

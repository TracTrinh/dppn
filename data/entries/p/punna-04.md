# Puṇṇa 04

## English source

Puṇṇa 04. A servitor (*dabbigāhaka*) who held the oblation ladles for the seven sages, mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Assalāyanasutta</span> (MN.ii.157; MN­a.ii.785); they were rebuked by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asitadevala</span> for their pretensions regarding the superiority of Brahmins.

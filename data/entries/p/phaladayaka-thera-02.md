# Phaladāyaka Thera 02

## English source

Phaladāyaka Thera 02. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> he was an ascetic well versed in the Vedas who, seeing the Buddha, gave him a *puṇḍarīka*-fruit. One hundred and seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumaṅgala</span> (Ap.i.160f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Susārada Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.167.

# Phalakadāyaka Thera

## English source

Phalakadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a waggon builder (*yānakāra*), and gave a plank of sandalwood to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Fifty-seven *kappas* ago he was king four times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhavanimmita</span> (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Santa</span>) (Ap.i.174). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Thera</span> (No. 13). Thag­a.i.199f.

# Paṇḍukaṇṇa

## English source

Paṇḍukaṇṇa. A juggler (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nāṭaka</span>) who was among those who tried, but without success, to make <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Panāda</span> laugh. He entered with his troupe into a blazing fire and there remained until it burnt out. The people sprinkled the ashes with water, and Paṇḍukaṇṇa and his troupe rose up, wearing flowers and dancing. Ja.iv.324.

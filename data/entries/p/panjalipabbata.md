# Pañjalipabbata

## English source

Pañjalipabbata. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcalipabbata</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajjalitapabbata</span>. A mountain in South Ceylon, at the source of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Karindanadī</span>. Here <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theraputtābhaya</span> lived after he renounced the world and became an *Arahant*. Mhv.xxxii.14; Thūpavaṁsa 77.

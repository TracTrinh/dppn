# Parakkama 01

## English source

Parakkama 01. (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkanta</span>). A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍyan</span> king, probably the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vikkamapaṇḍu</span>, who ruled over Ceylon for two years (1051-52). He was slain by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Coḷas</span>. Cv.lvi.16.

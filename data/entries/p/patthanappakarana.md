# Paṭṭhānappakaraṇa

## English source

Paṭṭhānappakaraṇa. The last “book” of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span>. It might be described as the book of “causes,” and deals with the twenty-four *paccayas* or modes of relations between things, mental and material. Its Commentary was written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> at the request of a monk called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Buddhaghosa</span>.

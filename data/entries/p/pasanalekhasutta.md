# Pāsāṇalekhasutta

## English source

Pāsāṇalekhasutta. Three kinds of persons: like carvings on rock, on the ground and on water. The first is easily angered and his anger lasts long; that of the second does not last long; the third is easily reconciled. AN.i.283.

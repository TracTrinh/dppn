# Paṭhavīsutta 01

## English source

Paṭhavīsutta 01. Seven small balls of clay, as big as jujube kernels, are infinitely smaller than the earth. So with the Ariyan disciple – when he has won understanding, the *dukkha* that remains is infinitely smaller than that which he has destroyed. SN.ii.135f.; repeated at SN.v.462f.

# Pubbayogāvacarasutta

## English source

Pubbayogāvacarasutta. One of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> as introduction to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khaggavisāṇasutta</span>. It dealt with the five advantages of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pubbayogāvacara</span>. Snp­a.i.47.

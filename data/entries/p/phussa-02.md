# Phussa 02

## English source

Phussa 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Thera</span>. He was the son of a ruler of a province and was trained in all accomplishments. Having heard a great Thera preach, he left the world and joined the Saṅgha. He practised *jhāna* and became an *Arahant*. One day an ascetic named Paṇḍarassagotta heard him preach and questioned him on the future progress of *Bhikkhus*. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa</span>’s reply is contained in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span>, vs. 949-80; Thag­a.ii.82f.

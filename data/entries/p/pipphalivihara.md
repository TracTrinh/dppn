# Pipphalivihāra

## English source

Pipphalivihāra. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabbagirivihāra</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcālavihāra</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacelivihāra</span>. A monastery in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soṇagiripāda</span> in Ceylon. It was the residence of a monk named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soṇa</span> (Vibh­a.439) (*q.v.*). AN­a.i.225 calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcāla</span> and MN­a.ii.887 <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paceli</span>.

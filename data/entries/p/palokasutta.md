# Palokasutta

## English source

Palokasutta. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> that the world (*loka*) is so-called from its transitory nature (*palokadhamma*). In the teachings of the Ariyans the world consists of eye, objects, etc. SN.iv.53.

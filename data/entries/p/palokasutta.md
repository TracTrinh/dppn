# Palokasutta

## English source

Palokasutta. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> that the world (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">loka</span>) is so-called from its transitory nature (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">palokadhamma</span>). In the teachings of the Ariyans the world consists of eye, objects, etc. SN.iv.53.

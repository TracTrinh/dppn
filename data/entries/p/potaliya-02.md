# Potaliya 02

## English source

Potaliya 02. A wanderer (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Paribbājaka</span>) probably identical with the above (1). A conversation he had with the Buddha is recorded in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>. At the end of the discussion he declared himself the Buddha’s follower. AN.ii.100f.

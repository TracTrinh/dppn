# Pañcasattatimandira

## English source

Pañcasattatimandira. A building erected in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. for “the reception of the magic water and the magic thread given him by the yellow robed ascetics” (Cv.lxxiii.73). Geiger suggests that the building was used for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">paritta</span>-ceremonies. Cv.Trs.ii.9, n.2.

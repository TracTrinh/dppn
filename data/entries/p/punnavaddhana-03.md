# Puṇṇavaḍḍhana 03

## English source

Puṇṇavaḍḍhana 03. The name given to a kind of precious garment. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇḍalī</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dvāramaṇḍala</span> sent several of these garments to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghābhaya</span> through <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūranimila</span>, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghābhaya</span> gave a pair himself to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūranimila</span>. Mhv.xxiii.33, 37; Mhv­ṭ.450.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇa</span> was probably another variation of the name, e.g., Mhv­ṭ.538.

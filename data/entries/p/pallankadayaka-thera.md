# Pallaṅkadāyaka Thera

## English source

Pallaṅkadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. He once gave a couch (*pallaṅka*), with cushions, etc., to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha Buddha</span>. Twenty thousand *kappas* ago he was king three times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suvaṇṇābha</span> (Ap.i.175). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.202f.

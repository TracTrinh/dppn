# Puṇṇā 04

## English source

Puṇṇā 04. The slave girl of the Brahmin soothsayer of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāṇacchandajātaka</span> (Ja 289). When asked what boon she desired, she answered, “A pestle and mortar and a winnowing basket.” Ja.ii.428, 429.

# Pārāsariya 02

## English source

Pārāsariya 02. A Brahmin teacher mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indriyabhāvanāsutta</span>. He is said, by his pupil <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttara</span>, to have taught that those who have developed their <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">indriyas</span> could neither see forms with their eyes nor hear sounds with their ears (MN.iii.298). He is perhaps identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāpariya Thera</span>.

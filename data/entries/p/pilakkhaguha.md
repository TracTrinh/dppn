# Pilakkhaguhā

## English source

Pilakkhaguhā. A cave near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosambī</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Paribbājaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sandaka</span> is said to have stayed there. Near by was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devakaṭasobbha</span> (MN.i.513). The cave was so-called because a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pilakkha</span>-tree grew in front of it. MN­a.ii.687.

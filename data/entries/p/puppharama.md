# Pupphārāma

## English source

Pupphārāma. A central monastic establishment in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍhanapura</span> (modern Kandy) in Ceylon. It formed the headquarters of the Siamese monks under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli</span>, who came to Ceylon at the invitation of the king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>. Cv.c.86, 141.

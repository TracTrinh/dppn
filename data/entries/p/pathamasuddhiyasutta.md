# Paṭhamasuddhiyasutta

## English source

Paṭhamasuddhiyasutta. The four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span>, which, when cultivated, flow to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nibbāna</span>, just as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ganges</span> flows to the east. SN.v.307.

# Pañcasikhasutta

## English source

Pañcasikhasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcasikha</span> visits the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span> and asks how it is that some beings are wholly set free in this very life, while others are not. The Buddha enlightens him. SN.iv.103f.

# Pesalā-atimaññanāsutta

## English source

Pesalā-atimaññanāsutta. Once when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅgīsa</span> was at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggāḷavacetiya</span> with his tutor, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigrodhakappa</span>, he found himself despising his friendly colleagues, proud of his own skill of improvisation. This discovery made him repent of his conceit and admonish himself. SN.i.187f.

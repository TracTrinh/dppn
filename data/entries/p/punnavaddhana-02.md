# Puṇṇavaḍḍhana 02

## English source

Puṇṇavaḍḍhana 02. One of the gardens laid out by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> (Cv.lxxix. 9). In it was a tank, which was connected with the Toyavāpī by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarassatī</span> Canal. From this branched off, to the west, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yamunā</span> Canal. Cv.46, 47.

# Pañcālacaṇḍī

## English source

Pañcālacaṇḍī. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷanī Brahmadatta</span>. Her marriage with King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Videha</span>, which was accomplished by the wisdom and diplomacy of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahosadha</span>, forms the main theme of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Ummaggajātaka</span>. She bore a son to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Videha</span>, who succeeded him ten years after the marriage.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcālacaṇḍī</span> is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sundarī</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandā</span>). Ja.vi.409, 410, 426, 427, 434, 438, 445, 453, 454; 466, 478.

# Padumapupphiya Thera

## English source

Padumapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago, while picking lotuses, he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Buddha</span> and offered him a flower. He later joined the Saṅgha. Forty-eight *kappas* ago he was king eighteen times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumabhāsa</span>. Ap.i.132.

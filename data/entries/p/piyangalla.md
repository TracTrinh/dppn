# Piyaṅgalla

## English source

Piyaṅgalla. A village of Ceylon, in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhivāta</span> district. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> relates an incident of a monk of this village who wished to have a share in the building of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>, in spite of the orders of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>. Mhv.xxx.29ff.

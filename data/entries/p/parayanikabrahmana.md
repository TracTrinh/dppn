# Pārāyaṇikabrāhmanā

## English source

Pārāyaṇikabrāhmanā. The name given to the sixteen disciples of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span> (*q.v.*). Each of them had one thousand followers. At the end of the *Suttas* contained in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇavagga</span>, they all expressed their desire to join the Saṅgha, and the Buddha ordained them by the *ehi-bhikkhu pabbajjā*. Vin-a.i.241.

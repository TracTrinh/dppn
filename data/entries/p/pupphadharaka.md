# Pupphadhāraka

## English source

Pupphadhāraka. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was an ascetic wearing bark and antelope skin. Seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, he held over him a canopy of *pāricchattaka*-flowers. Eighty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantadharaṇa</span>. Ap.i.244.

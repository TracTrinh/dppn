# Phussadeva 02

## English source

Phussadeva 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussadeva Thera</span>. An eminent teacher of the Vinaya (Vin.v.3) in Ceylon. He was a contemporary of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissa</span>, from whose views his own often differed. See Vin-a.i.263; ii.456, 495; iii. 651, 653; iv. 890.

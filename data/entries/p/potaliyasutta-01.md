# Potaliyasutta 01

## English source

Potaliyasutta 01. A discussion between the householder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Potaliya</span> and the Buddha as to what constitutes true retirement from household life. The Buddha shows, by means of various similes, that the pleasures of the senses are unsatisfying and dangerous, and should be avoided. Such renunciation brings higher knowledge. MN.i.359 ff.

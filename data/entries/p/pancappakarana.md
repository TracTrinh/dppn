# Pañcappakaraṇa

## English source

Pañcappakaraṇa. Name given to the collection of the books of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span>, with the exception of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasangaṇi</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vibhaṅga</span>. There is a Commentary on these by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda Vanaratana</span>. PLC.210; Gv.64-75.

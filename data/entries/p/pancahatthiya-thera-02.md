# Pañcahatthiya Thera 02

## English source

Pañcahatthiya Thera 02. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span> passing along the street with his followers and scattered five handfuls of lilies on his path. Thirteen *kappas* ago he was king five times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabhāsammata</span>. Ap.i.193.

# Pulinacaṅkamiya Thera

## English source

Pulinacaṅkamiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a hunter who, seeing the covered walk (*caṅkama*) of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, scattered sand over it (Ap.ii.418). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandaka</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.299.

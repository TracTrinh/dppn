# Phaggunasutta 02

## English source

Phaggunasutta 02. Contains an account of the Buddha’s visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phagguna</span> (1) when the latter lay ill. At the end of the *Sutta* is a list of six advantages of hearing the Dhamma and of testing its goodness in time. AN.iii.379 ff.

# Piyadassī 03

## English source

Piyadassī 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. He was present with sixty thousand others, from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetārāma</span>, at the foundation ceremony of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span> (Dpv.xix.15; Mhv.xxix.32). He stood on the north-east side at the ceremony, and at the close of it he preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>. Ibid., 65; Mhv­ṭ. 531.

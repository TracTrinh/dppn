# Pubbavideha

## English source

Pubbavideha. The eastern of the four continents (*mahādīpā*) which compose a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavāḷa</span> (AN.i.227; v.59). It is seven thousand leagues in extent (Snp­a. 443; 8,000 says Bv­a.112) and its chief tree is the Acacia (*Sirīsa*) (AN­a.i.264; MN­a.ii.947; Vsm.i.206, etc.). It is the first *mahādīpa* visited by a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> when on tour (Bv­a.131). See also *s.v.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Videha</span>.

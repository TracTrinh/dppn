# Pulinuppādaka Thera

## English source

Pulinuppādaka Thera. An *Arahant*. One hundred thousand *kappas* ago he was an ascetic named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devala</span> with eighty thousand followers. He erected a *Thūpa* of sand and honoured it in the name of the Buddha (Ap.ii.426 ff). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirima</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.280f.

# Phaggunasutta 03

## English source

Phaggunasutta 03. A discussion between the Buddha and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moliya Phagguna</span> as to whether anyone feeds on consciousness, exercises contact, feels, has craving, etc. The Buddha says that the question is badly formed; all these activities are conditioned by other activities, and so on. SN.ii.12 ff.

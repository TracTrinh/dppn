# Potthā

## English source

Potthā. Wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vasabha</span>’s uncle, the *senāpati* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subha</span>. She saved the life of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vasabha</span> and, later, when he became king, he made her his queen (Mhv.xxxv.70). She built a *Thūpa* and a temple attached to the *Catussāla* in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> (Mhv.xxxv.90).

# Pāpaṇika 01

## English source

Pāpaṇika 01. The shopkeeper who neglects his duties at early dawn, at midday, and at eventide, does not prosper; nor does the monk who similarly neglects his duties. AN.i.115.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

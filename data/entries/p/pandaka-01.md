# Paṇḍaka 01

## English source

Paṇḍaka 01. A monk, friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span>, who lived in a village near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosambī</span>. He was found guilty of having taken what did not belong to him and also of unchastity. Vin.iii.67.

# Pulinapūjaka Thera 01

## English source

Pulinapūjaka Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he cleared the foot of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>’s Bodhi tree and scattered fresh sand around it. Fifty-three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Pulina</span>. Ap.i.79.

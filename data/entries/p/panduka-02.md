# Paṇḍuka 02

## English source

Paṇḍuka 02. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> usurper. He killed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mittasena</span> and reigned for five years over Ceylon (433-8 CE.), during which time he was unsuccessful in his attempts to kill <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātusena</span>, the rightful heir. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍuka</span>’s son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parinda</span>. Cv.xxxviii.11, 21, 29.

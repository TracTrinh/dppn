# Pācīnasuttā

## English source

Pācīnasuttā. A group of three *Suttas*, in all of which it is stated that just as certain rivers (e.g. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṅgā</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yamunā</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aciravatī</span>, etc.) tend to flow eastward, so the monk who cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path tends to *Nibbāna*. SN.v.38f.

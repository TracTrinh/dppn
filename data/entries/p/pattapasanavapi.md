# Pattapāsāṇavāpī

## English source

Pattapāsāṇavāpī. A tank in Ceylon, formed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna II</span>. by the damming up of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kadambanadī</span> (Cv.xli.61). It was restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lx.50) and again by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxxix.34.

# Pāṇāsutta 01

## English source

Pāṇāsutta 01. Just as whatsoever creatures adopt the four postures do so in dependence on the earth, so does a monk develop the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span>, dependent on virtue. SN.v.78.

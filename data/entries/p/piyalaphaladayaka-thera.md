# Piyālaphaladāyaka Thera

## English source

Piyālaphaladāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a hunter, and seeing the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Nārada</span>, he offered him a *piyāla*-fruit (Ap.i.440f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piṇḍola Bhāradvāja</span> (Thag­a.i.245). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phaladāyaka</span>.

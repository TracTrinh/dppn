# Pubbasutta 01

## English source

Pubbasutta 01. (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hetusutta</span>). The Buddha tells the monks of how, even before the Awakening, he cultivated the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhipādas</span>, and of how their development brought various attainments, such as clairvoyance, etc. SN.v.263 ff.

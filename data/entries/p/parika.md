# Pārikā

## English source

Pārikā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārī</span>. A hunter’s daughter, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dukūlaka</span> and mother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suvaṇṇāsama</span> (the Bodhisatta). For details see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāmajātaka</span> (Ja 540). Pārikā was a former birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddā Kāpilānī</span>. Ja.vi.95; Mil.123.

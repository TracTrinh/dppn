# Paṭicchannasutta

## English source

Paṭicchannasutta. Three things which are practised in secret: the ways of women, the chants of Brahmins, the views of perverse men; and three others which are there for all to see: the sun, the moon, and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dhammavinaya</span> of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tathāgata</span>. AN.i.282.

# Pāpaka

## English source

Pāpaka. A monk who, believing that his name was of ill omen, wished to change it. The Buddha preached to him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāmasiddhijātaka</span> (Ja 97, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>) to show that a name has no importance. Ja.i.401f.

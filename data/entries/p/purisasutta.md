# Purisasutta

## English source

Purisasutta. The Buddha, in answer to a question of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span>, tells him that three kinds of inward experience arise in a man for his bane – greed, hate, and delusion. SN.i.70.

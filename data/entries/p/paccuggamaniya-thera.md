# Paccuggamanīya Thera

## English source

Paccuggamanīya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> and followed him with rapt gaze. Twenty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saparivāra</span>. Ap.i.240.

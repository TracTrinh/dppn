# Paṭhamacetiya

## English source

Paṭhamacetiya. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span> built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span>, on the spot where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> alighted on his first visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> and the king’s palace. Mhv.xiv.45; xx.20; Vin-a.i.79. One of the eight Bodhi saplings was planted there. Mhv.xix.61.

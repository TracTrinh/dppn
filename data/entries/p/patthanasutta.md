# Paṭṭhānasutta

## English source

Paṭṭhānasutta. Three good results for which the good life should be lived. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttasaṅgaha</span> No.29; Iti.67f. Perhaps the correct name is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Patthanāsutta</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udāna</span> calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sukkasutta</span>.

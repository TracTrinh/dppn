# Punabbasumitta 01

## English source

Punabbasumitta 01. A rich merchant in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. He built a monastery, one *yojana* in extent, on the site of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>, the ground of which he bought by covering the whole with golden bricks. Ja.i.94; Bv.xx.30; DN­a.ii.424.

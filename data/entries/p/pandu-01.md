# Paṇḍu 01

## English source

Paṇḍu 01. Ancestor of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṇḍavas</span> (Cv.lxiv.43). The name is also for those who claimed to belong to that dynasty – e.g., in the case of the kings of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span>, e.g., Mhv.vii.50. See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pandū</span>.

See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍurājā</span>.

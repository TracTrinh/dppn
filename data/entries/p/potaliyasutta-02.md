# Potaliyasutta 02

## English source

Potaliyasutta 02. The Buddha tells the *Paribbājaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Poṭaliya</span>, who visits him, of four kinds of persons found in the world: those who praise and dispraise rightly and seasonably, and those who praise and dispraise wrongly and unseasonably. AN.ii.100ff.

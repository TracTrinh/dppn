# Pāṭalipupphiya Thera

## English source

Pāṭalipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he was a *seṭṭhiputta* who, seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span>, offered him a lapful of *pāṭalī*-flowers. Sixty-three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhisammata</span>. Ap.i.122f.

## English source

Pañcasataraṭṭha. A district in Ceylon (the modern Pansiyapattu to the North east of Kandy) where King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senāratna</span> once deposited the Tooth Relic to guard it from his enemies. Cv.xcv.9.

# Parakkama 04

## English source

Parakkama 04. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span> king who deposed Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līlāvatī</span> and captured the throne of Ceylon. He ruled in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthinagara</span>, but was captured by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māgha</span> and tortured to death. Cv.lxxx.52ff., 71.

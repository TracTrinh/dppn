# Paribbājakasutta 01

## English source

Paribbājakasutta 01. A Brahmin *Paribbājaka* asks the Buddha how far the Dhamma is *sandiṭṭhiko, akāliko, ehipassiko, opanāyiko*, and *paccattaṁ veditabbo viññūhi*. The Buddha explains. AN.i.157f.

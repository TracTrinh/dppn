# Paribbājakasutta 01

## English source

Paribbājakasutta 01. A Brahmin <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Paribbājaka</span> asks the Buddha how far the Dhamma is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sandiṭṭhiko, akāliko, ehipassiko, opanāyiko</span>, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">paccattaṁ veditabbo viññūhi</span>. The Buddha explains. AN.i.157f.

# Puṇḍarīkasutta

## English source

Puṇḍarīkasutta. A monk, staying in a forest tract in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>, once entered a lotus pool and inhaled the scent of a lotus. The *Deva* of the forest, desirous of his welfare, called him “scent thief” and engaged him in conversation, whereby he was greatly agitated. SN.i.204f.

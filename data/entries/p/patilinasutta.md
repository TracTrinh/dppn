# Paṭilīnasutta

## English source

Paṭilīnasutta. A monk, who has shaken off various speculations, has given up searching for sense pleasures and going on other quests and has obtained calm by abandoning pleasure and pain, such a one is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">paṭilīna</span> (withdrawn). AN.ii.41f.

# Paṭilīnasutta

## English source

Paṭilīnasutta. A monk, who has shaken off various speculations, has given up searching for sense pleasures and going on other quests and has obtained calm by abandoning pleasure and pain, such a one is called *paṭilīna* (withdrawn). AN.ii.41f.

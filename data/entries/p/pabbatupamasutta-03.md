# Pabbatūpamasutta 03

## English source

Pabbatūpamasutta 03. Same as (2) above, except that the simile used is that of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālayas</span> as it is, compared with what it would be if it were wasted away to the size of seven grains of gravel the size of mustard seeds. SN.v.465.

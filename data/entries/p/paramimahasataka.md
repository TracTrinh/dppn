# Pāramīmahāsataka

## English source

Pāramīmahāsataka. A Pāli poem of one hundred verses, in twelve sections, dealing with the ten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pāramitā</span>, written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammakitti</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṅgharāja</span>. The poem is based on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jātaka</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cariyāpiṭaka</span>. PLC. 242.

# Puggalasutta 04

## English source

Puggalasutta 04. Incalculable is the beginning of *Saṁsāra*. The bones of a single person faring on for an aeon, if collected, would be a pile as great as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vepulla</span>. SN.ii.185.

# Pacchimārāma

## English source

Pacchimārāma. A monastery, probably to the west of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. It was founded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. and contained twenty-two *pariveṇas* and numerous other buildings.

# Parittābhā

## English source

Parittābhā. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> included among the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ābhā</span> gods (MN.iii.102). They belong to the plane of the second <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhāna</span> (Vibh­a.520). Beings are born there by virtue of absorbing the idea of lesser brilliancy (MN.iii.147). Their life-span is two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span>. Abhidh-s.22.

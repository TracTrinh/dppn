# Pāsasutta 01

## English source

Pāsasutta 01. Preached at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migadāya</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isipatana</span>. The Buddha tells the monks that he realized supreme emancipation by means of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yonisomanasikāra</span>, and asserts that he is free from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>’s snares. SN.i.105.

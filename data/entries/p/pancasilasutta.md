# Pañcasīlasutta

## English source

Pañcasīlasutta. The five things, being possessed of which makes women to be born in purgatory: the taking of life, theft, wrong sensuous indulgence, falsehood, the use of intoxicants. These are to be guarded against. SN.iv.245.

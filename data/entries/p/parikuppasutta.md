# Parikuppasutta

## English source

Parikuppasutta. Five kinds of persons who lie festering (*parikuppa*) in hell: those who kill mother, father, or *Arahant*, maliciously draw blood from the Buddha, or create dissension in the Saṅgha. AN.iii.146.

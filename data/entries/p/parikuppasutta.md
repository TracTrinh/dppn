# Parikuppasutta

## English source

Parikuppasutta. Five kinds of persons who lie festering (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">parikuppa</span>) in hell: those who kill mother, father, or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>, maliciously draw blood from the Buddha, or create dissension in the Saṅgha. AN.iii.146.

# Pūvapabbatavāsītissa

## English source

Pūvapabbatavāsītissa. A monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pūvagalla</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>. Because in a past birth he had given a meal of peacocks’ flesh, he got that flesh wherever he went. For his story see Ras.ii.27f.

# Pilotikakamma

## English source

Pilotikakamma. A chapter in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apadāna</span> which mentions various incidents in the lives of the Bodhisatta, as a result of which the Buddha, in his last life, had to suffer physical ailments and calumny (Ap.i.299f; repeated in Ud­a.263f). See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pubbakammapiloti</span>.

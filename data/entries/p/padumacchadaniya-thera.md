# Padumacchadaniya Thera

## English source

Padumacchadaniya Thera. An *Arahant*. He offered a lotus at the pyre of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Forty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumissara</span>. Wherever he went a canopy of lotuses spread itself over him. Ap.i.98.

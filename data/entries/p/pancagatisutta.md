# Pañcagatisuttā

## English source

Pañcagatisuttā. A series of *Suttas* in which the Buddha declares that, through not understanding the four Ariyan truths, beings continue to be born in one or other of the five conditions: as humans, animals, *Petas*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>, or in the *nirayas*. SN.v.474ff.

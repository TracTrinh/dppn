# Pabbhāradāyaka Thera

## English source

Pabbhāradāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. He once cleaned the shed (*pabbhāra*) in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Buddha</span> kept his drinking water and provided him with a pot. Twenty-two *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Susuddha</span>. Ap.i.252.

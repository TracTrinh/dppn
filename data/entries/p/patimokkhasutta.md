# Pāṭimokkhasutta

## English source

Pāṭimokkhasutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pātimokkhasutta</span>. A monk asks the Buddha for a brief teaching. The Buddha tells him that he should dwell in the self-control of the *Pātimokkha*, well equipped in his range of practice (*ācāragocarasampanno*), seeing danger in the minutest faults and undertaking the precepts. Thus will he be able to develop the four *satipaṭṭhāna*s. SN.v.187.

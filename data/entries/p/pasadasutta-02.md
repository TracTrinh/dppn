# Pasādasutta 02

## English source

Pasādasutta 02. The four best faiths: in the Buddha, the Noble Eightfold Path, the Dhamma, which is passionless, and the Saṅgha of monks. AN.ii.34f.; cf. AN.iii.36f.; quoted in Vsm.i.293; it is found in Iti.87.

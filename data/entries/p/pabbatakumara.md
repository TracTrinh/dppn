# Pabbatakumāra

## English source

Pabbatakumāra. The son of Dhananda. He was kidnapped by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cāṇakka</span> who brought him up with his protégé, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candagutta</span>. On discovering that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabbata</span> was the weaker, he contrived to have him murdered as he slept. For details see Mhv­ṭ.183ff.

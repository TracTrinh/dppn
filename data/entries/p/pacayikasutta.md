# Pacāyikasutta

## English source

Pacāyikasutta. Few are they that pay respect to the elders of the clan; more numerous those that do not (SN.v.468). Both the text and the *uddāna* call this *Sutta Pacāyika*, but the correct name is *Apacāyika*, and it should be altered to this.

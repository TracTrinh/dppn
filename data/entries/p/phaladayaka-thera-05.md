# Phaladāyaka Thera 05

## English source

Phaladāyaka Thera 05. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> he was an ascetic living on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhāgīrathī</span> and gave to the Buddha all the fruits he had gathered for his own meal.

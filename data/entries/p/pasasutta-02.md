# Pāsasutta 02

## English source

Pāsasutta 02. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isipatana</span>. The Buddha declares that he is free from all snares, both celestial and human, and admonishes the monks to wander about for the good of the many. He himself was going to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senānigama</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uruvelā</span> to preach. SN.i.106.

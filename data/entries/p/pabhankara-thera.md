# Pabhaṅkara Thera

## English source

Pabhaṅkara Thera. An *Arahant*. He once saw the *cetiya* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttama Buddha</span> covered with trees and creepers and quite inaccessible. He cleared it and made it ready for worship. Ap.i.269-70.

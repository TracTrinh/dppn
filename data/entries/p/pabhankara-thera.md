# Pabhaṅkara Thera

Pabhaṅkara Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. He once saw the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttama Buddha</span> covered with trees and creepers and quite inaccessible. He cleared it and made it ready for worship. Ap.i.269-70.

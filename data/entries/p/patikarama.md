# Pāṭikārāma

## English source

Pāṭikārāma. A park near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesālī</span>, where the Buddha was staying, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sunakkhatta</span>, having failed to impress him as to the greatness of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Korakkhattiya</span>, left the Saṅgha and went about abusing the Buddha. Ja.i.389; cp. Ja.i.77.

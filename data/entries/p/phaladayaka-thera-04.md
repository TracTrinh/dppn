# Phaladāyaka Thera 04

## English source

Phaladāyaka Thera 04. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> came to him for alms after having arisen from *samādhi*, and he gave the Buddha various fruits. Fifty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekajjha</span>. Ap.i.239.

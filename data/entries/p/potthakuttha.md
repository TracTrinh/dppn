# Potthakuṭṭha

## English source

Potthakuṭṭha. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> in the service of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IV</span>. He erected and endowed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṭambiya</span> *padhānaghara*, and built houses in the Kuppurā *pariveṇa*, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurundapillaka</span> *vihāra* and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Rājaghara</span>. When the king died, he administered the kingdom, threw the sub-king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāsiva</span> into prison and set <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Datta</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanapiṭṭhi</span> on the throne. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Datta</span> died, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Potthakuṭṭha</span> had <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hatthadāṭha</span> crowned king. Later, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇavamma</span> rebelled against him, he ate poisoned food, provided by his friend, the chief of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Merukandara</span>, and died. Cv.xlvi.19, 39, 44; xlvii.55, 61.

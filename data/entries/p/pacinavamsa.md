# Pācīnavaṁsa

## English source

Pācīnavaṁsa. The name of Mount <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vepulla</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. The inhabitants were called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tivarā</span>, and it took them four days to climb the mountain and four days to descend.

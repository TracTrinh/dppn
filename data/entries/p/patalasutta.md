# Pātālasutta

## English source

Pātālasutta. The worldlings speak of a bottomless pit (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pātāla</span>) in the mighty ocean. But the real <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pātāla</span> is painful bodily feeling, which brings about weeping and wailing and lamentation. SN.iv.206f.

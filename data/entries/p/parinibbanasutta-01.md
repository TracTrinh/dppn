# Parinibbānasutta 01

## English source

Parinibbānasutta 01. An account of the death of the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusinārā</span>. It contains the last words of the Buddha and stanzas of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahampati</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span>, uttered immediately after his death. SN.i.157; cp. DN.ii.156f.; on their difference, see KS.i.196, n.1.

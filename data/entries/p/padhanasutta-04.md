# Padhānasutta 04

## English source

Padhānasutta 04. The four right efforts: for the non­arising of evil, for the abandoning of evil, for the arising of profitable states, and for the increase and fulfilment of such states. AN.ii.15; cp. DN.ii.120; MN.ii.11, etc.

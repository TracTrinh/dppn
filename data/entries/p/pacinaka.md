# Pācīnakā

## English source

Pācīnakā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pācīnakas</span>. By this name are described the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vajjiputtaka</span> monks who raised the Ten Points which occasioned the Second Council (Mhv.iv.47,48). They were so-called because they belonged to the East (Mhv­ṭ.165,166).

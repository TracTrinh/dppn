# Poṭṭhapādasutta

## English source

Poṭṭhapādasutta. A discussion between the Buddha and the *Paribbājaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Poṭṭhapāda</span>, held at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallikārāma</span> (DN.i.178-203). It deals with the question of *jhāna* and of the soul, and also with the infinity and eternalism of the world.

This *Sutta* is significant as containing a list of the topics which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paribbājakas</span> appear to have discussed at their meetings.

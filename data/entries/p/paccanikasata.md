# Paccanīkasāta

## English source

Paccanīkasāta. A Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, to whom the Buddha refused to preach (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paccaṇīkasutta</span>). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (SN­a.i.205) that the Brahmin was so-called (“Gainsayer”) because he took delight in opposing everything that anyone else said.

# Pabbatārāma

## English source

Pabbatārāma. A monastery built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabbata</span>, minister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span>. It is probably the same that is mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā</span> (p.616) as lying to the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessagiri</span> *vihāra* and near the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāsobbhakaṇḍaka</span>. (Mhv.xxxiii.90)

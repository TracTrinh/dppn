# Pācīnadesa

## English source

Pācīnadesa. The Eastern Province of Ceylon. It was less important than the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span> (See, e.g., Cv.xlviii.33, 41). It is also called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pubbadesa</span> (e.g., Cv.xlv.21) and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puratthimadesa</span> (Cv.xli.33).

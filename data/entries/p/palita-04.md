# Pālita 04

## English source

Pālita 04. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>, a previous birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhula</span> (*q.v.*). SN­a.iii.26; MN­a.ii.722,1023; but see Snp­a.i.341, where he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkha</span>. At AN­a.i.143 his name is given as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṭhavindhara</span>.

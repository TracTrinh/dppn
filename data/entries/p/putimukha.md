Pūtimukha. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span> who had been a monk in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span> and who had brought a dissension between two holy monks by carrying tales from one to the other.

# Patiṭṭhāna

## English source

Patiṭṭhāna. The capital of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aḷaka</span>, the first place to be passed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span>’s disciples on their way to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> (Snp.vs.1011). It is identified with the Baithana or Paitana of Ptolemy.

# Pabbatasutta 02

## English source

Pabbatasutta 02. An aeon is longer than the time taken by a man to waste away a mountain one league high, one long, and one wide, by stroking it once in every hundred years with a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāsī</span> cloth. SN.ii.181.

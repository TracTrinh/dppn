# Pakkantasutta

## English source

Pakkantasutta. The Buddha addresses the monks at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>, soon after <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span> had seceded from the order, and tells them that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>’s gain was his ruin, in the same way as the flowering of the plaintain, the bamboo and the rush. SN.ii.241.

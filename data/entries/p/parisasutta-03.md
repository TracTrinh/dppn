# Parisāsutta 03

## English source

Parisāsutta 03. On the eight assemblies: *Khattiya*, *Brāhmaṇa*, householder, *samaṇa*, *Cātummahārājika*, *Tāvatiṁsa*, *Māra* and *Brahma*. The Buddha visits them all and preaches to them. AN.iv.307f.

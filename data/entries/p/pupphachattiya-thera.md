# Pupphachattiya Thera

## English source

Pupphachattiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he made a parasol of lotus flowers, which he held over <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Seventy-four *kappas* ago he was king nine times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jalasikha</span>. Ap.i.264f.

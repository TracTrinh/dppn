# Parakkamabāhu 06

## English source

Parakkamabāhu 06. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu V</span>. King of Ceylon (Cv.xc.1; see Codr.83, 89, and Cv.Trs. ii. 212, n.1). He was, perhaps, the brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu IV</span>., and, probably, had his capital at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṅgāsiripura</span>. The period of his reign is not definitely known, but it was somewhere between 1348 and 1360 CE.

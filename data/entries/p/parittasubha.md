# Parittasubhā

## English source

Parittasubhā. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> belonging to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhas</span> (MN.iii.102). Beings are born among them after attaining the third *jhāna* (Vibh­a. 507). Their life-span is sixteen *kappas*. Abhidh-s. 23.

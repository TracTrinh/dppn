# Puṇṇa 03

## English source

Puṇṇa 03. Slave of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meṇḍaka</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>). He was one of the five persons of Great Merit (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Pañca Mahā Puññā</span>) (AN­a.i.219; Dhp­a.i.385). When he ploughed the field with a single plough he made fourteen furrows, seven on each side. Vsm.383.

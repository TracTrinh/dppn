# Niddātandisutta

## English source

Niddātandisutta. Preached in answer to the question of a *Deva*: sloth, drowsiness and surfeit of food prevent understanding of the Noble Eightfold Path. SN.i.7.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

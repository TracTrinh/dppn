# Naḷakapānasutta 02

## English source

Naḷakapānasutta 02. The Buddha, having preached to the monks in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Palāsavana</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Naḷakapāna</span> till late at night, asks <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> to continue, as he has pain in his back and wishes to rest. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> thereupon takes up the sermon and tells the monks of the necessity for *saddhā*, *hiri*, *ottappa*, *viriya* and *paññā*, for the performance of good works. The Buddha returns and praises <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. AN.v.122ff.

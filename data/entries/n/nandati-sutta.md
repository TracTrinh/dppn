# Nandati Sutta

## English source

Nandati Sutta. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> visits the Buddha and tells him of various sources of gladness: children, cattle and sense pleasures. The Buddha replies that these are really all sources of sorrow. SN.i.6; op. ibid.,107.

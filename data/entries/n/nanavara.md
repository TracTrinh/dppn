# Ñāṇavara

## English source

Ñāṇavara. An author of Pagan. He wrote several works in Pāli, including the Rājādhirājanāmattappakāsinī. He was tutor to the king Mahārājādhipati. He translated the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhānappadīpikā</span> into Burmese.

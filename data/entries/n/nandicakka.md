# Nandicakka

## English source

Nandicakka. An Elder who came to Ceylon at the head of a chapter of monks from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rakkhaṅga</span>, at the request of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya</span>, in order to confer the *upasampadā* ordination on the monks of Ceylon. Cv. xciv.15.

# Nārāyanasaṅghāṭabala

## English source

Nārāyanasaṅghāṭabala. The name given to a certain measure of physical strength. It was the equivalent of the strength of ten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chaddanta</span> elephants and was the strength of the Buddha. Vibh­a.397; Snp­a.ii.401.

# Nettippakaraṇa

## English source

Nettippakaraṇa Also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nettigantha</span>. An exegetical work on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Piṭakas</span>, traditionally ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span>.

There exists a Commentary on it by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammapāla</span> (Gv.59, 60; Sās-d.65).

Ñāṇābhivaṁsa wrote a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span> on it. Svd.1215.

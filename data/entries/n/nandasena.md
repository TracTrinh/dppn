# Nandasena

## English source

Nandasena. An *Upāsaka* of a village near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. His wife, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandā</span>, was a wicked woman and, after death, became a *Peta*. When she revealed herself to him, Nandasena gave alms in her name, and she gained happiness. Pv.ii.4; Pv-a.89ff.

# Nibbedhikapariyāya

## English source

Nibbedhikapariyāya. A comprehensive discourse addressed to the monks on sense desires, their source, their variety, their fruit, and the steps leading thereto. AN.iii.410f.; it is often quoted, e.g., Ud­a.176; Dhs-a.369.

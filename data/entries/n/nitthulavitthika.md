# Niṭṭhulaviṭṭhika

## English source

Niṭṭhulaviṭṭhika. A village in the district of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Giri</span> in Ceylon, the birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṭha-imbara</span> (Mhv.xxiii.49). It is probably identical with the village (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Niṭṭhulaveṭṭhi</span>) given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pottakuṭṭha</span> for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṭambiya</span> *padhānaghara*. Cv.xlvi.20.

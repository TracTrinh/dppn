# Ñāṇasaññaka Thera 01

## English source

Ñāṇasaññaka Thera 01. An arahant. In a past life he met <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> and paid him homage. Seventy-three *kappas* ago he became king sixteen times (Ap.i.140f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhāhu</span> (Thag-a.i.124).

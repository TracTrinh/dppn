# Nandā 02

## English source

Nandā 02. One of the four wives of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magha</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magha</span> and his friends built their hall, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandā</span> had a pond built in the grounds. As a result, she was reborn as the mate of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>, and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandāpokkharaṇī</span> came into existence on account of her merit. Ja.i.201ff.; Dhp­a.i.269f.

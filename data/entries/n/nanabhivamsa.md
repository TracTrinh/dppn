# Ñāṇābhivaṁsa

## English source

Ñāṇābhivaṁsa. A leader of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārupaṇas</span> in their controversy with the *Ekaṁsikas* in Burma (Bode: *op.cit.*, 76). He wrote several *ṭīkās* and a Burmese translation of the Jātakas and the *Rājādhirājavilāsinī* (Bode: *op.cit.*, 78; Svd.vs.1215).

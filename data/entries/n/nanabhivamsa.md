# Ñāṇābhivaṁsa

## English source

Ñāṇābhivaṁsa. A leader of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārupaṇas</span> in their controversy with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Ekaṁsikas</span> in Burma (Bode: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">op.cit.</span>, 76). He wrote several <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkās</span> and a Burmese translation of the Jātakas and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Rājādhirājavilāsinī</span> (Bode: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">op.cit.</span>, 78; Svd.vs.1215).

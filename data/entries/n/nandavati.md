# Nandavatī

## English source

Nandavatī. A nun, sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thullanandā</span>. Her other two sisters were <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sundarīnandā</span>. They were all married to the same Brahmin and all left the world after his death. Vin.iv.211, 259.

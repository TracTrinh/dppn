# Nakhasikhāsutta 01

## English source

Nakhasikhāsutta 01. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. Even as the mighty earth is many times greater than the pinch of dust taken on the tip of one’s finger-nail, so also is the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, destroyed by the Ariyan disciple of vision and understanding, greater than the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span> which remains undestroyed. SN.ii.133; SN.v.459.

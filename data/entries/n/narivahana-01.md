# Nārivāhana 01

## English source

Nārivāhana 01. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sujāta</span>. He lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nārivāhananagara</span>, and when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span> came there, he entertained him and the monks for seven days, and at the end of that time handed the kingdom over to his son and joined the Saṅgha. Bv­a.190.

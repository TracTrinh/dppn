# Nāgamālaka

## English source

Nāgamālaka. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> to the north of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirīsamāla</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana Buddha</span> preached the Dhamma there and twenty thousand people realized the truth. To the north was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asokamālaka</span> (Mhv.xv.118,153). In <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgamālaka</span> was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāsobbhakaṇḍakacetiya</span>, and, near it, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūlatthana</span> built another *cetiya*. Mhv­ṭ.355.

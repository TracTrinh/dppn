# Nandasutta 01

## English source

Nandasutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nanda</span> *Devaputta* visits the Buddha and recites a stanza to the effect that men should acquire merit in order to obtain bliss. The Buddha tells him that men should rather aspire to final peace. SN.i.62; cp. SN.i.2, where the same verses are given.

# Nandā 07

## English source

Nandā 07. A nun (evidently distinct from No. 6) mentioned as having died at Ñātika and having been reborn spontaneously in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhāvāsā</span>, there to pass away, never to return. DN.ii.91; SN.v.356f.

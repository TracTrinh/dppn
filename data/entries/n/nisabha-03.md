# Nisabha 03

## English source

Nisabha 03. Also called Mahā Nisabha, chief among the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dhutaṅgadharas</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>. It was his example that prompted <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span> to strive for a similar honour. Thag­a.ii.134f.; SN­a.ii.135f.; AN­a.i.85f.

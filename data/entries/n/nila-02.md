# Nīla 02

## English source

Nīla 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nīla Thera</span>. He belonged to a family of flower sweepers. He joined the Saṅgha and became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> in the tonsure hall. When he came to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> in search of a rag robe a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Brahmā</span> saw him and stood worshipping him. Other <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahmas</span> heard of this, and all worshipped him. SN­a.ii.217.

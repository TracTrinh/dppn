# Na tumhākaṁ Sutta

## English source

Na tumhākaṁ Sutta. The eye is not yours, nor objects, nor eye-consciousness – even so with the other senses. They should therefore be put away. Such putting away is for your profit and welfare (SN.4.81-2).

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

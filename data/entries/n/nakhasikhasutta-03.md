# Nakhasikhāsutta 03

## English source

Nakhasikhāsutta 03. No material form, even as much as can be taken up on the tip of the finger-nail, is permanent. It is the same with the other <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span>. Therefore is the holy life set forth for the utter destruction of suffering. SN.iii.147.

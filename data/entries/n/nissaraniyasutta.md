# Nissaraṇīyasutta

## English source

Nissaraṇīyasutta. A monk, who is not obsessed by thoughts of lust, ill-will, hurt, form and his own body (*sakkāya*), possesses the five elements of escape. AN.iii.245f.

## Vietnamese translation

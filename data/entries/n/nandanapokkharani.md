# Nandanapokkharaṇī

## English source

Nandanapokkharaṇī. A lake of great splendour in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandanavana</span>, having one hundred bathing places and one thousand inlets, and covered with the five kinds of lotus. Ja.ii.189; cp. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandāpokkharaṇī</span>. Elsewhere (e.g., Ja.vi.333) it is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandanavanapokkharaṇī</span>.

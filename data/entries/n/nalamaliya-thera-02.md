# Naḷamāliya Thera 02

## English source

Naḷamāliya Thera 02. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span> and gave him a garland of reed flowers (Ap.ii.412). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhaniya</span> Thera (Thag­a.i.347).

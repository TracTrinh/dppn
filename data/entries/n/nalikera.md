# Nāḷikera

## English source

Nāḷikera. An island, with many attendant islands. When the country of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bharu</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>) was destroyed because he took bribes, those who had blamed him for his unrighteousness were saved and found shelter in the islands round Nāḷikera. Ja.ii.173.

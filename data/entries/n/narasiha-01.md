# Narasīha 01

## English source

Narasīha 01. King of India, ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇḍuveṭhi</span>, and friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇavamma</span>. The latter helped him to defeat the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vallabha</span> king, and was rewarded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narasīha</span>’s help in acquiring the throne of Ceylon. Cv.xlvii.4-49.

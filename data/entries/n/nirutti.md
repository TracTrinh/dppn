# Nirutti

## English source

Nirutti. A work on exegesis, ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāyana</span> and divided into two parts: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Cūḷa Nirutti</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Nirutti</span> (Gv.59, 65; Svd.1233f). A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span> on it exists, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Niruttisāramañjūsā</span>, written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhammaguru</span>. Bode, 29; Gv.60.

# Nandivāpigāma

## English source

Nandivāpigāma. A village in Ceylon, residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātusena</span>, father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhānāma</span> (Cv.xxxviii.14). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gokaṇṇa</span>, officer of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span>, was once defeated there (Cv.lxx.72). The village is perhaps identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandigāma</span>.

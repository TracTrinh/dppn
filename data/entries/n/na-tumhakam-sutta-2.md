# Na tumhākaṁ Sutta

## English source

Na tumhākaṁ Sutta. None of the *khandhas* belong to you; therefore they should be put away. The putting of them away will be for your profit and welfare. (SN.3.33-4).

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

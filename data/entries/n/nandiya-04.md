# Nandiya 04

## English source

Nandiya 04. A *Paribbājaka* who visits the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> and asks him the conditions for the attainment of *Nibbāna*. The Buddha teaches him the Noble Eightfold Path. SN.v.11.

# Nāgadatta 01

## English source

Nāgadatta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgadatta Thera</span>. He once lived in a forest tract in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> and was inclined to be indolent. A *Deva*, noticing this, admonished him, and it is said that Nāgadatta paid heed to the warning. SN.i.200.

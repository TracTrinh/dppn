# Naḷiṇi

## English source

Naḷiṇi. The kingdom of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessavaṇa</span>. Ja.vi.313; but Vv-a (339, 340) explains Naḷiṇi as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kīḷanaṭṭhāna</span>. This agrees with DN.iii.202, where mention is made of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuvera</span> Naḷiṇi as one of the beauties of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessavaṇa</span>’s kingdom.

# Nimmānaratī

## English source

Nimmānaratī. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>, inhabiting the fifth of the six <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span>-worlds (DN.i.218; MN.i.289, etc.; SN.i.133, etc.; AN.i.210, etc. For their life-span see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Comp.</span>140f).

They are so-called because they delight in their own creations. They can create any form in any colour. NidA.109; Iti-a.234; Vibh­a.519.

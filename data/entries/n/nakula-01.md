# Nakula 01

## English source

Nakula 01. Second of the five sons of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span>, the others being <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajjuna</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhimasena</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yudhiṭṭhila</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahadeva</span>. All of them became husbands of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhā</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>).

# Nigguṇḍipupphiya Thera 01

## English source

Nigguṇḍipupphiya Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was the monastery attendant (*ārāmika*) of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, and once gave a *nigguṇḍi* flower to the Buddha. Thirty-five *kappas* ago he was king, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Patāpa</span> (Ap.i.205). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vīra</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.50f.

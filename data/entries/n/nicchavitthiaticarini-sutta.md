# Nicchaviṭṭhiaticārinī Sutta

## English source

Nicchaviṭṭhiaticārinī Sutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> reports to the Buddha that while descending <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span> he saw a flayed woman going through the air. The Buddha replies that the woman was an adulteress of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. SN.ii.259.

# Ñātakasutta

## English source

Ñātakasutta. If a well-known monk encourages others to act and speak contrary to the ordinances of the Dhamma and to have ideas contrary to them, he lives to the harm of a large number of people. AN.i.106.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

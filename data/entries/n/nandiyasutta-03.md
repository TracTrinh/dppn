# Nandiyasutta 03

## English source

Nandiyasutta 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandiya</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyan</span>, is taught by the Buddha that the Ariyan disciple who is possessed of unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, the Dhamma and the Saṅgha, and who has the Ariyan qualities, is bound for enlightenment.

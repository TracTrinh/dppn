# Nāthasutta

## English source

Nāthasutta. Two *Suttas* on the qualities which give protection to a monk: virtue, learning, good friends, affability, skill in performance of duties, fondness for truth, energy, contentment, mindfulness, wisdom. AN.v.23f. 26f.

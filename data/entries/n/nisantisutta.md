# Nisantisutta

## English source

Nisantisutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> how a monk who is apt at *attha*, *Dhamma*, *vyañjana*, *nirutti* and *pubbāparānusandhi*, comes speedily to grasp things and does not forget about that which he has grasped.

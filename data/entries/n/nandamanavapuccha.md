# Nandamāṇavapucchā

## English source

Nandamāṇavapucchā. The questions asked of the Buddha by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nanda</span> *māṇava*, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span>, and the Buddha’s replies thereto. It forms the seventh *Sutta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇavagga</span> (Snp.vs.1077-83) and is commented upon in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Niddesa</span>. CNid.26ff.

# Naḷamāliya Thera 01

## English source

Naḷamāliya Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Once he gave a fan, made of reeds, to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>, who praised his gift. He became king many times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subbata</span> and eight times under that of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māluta</span> (Ap.i.143f.) He is probably identical with Kuṭivihāriya Thera (Thag­a.i.131).

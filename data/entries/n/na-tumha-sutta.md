# Na tumhā Sutta

## English source

Na tumhā Sutta. The body is not yours nor is it any other’s. It is brought about by actions in the past, etc. Thus does the Ariyan disciple comprehend the causal law. This *Sutta* influenced <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pitamalla</span> Thera to join the Saṅgha (SN.2.64, MN­a. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">I</span>. 190).

# Nāgitasutta 02

## English source

Nāgitasutta 02. The circumstances are the same as those of No. 1. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgita</span> that he is pleased with monks who do not live in the village, but who seek the forest and stave off gains and flattery, but to him the best is to walk on the highway unattached. AN.iii.341ff.; cp. ibid., iv.341ff.

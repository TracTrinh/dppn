# Na jirati Sutta

## English source

Na jirati Sutta. A series of questions asked by a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> and the Buddha’s answers. The first question is, “What doth decay and what doth not?” The answer is that material shapes of mortals decay, but not their name (SN.1.43)

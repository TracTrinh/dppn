# Naṭakuvera

## English source

Naṭakuvera. A musician of the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, whose queen was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākātī</span>. For details see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākātījataka</span> (Ja 327) Ja.iii.91ff.; v.424. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Naṭakuvera</span> is identified with the discontented monk with reference to whom the Jātaka was preached.

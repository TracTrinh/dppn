# Nāgasutta 01

## English source

Nāgasutta 01. Once, when the Buddha was seated under the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajapāla</span> Banyan tree, soon after his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> assumed the form of a fearsome elephant and tried to frighten him. But the Buddha recognised <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> and sent him away discomfited. SN.i.103f.

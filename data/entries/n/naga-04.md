# Nāga 04

## English source

Nāga 04. An Elder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāraḷiyagiri</span> in Ceylon. For eighteen years he gave up teaching the Dhamma, but later he taught the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātukathā</span>, and his memory of the contents was perfect.

# Nandanavimānavatthu

## English source

Nandanavimānavatthu. A story of a man who looked after his parents and continued to do so after his marriage. He was later born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, where he was visited by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>. Vv.vii.2; Vv-a.300f.

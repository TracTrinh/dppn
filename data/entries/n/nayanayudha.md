# Nayanāyudha

## English source

Nayanāyudha. One of the four most powerful weapons in the world. It belongs to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yama</span>, and seems to be comparable to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siva</span>’s third eye. At a glance from this “weapon” many thousands of *kumbhaṇḍas* are shattered to bits. Snp­a.i.225.

# Nāgita Thera 02

## English source

Nāgita Thera 02. For some time the personal attendant of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> (DN.i.151; DN­a.i.310; AN.iii.31, 341; iv.341; Ja.iv.95, etc.). He was the maternal uncle of the novice <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīha</span>, who is said to have addressed him by the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span>, his *gotta* name. He was fat and, therefore, lazy; he got most of his work done by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīha</span>.

# Na santi Sutta

## English source

Na santi Sutta. A set of verses spoken before the Buddha by a number of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Satullapa</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> on how to escape sense-desires.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mogharāja</span> was present, and, by asking a question of the Buddha, he helped to clear away a possible misunderstanding. SN.1.22f.; SN­a.i.50.

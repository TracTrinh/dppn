# Nālā 02

## English source

Nālā 02. Wife of the *Ādipāda* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya</span>. She was the daughter of his maternal uncle and was under the protection of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena I</span>., but <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya</span> married her during an absence of the king and took her to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthinagara</span>. The king, however, forgave him. Cv.l.9; see also Cv.Trs.i. 138, n.3.

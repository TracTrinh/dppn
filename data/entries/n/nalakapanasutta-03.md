# Naḷakapānasutta 03

## English source

Naḷakapānasutta 03. The circumstances are the same as in (2), but the qualities mentioned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> differ – *saddhā*, *hiri*, *ottappa*, *viriya*, *sotāvadhāna*, *dhammadhāraṇā*, *atthūpaparikkhā*, *dhammānudhammapaṭipatti*, and *appamāda*. AN.v.125ff.

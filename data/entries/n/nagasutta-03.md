# Nāgasutta 03

## English source

Nāgasutta 03. Snakes which dwell in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span>, when grown and strong, find their way into the sea, where they grow even greater. Even so do monks, who develop the Noble Eightfold Path (SN.v.47).

# Nālā 01

## English source

Nālā 01. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magadha</span>, near the Bodhi-tree at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gayā</span>. It was the birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upaka</span> (Thīg.294; Thīg­a.225). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhavaṁsa Commentary</span> (p.3) speaks of a Brahmin village <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nālā</span>, where the Buddha spent his eleventh rainy season.

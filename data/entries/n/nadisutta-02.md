# Nadīsutta 02

## English source

Nadīsutta 02. It is as impossible to persuade a monk, devoted to detachment, to return to the lower life, as it would be to make the eastward flowing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ganges</span> to flow westward. SN.v.53; cp. iv. 191; v.136, 300.

# Nālikeradāyaka Thera

## English source

Nālikeradāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a parkkeeper in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> and gave to the Buddha a *nālikera*-fruit (Ap.ii.447f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇḍala</span> Thera (Thag­a.i.72) or with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khitaka Thera</span> (Ibid., 315).

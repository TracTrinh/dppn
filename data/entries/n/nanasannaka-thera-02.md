# Ñāṇasaññaka Thera 02

## English source

Ñāṇasaññaka Thera 02. An arahant. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he saw a coco-palm and, with his mind ﬁxed on the Buddha, thought of the Buddha’s praises. Seventy-three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulinapupphiya</span> (Ap.i.161f.). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyañjaha</span>. Thag-a.i.169.

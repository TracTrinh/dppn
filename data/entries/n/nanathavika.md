# Ñāṇathavika

## English source

Ñāṇathavika. An arahant. One hundred thousand *kappas* ago he was a hermit, and, seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Padumuttara</span>, worshipped him with reverence and sang his praises (Ap.ii.392f.). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yasadatta</span> Thera. Thag-a.i.428.

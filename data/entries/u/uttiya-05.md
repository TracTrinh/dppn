# Uttiya 05

## English source

Uttiya 05. One of the Theras who accompanied <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> on his mission to Ceylon (Mhv.xii.8; Dpv.xii.12; Vin-a.i.70; Mbv.116). King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirimeghavaṇṇa</span> had an image of Uttiya made and placed in the image house which he built at the south-eastern corner of his palace. Cv.xxxvii.87.

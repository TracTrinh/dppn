# Uttarajīva

## English source

Uttarajīva. A monk of Pagan, who came to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> in Ceylon in CE 1154. He was accompanied by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chapaṭa</span> and brought with him a copy of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddanīti</span> which had just been written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggavaṁsa</span>. PLC. 185.

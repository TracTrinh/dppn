# Ugga 08

## English source

Ugga 08. A township (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nigama</span>) in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>. The Buddha stayed there at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddārāma</span> (Thag­a.i.74). The town was the residence of the banker <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ugga</span>, and was once a stronghold of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigaṇṭhas</span>; after the conversion of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ugga</span>’s family, through <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Subhaddā</span>’s intervention, the people became faithful followers of the Buddha and for some time <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span> lived there, at the Buddha’s special bidding, to preach to the new converts (Dhp­a.iii.465-9; according to Thag­a.i.65 <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Subhaddā</span> also lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ugga</span>, in a family of unbelievers). Probably the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uggārāma</span>, mentioned in the story of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅgaṇika Bhāradvāja</span> (Thag­a.i.339; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brethren</span>, 157, n.4), was also in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ugga</span>, in which case it was near the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="15" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇḍiya</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="16" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuru</span> country.

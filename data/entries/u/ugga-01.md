# Ugga 01

## English source

Ugga 01. A banker in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana Buddha</span>; he was one of the Buddha’s chief lay-supporters and built for him a residence, half a league in extent, on the site of the later <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. Ja.i.94; Bv.xxiv.24.

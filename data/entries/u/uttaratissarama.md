# Uttaratissārāma

## English source

Uttaratissārāma. A monastery in Ceylon, built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>, minister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span>. It was dedicated to the Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kambugalla</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapikkala</span>?). Mhv.xxxiii.92; Mhv­ṭ.622.

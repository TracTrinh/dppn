# Uttarakā

## English source

Uttarakā. A village of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bumus</span>. The Buddha once stayed there and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sunakkhatta</span> was in his company. At that time <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Korakkhattiya</span> was also staying there. DN.iii.6.

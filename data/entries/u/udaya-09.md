# Udaya 09

## English source

Udaya 09. King of Ceylon, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya III</span>. (A.C. 934-937). He was the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span>, a younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>., and his mother was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittī</span> or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittā</span>. He was first <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yuvarāja</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dappula IV</span>. and later succeeded him as king. Cv.liii.4, 13ff; Cv.Trs.i.172, n.5 and 174, n.6.

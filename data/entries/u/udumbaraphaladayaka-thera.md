# Udumbaraphaladāyaka Thera

## English source

Udumbaraphaladāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> he was a householder.

Meeting the Buddha walking along the bank of the river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinatā</span>, he plucked some figs and gave them to him (Ap.i.295).

He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paccaya</span> Thera. See Thag­a.i.341.

# Uruvelasutta 01

## English source

Uruvelasutta 01. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. The Buddha tells the monks how, when he was at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uruvelā</span>, under the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajapāla</span> tree, he realised that there was no one in the world worthy to be his teacher. So he decided to obey and serve the Dhamma. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahampati</span> appeared and told him that such had been the custom of previous Buddhas also (AN.ii.20f).

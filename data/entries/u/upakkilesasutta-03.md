# Upakkilesasutta 03

## English source

Upakkilesasutta 03. Four things prevent the sun and the moon from shining with their full brilliance – clouds, mist, smoke and dust and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhu</span>. Similarly four things diminish the holiness of ascetics and recluses – intoxicants, sex, money and wrong livelihood (AN.ii.53f).

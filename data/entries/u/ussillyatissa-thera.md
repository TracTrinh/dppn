# Ussillyatissa Thera

## English source

Ussillyatissa Thera. He was a resident of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span>. When the monks of this *vihāra* tried to introduce the *Vaitulya* heresy, he refused to be associated with them and went, with a few others, to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇagirivihāra</span>. There they formed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāgaliya</span> sect under a monk named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāgala</span>. PLC.66.

# Uluṅkasaddaka

## English source

Uluṅkasaddaka. The nickname of a young novice who lived with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span> and who later burnt down the Elder’s hut in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>.

His story is related in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṭidūsakajātaka</span> (Ja 321). (Ja.iii.71ff).

# Uppalavaṇṇā 02

## English source

Uppalavaṇṇā 02. One of the two daughters of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa I</span>. of Ceylon, the other being <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bodhī</span>. The king built a *vihāra* and called it by his own name together with those of his daughters. Cv.xxxix.11; see also Cv.Trs.i.43, n.7.

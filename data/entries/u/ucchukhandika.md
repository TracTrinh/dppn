# Ucchukhaṇḍika

## English source

Ucchukhaṇḍika. A Thera. He was a gate-keeper in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> during the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> and once gave to the Buddha a cut of sugar cane (Ap.ii.393). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosiya</span> Thera (Thag­a.i.431f).

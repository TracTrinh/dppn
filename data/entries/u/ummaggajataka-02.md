# Ummaggajātaka 02

## English source

Ummaggajātaka 02. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantapāsādikā</span> (iv.742) mentions a work called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gūḷha Ummaggajātaka</span> in a list of heretical works which pass off as the word of the Buddha, but which are the teachings of unbelievers.

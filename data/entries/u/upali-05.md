# Upāli 05

## English source

Upāli 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli Thera</span>. Head of the chapter of monks sent from Siam, at the request of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>, to re-establish the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Upasampadā</span> ordination in Ceylon. He was held in great esteem by the king of Ceylon and often preached to him. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli</span> died in Ceylon of an incurable disease of the nose, and his funeral obsequies were held with great solemnity. Cv.c.71, 94, 117, 127, 142.

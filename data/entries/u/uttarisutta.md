# Uttarisutta

## English source

Uttarisutta (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttarimanussadhammasutta</span>). There are six things without getting rid of which it is impossible to obtain qualities of a transcendental nature (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">uttarimanussadhammā</span>), to say nothing of Ariyan insight and wisdom.

Those things are forgetfulness, want of discrimination, lack of control of the senses, intemperance in eating, deceitfulness and prattle. AN.iii.430.

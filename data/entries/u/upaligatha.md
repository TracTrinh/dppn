# Upāligāthā

## English source

Upāligāthā. The stanzas in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli</span> *gahapati* uttered the Buddha’s praises when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nātaputta</span> asked him what kind of man was his new-found teacher (MN.i.386f). These verses are often quoted; they contain one hundred epithets as applied to the Buddha (Vin-a.i.257).

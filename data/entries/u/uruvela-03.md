# Uruvelā 03

## English source

Uruvelā 03. A village to which Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sugalā</span> (*q.v.*) fled, taking the sacred relics, the Alms Bowl and the Tooth Relic (Cv.lxxiv.88). It is identified with Etimole about five or six miles south-east of Monarāgala (Cv.Trs.ii.29, n.4). It is perhaps to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uruvelamaṇḍapa</span>.

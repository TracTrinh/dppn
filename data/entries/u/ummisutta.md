# Ummisutta

## English source

Ummisutta. Four perils have to be faced by those who go down into the water: waves, sharks, whirlpools and sea-monsters. Like perils await those who go from the household life to homelessness. AN.ii.123ff. This *Sutta* is included as part of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cātumasutta</span> (MN.i.460f).

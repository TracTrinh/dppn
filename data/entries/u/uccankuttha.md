# Uccaṅkuṭṭha

## English source

Uccaṅkuṭṭha. A locality in South India; it was the residence of many famous troop-leaders, whom <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span> won over to his side in his fight against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxxvii.78.

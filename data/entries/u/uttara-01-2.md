# Uttarā 01

## English source

Uttarā 01. A Therī. She was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapilavatthu</span> in a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākyan</span> family. She became a lady of the Bodhisatta’s court and later renounced the world with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajāpatī Gotamī</span>. When she was developing insight, the Buddha appeared before her to encourage her and she became an *Arahant*. Thīg.v.15; Thīg­a.21f.

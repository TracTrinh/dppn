# Uttaravinicchaya

## English source

Uttaravinicchaya. A commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayapiṭaka</span>, written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span> as a supplement to his own <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayavinicchaya</span>.

In manuscripts the two works are usually found together.

It was dedicated by the author to one of his pupils <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkhapāla</span>.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vācissara</span> wrote a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span> on it. Gv.59, 62.

The work has been published by the PTS (1928).

# Uppaṭikasutta

## English source

Uppaṭikasutta. On the five controlling faculties (*indriyāni*) – of discomfort, of unhappiness, of ease, of happiness and of indifference – and on how they are conditioned and how they cease to be (SN.v.213f). According to the Commentary (SN­a.iii.192), the title of this *Sutta* should be <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uppaṭipātikasutta</span> (the *Sutta* which deals with reference to what has gone before).

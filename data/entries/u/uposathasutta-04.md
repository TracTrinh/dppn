# Uposathasutta 04

## English source

Uposathasutta 04. (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uposathaṅgasutta</span>). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Visākhā</span>, having taken the *uposatha* vows, visits the Buddha at noontide in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migāramātupāsāda</span>. The Buddha explains to her that there are various ways of observing the *uposatha*; these he describes as the herdsman’s *uposatha*, the *uposatha* of the naked ascetics and the *uposatha* of the Ariyans. AN.i.206f.

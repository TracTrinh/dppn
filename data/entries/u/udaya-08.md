# Udaya 08

## English source

Udaya 08. King of Ceylon, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya II</span>. (A.C. 885-896), a younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. and afterwards his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yuvarāja</span> (Cv.li.63, 90ff; Cv.Trs.i.156, n.4). He succeeded <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. and reigned eleven years. During his reign the province of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> was brought once more under the rule of the king.

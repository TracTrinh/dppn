# Uṇhavalāhakā

## English source

Uṇhavalāhakā. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> who live in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cātummahārājika</span> world (NidA.108; Vibh­a.519). When they wish for heat to revel their bodies, the weather becomes hot (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uṇhasutta</span> above.)

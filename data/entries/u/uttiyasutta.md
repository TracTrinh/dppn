# Uttiyasutta

## English source

Uttiyasutta or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttikasutta</span> 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span> Thera visits the Buddha and asks him for an explanation of the five sensual elements (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kāmaguṇā</span>) mentioned by him. The Buddha explains them, and declares that they should be abandoned in order that the Noble Eightfold Path might be cultivated. SN.v.22.

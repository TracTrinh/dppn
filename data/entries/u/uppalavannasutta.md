# Uppalavaṇṇāsutta

## English source

Uppalavaṇṇāsutta. Records a conversation between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uppalavaṇṇā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> (SN.i.131f). The ideas are the same as those contained in the verses found in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Therīgāthā</span> (vv.230-5) but the wording is somewhat different.

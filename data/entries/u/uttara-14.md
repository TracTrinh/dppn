# Uttara 14

## English source

Uttara 14. The name of the Bodhisatta in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha Buddha</span>. He spent eighty crores in giving alms to the Buddha and the monks and later joined the Saṅgha. Ja.i.37-8; Bv.xii.11.

# Udakāsanadāyaka Thera

## English source

Udakāsanadāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Thirty-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he had been an ascetic, and at the door of his hermitage he placed a bench for travellers and provided water for them.

Fifteen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhisāma</span> (Ap.i.218).

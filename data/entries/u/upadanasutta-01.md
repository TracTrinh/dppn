# Upādānasutta 01

## English source

Upādānasutta 01. Enjoyment brings about craving, grasping, becoming, birth, etc., and, in turn, the whole mass of suffering.

It is like a fire with many loads of faggots, constantly supplied with fuel. SN.ii.84f.

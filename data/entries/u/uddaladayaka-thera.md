# Uddāladāyaka Thera

## English source

Uddāladāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth, thirty-one *kappas* ago, he saw a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakudha</span>, near a river, and gave him an *uddālaka* flower (Ap.i.225).

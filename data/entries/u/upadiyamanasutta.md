# Upādiyamānasutta

## English source

Upādiyamānasutta. When asked by a certain monk to give a topic for reflection, the Buddha tells him that if a man clings, he is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>’s bondsman. If he cling not, he is free. The Buddha asks the monk what be understands by this and is pleased with the explanation. The monk meditates on this and becomes an *Arahant*. SN.iii.73f.

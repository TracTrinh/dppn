# Upassayadāyakavimāna

## English source

Upassayadāyakavimāna. The abode of a pious man who was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> as a result of having given a night’s shelter to a holy monk. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vimāna</span> was of gold and was twelve <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yojanas</span> in height. Vv.64; Vv-a.291f.

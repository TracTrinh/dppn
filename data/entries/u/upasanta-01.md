# Upasanta 01

## English source

Upasanta 01. One of the two chief disciples of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> (Bv.xv.19; Ja.i.39). He was the son of the chaplain of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sucandaka</span> and the friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Santa</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Santa</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upasanta</span> visited the Buddha and for seven days entertained the Buddha and his monks. The two entered the Saṅgha with ninety-eight thousand followers. Bv­a.179f.

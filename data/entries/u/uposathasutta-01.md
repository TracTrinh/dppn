# Uposathasuttā 01

## English source

Uposathasuttā 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> dealing with questions asked by monks of the Buddha as to why certain <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span> (egg-born, womb-born, moisture-born and born without parents) should keep the fast day, divesting themselves of their <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> forms! The Buddha replies that they do so because they wish to be born in happy states SN.iii.241f.

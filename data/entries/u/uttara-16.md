# Uttara 16

## English source

Uttara 16. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> in his last birth (Bv.xi.21). He was the Bodhisatta. SN­a.ii.67; DN­a.ii.488; but see Ja.i.37 and Bv.xi.11, where the Bodhisatta’s name is given as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jaṭila Raṭṭhika</span>.

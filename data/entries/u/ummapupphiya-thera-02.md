# Ummāpupphiya Thera 02

## English source

Ummāpupphiya Thera 02. An Arahant. In a past birth he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> wrapt in *samādhi* and offered him, in homage, azure (*ummā*-) flowers, which formed a canopy above the Buddha’s head. Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ummāpupphiya</span> was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span>. Fifty-five *kappas* ago he was a king called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantacchadana</span>. Ap.i.258.

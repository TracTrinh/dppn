# Udakadāyikā Therī

## English source

Udakadāyikā Therī. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. In a previous birth she was a water-carrier and maintained her children on her wages. Having nothing else to give, she regularly provided water in a bath for others. As a result, she was born in heaven and was fifty times queen of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span>-king and twenty times queen of kings on earth. She could produce rain at will, and her body knew neither heat nor dirt. Ap.ii.5212.

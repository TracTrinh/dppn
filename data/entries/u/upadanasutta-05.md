# Upādānasutta 05

## English source

Upādānasutta 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambukhādaka</span> asks <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> what grasping is. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> says there are four kinds: grasping after sensuality, opinion, rule and ritual and theory of self.

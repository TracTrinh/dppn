# Upaṭṭhāyaka Thera

## English source

Upaṭṭhāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he provided <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> with a personal attendant (*upaṭṭhāka*). Fifty-seven *kappas* ago he was born as a king, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Balasena</span>. Ap.i.241.

# Uddakasutta

## English source

Uddakasutta. Preached by the Buddha. He states therein how <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uddaka Rāmaputta</span>, unjustifiably, claims to have mastered all learning and all <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, and explains what such learning and mastery really are (SN.v.83f).

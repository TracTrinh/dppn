# Uruvelasutta 02

## English source

Uruvelasutta 02. The Buddha tells the monks how a number of Brahmins had visited him at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uruvelā</span> and asked him if it were true that he did not reverence old men? He taught them how it was not old age which deserved reverence, but the qualities which men possessed, and he set forth those qualities (AN.ii.22f).

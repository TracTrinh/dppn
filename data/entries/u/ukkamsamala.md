# Ukkaṁsamāla

## English source

Ukkaṁsamāla. A learned monk of Ava. He was well versed in literature and wrote two books dealing with the Pāḷi language, the *Vaṇṇabodhana* and the *Likhananaya* (Sās., p.120).

# Upayasutta

## English source

Upayasutta (wrongly called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāyasutta</span>). Attachment (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upaya</span>) is bondage, aloofness is freedom. With the abandonment of lust, lust’s foothold is cut off and, thereby, rebirth, etc., is destroyed. SN.iii.53.

# Sīdā 01

## English source

Sīdā 01. A large river flowing from *Uttarahimavā*; its specific gravity is so slight that nothing can float on it. It flows through the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcanapabbata</span> and many thousands of ascetics live on its banks. Ja.vi.100, 101.

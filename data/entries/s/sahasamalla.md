# Sāhasamalla

## English source

Sāhasamalla. A king of Ceylon who belonged to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Okkāka</span> dynasty and reigned for only two years, till he was deposed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayasmanta</span>. Cv.lxxx.32; but see Cv.Trs.ii.130, n.1; the date of his accession (August 23rd, 1200) is said to be the only absolutely certain date in the history of Ceylon.

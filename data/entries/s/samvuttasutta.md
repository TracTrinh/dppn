# Saṁvuttasutta

## English source

Saṁvuttasutta. The three spheres – <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kāma</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">rūpa</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">arūpa</span> – must be given up and three kinds of training must be developed: greater virtue (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">adhisīla</span>), greater thought, greater insight. AN.iv.444.

# Saṁvuttasutta

## English source

Saṁvuttasutta. The three spheres – *kāma*, *rūpa*, *arūpa* – must be given up and three kinds of training must be developed: greater virtue (*adhisīla*), greater thought, greater insight. AN.iv.444.

# Samavāsasuttā

## English source

Samavāsasuttā. Two *Suttas* one preached to some householders on the road between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Verañjā</span>, the other to the monks on four ways of living together: a vile man with a vile woman, a vile man with a *Devī*, a *Deva* with a vile woman, a *Deva* with a *Devī*. A.ii.57ff.

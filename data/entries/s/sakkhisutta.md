# Sakkhisutta

## English source

Sakkhisutta. A monk who does not know, for a fact, what things partake of failure, of stability, distinction and penetration, and is not strenuous, zealous, or helpful such a one is incapable (*abhabba*) of any achievement. AN.iii.426.

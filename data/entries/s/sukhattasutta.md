# Sukhattasutta

## English source

Sukhattasutta. A farmer must first plough and harrow his field and then grow his seed at the proper season. He must further let water in and out as required. A monk must carry out the three preliminaries for *Arahant*-ship: training in the higher morality, higher thought, higher insight. AN.i.229.

# Sukhattasutta

## English source

Sukhattasutta. A farmer must first plough and harrow his field and then grow his seed at the proper season. He must further let water in and out as required. A monk must carry out the three preliminaries for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship: training in the higher morality, higher thought, higher insight. AN.i.229.

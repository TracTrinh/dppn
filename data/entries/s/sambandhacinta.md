# Sambandhacintā

## English source

Sambandhacintā. A work of the twelfth century by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅgharakkhita</span>. It is a grammatical treatise dealing with the Pāli verb and its use in syntax, together with a description of the six *kārakas* used with the verb in the sentence (PLC.199). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya Thera</span> of Pagan wrote a *ṭīkā* on it. Bode, 22.

# Sobhatisutta

## English source

Sobhatisutta. Monks, nuns and lay disciples, both male and female, who are accomplished in wisdom, disciplined, confident, deeply learned, hearers of the Dhamma, living according to the Dhamma – these illumine the religion. AN.ii.8.

# Sataraṁsika Thera

## English source

Sataraṁsika Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. In the past he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> and worshipped him. In this life he joined the Saṅgha at the age of seven, and rays constantly issued from his body. Sixty thousand <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was king four times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Roma</span>. Ap.i.104f.

# Sarīraṭṭhasutta

## English source

Sarīraṭṭhasutta. Ten conditions inherent in the body: cold and heat, hunger and thirst, evacuation and urination, restraint of body, speech, living, and the aggregate that produces becoming (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bhavasaṅkhāra</span>). AN.v.88.

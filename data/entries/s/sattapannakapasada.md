# Sattapaṇṇakapāsāda

## English source

Sattapaṇṇakapāsāda. A building in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> for the residence of the monks, evidently built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vohārikatissa</span> (Mhv.xxxvi.32). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> Commentary (Mhv­ṭ. 662) says that it was attached to the palace.

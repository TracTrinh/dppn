# Siddhattha 04

## English source

Siddhattha 04. A son of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa IV</span>. He was appointed governor of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Malaya</span> district and came to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Malayarājā</span>. He died young, however, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span> built a hall for the monks in his name and instituted an offering of alms. Cv.lii.68f.

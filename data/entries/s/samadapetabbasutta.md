# Samādapetabbasutta

## English source

Samādapetabbasutta. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> of three particulars regarding which advice should be given to one’s loved ones on unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, the Dhamma, and the Saṅgha. The results of such loyalty are unchanging. AN.i.222.

# Saññasāmika Thera

## English source

Saññasāmika Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he mastered the Vedas when only seven years old. He prepared a great sacrifice about which he consulted <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. The Buddha taught him that happiness was not to be found that way, and the boy, following his advice, was reborn in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span>. Ap.i.261.

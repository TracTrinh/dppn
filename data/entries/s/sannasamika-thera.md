# Saññasāmika Thera

## English source

Saññasāmika Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he mastered the Vedas when only seven years old. He prepared a great sacrifice about which he consulted <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. The Buddha taught him that happiness was not to be found that way, and the boy, following his advice, was reborn in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span>. Ap.i.261.

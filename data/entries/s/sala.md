# Sāla

## English source

Sāla. Brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paduma Buddha</span> and, later, his Chief Disciple. The people of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Usabhavatī</span> gave him a special <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kaṭhina</span> robe, in the making of which the Buddha himself assisted. Bv.ix.21; Bv­a.147f.

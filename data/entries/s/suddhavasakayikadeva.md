# Suddhāvāsakāyikādevā

## English source

Suddhāvāsakāyikādevā. A group of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>, inhabitants of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhāvāsā</span>, who appeared before the Buddha and recited three verses in praise of the Saṅgha.

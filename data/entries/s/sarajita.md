# Sarājita

## English source

Sarājita. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Niraya</span> in which those who die in battle are born (SN.iv.311). The Commentary (SN­a.iii.100) says that it is not a distinctive purgatory, but a part of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Avīci</span>, where fighters of all sorts fight in imagination. Cf. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarañjita</span>.

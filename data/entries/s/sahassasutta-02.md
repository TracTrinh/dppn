# Sahassasutta 02

## English source

Sahassasutta 02. One thousand nuns once visited the Buddha in the Royal Park (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājakārāma</span>) at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. He taught them that those possessed of unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, the Dhamma, and the Saṅgha, and cultivate the virtues dear to the Ariyans, they are assured of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>. Ja.v.360.

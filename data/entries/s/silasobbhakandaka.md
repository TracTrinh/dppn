# Silāsobbhakaṇḍaka

## English source

Silāsobbhakaṇḍaka. A village in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> lived for some time during the usurpation of his throne by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷas</span> (Mhv.xxxiii.51). The village was to the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessagiri</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> and near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabbatavihāra</span>. Mhv­ṭ.616.

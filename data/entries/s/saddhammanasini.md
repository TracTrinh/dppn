Saddhammanāsinī. A *ṭīkā* on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccāyana</span>’s grammar, by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siridhammavilāsa</span> of Pagan. Bode, 26.

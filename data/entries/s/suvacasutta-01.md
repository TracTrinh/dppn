# Suvacasutta 01

## English source

Suvacasutta 01. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> tells the Buddha of seven things which lead to a monk’s growth: reverence for the Buddha, the Dhamma, the Saṅgha, the training, concentration, fair speech, and good friendship. AN.iv.29.

# Subhadda 02

## English source

Subhadda 02. A youth who joined the Saṅgha under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇḍaññā Buddha</span> with ten thousand others, and became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. He was the Buddha’s <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Aggasāvaka</span>. Bv.iii.30; Ja.i.30; Bv­a.111.

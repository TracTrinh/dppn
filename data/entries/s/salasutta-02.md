# Sālāsutta 02

## English source

Sālāsutta 02. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sālā</span>. Just as the lion is the chief of animals, so is insight chief of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bodhipakkhiyā dhammā</span> (a list of which is given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span>). SN.v.227; on the title of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span>, see KS.v.202, n.3.

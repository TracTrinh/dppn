# Santuṭṭhisutta

## English source

Santuṭṭhisutta. Four things are easily available: rag robes, scraps of food, the root of a tree, and fermented cow’s urine (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pūtimutta</span>). A monk should learn to be content with these. AN.ii.26.

# Saṅkhapāla 03

## English source

Saṅkhapāla 03. A lake in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahiṁsakaraṭṭha</span>. It was the residence of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkhapāla</span>. From the lake rose the river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇṇapeṇṇā</span>. Ja.v.162.

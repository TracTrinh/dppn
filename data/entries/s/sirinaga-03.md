# Sirināga 03

## English source

Sirināga 03. A Brahmin. Wishing to become king of Ceylon, he sought to obtain the treasures from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇa Mahā Vihāra</span>. But <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bahula</span>, who knew the secret passage, refused to help him and was put to death. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirināga</span> pillaged the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Madhupiṭṭhiyacetiya</span>, and, with its wealth, became king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. Later he fell ill of gastric disease and was reborn in hell. Ras.ii.7f.

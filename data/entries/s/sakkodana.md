# Sakkodana

## English source

Sakkodana. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyan</span>, one of the five sons of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhahanu</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccānā</span> (Mhv.ii.20; Dpv.iii.45). He was brother to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhodana</span>, the Buddha’s father. MN­a.i.289.

# Sucaritasutta

## English source

Sucaritasutta. The Buddha says, in answer to a question, that those who practise good conduct are born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhabba</span>-world, because such is their wish. SN.iii.250.

# Sattapaduminiya Thera

## English source

Sattapaduminiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was a Brahmin, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nesāda</span>, and, seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> in the forest, he swept his hut and offered lotus flowers. Seven *kappas* ago he was king four times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pādapāvara</span>. Ap.i.254.

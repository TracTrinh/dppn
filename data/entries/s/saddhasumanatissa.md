# Saddhāsumanatissa

## English source

Saddhāsumanatissa. A monk of Ceylon. He joined the Saṅgha after gaining his parent’s (Sās-d.85f) consent with great, difficulty. Once, when on pilgrimage to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgadīpa</span>, he saw an assembly of monks, and, moved by the sight, sat, under a tree and developed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship.

# Sumanadevī 03

## English source

Sumanadevī 03. Step sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khallāṭanāga</span>. She had three sons – <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttara</span> who conspired against the king. On the failure of their conspiracy, they jumped into the fire on the spot where now stands the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagiri</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span>. Mhv­ṭ. 612.

# Soṇa 10

## English source

Soṇa 10. A *gahapatiputta* (son of a householder) of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. He is mentioned as having had two conversations with the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veḷuvana</span>: one on the impermanence of the body, feelings, etc., their origin and their cessation (SN.iii.48f); and, on another occasion, as to why some beings achieve complete cessation in this life and others do not. SN.iv.113.

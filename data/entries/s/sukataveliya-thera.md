# Sukaṭaveliya Thera

## English source

Sukaṭaveliya Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span> he was a garland-maker, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asita</span>, and one day, while on his way with a garland to the king, he met the Buddha and offered it to him. Fifty-two *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dvebhāra</span>. Ap.i.217.

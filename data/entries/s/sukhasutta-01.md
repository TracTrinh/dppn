# Sukhasutta 01

## English source

Sukhasutta 01. A monk who delights in Dhamma, in growth, in renunciation, in solitude, in being free of ill-will, and in non-diffuseness – such a one lives in happiness and contentment and will entirely destroy the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span>. AN.iii.431.

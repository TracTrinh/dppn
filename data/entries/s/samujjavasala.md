# Samujjavasālā

## English source

Samujjavasālā. A building in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. The clay from under the northern steps of the building was used for the construction of utensils used in the coronation festival of the kings of Ceylon. Mhv­ṭ. 307.

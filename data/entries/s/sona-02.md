# Soṇa 02

## English source

Soṇa 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soṇa Thera</span> The enemy and rival of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Buddha</span>, corresponding to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>. He conspired with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Paduma</span> to kill the Buddha, but was unsuccessful. Bv­a.174f.; for details see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī</span>.

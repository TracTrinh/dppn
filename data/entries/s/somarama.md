# Somārāma

## English source

Somārāma. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> built in the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somadevī</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span>, on the spot where she had seen, while in hiding, a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sāmaṇera</span>, who had used his hand to screen himself while urinating. Mhv.xxxiii.84; for identification see Mhv.Trs.235, n.3.

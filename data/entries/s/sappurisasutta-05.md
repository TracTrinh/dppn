# Sappurisasutta 05

## English source

Sappurisasutta 05. The 113th *Sutta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhimanikāya</span>, preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. The good monk does not exalt himself or disparage others on grounds of family distinction, of eminent wealth, personal fame, gifts and instruction received, capacity for preaching, knowledge of the Dhamma, observance of austere practices, etc.; the bad monk does. MN.iii.37-45.

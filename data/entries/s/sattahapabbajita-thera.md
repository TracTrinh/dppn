# Sattāhapabbajita Thera

## English source

Sattāhapabbajita Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he had a quarrel with his kinsmen and joined the Saṅgha under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> for seven days. Sixty-seven *kappas* ago he was king seven times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sunikkhamma</span>. Ap.i.242.

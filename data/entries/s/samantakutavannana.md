# Samantakūṭavaṇṇanā

## English source

Samantakūṭavaṇṇanā. A Pāli poem, of about eight hundred verses, written in the thirteenth century by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vedeha</span> Thera*,* at the request of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhula</span>, a monk. It contains a description of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantakūṭa</span> and the stories connected with it. PLC. 223f.

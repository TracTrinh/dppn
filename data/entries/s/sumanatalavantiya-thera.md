# Sumanatālavantiya Thera

## English source

Sumanatālavantiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he gave to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> a fan (*tālavanta*) covered with jasmine-flowers (Ap.i.293 = Ap.ii.408). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sātimattika</span>. Thag­a.i.359.

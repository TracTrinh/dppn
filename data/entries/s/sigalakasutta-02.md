# Sigālakasutta 02

## English source

Sigālakasutta 02. Some old jackals, afflicted with mange, can go wherever they like. There are some <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākyan</span> monks who cannot get even so much release. SN.ii.127; the Commentary (SN­a.ii.169) says that the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> was preached in reference to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>.

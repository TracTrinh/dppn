# Saddhammopāyana

## English source

Saddhammopāyana. A treatise in verse, in nineteen chapters, dealing with various topics, such as the difficulties of being born as a human, etc., by an author named *Abhayagiri Kavicakravarti* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, probably of the thirteenth century. A Commentary exists on it, called the *Saddhammopāyanaviggaha*. PLC.212.

# Saddhammaṭṭhitikā

## English source

Saddhammaṭṭhitikā. A Commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Niddesa</span>, written at the request of *Deva* Thera by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upasena</span> of Ceylon (Gv.61; Sās.69; PLC.117). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāsanavaṁsa</span> (p.69) calls it *Saddhammapajjotikā*, and it is probably known by that name in Ceylon.

# Sāmagalla

## English source

Sāmagalla. A village in Ceylon (Mhv.xxxiii.52 f). At the time of the compilation of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā</span> (Mhv­ṭ. 616), it was called Moragalla. Its full name was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mātuvelaṅga Sāmagalla</span>. It was in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Malaya</span> country, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī Abhaya</span> lived there during a part of his exile in the house of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tanasīva</span>.

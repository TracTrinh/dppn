# Sunisāvimānavatthu

## English source

Sunisāvimānavatthu. The story of a woman of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. She had no family, and one day, seeing an *Arahant* there begging for alms, she gave him a piece of cake (*pūvabhāgaṁ*). After death she was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> learnt her story. Vv.i.13; Vv-a.61.

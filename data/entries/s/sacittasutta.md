# Sacittasutta

## English source

Sacittasutta. Like a man or woman fond of self-adornment, examining the reflection of the face to see if it is clean, even so should a monk examine himself, and, finding evil qualities in himself, should strive to get rid of them as earnestly as though his head were on fire. AN.v.92f.

# Sattipaṇṇiya Thera

## English source

Sattipaṇṇiya Thera. An *Arahant*. One hundred thousand *kappas* ago he offered a *satti*-flower to the body of the Buddha (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara</span>) when it was being taken for cremation (Ap.ii.406).

He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimala</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.377.

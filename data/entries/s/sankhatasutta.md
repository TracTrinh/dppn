# Saṅkhatasutta

## English source

Saṅkhatasutta. There are three condition marks in that which is “conditioned” (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṅkhata</span>). Its genesis is apparent, likewise its passing away and its changeability while it persists. AN.i.152.

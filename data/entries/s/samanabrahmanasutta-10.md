# Samaṇabrāhmaṇasutta 10

## English source

Samaṇabrāhmaṇasutta 10. Recluses and Brahmins who have gained, are gaining, and will gain, the highest wisdom, do so through understanding of the Four Noble Truths. SN.v.416f.

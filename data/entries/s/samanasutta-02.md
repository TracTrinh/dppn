# Samaṇasutta 02

## English source

Samaṇasutta 02. On the four kinds of monk to be found in the Saṅgha <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpannas</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sakadāgāmīs</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Anāgāmīs</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. These are not to be found among the followers of contrary teachings. AN.ii.238.

# Samaṇasutta 02

## English source

Samaṇasutta 02. On the four kinds of monk to be found in the Saṅgha *Sotāpannas*, *Sakadāgāmīs*, *Anāgāmīs* and *Arahants*. These are not to be found among the followers of contrary teachings. AN.ii.238.

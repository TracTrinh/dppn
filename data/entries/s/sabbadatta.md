# Sabbadatta

## English source

Sabbadatta. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rammanagara</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>).

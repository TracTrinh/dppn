# Senaratana

## English source

Senaratana. King of Ceylon (1604-35 CE). He was the cousin and successor of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya I</span>. and had his capital at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍhanapura</span> (Kandy). Because the Paraṅgī (Portuguese) did much damage to Buddhism, he removed the Tooth Relic to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcasata</span>. He had three sons: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumārasīha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayapāla</span>, and the best known of all, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha</span> (afterwards <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha II</span>.). He divided the country among his sons seven years before his death. Cv.xcv.1ff.

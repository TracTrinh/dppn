# Sabbagandhiya Thera

## English source

Sabbagandhiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he offered flowers and incense to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> and gave him a garment of *koseyya*-cloth. Fifteen *kappas* ago he was a king, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sucela</span>. Ap.i.248f.

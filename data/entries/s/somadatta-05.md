# Somadatta 05

## English source

Somadatta 05. A Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. He once played dice with another Brahmin, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soma</span>, and won the latter’s possessions, including his upper garment and signet ring. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soma</span> said he could not walk home barefoot, nor face his family without his ring, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somadatta</span> returned his winnings and the two became great friends. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somadatta</span> was later sentenced to death for repeated adultery. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soma</span> discovered this he offered his life instead and was killed. He was reborn as a *Deva* and took <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somadatta</span> to the *Deva* world for a week, sending him back with a wish-conferring gem. Later <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somadatta</span> too was born near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soma</span>. Ras.i.46f.

# Sobhita 08

Sobhita 08. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sobhita Thera</span> He belonged to a Brahmin family of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">SƒÅvatthƒ´</span> and, after hearing the Buddha preach, entered the Sa·πÖgha, attaining <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship.

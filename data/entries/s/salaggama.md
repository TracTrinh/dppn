# Sālaggāma

## English source

Sālaggāma. A village in Ceylon given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi III</span>. to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mayettikassapāvāsa</span> *vihāra* (Cv.xliv.121). A river flowed through the village, and over the river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devappatirāja</span> built a bridge of forty staves (Cv.lxxxvi.41). Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu IV</span>. gave the village to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāyasatti</span> Thera of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhupariveṇa</span>. Cv.xc.92; see Cv.Trs.ii.209, n.2.

# Sīvalī 03

## English source

Sīvalī 03. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āmaṇḍagāmaṇī</span> and sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷābhaya</span>. She reigned in Ceylon for four months (in 93 CE.); she was then dethroned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Iḷanāga</span>. Her surname was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revatī</span>. Mhv.xxxv.14; Dpv.xxi.40f.

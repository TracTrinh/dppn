# Sāpūga

## English source

Sāpūga. A village of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koḷiyans</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> once stayed, and where he preached to the inhabitants. They were called Sāpūgiyā. AN.ii.194.

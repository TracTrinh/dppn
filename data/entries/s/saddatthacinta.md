# Saddatthacintā

## English source

Saddatthacintā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddatthabhedacintā</span>. A grammatical work by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhammasiri</span>. Gv.62, 72; Svd. 1246. Bode, 20, 22. There are several commentaries on it, the best known being the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Ṭīkā</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> of Pagan. There exist also a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nissaya</span> and a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dipanī</span> on the work.

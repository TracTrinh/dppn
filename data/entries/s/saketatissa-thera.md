# Sāketatissa Thera

## English source

Sāketatissa Thera. He was not fond of learning, saying that he had no time for it. When asked by the others, “Have you time for death?” he left them and went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇikāravālikasamudda</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>. There, during the rainy season, he was very helpful to the monks, both young and old, and at the end of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vassa</span>, on the full moon day, he preached a sermon which greatly inspired his listeners. AN­a.i.44; cf. DN­a.iii.1061.

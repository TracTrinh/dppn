# Saṅgharakkhita 02

## English source

Saṅgharakkhita 02. A monk, probably of Ceylon. Reference is made (Vsm.194; Dhs­a.200) to a novice under him who, seeing the king on an elephant’s back, developed thoughts of the foulness of the body and became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>.

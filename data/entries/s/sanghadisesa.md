# Saṅghādisesa

## English source

Saṅghādisesa. The second division of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārājikā</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayapiṭaka</span>. It comprises thirteen rules, violation of which involves temporary separation from the Saṅgha.

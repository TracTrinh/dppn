# Subrahmā 03

## English source

Subrahmā 03. A Brahmin who will be the father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya Buddha</span>. His wife will be <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmavatī</span> (Dhs-a.415; Vsm.434). He will be the chaplain of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkha</span> (Anāg.vs.96). According to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> (Mhv.xxxii.82) he is identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span>.

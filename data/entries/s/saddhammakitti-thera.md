# Saddhammakitti Thera

## English source

Saddhammakitti Thera. A pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ariyavaṁsa</span>. He lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ketumatī</span> (Taungo) and wrote the famous <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekakkharakosa</span>, and, probably, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sirivicittālaṅkāra</span>. Bode, 45 and n.3.

# Sakhāsutta 01

## English source

Sakhāsutta 01. Seven qualities which make a man a desirable friend: he gives what is hard to give, does what is hard to do, bears what is hard to bear, confesses his own secrets, keeps others’ secrets, does not forsake one in time of need, and does not despise one in time of one’s ruin. AN.iv.31.

# Sīhāsanadāyaka Thera 01

## English source

Sīhāsanadāyaka Thera 01. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. He erected a throne in honour of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> and made offerings to it. He was eight times king under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīluccaya</span>. Ap.i.188f.

# Samuddadatta 01

## English source

Samuddadatta 01. A monk whom <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span> persuaded to join him in trying to bring about schism in the Saṅgha (Vin.ii.196; iii.171). He was a favourite of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thullanandā</span>. Vin.iv. 66.

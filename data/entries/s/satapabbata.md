# Sātapabbata

## English source

Sātapabbata. A mountain in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhimadesa</span>, the abode of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sātāgira</span> (Snp­a.i.197). Many other <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span> also lived there, three thousand of whom were present at the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span>. DN.ii.257.

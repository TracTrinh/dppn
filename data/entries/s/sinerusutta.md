# Sinerusutta

## English source

Sinerusutta. The *dukkha* destroyed by the Noble Disciple (*Arahant*) compared with what is yet left to him until his death, is like seven grains of sand on the top of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sineru</span>. SN.v.457f.

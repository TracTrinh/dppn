# Sabhāsammata

## English source

Sabhāsammata. Thirteen *kappas* ago there were five kings of this name, previous births of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcahatthiya Thera</span>. Ap.i.193.

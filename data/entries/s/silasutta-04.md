# Sīlasutta 04

## English source

Sīlasutta 04. A monk who is virtuous, learned, has a pleasant and smooth speech, is able to develop the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span> at will, and has attained the destruction of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span> – such a one has achieved his goal. AN.iii.113.

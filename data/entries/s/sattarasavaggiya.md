# Sattarasavaggiyā

## English source

Sattarasavaggiyā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sattarasavaggiyas</span>. A group of 17 monks in the Buddha’s time, who seem to have incurred the enmity of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chabbaggīyas</span>. The latter turned them out of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> as soon as they had prepared it, and were violent towards them. Vin.ii.166; cf, Dhp­a.iii.48f.

# Saddhammañāṇa

## English source

Saddhammañāṇa. A scholar of Pagan of the early fourteenth century. He wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Vibhatyattha</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Chandosāraṭṭhavikāsinī</span> (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Vuttodayapañcikā</span>) on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vuttodaya</span>, and translated the Sanskrit grammar <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Kātantra</span> into Pāli. Bode, 26.

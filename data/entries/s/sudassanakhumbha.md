# Sudassanakhumbha

## English source

Sudassanakhumbha. A water pot, set on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Erāvaṇa</span>, for the use of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>. It is thirty leagues in circumference, and above it is a canopy twelve leagues wide made of precious stones. Surrounding it are thirty-two other pots. Dhp­a.i.273; Snp­a.i.369.

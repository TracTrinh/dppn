# Seniya 01

## English source

Seniya 01. A naked ascetic who practised the “Canine vow,” behaving like a dog. After his visit to the Buddha, as recorded in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuravatikasutta</span> (*q.v.*), he joined the Saṅgha and, in due course, became an *Arahant*. MN.i.387ff.

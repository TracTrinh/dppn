# Sūkaranijjhara

## English source

Sūkaranijjhara. A dyke constructed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. at the junction of the rivers <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkhavaḍḍhamānaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumbhīlavāna</span>. A canal was carried from there to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Gallaka</span> tank. Cv.lxviii.33f.

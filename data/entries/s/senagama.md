# Senāgāma

## English source

Senāgāma. A village and fortification near the *Kālavāpi*. It is mentioned in the account of the campaigns of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. and was once the headquarters of his *senāpati*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Deva</span>. Cv.lxx.131f., 245.

# Siddhattha 03

## English source

Siddhattha 03. An eminent monk in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>. Foreseeing dangers lying ahead, he prevented the king from marking out a very large site for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span> (Mhv.xxix.52). He was evidently at the head of the monks present at the Foundation Ceremony of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. See Mhv­ṭ. 522, 524.

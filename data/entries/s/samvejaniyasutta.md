# Saṁvejanīyasutta

## English source

Saṁvejanīyasutta. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vejanīyasutta</span>. Four spots connected with the Buddha – the scenes of his birth, his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, the preaching of his first sermon, and his death – which should be looked upon with emotion by believers. AN.ii.120; DN.ii.140.

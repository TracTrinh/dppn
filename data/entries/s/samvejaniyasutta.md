# Saṁvejanīyasutta

## English source

Saṁvejanīyasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">v.l.</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vejanīyasutta</span>. Four spots connected with the Buddha – the scenes of his birth, his Awakening, the preaching of his first sermon, and his death – which should be looked upon with emotion by believers. AN.ii.120; DN.ii.140.

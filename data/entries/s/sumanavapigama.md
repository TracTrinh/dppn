# Sumanavāpigāma

## English source

Sumanavāpigāma. A village in Ceylon, four *yojanas* to the south-east of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. Precious stones appeared there, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> wished to build the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. Mhv.xxviii.18.

# Sannidhāpaka Thera

## English source

Sannidhāpaka Thera. An *Arahant*. He had been a householder, and later an ascetic in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>. He gave the Buddha a gourd (*āmaṇḍa*) and water to drink. Forty-one *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Arindama</span>. Ap.i.97.

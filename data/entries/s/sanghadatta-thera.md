# Saṅghadatta Thera

## English source

Saṅghadatta Thera. He lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Lenavihāra</span> and for twelve years, during the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brāhmaṇatiya</span> famine, a deity looked after him. In the past he had given a meal to a hungry dog. Ras.ii.181f.

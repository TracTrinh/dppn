# Saṅkhavaḍḍhamāna

## English source

Saṅkhavaḍḍhamāna. A river in Ceylon, which unites with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumbhīlavāna</span>. At the spot where they unite, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūkaranijjhara</span> was constructed. Cv.lxviii.32; see Cv.Trs.i.279, n.4.

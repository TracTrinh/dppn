# Satipaṭṭhānasutta 02

## English source

Satipaṭṭhānasutta 02. A monk who is mindful regarding the rise and fall of things sees nothing attractive in the body, is conscious of the repulsiveness of food, has distaste for the world, and perceives impermanence in the “Compounded” such a one becomes either an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> in this life or an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Anāgāmī</span>. AN.iii.142.

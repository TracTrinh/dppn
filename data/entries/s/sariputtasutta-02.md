# Sāriputtasutta 02

## English source

Sāriputtasutta 02. The Buddha instructs <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> on how to train oneself in order to get rid of notions of “I” and “mine.” The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> contains a quotation from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udayapañha</span>. AN.i.133.

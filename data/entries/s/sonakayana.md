# Soṇakāyana

## English source

Soṇakāyana. A youth who, according to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhā Moggallāna</span>, went about saying that the Buddha proclaimed the ineffectiveness of all deeds, and thereby preached the annihilation of the world. The Buddha said he did not know Soṇakāyana, even by sight. AN.ii.232.

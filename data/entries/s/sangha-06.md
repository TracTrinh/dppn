# Saṅghā 06

## English source

Saṅghā 06. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittaggabodhi</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devā</span> and wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. She had a son (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa V</span>.). She built the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghasenapabbata</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ārāma</span> and placed a sapphire diadem on the stone image of the Buddha. Cv.i.58; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">li</span>. 6, 9, 86; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghā</span>’s son (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa V</span>.) is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dvayābhiseka sañjāta</span> (born of the twice-anointed queen). Tradition has it that after the death of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. she became the wife of his successor, who made her his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mahesī</span>.

# Sirivaḍḍha 13

## English source

Sirivaḍḍha 13. A householder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. When he was ill he sent word to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> asking him to visit him. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, went and preached to him on the four *satipaṭṭhāna*s. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍha</span> became an *Anāgāmī*. SN.v.176f.

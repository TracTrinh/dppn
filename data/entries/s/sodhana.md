# Sodhana

## English source

Sodhana. The elder brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span>, who later became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapilamaccha</span> (*q.v.*). His mother was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sādhinī</span> and his sister <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāpanā</span>. He entered the Saṅgha with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span>, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, and lived in the forest, engaged in meditation, attaining *Arahant*-ship soon after. Dhp­a.iv.37; Snp­a.i.305f.

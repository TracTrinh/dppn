# Saddanīti

## English source

Saddanīti. A very important grammatical work by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggavaṁsa</span> of Pagan. A few years after its completion in 1154, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttarajīva</span> visited the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> in Ceylon, and took with him, as a gift, a copy of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddanīti</span>, which was received with enthusiastic admiration. Gv.63, 72; Svd.1238; Bode, 16, 17.

# Saṅghupaṭṭhāka Thera

## English source

Saṅghupaṭṭhāka Thera. An *Arahant*. He was a servant in the monastery of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span> and waited on the Saṅgha with great devotion. Seven *kappas* ago he was king seven times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samotthata</span>. Ap.i.191.

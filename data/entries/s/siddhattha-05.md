# Siddhattha 05

## English source

Siddhattha 05. A monk of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uposathārāma</span>, to whom King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span> gave over the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rajatavihāra</span>. The monk had it repaired and had many additions made to it. Cv.c.238ff.

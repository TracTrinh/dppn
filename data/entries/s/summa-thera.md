# Summa Thera

## English source

Summa Thera. An incumbent of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpavihāra</span> in Ceylon. He was evidently an eminent commentator, and is quoted by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span>. *Tipiṭaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Nāga</span> was his pupil.

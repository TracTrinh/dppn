# Sabrahmakānisutta

## English source

Sabrahmakānisutta. Families in which parents are honoured and worshipped are like those in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> resides, or kindly teachers, or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>, or those worthy of offerings. AN.ii.70.

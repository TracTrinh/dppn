# Sādhuka

## English source

Sādhuka. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isidatta</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Purāṇa</span> once stayed (SN.v.348). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (SN­a.iii.215) the village belonged to them.

## Vietnamese translation

Sādhuka. Một ngôi làng ở Kosala, nơi Isidatta và Purāṇa từng lưu trú (SN.v.348). Buddhaghosa nói (SN­a.iii.215) rằng ngôi làng này thuộc về họ.

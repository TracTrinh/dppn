## English source

Sumanadāmadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he stood in front of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>, with a garland of *sumana*-flowers in his hand to honour him. Ap.i.293.

# Saddabindu

## English source

Saddabindu. A grammatical work by Kyocvā of Pagan. A Commentary on it, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līnatthavisodhanī</span>, is ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ñāṇavilāsa</span> of Pagan. There is also a *ṭīkā* called *Saddabinduvinicchaya* by *Sirisaddhammakitti* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Phussadeva</span>. Gv.64, 73; Sās.76; Bode, 25 and n.4.

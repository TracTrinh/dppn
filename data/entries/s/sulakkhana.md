# Sulakkhaṇā

Sulakkhaṇā. Wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Añjanasakka</span> and mother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Pajāpatī Gotamī</span>. Ap.ii.538 (vs. 115); but see Mhv.ii.18, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Añjana</span>’s wife is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yasodharā</span>. This may have been another wife.

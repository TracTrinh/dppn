# Sattiyā Sutta

## English source

Sattiyā Sutta. The Buddha tells a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sakkāyadiṭṭhi</span> should be got rid of by a monk as though he were smitten down by an impending sword. SN.i.13.

# Sūra-ambavana

## English source

Sūra-ambavana. A place in Ceylon, mentioned in the account of the wars of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxx.87). It evidently formed part of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambavana</span>, the district round the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambaṅgaṅga</span>. Cv.Trs.i.294, n.3.

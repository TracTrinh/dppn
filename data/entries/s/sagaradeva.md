# Sagaradeva

## English source

Sagaradeva. A king who dug the ocean (DN­a.i.91; MN­a.ii.689). The reference is evidently to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sagara</span> (*q.v.*), who, through anger, caused the earth to be excavated, and the earth came thus to have the ocean as her bosom. From this the ocean has come to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāgara</span>. *Mahā Bhārata* xii.29.

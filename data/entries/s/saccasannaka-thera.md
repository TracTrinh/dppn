# Saccasaññaka Thera

## English source

Saccasaññaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Twenty-nine *kappas* ago he heard <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span> preach, and was reborn in the *Deva* world. Twenty-six *kappas* ago he was King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekaphusita</span> (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekapaññita</span>).

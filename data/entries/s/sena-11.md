# Sena 11

## English source

Sena 11. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> usurper who, with his companion <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Guttika</span>, both of them horse dealers, defeated <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūratissa</span> and occupied the throne at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> for twenty-two years (177-55 B.C.). At the end of that time they were overpowered by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asela</span>. Mhv.xxi.10f.; Dpv.xviii.47f.; Cv.lxxxii.21.

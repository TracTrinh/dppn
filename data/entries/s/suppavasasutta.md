# Suppavāsāsutta

## English source

Suppavāsāsutta. The Buddha visits <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppavāsā</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sajjanela</span> and is entertained by her. He preaches to her that an Ariyan *upāsikā*, who gives food, gives four things to the receiver of the food: life, beauty, happiness and strength and is sure of happiness in later lives. AN.ii.62f.

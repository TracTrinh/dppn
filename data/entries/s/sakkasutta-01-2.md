# Sakkāsutta 01

## English source

Sakkāsutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāma</span> visits the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigrodhārāma</span>, where he was convalescing, and questions him regarding knowledge and concentration. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, wishing to save the Buddha’s strength, takes <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāma</span> aside and talks to him of *sīla*, *samādhi* and *paññā*, both of the learner (*sekha*) and of the adept (*asekha*). AN.i.219f.

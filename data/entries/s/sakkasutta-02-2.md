# Sakkāsutta 02

## English source

Sakkāsutta 02. A large number of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span> visit the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigrodhārāma</span>, and he impresses on them the very great advantage of keeping the fast day well.

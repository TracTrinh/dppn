# Sumaṅgala 11

## English source

Sumaṅgala 11. A monk of Ceylon, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. He wrote a *ṭīkā* on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammāvatāra</span>, called the *Abhidhammatthavibhāvinī* (PLC. 108, 173). He also wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāraṭṭhasālinī</span>, on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saccasaṅkhepa</span>. PLC.200; Gv.62, 72.

# Saccanāmā

Saccanāmā. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabbanāmā</span>. One of the two chief women disciples of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadassī Buddha</span>. Bv.xvi.19; Ja.i.39.

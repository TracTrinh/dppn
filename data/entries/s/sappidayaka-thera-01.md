# Sappidāyaka Thera 01

## English source

Sappidāyaka Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Fifty *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samodaka</span>. Ap.i.184.

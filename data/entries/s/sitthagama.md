# Sitthagāma

## English source

Sitthagāma. A village which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena IV</span>. converted into a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pariveṇa</span>, evidently for his retirement (Cv.liv.6). Later, a monk, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammamitta</span>, lived there and wrote a Commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhamma</span>. Cv.liv.35.

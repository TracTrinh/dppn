# Subhadeva

## English source

Subhadeva. Uncle of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayanāga</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayanāga</span> had <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhadeva</span>’s hands and feet cut off and left him behind, that he might bring about division in the kingdom of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vohārikatissa</span>. When the time was come, he sent word to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayanāga</span>, and the latter seized the throne. Mhv.xxxvi.45f.; Mhv­ṭ.663.

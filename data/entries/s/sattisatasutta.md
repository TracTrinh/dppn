# Sattisatasutta

## English source

Sattisatasutta. A wise householder should be glad if an offer were made to him that he should comprehend the Four Noble Truths after being tormented with one hundred spears three times a day for one hundred years. For incalculable is *Saṁsāra*. SN.v.440.

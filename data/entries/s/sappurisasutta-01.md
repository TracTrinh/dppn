# Sappurisasutta 01

## English source

Sappurisasutta 01. The unworthy man (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">asappurisa</span>) always speaks what is discreditable to another, never what is discreditable to himself, and always sings his own praises. The worthy man is just the reverse. AN.ii.77.

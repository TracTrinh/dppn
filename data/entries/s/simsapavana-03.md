# Siṁsapāvana 03

## English source

Siṁsapāvana 03. A grove to the north of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Setavyā</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumāra Kassapa</span> once stayed. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> also once stayed there during a journey (Dhp­a.i.59). It was the scene of the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāyāsisutta</span>. DN.ii.316.

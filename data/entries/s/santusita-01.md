# Santusita 01

## English source

Santusita 01. Chief of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span> world (DN.i.218; AN.iv.243; SN.iv.280). It was the name of the Bodhisatta when he was in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span> (Bv­a. 45; Ja.i.48) and also that of his successor (Ja.i.81). At important festivals, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Santusita</span> appears with a yak tail whisk, e.g., Mhv.xxxi.78.

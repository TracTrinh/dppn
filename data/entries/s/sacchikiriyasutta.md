# Sacchikiriyāsutta

## English source

Sacchikiriyāsutta. The eight releases must be realized by one’s own person; former life by recollections; the death and rebirth of beings by sight; and the destruction of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span> by wisdom. AN.ii.182.

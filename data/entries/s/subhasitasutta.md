# Subhāsitasutta

## English source

Subhāsitasutta. The Buddha tells the monks that good speech is that which is spoken well, righteously, affectionately and truthfully. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅgīsa</span>, who is present, renders the Buddha’s speech into verse. SN.i.188; cf. Snp., p.78f.

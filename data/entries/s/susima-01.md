# Susīma 01

## English source

Susīma 01. The Bodhisatta in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span>. He was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Sāla</span> Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Campaka</span> and became an ascetic of great power. He heard the Buddha preach at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudassana</span> and was converted. Ja.i.39; Bv.xv.9f.; Bv­a.180.

# Saddasaññaka Thera 02

## English source

Saddasaññaka Thera 02. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he saw an eclipse and heard the great tumult which announced the arrival of a Buddha in the world. With devoted heart he thought of the Buddha, though he did not see him. Ap.i.245.

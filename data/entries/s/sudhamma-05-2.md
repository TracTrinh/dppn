# Sudhammā 05

## English source

Sudhammā 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudhammā Therī</span>. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. She lived in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. At the Buddha’s wish, she took a branch of the Bodhi-tree with her and planted it in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sāgaruyyāna</span> in Ceylon. Dpv.xvii.19f.; Mhv.xv.147f.

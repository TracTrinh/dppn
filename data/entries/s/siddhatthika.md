# Siddhatthikā

## English source

Siddhatthikā. One of the seven heterodox sects which branched off in the second century after the Buddha’s death (Mhv.v.12; Dpv.v.54). They belonged to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Andhakas</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>) and held the same views. Kv.104; Introd.xx.

# Somadattajātaka 01

## English source

Somadattajātaka 01. (Ja 211). The story of the Bodhisatta when he was born as the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggidatta</span>. For details see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggidatta</span> (Ja.ii.164-7). The story was related in reference to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lāḷudāyi</span> who is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggidatta</span>, and is repeated in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammapada Commentary</span>, Dhp­a.iii.123ff.

# Sudassī

## English source

Sudassī. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> world, one of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhāvāsā</span>. The inhabitants of this world are friendly with those of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Akaniṭṭhā</span>. (DN.ii.52; MN.i.259; DN.iii.237; MN.iii.103; Khp­a.120; Vsm.473). Some <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Anāgāmīs</span> obtain <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Parinibbāna</span> in Sudassī. Paṭis-a.319.

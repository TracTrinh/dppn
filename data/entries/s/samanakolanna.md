# Samaṇakolañña

## English source

Samaṇakolañña. A king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāliṅga</span>. He was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span>, but when he was riding his elephant through the air, he could not pass over the spot where the Bodhi-tree was (Mil. p. 256). The reference is evidently to the story in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāliṅgabodhijātaka</span> (Ja 479), but there the king’s name is not given. Ja.iv.232f.

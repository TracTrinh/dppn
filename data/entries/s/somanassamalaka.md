# Somanassamālaka

## English source

Somanassamālaka. A sacred spot in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span> preached during his visit to Ceylon (Mhv.xv.159). Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span>, brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span>, built a *cetiya* there. Mhv­ṭ. 358.

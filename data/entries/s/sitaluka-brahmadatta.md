# Sītāluka Brahmadatta

## English source

Sītāluka Brahmadatta. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. He left the world and lived in the forest, but there he lacked food and drinks and was troubled by heat and cold and insects. He wished to go elsewhere, but mastered the desire and, after living there for seven years, became a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span>. His verse is included in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khaggavisāṇasutta</span>. Snp.vs.52; Snp­a.i.101.

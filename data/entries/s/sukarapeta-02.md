# Sūkarapeta 02

## English source

Sūkarapeta 02. A monk in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, reborn as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span> on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>, where he was seen by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada</span>. He had been restrained in his bodily actions, but had an evil tongue. He had the face of a pig. Pv.i.3; Pv-a.9f.

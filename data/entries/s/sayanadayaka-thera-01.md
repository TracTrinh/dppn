# Sayanadāyaka Thera 01

## English source

Sayanadāyaka Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago be gave a bed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Fifty-one *kappas* ago he was a king, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Varuṇa</span>. Ap.i.98f.

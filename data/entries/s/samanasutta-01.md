# Samaṇasutta 01

## English source

Samaṇasutta 01. A monk has three pursuits: training in the higher morality, higher thought and higher insight. He must follow these pursuits with keenness; otherwise his presence in the Saṅgha will be like that of an ass in a herd of cattle. AN.i.229.

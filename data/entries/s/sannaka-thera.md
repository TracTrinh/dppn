# Saññaka Thera

## English source

Saññaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he saw the rag robe of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span> hanging on a tree and worshipped it. Four *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dumasāra</span>. Ap.i.120.

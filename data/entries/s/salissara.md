# Sālissara

## English source

Sālissara. A sage; the chief disciple of the Bodhisatta in his birth as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarabhaṅga</span>. For his story see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarabhaṅgajātaka</span> (Ja 522) and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indriyajātaka</span> (Ja 423). He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. Ja.iii.469; v.151.

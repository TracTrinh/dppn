# Sālāsutta 01

## English source

Sālāsutta 01. The Buddha, while staying at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sālā</span>, addresses the monks, teaching them the necessity of the teaching the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s by novices, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sekhas</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. SN.v.144f.

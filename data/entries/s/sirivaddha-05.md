# Sirivaḍḍha 05

## English source

Sirivaḍḍha 05. An ascetic who gave grass for his seat to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Buddha</span>. He was originally a rich man and had left the world. The Buddha preached to him and his followers. Bv­a. 192,193.

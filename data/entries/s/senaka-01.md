# Senaka 01

## English source

Senaka 01. A minister of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vedeha</span>. See the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Ummaggajātaka</span> (Ja 546). a large portion of which is devoted to the battle of wits between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahosadha</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senaka</span> once killed a courtezan in order to take her ornaments (Ja.vi.382). He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saccaka</span>. Ja.vi.478.

# Sakacittaniya Thera

## English source

Sakacittaniya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, he made a *Thūpa* of bamboos in the name of the Buddha and offered flowers to it. Eighty *kappas* ago he was a king. Ap.i.111f.

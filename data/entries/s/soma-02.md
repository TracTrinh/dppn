# Soma 02

## English source

Soma 02. A *Deva* to whom sacrifice is offered; he is generally mentioned with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Varuṇa</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajāpatī</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yama</span> (DN.244;ii.259; Ja.v.28; vi.201, 568,571). In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āṭānāṭiyasutta</span> (DN.iii.204) he is spoken of as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> chief. He is identified with the Moon god of later literature (e.g., Cv.lxii.5; lxiii.14), the founder of the *Somavaṁsa* (dynasty).

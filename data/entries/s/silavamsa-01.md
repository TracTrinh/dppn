# Sīlavaṁsa 01

## English source

Sīlavaṁsa 01. A monk of Ava, of the fifteenth century. He wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhālaṅkāra</span>, a poetical version of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedhakathā</span>, a poem on his native city, therein called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabbatabbhantara</span>. He also wrote an *atthayojanā* on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nettippakaraṇa</span> in Burmese, and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇavatthu</span>. Bode, 43.

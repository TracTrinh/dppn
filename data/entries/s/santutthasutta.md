# Santuṭṭhasutta

## English source

Santuṭṭhasutta. The Buddha speaks in praise of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span>, his contentment with whatever he receives in the way of robes, alms, lodgings and medicaments. He is an example worthy of imitation. SN.ii.194.

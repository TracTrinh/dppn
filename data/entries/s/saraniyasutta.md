# Sārāṇīyasutta

## English source

Sārāṇīyasutta. A king must remember where he was born, where he was anointed, and where he won a battle. A monk must remember where he was ordained, where he realized the four Ariyan Truths, and where he attained <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship. AN.i.106f.

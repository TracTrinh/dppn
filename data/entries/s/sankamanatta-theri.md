# Saṅkamanattā Therī

## English source

Saṅkamanattā Therī. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇḍañña Buddha</span> walking along the road, she came out of her house and prostrated herself. The Buddha touched her head with his foot. Ap.ii.514.

# Saṅgāravasutta 06

## English source

Saṅgāravasutta 06. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅgārava</span>, in answer to a question, that wrong view, wrong thinking, speech, action, living, effort, mindfulness, concentration, knowledge and release, are the “hither shore” and their opposites the “further shore.” AN.v.232f.

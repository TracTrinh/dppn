# Sigālakasutta 01

## English source

Sigālakasutta 01. An old jackal, afflicted by mange, finds no pleasure in lonely places, or in the woods, or in the open air. Wherever he goes he falls into misfortune and disaster. Even so is a monk whose heart is possessed by gains, favours, or flattery. SN.ii.230.

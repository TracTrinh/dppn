# Samiddhisutta 01

## English source

Samiddhisutta 01. The story of a nymph who tried to tempt <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi Thera</span>. The story is similar to the introductory story of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhijātaka</span>, but the discussion between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi</span> and the *Devatā* is given at greater length. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi</span> told her of his aim in leading the religious life, she wished to know more of the Buddha’s teaching, and asked him to find an opportunity for her to see the Buddha. This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi</span> did, and the Buddha preached to her. SN.i.8 ff.

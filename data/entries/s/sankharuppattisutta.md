# Saṅkhāruppattisutta

## English source

Saṅkhāruppattisutta. The 120th *Sutta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhimanikāya</span>. A monk who possesses the five *saṅkhāras* (“plastic forces”) of faith, virtue, learning, munificence and wisdom, if he cultivate and develop them, can be reborn in any condition or world he may desire; he can even, thereby, win *Nibbāna* (MN.iii.99ff).

The *Sutta* contains a long list of *Deva*-worlds and *Brahma* worlds.

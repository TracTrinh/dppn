# Sañjaya 03

## English source

Sañjaya 03. Father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessantara</span>. He was the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sivi</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetuttara</span>, and after his father’s death succeeded him as king. His wife was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phusatī</span>. He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhodana</span> of the present age. See the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessantarajātaka</span> (Ja 547) for details. He is mentioned in a list of kings at Dpv.iii.42.

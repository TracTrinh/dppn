# Sudhāpiṇḍiya Thera

## English source

Sudhāpiṇḍiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he gave mortar (*sudhāpiṇḍa*) for the construction of the *cetiya* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Thirty *kappas* ago he was king thirteen times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṭisaṅkhāra</span>. Ap.i.133.

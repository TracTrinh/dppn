# Sambahulasutta 02

## English source

Sambahulasutta 02. A deity in a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosalan</span> forest tract laments when the monks, who have been living there, depart on tour. Another deity comforts him saying that monks are free and own no home. SN.i.199.

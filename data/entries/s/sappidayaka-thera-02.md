# Sappidāyaka Thera 02

## English source

Sappidāyaka Thera 02. Seventeen *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jutideva</span>. Ap.i.212.

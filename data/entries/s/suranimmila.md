# Sūranimmila

## English source

Sūranimmila. One of the ten warriors of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>. For details see Mhv.xxiii.19f. According to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rasavāhinī</span> (ii.71) he was so-called because he drank a large quantity of toddy before the attack on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijitapura</span>.

# Sumanāveḷiya Thera

## English source

Sumanāveḷiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he placed a bunch of *sumana*-flowers in front of the seat of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span>. After death he was born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nimmanaratī</span>-world; eleven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahassāra</span>. Ap.i.264.

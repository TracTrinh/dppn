# Sudhammā 04

## English source

Sudhammā 04. The sixth daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kikī</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. She is identified sometimes with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Māyā</span> (e.g., Ja.vi.481) and sometimes with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadinnā</span>, e.g., Ap.ii.546, 548; Thīg­a.104, 114.

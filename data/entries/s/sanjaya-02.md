# Sañjaya 02

## English source

Sañjaya 02. A *rājā* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tagara</span>. He renounced the world with ninety crores of others and became an ascetic. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadassī Buddha</span> preached to them and they all attained *Arahant*-ship. Bv.xvi.3; Bv­a.183.

# Saddasaññaka Thera 01

## English source

Saddasaññaka Thera 01. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he heard <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Buddha</span> preach in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. Ap.i.131.

## Vietnamese translation

Saddasaññaka Thera 01. Một vị *Arahant*. Chín mươi hai *kappa* trước ngài đã nghe Phussa Buddha thuyết pháp ở Himavā. Ap.i.131.

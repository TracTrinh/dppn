# Soṇa 07

## English source

Soṇa 07. A minister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sena</span> and a follower of the heretic monk, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghamitta</span>. He helped <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghamitta</span> in the despoliation of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lohapāsāda</span> and other buildings. He was killed in an attempt to destroy the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpārāma</span> (Mhv.xxxvii.10, 13, 28). In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpavaṁsa</span> (Dpv.xxii.70, 71) he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāpasoṇa</span>.

Chưa dịch.

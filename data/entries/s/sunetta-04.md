# Sunetta 04

## English source

Sunetta 04. A teacher of old. He had numerous disciples, and those who followed his teachings were reborn in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> world and in various other worlds. Then Sunetta, seeing that some among his disciples were as good as himself, developed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mettā</span> to a much greater degree; but even so, he could not free himself from birth, old age, etc. It was because he had not comprehended Noble Conduct, Noble Concentration, Noble Wisdom, Noble Release. AN.iv.103f.; he is referred to at AN.iii.371; iv.135.

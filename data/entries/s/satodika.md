# Sātodikā

## English source

Sātodikā. A river in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suraṭṭha</span> (Surat). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sālissara</span> lived in a hermitage on its banks after he left the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaviṭṭhaka</span> hermitage. Ja.iii.463; but at Ja.v.133 it is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meṇḍissara</span> who lived there.

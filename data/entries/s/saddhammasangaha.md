# Saddhammasaṅgaha

## English source

Saddhammasaṅgaha. A Chronicle, in eleven chapters, containing a history of Buddhism, commencing with the three Convocations. It was written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammakitti</span>, a monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayodhyā</span>, and probably belonged to the fourteenth century.

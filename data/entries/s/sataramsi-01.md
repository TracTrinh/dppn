# Sataraṁsi 01

## English source

Sataraṁsi 01. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> of ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago to whom <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambayāgudāyaka</span>, in a previous birth, gave a meal of mango rice-gruel (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ambayāgu</span>). Ap.i.284.

See also under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sigālapitā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambula Kaccāyana</span>, who are mentioned as having given him <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tāla</span>-fruits as offerings.

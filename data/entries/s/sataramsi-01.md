# Sataraṁsi 01

## English source

Sataraṁsi 01. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> of ninety-four *kappas* ago to whom <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambayāgudāyaka</span>, in a previous birth, gave a meal of mango rice-gruel (? *ambayāgu*). Ap.i.284.

See also under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sigālapitā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambula Kaccāyana</span>, who are mentioned as having given him *tāla*-fruits as offerings.

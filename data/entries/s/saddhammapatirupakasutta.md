# Saddhammapaṭirūpakasutta

## English source

Saddhammapaṭirūpakasutta. The Buddha explains to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span> how it comes about in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sāsana</span> that there are more precepts and less members of the Saṅgha becoming <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. Then a counterfeit doctrine arises and the true doctrine disappears. SN.ii.223f.

# Sīhalacetikā

## English source

Sīhalacetikā. It is said that once sixty monks heard a Sinhalese girl singing in her own language, on birth, old age, and death. They reflected on her words and became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. Snp­a.ii.397.

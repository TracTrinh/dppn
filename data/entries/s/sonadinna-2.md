# Soṇadinnā

## English source

Soṇadinnā. An *upāsikā* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nālandā</span>, a very good woman. She listened to the Buddha preaching and became a *Sotāpanna*. After death she was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> met her and learnt her story. Vv.ii.6; Vv-a.114f.

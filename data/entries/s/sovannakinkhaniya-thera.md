# Sovaṇṇakiṅkhaṇiya Thera

## English source

Sovaṇṇakiṅkhaṇiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> he was an ascetic, and built a *Thūpa* of sand in the name of the Buddha. Because he was too ill to visit the Buddha, he offered it *soṇṇakiṅkhanika* flowers. Ap.ii.388.

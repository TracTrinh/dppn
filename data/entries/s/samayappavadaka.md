# Samayappavādaka

## English source

Samayappavādaka. A descriptive epithet of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallikārāma</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. It was so-called because teachers of diverse views used to meet there and state their opinions (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">attano attano samayaṁ pavadanti</span>). MN­a.ii.710; DN­a.ii.365.

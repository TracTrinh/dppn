# Saparivāriya Thera

## English source

Saparivāriya Thera. An *Arahant*. He built a palisade of sandalwood round the *Thūpa* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>. Fifteen *kappas* ago he was king eight times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pamatta</span> (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samagga</span>). Ap.i.172.

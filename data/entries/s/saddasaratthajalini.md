# Saddasāratthajālinī

## English source

Saddasāratthajālinī. An important grammar by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgita</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khantakakhipa</span>) of Sagu. It was written under the patronage of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisīhasūra</span>. There is a Commentary on it, called *Sāramañjūsā*, and a *ṭīkā* by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vepullabuddhi</span>. Bode, 27 and n.5, 28; Ov. 64, 74; Svd.1249.

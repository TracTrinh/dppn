# Sundarikā 01

## English source

Sundarikā 01. A river in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>, reputed to be efficacious in washing away sins (MN.i.39). There <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sundarika Bhāradvāja</span> held sacrifices in honour of Agni and met the Buddha during such a sacrifice. SN.i.167; Snp.p.79, etc.

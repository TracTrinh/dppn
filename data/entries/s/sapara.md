# Sapara

## English source

Sapara. A province in Ceylon (Cv.lxviii.8), also called Saparagamu (Cv.xciv.12). It is said to have derived its name from the inhabitants, the *Saparā* or *Sabarā* (*Savarā*), probably another name for the Veddas.

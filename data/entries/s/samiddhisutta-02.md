# Samiddhisutta 02

## English source

Samiddhisutta 02. Describes the unsuccessful attempt of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> to frighten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi Thera</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> made a tremendous noise near him, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi</span> sought the Buddha’s advice. The Buddha explained to him that the noise was made by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>, and the next time he came <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddhi</span> challenged him to do his worst. SN.i.119 f.

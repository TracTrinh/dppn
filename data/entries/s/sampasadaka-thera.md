# Sampasādaka Thera

## English source

Sampasādaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago, when in danger of his life, he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> and asked for his protection. The Buddha exhorted him to put his faith in the Saṅgha. He died soon afterwards and was reborn in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span>. Ap.i.250.

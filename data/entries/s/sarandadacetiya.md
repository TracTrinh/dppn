# Sārandadacetiya

## English source

Sārandadacetiya. A shrine of pre-Buddhistic worship at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesālī</span>. It was dedicated to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sārandada</span>, but, later, a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> was erected on the site for the Buddha and his Saṅgha. DN.ii.75, 102; Ud.vi.1; DN­a.ii.521; Ud­a.323; AN­a.ii.701.

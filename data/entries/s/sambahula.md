# Sambahula

## English source

Sambahula. A chief of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amaranagara</span>, where he and his brother, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumitta</span>, ruled. He heard <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> preach at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amaruyyāna</span> and became an *Arahant* (Bv­a.186). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambala</span> 1.

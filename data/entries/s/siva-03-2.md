# Sīva 03

## English source

Sīva 03. King of Ceylon (522 CE.). He was the maternal uncle of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumāradhātusena</span>, whom he killed in order to seize the throne. He reigned only twenty-five days, and was killed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatisssa</span>. Cv.xli.1-5.

# Tintasīsakola

## English source

Tintasīsakola. A region, thirty leagues in extent, near the spot where the stream from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anotatta</span> falls from a height of sixty leagues. The soil, being constantly sprinkled by the drops of water, is extremely soft and plastic and clay was obtained from there for the building of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. Mhv­ṭ.515.

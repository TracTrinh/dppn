# Tissavaḍḍhamānaka

## English source

Tissavaḍḍhamānaka. A locality in Ceylon, to the east of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> (Mhv.xxxv.84). It contained the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mucelavihāra</span> and a tank of the same name (Mhv.xxxvii.48).

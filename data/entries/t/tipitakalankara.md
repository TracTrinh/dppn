# Tipiṭakālaṅkāra

## English source

Tipiṭakālaṅkāra. A monk of Prome in Burma. He enjoyed the patronage of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Surakitti</span>, king of Burma, but for a time lived in retreat in Tiriyapabbata. Among his works are the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yasavaḍḍhanavatthu</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayālaṅkāraṭīkā</span>. Sās., p.106; Bode, 53f.

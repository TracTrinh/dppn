# Thūlathana

## English source

Thūlathana. Second son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhātissa</span> and king of Ceylon (59 B.C.).

On his father’s death the ministers crowned him king, but after a reign of only one month and a few days his elder brother <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lañjatissa</span> overpowered him and seized the throne.

Thūlathana built the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kandaravihāra</span> (Mhv.xxxiii.15ff) and a *cetiya* on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirīsamālaka</span> (Mhv­ṭ.355).

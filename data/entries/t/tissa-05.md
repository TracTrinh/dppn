# Tissa 05

## English source

Tissa 05. An *Aggasāvaka* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. He was the Buddha’s brother and, having renounced the household, became an ascetic. On hearing that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span> had become Buddha, he visited him but expressed great disappointment on discovering that he ate flesh food (*āmagandha*). The Buddha taught him that *āmagandha* was not really flesh but the *kilesas* which corrupt the heart, and he preached to him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āmagandhasutta</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> immediately entered the Saṅgha and became an *Aggasāvaka* (Bv.xxv.39; Snp­a.i.280-2, 293; DN.ii.4). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>’s father was born as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhadda</span> in this age. Ap.i.101.

# Tikaṇṇipupphiya

## English source

Tikaṇṇipupphiya. An *Arahant* Thera. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was born in heaven, and realising that this was due to the Buddha’s teaching, he offered a flower in the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>.

Seventy-three *kappas* ago he became king four times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Naruttama</span>. Ap.i.195.

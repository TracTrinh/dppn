# Tāpanā

## English source

Tāpanā. The younger sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sodhana</span>, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. Her mother was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sādhanī</span>. They all entered the Saṅgha, but introduced dissension there.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span> was reborn as a fish in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aciravatī</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāpanā</span> was born in the *Mahā Niraya*. Snp­a.i.305f, 309; Dhp­a.iv.37, 39.

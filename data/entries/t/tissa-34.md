# Tissa 34

## English source

Tissa 34. Nephew of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khallāṭanāga</span> and son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumanadevī</span>, step-sister to the king. With his brothers, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttara</span>, he conspired to kill the king. But the conspiracy failed and they committed suicide. Mhv­ṭ.612.

# Tissametteyyasutta

## English source

Tissametteyyasutta. Preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> (7) and his friend <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya</span>, at the latter’s request. It deals with the evils that follow in the train of sexual intercourse. Snp.p.160f; Snp­a.ii.535f.

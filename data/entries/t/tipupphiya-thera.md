# Tipupphiya Thera

## English source

Tipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he offered three flowers to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭalī</span>, the Bodhi-tree of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Thirty-three *kappas* ago he became king thirteen times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantapāsādika</span>. Ap.i.136.

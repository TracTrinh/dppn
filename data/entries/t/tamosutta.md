# Tamosutta

## English source

Tamosutta. The four types of people found in the world – those who, being in darkness, are bound for darkness, those who are in darkness, but are bound for light, etc. AN.ii.85; cf. Pp. p.51; and SN.i.93, where the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> is addressed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span>.

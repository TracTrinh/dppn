# Tissa 17

## English source

Tissa 17. A monk, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asubhakammikatissa</span>. He is mentioned in the Commentaries (Vibh­a.270) as an example of a good friend, devoted to the contemplation of *asubha*, association with whom helps one to get rid of lust. His teacher was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭapabbata</span> *vihāra*. Mhv­ṭ.553.

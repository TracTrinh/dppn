# Taruṇasutta

## English source

Taruṇasutta. In him who contemplates the enjoyment of what makes for enfettering, craving grows and a consequent mass of *dukkha*, like a sapling which is well tended; but in him who contemplates misery in all enslaving things, craving, etc., is destroyed. SN.ii.88f.

# Taruṇasutta

## English source

Taruṇasutta. In him who contemplates the enjoyment of what makes for enfettering, craving grows and a consequent mass of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, like a sapling which is well tended; but in him who contemplates misery in all enslaving things, craving, etc., is destroyed. SN.ii.88f.

# Ti-uppalamāliya Thera 01

## English source

Ti-uppalamāliya Thera 01. An Arahant. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, he was a monkey on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candabhāgā</span>, and, having seen the Buddha, he offered him three lotuses. Shortly afterwards he fell from a rock, died, and was reborn in heaven. Ap.i.277.

# Taraṇiya Thera 01

## English source

Taraṇiya Thera 01. An Arahant. In a previous birth he took the Buddha and his monks across a river which was in flood. Thirteen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he became king five times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabhogavā</span> (Ap.i.204f).

He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambhūta Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.47.

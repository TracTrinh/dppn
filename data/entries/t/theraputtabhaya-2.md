# Theraputtābhaya

## English source

Theraputtābhaya. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rasavāhinī</span> (ii.92f.) contains a story of his youth when he was a novice in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappakandaravihāra</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṭhayimbara</span> visited the place and ate the coconuts, throwing the husks about. The novice beat him soundly.

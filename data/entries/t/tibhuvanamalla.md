# Tibhuvanamalla

## English source

Tibhuvanamalla, also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tilokamalla</span>. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu II</span>. (Cv.lxxxvii.16). He was in command of the troops stationed between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambuddoṇi</span> and the Southern sea and he lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vatthalagāma</span>. Cv.lxxxviii.20.

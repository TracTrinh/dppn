# Tilamuṭṭhidāyaka Thera

Tilamuṭṭhidāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ninety-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he gave a handful of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tila</span> seeds to the Buddha, who, reading his thoughts, appeared before him in a mind-created body.

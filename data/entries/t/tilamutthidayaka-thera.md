# Tilamuṭṭhidāyaka Thera

## English source

Tilamuṭṭhidāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave a handful of *tila* seeds to the Buddha, who, reading his thoughts, appeared before him in a mind-created body. Sixteen *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandiya</span>. Ap.i.235.

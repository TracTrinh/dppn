# Tiṇakaṭṭhasutta

## English source

Tiṇakaṭṭhasutta. Incalculable is the beginning of *Saṁsāra*. If a man were to collect all the grasses and twigs of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambudīpa</span>, the number of his mothers would surpass them. SN.ii.178.

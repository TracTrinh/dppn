# Tissasutta 02

## English source

Tissasutta 02. The story of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> (No.14) being taken to the Buddha because he complained of distaste for the monk’s life. By means of an allegory the Buddha teaches him how he can attain *Nibbāna*, and promises to help him to do so. SN.iii.106f.

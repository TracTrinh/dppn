# Tālavantadāyaka Thera

## English source

Tālavantadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he gave a fan made of palm leaf to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span>. Sixty-three *kappas* ago he became king several times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahārāma</span>. Ap.i.211.

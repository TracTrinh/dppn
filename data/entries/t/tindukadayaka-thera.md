# Tindukadāyaka Thera

## English source

Tindukadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was a monkey who saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> and gave him and his monks *tinduka* fruits to eat. Fifty-seven *kappas* ago he became king, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upananda</span>. Ap.i.200f.

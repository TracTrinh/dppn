# Tiraṁsiya Thera

## English source

Tiraṁsiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he was a hermit. He saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> and spoke verses in praise of him, extolling his lustre as surpassing that of the sun and of the moon. Sixty-one *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ñāṇadhara</span>. Ap.i.256f.

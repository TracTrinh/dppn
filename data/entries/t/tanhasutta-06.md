# Taṇhāsutta 06

## English source

Taṇhāsutta 06. Preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhula</span>, as question and answer. Craving for objects of sense is fleeting, and leads, therefore, to unhappiness. SN.ii.248, 251.

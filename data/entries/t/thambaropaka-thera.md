# Thambāropaka Thera

## English source

Thambāropaka Thera. An *Arahant*.

In the past he set up a flagstaff over the *cetiya* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadassī Buddha</span> and, climbing to the top of it, decked it with jasmine flowers. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he became king sixteen times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpasikha</span> (Ap.i.171).

He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paripuṇṇaka Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.190.

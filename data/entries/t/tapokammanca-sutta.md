# Tapokammañca Sutta

## English source

Tapokammañca Sutta. As the Buddha sits under the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajapālanigrodha</span> tree, soon after the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, rejoicing in freedom from toil, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> approaches and tells him that his purity is but a delusion. The Buddha rebukes him and proves him to be wrong. SN.i.103.

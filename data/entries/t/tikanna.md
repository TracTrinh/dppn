# Tikaṇṇa

## English source

Tikaṇṇa. A Brahmin. He once visited the Buddha and spoke in praise of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tevijja</span> Brahmins. The Buddha explained to him that the threefold lore of the Ariyan disciple was a different and a far nobler thing. The Brahmin accepted the Buddha as his teacher. AN.i.164f; cp. DN.i.73ff.

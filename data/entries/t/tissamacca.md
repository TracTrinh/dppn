# Tissāmacca

## English source

Tissāmacca. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Venisāla</span>. Having heard the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇāvibhaṅgasutta</span> in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissamahārāma</span>, he never ate without giving a share of his food to monks. He was later born as a tree deity near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷatindukavihāra</span>. Ras.ii.163f.

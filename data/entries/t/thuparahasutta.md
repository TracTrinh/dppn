# Thūpārahasutta

## English source

Thūpārahasutta. There are four persons worthy of a *Thūpa* – a Buddha, a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span>, a Buddha’s disciple and a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span>. AN.ii.245.

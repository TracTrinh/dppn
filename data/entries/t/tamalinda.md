Tāmalinda. One of the four companions of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chapaṭa</span> and a founder of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sīhalasaṅgha</span> in Burma (Sās., p.65). He later founded a sect of his own. Bode, 24.

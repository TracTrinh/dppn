# Tissa 41

## English source

Tissa 41. A novice of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcaggalalena</span>. While travelling through the air he heard the daughter of the artisan of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Girigāma</span> singing, after having bathed with her companions in a lotus-pond. Being attracted by the sound, he lost his power of travelling through the air. MN­a.i.353; Snp­a.i.70.

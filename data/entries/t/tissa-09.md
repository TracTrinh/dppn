# Tissa 09

## English source

Tissa 09. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissametteyya</span>. A disciple of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span>. He visited the Buddha with his colleagues and when the Buddha answered his questions, he and his thousand pupils became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> was his personal name and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya</span> that of his clan. Snp., vs.1040-2; Snp­a.ii.588.

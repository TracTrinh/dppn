# Tamālapupphiya Thera

## English source

Tamālapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he owned a *vimāna* with eighty thousand golden pillars. He offered a *tamāla* flower to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>. Twenty *kappas* ago he was a king called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candatittha</span>. Ap.i.197.

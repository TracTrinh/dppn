# Tissa 08

## English source

Tissa 08. The personal name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya</span>, friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> (7). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya</span> was his *gotta*-name by which he became known (Snp­a.ii.536; NidA.184). In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttanipāta</span> (vs.814) he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissametteyya</span>.

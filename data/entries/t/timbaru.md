# Timbaru

## English source

Timbaru. A chieftain of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhabbas</span> and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suriyavaccasā</span> (DN.ii.266, 268; see also Mhv­ṭ.576). He was present at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span>. (DN.ii.258; see Hopkins: *Epic Mythology*, *s.v.* Tumburu).

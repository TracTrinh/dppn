# Taraṇiya Thera 04

## English source

Taraṇiya Thera 04. An Arahant. In a previous birth he was a tortoise in the river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinatā</span>, and, seeing that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> wished to cross the river, he took him on his back. Ap.ii.428f.

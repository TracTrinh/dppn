# Tikicchaka Thera

## English source

Tikicchaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he was a physician of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span>, and cured <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>, the attendant of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Eight *kappas* ago he was king under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabbosadha</span> (Ap.i.190). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tekicchakāni Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.442.

# Therīgāthā

## English source

Therīgāthā. The ninth book of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuddakanikāya</span>. It corresponds to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span> and is a unique collection in the literature of the world.

Published by the PTS (1883), and translated by Mrs. Rhys Davids as *Psalms of the Sisters*.

# Tiṇa Sākiyā

## English source

Tiṇa Sākiyā. The name given to those <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span> who held reeds in their mouths in order to escape slaughter when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Viḍūḍabha</span> massacred the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span>. For details see Dhp­a.i.359.

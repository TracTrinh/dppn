# Tassasutaṁ Suttā

## English source

Tassasutaṁ Suttā. A group of *Suttas* in which the Buddha is asked why beings are born among the different kinds of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span>.

He replies that it was because men who had practised double dealing, having heard that birth among <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span> was happy, wish to be born there. SN.iii.243f.

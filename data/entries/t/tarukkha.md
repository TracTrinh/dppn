# Tārukkha

## English source

Tārukkha. One of the most eminent *Mahā Sāla* Brahmins, contemporary of the Buddha.

He was present at the assemblies of the Brahmins in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Icchānaṅgala</span> and in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Manasākaṭa</span>.

He was the teacher of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhāradvāja</span>, companion of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāseṭṭha</span>. Snp., p.115f; Snp­a.i.372; ii.462; DN.i.235; MN.ii.202.

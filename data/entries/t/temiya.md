# Temiya

## English source

Temiya. The name of the Bodhisatta in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūgapakkhajātaka</span> (Ja 538). He was so-called because on the day of his birth there were great rains throughout the kingdom and he was born wet. Ja.vi.3.

# Therānambandhamālaka

## English source

Therānambandhamālaka. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span> erected the funeral pyre of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span>. Later he erected a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Thūpa</span> there over half the remains (Mhv.xx.42f).

# Tālavatthuvihāra

## English source

Tālavatthuvihāra. A monastery in Ceylon. It was restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi V</span>., who also gave to it the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇṇabhatta</span>. He appears to have renamed the village <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sena</span>, probably after its original founder. Cv.xlviii.8; Cv.Trs.i.111, n.1.

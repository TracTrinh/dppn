# Ṭhitisutta 03

## English source

Ṭhitisutta 03. The Buddha praises not steadfastness in good actions, but growth in respect of them. In this <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> the Buddha explains by means of similes how this is done. AN.v.96f.

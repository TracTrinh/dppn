# Tissarakkhā

## English source

Tissarakkhā. The second queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>; he married her four years before his death. She was very jealous of the attention paid by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> to the Bodhi-tree, and caused it to be killed by means of poisonous thorns (Mhv.xx.3ff).

# Tikaṇḍipupphiya Thera

## English source

Tikaṇḍipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he saw the Buddha <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumaṅgala</span> in a grove and offered him a *tikaṇḍi* flower. Forty-six *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apilāpiya</span>. Ap.i.201f.

# Tissa 06

## English source

Tissa 06. A monk who was reborn as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> with great <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhi</span>-powers. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> visited him soon after his birth in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span>-world and asked him questions about <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahmas</span> who were assured of salvation (AN.iii.331; iv.75ff). He was evidently the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> mentioned as being present at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samaya</span>. DN.ii.261; DN­a.ii.692.

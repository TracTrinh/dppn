# Tissa 06

## English source

Tissa 06. A monk who was reborn as a *Brahma* with great *iddhi*-powers. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> visited him soon after his birth in the *Brahma*-world and asked him questions about <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> and *Brahmas* who were assured of salvation (AN.iii.331; iv.75ff). He was evidently the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> mentioned as being present at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samaya</span>. DN.ii.261; DN­a.ii.692.

# Tālacchiggalūpamasutta

## English source

Tālacchiggalūpamasutta. It is said that, after hearing this <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayarājakumāra</span> became a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span> (Thag­a.i.83). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> is probably that of the turtle and the floating trap. Cp. MN.iii.169, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chiggalasutta</span>, SN.v.455.

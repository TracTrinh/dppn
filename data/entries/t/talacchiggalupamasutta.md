# Tālacchiggalūpamasutta

## English source

Tālacchiggalūpamasutta. It is said that, after hearing this *Sutta*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayarājakumāra</span> became a *Sotāpanna* (Thag­a.i.83). The *Sutta* is probably that of the turtle and the floating trap. Cp. MN.iii.169, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chiggalasutta</span>, SN.v.455.

# Tissasutta 03

## English source

Tissasutta 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>, hearing the Buddha report a conversation between two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">devatās</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>, visits the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> in order to discover if the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> had knowledge of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sa-upādisesa</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">anupādisesa</span>.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> tells him what he knows and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> describes his visit to the Buddha. The Buddha tells him that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> had omitted to mention the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t18">animittavihārī puggala</span>, which he then proceeds to explain (AN.iv.77f; cp. AN.iii.332f).

# Tivarā

## English source

Tivarā. The name given to the inhabitants of Mount <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vepulla</span>, then known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pācīnavaṁsa</span>, near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. Their term of life was forty thousand years. SN.ii.190.

# Telamakkhiya Thera

## English source

Telamakkhiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he rubbed oil on the *vedikā* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>’s Bodhi-tree. Twenty-four *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Succhavi</span>. Ap.i.230f.

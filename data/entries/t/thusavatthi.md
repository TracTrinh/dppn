# Thusavaṭṭhi

## English source

Thusavaṭṭhi. A village in Ceylon where king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadāsa</span> effected a miraculous cure (Cv.xxxvii.124f). It was near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sīmā</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> passed through it (Mbv.136).

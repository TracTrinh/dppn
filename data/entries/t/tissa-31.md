# Tissa 31

## English source

Tissa 31. A paramour of Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anulā</span>. He was a wood-carrier and was therefore called *Dārubhatika Tissa*. He reigned for one year and one month and built a bathing-tank in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span>. He was poisoned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anulā</span>. Mhv.xxxiv.22ff.

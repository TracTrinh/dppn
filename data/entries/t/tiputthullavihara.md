# Tiputthullavihāra

## English source

Tiputthullavihāra. A monastery built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhopatissa II</span>. as an extension to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theravādins</span> objected to it, as the grounds lay within their boundary. The king refused to recognise their protest and the bhikkhus passed on him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pattanikkujjanakamma</span>. Cv.xiv.29ff.

# Therasutta 01

## English source

Therasutta 01. An Elder may be time-honoured, long gone forth, well-known, renowned, with a great following, a receiver of the requisites, learned, with a well-stored mind, but if he has wrong views and a perverted vision, he exists for the ill, the harm, of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> and men. AN.iii.114.

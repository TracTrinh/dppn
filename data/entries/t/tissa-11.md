# Tissa 11

## English source

Tissa 11. A monk. When the Buddha declared that in four months he would pass away, many monks were greatly excited, collecting in groups, not knowing what to do. But <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> remained aloof, determined to win <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship before the Buddha’s death. The others, misunderstanding him, reported to the Buddha that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> had no love for him, but the Buddha, having questioned him, praised his earnestness. Dhp­a.iii.267f.

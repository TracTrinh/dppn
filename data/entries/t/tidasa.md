# Tidasa

## English source

Tidasa. A name given to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, the inhabitants being called *Tidasā* (Ja.iii.357, 413; vi.168; v.20, 390). The Tidasa <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> are spoken of as being full of glory. SN.i.234.

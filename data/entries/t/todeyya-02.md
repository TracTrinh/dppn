# Todeyya 02

## English source

Todeyya 02. A disciple of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span> (Snp.vv.1006). He visited the Buddha, and his questions, with the answers given by the Buddha, are given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Todeyyamāṇavapucchā</span> (Snp.vv.1088-91). He became an *Arahant* (Snp­a.ii.597).

# Tissa 27

## English source

Tissa 27. A Brahmin youth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> who rebelled against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> in the fifth year of his reign. At that time <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷas</span> invaded Ceylon and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> sent word to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> asking him to fight them, and take the throne for himself; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> did, but was conquered by them (Mhv.xxxiii.38ff). See also *Brāhmaṇa Tissa*.

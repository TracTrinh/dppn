# Telakaṇḍarikā

## English source

Telakaṇḍarikā. A pious and generous woman, who gave ghee in large quantities to monks. She is mentioned in a story illustrating how monks will sometimes boast of their patrons. VbhA.483; Vsm.27.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

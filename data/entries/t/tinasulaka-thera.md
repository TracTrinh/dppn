# Tiṇasūlaka Thera

## English source

Tiṇasūlaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Many *kappas* ago he was a householder, and, seeing a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhūtagaṇa</span> Mountain, offered him a *tiṇasūla* flower. Eleven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dharaṇīruha</span> (Ap.i.179). He is evidently identical with *Dhammāsavapitu* Thera. Thag­a.i.215f.

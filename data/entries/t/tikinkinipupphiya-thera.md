# Tikiṅkinipupphiya Thera

## English source

Tikiṅkinipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave three *kiṅkini*-flowers to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> (Ap.ii.433). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittaka Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.78.

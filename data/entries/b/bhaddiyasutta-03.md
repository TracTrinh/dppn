# Bhaddiyasutta 03

## English source

Bhaddiyasutta 03. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddiya Thera</span> (2) that the Noble disciple who is a *Sotāpanna* is pleased with four things: unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, to the Dhamma, to the Saṅgha, and virtues that are dear to the Noble Ones. SN.v.403.

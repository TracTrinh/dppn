# Bhārasutta

## English source

Bhārasutta. The burden is the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upādānakkhandhas</span>, the burden-bearer is the person (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puggala</span>), the taking up of the burden is the lust that leads to rebirth, the laying down of the burden is passionless ceasing of craving. SN.iii.25f.

# Bhayasutta 06

## English source

Bhayasutta 06. or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhikkhusutta</span>. In the Ariyan disciple the five kinds of guilty dread are allayed; he is possessed by the four limbs of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span>, and the Ariyan method is, by him, well seen and penetrated by insight. SN.v.389.

# Bījasutta 01

## English source

Bījasutta 01. The five sorts of seed, if unspoilt and planted in good soil, and nourished by wind and water, will sprout and grow. Like to the five kinds of seed are the five kinds of consciousness; like to the earth the four *satipaṭṭhāna*s, like to water the lure of lust. SN.iii.54f.

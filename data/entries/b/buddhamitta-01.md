# Buddhamitta 01

## English source

Buddhamitta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhamitta Thera</span>. A colleague of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> and one of those at whose request he wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jātaka Commentary</span> (Ja.i.1; GV. 68). He is also mentioned as having requested <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> to write the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhima Commentary</span>. It is said that he lived with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mayūrarūpapaṭṭana</span>. MN­a.ii.1029.

# Buddhūpaṭṭhāyaka Thera

## English source

Buddhūpaṭṭhāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veṭambarī</span> and his father dedicated him to the service of the Buddha (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Sikhī</span>). Twenty-three *kappas* ago he became king four times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samaṇūpaṭṭhaka</span>. Ap.i.242.

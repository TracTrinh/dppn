# Bharu

## English source

Bharu. The name of a king, a country, and its capital. See the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bharujātaka</span> (Ja 213) and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bharukaccha</span>. The name of the king and the country in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppārakajātaka</span> (Ja 463) are also identical.

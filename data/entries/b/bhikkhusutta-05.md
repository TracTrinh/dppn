# Bhikkhusutta 05

## English source

Bhikkhusutta 05. The Buddha agrees with a group of monks that if when questioned by other sectarians as to the object of their holiness, they answer that it is the full knowledge of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, their answer is right; he proceeds to tell them what should be their answer if questioned as to what is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>. SN.iv.50f.

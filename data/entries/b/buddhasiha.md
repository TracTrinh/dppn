# Buddhasīha

## English source

Buddhasīha. A monk of Ceylon, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span>. It was at his request that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span> (1) wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rūpārūpavibhāga</span> (an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhamma</span> treatise). (PLC.108). According to one tradition (Sās-d.30) <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhasīha</span> himself was the author of the work, but the colophon to the book states otherwise.

# Bharattāla

## English source

Bharattāla. A village in Ceylon, given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IV</span>. for his maintenance of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāsiva</span> *padhānaghara*. Cv.xlvi.12.

## Vietnamese translation

Bharattāla. Một ngôi làng ở Ceylon, được Aggabodhi IV trao tặng để duy trì *padhānaghara* Dāṭhāsiva. Cv.xlvi.12.

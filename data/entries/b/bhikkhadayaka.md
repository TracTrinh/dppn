# Bhikkhādāyaka

## English source

Bhikkhādāyaka. Once a man of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> had just sat down to eat when he saw a monk, who had travelled far, standing outside his house. He emptied his food into the monk’s bowl. When he died he was reborn in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> in a golden palace twelve <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">yojanas</span> in height. Vv.vi.6; Vv-a.292f.

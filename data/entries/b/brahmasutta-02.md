# Brahmasutta 02

## English source

Brahmasutta 02. The scene is the same as in the above. The Buddha is reflecting on the five *indriyas* (*saddhā*, *sati*, etc.), as the way to *Nibbāna*, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahampati</span> visits him and agrees with him, relating how, when he was a monk named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahaka</span>, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, he developed the five *indriyas* and was born in the *Brahma* world. SN.v.232f.

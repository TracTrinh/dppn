# Bāhiyasutta 01

## English source

Bāhiyasutta 01. Relates the incident of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāhiya</span> (3) asking the Buddha for a lesson. SN.iv.63.

## Vietnamese translation

Bāhiyasutta 01. Kể lại sự việc Bāhiya (3) xin Đức Phật một bài giảng. SN.iv.63.

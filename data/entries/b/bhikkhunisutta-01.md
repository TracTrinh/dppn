# Bhikkhunīsutta 01

## English source

Bhikkhunīsutta 01. The same as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhikkhusutta</span> (2), substituting “nun” for “monk.” SN.ii.261.

## Vietnamese translation

Bhikkhunīsutta 01. Giống như Bhikkhusutta (2), nhưng thay "tỳ-kheo" thành "tỳ-kheo-ni". SN.ii.261.

# Bhaddasāla 03

## English source

Bhaddasāla 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddasāla Thera</span>, *v.1.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddanāma</span>. An *Arahant*. He accompanied <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> to Ceylon (Mhv.xii.7; Dpv.xii. 12, 38; Vin-a.i. 62, 70). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirimeghavaṇṇa</span> made an image of him and placed it in a shrine near his palace. Cv.xxxvii.87.

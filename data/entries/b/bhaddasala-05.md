# Bhaddasāla 05

## English source

Bhaddasāla 05. A general of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nanda</span>; he waged war against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candagutta</span>. In that war took place eighty Corpse Dances, where headless corpses arose and danced in frenzy over the battle field. Mil.292.

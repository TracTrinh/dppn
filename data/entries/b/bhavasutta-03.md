# Bhavasutta 03

## English source

Bhavasutta 03. There are three kinds of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bhava</span>: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kāma</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">rūpa</span>, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">arūpa</span>; these can be given up by training in further virtue, further thought, and further insight.

# Bodhisiñcaka Thera

## English source

Bodhisiñcaka Thera. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bodhisaññaka Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago, when a great festival was being held in honour of the Bodhi-tree of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, he sprinkled perfumed water on the tree. Thirty-three *kappas* ago he became king eight times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udakāsecana</span> (Ap.i.131).

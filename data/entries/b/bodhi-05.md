# Bodhi 05

## English source

Bodhi 05. A monk of Ceylon. At his request <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāmeghavaṇṇa</span> proclaimed a regulative act against the undisciplined monks of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span>. The monks, expelled under the act, conspired together and killed Bodhi. But the king renewed his exertions and, in memory of Bodhi, succeeded in purifying the Saṅgha. Cv.xliv.75.

# Bahalagaṅgā

## English source

Bahalagaṅgā. The name given to a portion of the river flowing from the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. The section is that which flows between the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tiyaggala</span> *pokkharaṇī* and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ummaggagaṅgā</span>. It flows through a rock for a distance of sixty leagues. Snp­a.ii.439; AN­a.ii.760; Ud­a.302; MN­a.i.586.

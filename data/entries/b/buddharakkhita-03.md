# Buddharakkhita 03

## English source

Buddharakkhita 03. A monk of Ceylon to whom is sometimes ascribed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jinālaṅkāra</span>. He is supposed to have lived about 426 B.C. on the west coast of Ceylon, at the head of a congregation of monks (PLC.18). According to others, he is said to have written both the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jinālaṅkāra</span> and its *Ṭīkā*, e.g., Gv.72.

# Bhoja 01

## English source

Bhoja 01. A Brahmin, one of the eight who read the auspicious marks on the Buddha’s body on the fifth day after his birth. Ja.i.56; in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milinda</span> (p.236) he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhoja</span>.

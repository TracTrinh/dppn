# Brahmadeva 03

## English source

Brahmadeva 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmadeva Thera</span>. The son of a Brahmin woman. Having joined the Saṅgha, he dwelt in solitude and became an *Arahant*. One day he went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> for alms, and, in due course, arrived at his mother’s house. She was in the habit of making an oblation to *Brahma*, but, on that day, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahampati</span> appeared before her and told her to bestow her gifts on her son. SN.i.140f.

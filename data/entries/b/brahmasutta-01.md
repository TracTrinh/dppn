# Brahmasutta 01

## English source

Brahmasutta 01. The Buddha is under the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajapālanigrodha</span>, soon after the Awakening, pondering on the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s as the only way to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nibbāna</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahampati</span> visits him and agrees with his sentiments. SN.v.167.

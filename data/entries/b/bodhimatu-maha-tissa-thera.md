# Bodhimātu Mahā Tissa Thera

## English source

Bodhimātu Mahā Tissa Thera. He came through the air to receive from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> a share of the food which the latter had obtained while fleeing from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷaṅganiyapiṭṭhi</span>. According to other accounts the Thera*’*s name was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṭumbiyaputtatissa</span>. AN­a.i.366.

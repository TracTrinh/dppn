# Brahmadatta 01

## English source

Brahmadatta 01. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāsi</span>. He captured <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> and murdered its king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghīti</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghīti</span>’s wife, but made peace later with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghīti</span>’s son, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghāvu</span>, restored to him his father’s kingdom, and gave him his own daughter in marriage. Vin.i.342ff.; Dhp­a.i.56f.

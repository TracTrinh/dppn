# Brahmaññasutta 02

## English source

Brahmaññasutta 02. The highest life is the Noble Eightfold Path, and its aim is the destruction of lust, hatred, and illusion. SN.v.26; cf. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmacariyasutta</span> (3).

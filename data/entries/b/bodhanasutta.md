# Bodhanāsutta

## English source

Bodhanāsutta. The Buddha explains to a monk, in answer to his question, that the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span> are so-called because they conduce to wisdom. SN.v.83.

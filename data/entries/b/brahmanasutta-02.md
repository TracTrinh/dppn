# Brāhmaṇasutta 02

## English source

Brāhmaṇasutta 02. A Brahmin visits the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> and asks why it is that the Buddha’s Dhamma will disappear soon after his death. The Buddha says it is because of the failure of men to realize the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s. SN.v.174.

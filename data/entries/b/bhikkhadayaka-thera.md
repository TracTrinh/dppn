# Bhikkhadāyaka Thera

## English source

Bhikkhadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he gave a spoonful of food to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Eighty-seven *kappas* ago he was king seven times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Reṇu</span> (Ap.i.140). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Godhika Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.124.

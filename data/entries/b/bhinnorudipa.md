# Bhinnorudīpa

## English source

Bhinnorudīpa. A monastery in Ceylon built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. for the Elder (probably <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāsiva</span>) who was living in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Pariveṇa</span>. The revenue from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭākārapiṭṭhi</span> was given for its maintenance. Cv.xlii.26.

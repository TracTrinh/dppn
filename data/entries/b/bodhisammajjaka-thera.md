# Bodhisammajjaka Thera

## English source

Bodhisammajjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the past he picked up leaves from the courtyard of a Bodhi-tree and cleaned it (Ap.ii.457). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.105f.

# Bhadda 01

## English source

Bhadda 01. A lay disciple of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nādikā</span> who, the Buddha declared, had destroyed the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">orambhāgiyasaṁyojanā</span> and had been born in the highest <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> worlds, thence to pass away entirely. DN.ii.92.

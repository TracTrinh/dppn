# Bhadrā Therī

## English source

Bhadrā Therī. She belonged to a clan of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span>, and left the world with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajāpatī Gotamī</span>. While she was meditating, the Buddha sent her a ray of glory and she attained <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship. Thīg.vs.9; Thīg­a.13.

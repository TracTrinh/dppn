# Bandhumā 01

## English source

Bandhumā 01. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. His wife was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> (Ja.1.41, etc.). He had two daughters who, in their later lives, were <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Māyā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uracchadā</span> (Ja.vi.480f). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekasāṭaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mettā Therī</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Bandhumā 01. Vua của Bandhumatī và là cha của Đức Phật Vipassī. Vợ ông là Bandhumatī (Ja.1.41, etc.). Ông có hai người con gái, trong những kiếp sau của họ, chính là Mahā Māyā và Uracchadā (Ja.vi.480f). Xem thêm Ekasāṭaka và Mettā Therī.

# Bhisadāyaka Thera 02

## English source

Bhisadāyaka Thera 02. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Thirty-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was an elephant, and seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span> in the forest, he gave him lotus stalks to eat.

# Bhikkhū Sutta 02

## English source

Bhikkhū Sutta 02. Whatever monks have destroyed the *āsavas* by personal knowledge and insight, have done so by cultivating and developing the four *iddhipādas*. It is the same for the past, present and future. SN.v.257.

# Bhikkhū Sutta 02

## English source

Bhikkhū Sutta 02. Whatever monks have destroyed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span> by personal knowledge and insight, have done so by cultivating and developing the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhipādas</span>. It is the same for the past, present and future. SN.v.257.

# Bherapāsāṇavihāra

## English source

Bherapāsāṇavihāra. A monastery in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. A story is told of a man who lived there, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttara</span>. He once went with some young novices into the forest to fetch grass for the fireplace; he had agreed to carry a certain load and they cheated him into taking a larger one. AN­a.i.442f.

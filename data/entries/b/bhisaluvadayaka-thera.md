# Bhisāluvadāyaka Thera

## English source

Bhisāluvadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave some lotus stalks and water to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhisa</span>. Ap.i.120.

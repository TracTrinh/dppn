# Bhikkhusutta 02

## English source

Bhikkhusutta 02. Once <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakkhaṇa</span> saw a *Bhikkhu*, born as a *Peta*, going through the air, his body, robes, etc., on fire. He had been a sinful monk in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. SN.ii.260.

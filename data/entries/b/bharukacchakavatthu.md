# Bharukacchakavatthu

## English source

Bharukacchakavatthu. The story of a monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bharukaccha</span> who having (in his dream) lain with a woman, thought he was guilty of a *pārājikā* offence. But <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli</span> ruled that he was blameless. Vin.iii.39; Vin-a.i.283.

## Vietnamese translation

Bharukacchakavatthu. Câu chuyện về một vị tỳ-kheo ở Bharukaccha, người (trong giấc mơ) đã ăn nằm với một phụ nữ, liền nghĩ rằng mình đã phạm tội *pārājikā*. Nhưng Upāli đã phán quyết rằng vị ấy vô tội. Vin.iii.39; Vin-a.i.283.

# Bhallātakadāyaka Thera

## English source

Bhallātakadāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Eighteen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was an ascetic and, seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> going through the air, invited him to his hermitage. There he gave the Buddha a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bhallātaka</span>-fruit (Ap.ii.398). He is probably, identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijitasena</span>. Thag.A.i.426.

# Bojjhaṅgasutta 01

## English source

Bojjhaṅgasutta 01. Among the four kinds of deeds – dark with a dark result, dark with a bright result, bright with a bright result and bright with a dark result – the seven kinds of wisdom (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅga</span>) are neither dark nor bright, and conduce to the waning of deeds. AN.ii.236f.

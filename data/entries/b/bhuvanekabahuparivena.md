# Bhuvanekabāhupariveṇa

## English source

Bhuvanekabāhupariveṇa. A monastic building in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Billaselavihāra</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hatthiselapura</span>, erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu</span>, younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu II</span>. (Cv.lxxxv.59).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu</span> was cremated there, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu IV</span>. erected a three-storied building on the spot, with a Buddha image. He also set up a statue of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu</span> with all ornaments and gave a maintenance village for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>. Cv.lxxxviii.54ff.

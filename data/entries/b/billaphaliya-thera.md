# Billaphaliya Thera

## English source

Billaphaliya Thera. An *Arahant*. He was an ascetic living on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candabhāgā</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. One day he gave the Buddha a *billa*-fruit (wood apple) from the tree which grew in his grove. Ap.ii.397.

# Buddhaghosuppatti

## English source

Buddhaghosuppatti. A very late account of the life of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span>; it is more a romance than a historical chronicle. For an account of this see Law, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Pāli Literature</span> p.558 f. The work has been translated and edited by Gray (London).

# Bhavasutta 02

## English source

Bhavasutta 02. On the three kinds of *bhava* in the *kāma* world, the *rūpa* world, and the *arūpa* world. SN.v.56.

## Vietnamese translation

Bhavasutta 02. Về ba loại *bhava* trong cõi *kāma*, cõi *rūpa*, và cõi *arūpa*. SN.v.56.

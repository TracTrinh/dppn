# Bhojanadāyaka Thera

## English source

Bhojanadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he gave a meal to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span>. Twenty-five *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amitābha</span>. Ap.i.253.

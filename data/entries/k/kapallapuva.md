# Kapallapūva

## English source

Kapallapūva. A cave near the gateway of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>, into which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Macchariya Kosiya</span> (*q.v.*) and his wife threw away the cakes which were left over after they had fed the Buddha and five hundred monks. From this act the spot took its name <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapallapūva</span> (Crock-cake). Ja.i.348; Dhp­a.i.373.

# Koṭṭha

## English source

Koṭṭha. The drum of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narasīha</span>, which he gave to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇavamma</span> in order that the latter might induce the people to accompany him in the ships. When they heard the drum they thought it was beaten by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narasīha</span> and forthwith went on board. Cv.x1vii.51.

# Kuntavarā

## English source

Kuntavarā. The soldiers of a district in South India who fought against the Sinhalese force that invaded their territory (Cv.lxxvi.246). They were subdued by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kesadhātu Kitti</span> (Cv.lxxvi.259).

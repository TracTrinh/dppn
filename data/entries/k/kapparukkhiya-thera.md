# Kapparukkhiya Thera

## English source

Kapparukkhiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he placed a *kappa-rukkha* made of cloth of various colours in front of the *Thūpa* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>.

Seven *kappas* ago he was king eight times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sucela</span> (Ap.i.90f).

He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappa Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.521f.

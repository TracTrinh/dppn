# Khomadāyaka Thera

## English source

Khomadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a merchant in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> and, having seen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> in the street, gave him a linen cloth (*khoma*). Twenty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sindhavasandana</span>. Ap.i.80f.

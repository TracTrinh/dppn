# Kosalasutta 02

## English source

Kosalasutta 02. Everything changes, even <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span>, king of Kāsi-Kosala, the whole universe, even <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Brahma</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> of the *Ābhassara* world. Instability and change are everywhere; therefore the wise loathe all, having a mind only to attain in this life the *anupādāparinibbāna* (AN.v.59ff).

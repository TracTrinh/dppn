# Kāḷagiribhaṇḍa

## English source

Kāḷagiribhaṇḍa. A district in Ceylon. The forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. fought there twenty battles before it could be brought under subjection (Cv.lxxii.62). It is identified with the modern Kalugalboḍa-raṭa. Cv.Trs.i.325, n.1.

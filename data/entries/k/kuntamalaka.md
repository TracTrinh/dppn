# Kuntamālaka

## English source

Kuntamālaka *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhamālaka</span>. A locality near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> through which passed the boundary of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span>. It was the point from which the king started to mark the *sīmā* (Mhv., p.331; Mbv.134; Dpv.xiv.28, 33).

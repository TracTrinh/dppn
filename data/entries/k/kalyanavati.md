# Kalyāṇavatī

## English source

Kalyāṇavatī. The first queen-consort of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittinissaṅka</span>.

After the death of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāhasamalla</span> she carried on the government of Ceylon for six months (according to some six years, 1202-1208 CE.) with the help of her general, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āyasmanta</span>.

She built a *vihāra* called the *Kalyāṇavatīvihāra* in the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇṇasālaka</span>. Cv.lxxx.34ff; also Cv.Trs.ii.130, n.3.

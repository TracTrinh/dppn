# Kappūrapariveṇa

## English source

Kappūrapariveṇa. A building in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span>, erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhopatissa II</span>. (Cv.xlv.29). Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi II</span>. built a *pāsāda* there (Cv.xlvi.21), and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena I</span>. erected a *pariccheda* (probably rows of single cells). (Cv.l.77) (See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappūrarmūlāyatana</span>).

# Kunta

## English source

Kunta. A throne (for an image) which was originally in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pācīnavihāra</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theravādins</span>, and was later set up beside the Bodhi-tree of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silākāla</span> (Cv.xli.31).

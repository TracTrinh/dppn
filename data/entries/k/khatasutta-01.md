# Khatasutta 01

## English source

Khatasutta 01. A man who praises and blames without scrutiny, who shows appreciation or displeasure without testing the object thereof – such a one carries with him an uprooted, lifeless self. Not so the man who has the opposite qualities. AN.ii.2f.

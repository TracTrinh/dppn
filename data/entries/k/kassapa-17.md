# Kassapa 17

## English source

Kassapa 17. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittaggabodhi</span>, ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. When his eldest brother was murdered by his paternal aunt, Kassapa fled to the court of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena I</span>., but, later, with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena</span>’s help, he won his father’s inheritance (Cv.l.54ff). He was probably killed by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Ādipāda</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittaggabodhi</span>. Cv.li.96; and Cv.Trs.i.157, n.2.

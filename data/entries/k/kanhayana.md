# Kaṇhāyāna

## English source

Kaṇhāyāna. The name of the family to which belonged <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāsudeva</span> (Ja.iv.84; vi.421) and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambaṭṭha</span> (DN.i.93).

The family was descended from a sage named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇha</span> (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇha</span> (4).)

# Kaṇḍarāyana

## English source

Kaṇḍarāyana *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇḍarāyana</span>. A Brahmin. He visited <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gundavana</span> near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span> and accused him of not paying due respect to elderly Brahmins, but when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> preached to him he expressed delight and offered himself as the Thera*’*s disciple. AN.i.67f.

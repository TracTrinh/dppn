# Khaṇḍigāma

## English source

Khaṇḍigāma. A village in Ceylon. It contained a narrow pass where a battle took place between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span>, in which the former was defeated. Later, the *Adhikārī* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nātha</span> suffered defeat in the same place. Cv.lxx.216-81, 298; see also Cv.Trs.i.305, n.6.

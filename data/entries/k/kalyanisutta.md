# Kalyāṇīsutta

## English source

Kalyāṇīsutta. No beautiful woman (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">janapadakaḷyānī</span>) can persistently possess the heart of a man who is fond of gain, favours, and flattery. SN.ii.235.

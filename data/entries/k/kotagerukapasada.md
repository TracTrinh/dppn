# Koṭagerukapāsāda

## English source

Koṭagerukapāsāda. A building attached to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittalapabbatavihāra</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhāgineyya Saṅgharakkhita</span> once lived there, and, during his illness, eight thousand *Arahants* and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>, with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> of the two *Deva*-worlds, waited on him. Mhv­ṭ.552.

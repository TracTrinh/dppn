# Kandalīpupphiya Thera

## English source

Kandalīpupphiya Thera. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kadalī Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was a farmer on the banks of the Sindhū, and seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> wrapt in meditation, offered him seven *kandalī*-flowers and paid him homage (Ap.i.257).

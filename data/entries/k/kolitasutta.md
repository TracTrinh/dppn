# Kolitasutta

## English source

Kolitasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> tells the monks at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> how, when he had entered the second <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhāna</span>, in his effort to attain to the “Ariyan Silence,” the Buddha appeared to him and exhorted him to persist in it (SN.ii.273).

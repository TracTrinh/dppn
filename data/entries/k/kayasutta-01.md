# Kāyasutta 01

## English source

Kāyasutta 01. Mindfulness relating to the body is the path to the Uncompounded (*asaṅkhata*). This the Buddha has taught his disciples. SN.iv.359.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

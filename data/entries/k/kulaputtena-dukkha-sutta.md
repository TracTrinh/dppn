# Kulaputtena dukkhā Suttā

## English source

Kulaputtena dukkhā Suttā. Three *Suttas*. A clansman who goes forth should live (1) in aversion from body etc., or (2) seeing impermanence in them, or (3) seeing no soul in them. SN.iii.179.

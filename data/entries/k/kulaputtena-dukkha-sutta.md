# Kulaputtena dukkhā Suttā

## English source

Kulaputtena dukkhā Suttā. Three <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span>. A clansman who goes forth should live (1) in aversion from body etc., or (2) seeing impermanence in them, or (3) seeing no soul in them. SN.iii.179.

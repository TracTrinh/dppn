# Kindadasutta

## English source

Kindadasutta. Records part of a conversation between a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> and the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> asks what one should give in order to obtain certain results, such as strength, beauty, etc. – the Buddha explains. SN.i.32.

# Kindadasutta

## English source

Kindadasutta. Records part of a conversation between a *Deva* and the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. The *Deva* asks what one should give in order to obtain certain results, such as strength, beauty, etc. – the Buddha explains. SN.i.32.

# Kacchakadaha

## English source

Kacchakadaha. A lake, evidently near the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷavallimaṇḍapa</span>. The Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷavallimaṇḍapa</span> filled his mouth with water from the lake before entering the village for alms, thus ensuring that his meditations should not be interrupted by needless conversation. Vibh­a.352.

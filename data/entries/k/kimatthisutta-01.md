# Kimatthisutta 01

## English source

Kimatthisutta 01. The first part is identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kimatthasutta</span>. If further questioned as to what is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, monks should answer, “eye is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">rūpa</span> objects are <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, eye-contact, etc.” SN.iv.138

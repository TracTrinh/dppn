# Kimatthisutta 01

## English source

Kimatthisutta 01. The first part is identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kimatthasutta</span>. If further questioned as to what is *dukkha*, monks should answer, “eye is *dukkha*, *rūpa* objects are *dukkha*, eye-contact, etc.” SN.iv.138

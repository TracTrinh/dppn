# Kulupakasutta 04

## English source

Kulupakasutta 04. The Buddha says that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span> has the qualities requisite to becoming a monk worthy of visiting families. He is not vexed even if they give scantily, tardily or disrespectfully. SN.ii.200.

# Kandaraka

## English source

Kandaraka. A wandering ascetic who visited the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Campā</span>. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kandarakasutta</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> (MN­a.ii.565) describes him as a *channaparibbājaka* (a clothed ascetic).

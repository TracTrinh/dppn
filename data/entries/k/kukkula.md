# Kukkuḷa

## English source

Kukkuḷa. One of the *nirayas*. King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Daṇḍakī</span> was born there because of his heinous crime (Ja.v.114, 143; Iti-a.195).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarabhaṅga</span> spoke of him as suffering there, sunk in a mass of glowing coals (*kukkuḷa*) one hundred leagues deep; huge glowing sparks fall on him and enter his body through nine sores (Ja.v.144).

Beside this *niraya* are the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gūthaniraya</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Simbalivana</span>. MN.iii.185.

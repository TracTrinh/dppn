# Kammahārattaka

## English source

Kammahārattaka. General of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khallāṭanāga</span>. He led a revolution against the king and killed him in the capital. The king’s brother <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span>, however, killed the general and took over the government (Mhv.xxxiii.33; see also Mhv­ṭ.612).

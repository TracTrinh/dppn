# Khadirasutta

## English source

Khadirasutta. It is just as impossible to destroy <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span> without realising the Four Noble Truths as it is to make a leaf-basket of acacia leaves, etc., or to fetch water in such a basket, or to use the leaves for a fan. SN.v.438.

# Khadirasutta

## English source

Khadirasutta. It is just as impossible to destroy *dukkha* without realising the Four Noble Truths as it is to make a leaf-basket of acacia leaves, etc., or to fetch water in such a basket, or to use the leaves for a fan. SN.v.438.

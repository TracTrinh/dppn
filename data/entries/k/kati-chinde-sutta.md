# Kati chinde Sutta

## English source

Kati chinde Sutta. Preached in answer to a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span>’s question as to how many bonds an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> should cut. Five, says the Buddha. SN.i.3.

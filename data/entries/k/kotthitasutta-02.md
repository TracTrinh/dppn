# Koṭṭhitasutta 02

## English source

Koṭṭhitasutta 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Koṭṭhita</span> asks <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> a series of questions as to why the holy life (*brahmacariyā*) is lived by the Blessed One, to all of which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> answers “No.” He then goes on to explain that the purpose of the holy life is the realisation of the four Ariyan truths. AN.iv.382f.

# Kitti 10

## English source

Kitti 10. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti Kesadhātu</span>. A general of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. He was one of the leaders of the Sinhalese army which fought against the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷas</span> (Cv.lxxvi.255, 269). He may be identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti</span> (9).

# Koṭalla

## English source

Koṭalla. Evidently the Pāli equivalent of Kauṭilya. He is mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Vaṁsa</span> as the author of a work on politics (Cv.lxiv.3) and also of a work on methods of warfare. Cv.lxx.56; see also Cv.Trs.i.243 n.1 and 291, n.3.

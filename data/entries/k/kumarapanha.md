# Kumārapañha

## English source

Kumārapañha. The fourth chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuddakapāṭha</span> (Khp.p.2).

It consists of ten questions which, according to the Commentaries (Khp-a.76; Thag­a.i.479), the Buddha asked the young <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sopāka</span>, then an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> though only seven years old, with the idea of giving him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upasampadā</span>-ordination.

The boy-<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> answered the questions and this conversation formed his ordination (see also Thag.v.485; Ap.i.64f).

These questions were elaborated by the nun of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kajaṅgalā</span> (see AN.v.54ff). (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>).

The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhima Commentary</span> (MN­a.ii.636) on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambalaṭṭhika Rāhulovādasutta</span> seems to indicate a set of questions called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumārapañha</span> in connection with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhula</span> when he was seven years old.

See also SN­a.iii.99, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittagahapati</span>, in a discussion with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigaṇṭha Nātaputta</span>, referred to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="15" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumārapañha</span>.

# Kassapa 04

## English source

Kassapa 04. A sage (*Isi*); one of the famous sages of yore, of whom ten are several times mentioned in the books (e.g., DN.i.104, 238; MN.ii.169, 200; AN.iii.224; iv.61; Ja.vi.99) as having been Brahmin sages, who composed and promulgated the mantras and whose compositions are chanted and repeated and rehearsed by the Brahmins of the present day. For details see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṭṭhaka</span>.

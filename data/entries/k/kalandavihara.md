# Kalandavihāra

## English source

Kalandavihāra. A *vihāra* built in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kālāyanakaṇṇikā</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Dāṭhika Mahā Nāga</span> (Mhv.xxxiv.89; Mhv­ṭ.635). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VII</span>. is also credited with having built a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalandavihāra</span> (Cv.xlviii.70), but he may have only renovated an already existing one. Near the *vihāra* was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brāhmaṇagāma</span> Mhv­ṭ.685.

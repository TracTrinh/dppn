# Kuliṅga

## English source

Kuliṅga. The name of a clan, probably Sinhalese. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda VI</span>. belonged to this clan (Cv.lxxx.15). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuliṅgas</span> were among the tribes sent to Ceylon by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> with the Bodhi-tree. Mhv.xix.2; see also Mhv.Trs.128, n.2, and Cv.Trs.i.29, n.2, and ii.126, n.5.

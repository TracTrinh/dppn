# Kuddālapaṇḍita

## English source

Kuddālapaṇḍita (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddālakapaṇḍita</span>). See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddālajātaka</span>.

He is mentioned in a list of six famous teachers of the past, who were well-known for their pious and holy lives. They had numerous followers and, after death, were born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span>-world (AN.iii.371, 373; iv.135).

The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddāla</span>-birth was one in which the perfection of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">paññā</span> was developed (Ja.i.46).

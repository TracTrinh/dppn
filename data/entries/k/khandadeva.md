# Khaṇḍadeva

## English source

Khaṇḍadeva. A monk. He had been a disciple of the Buddha and was born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aviha</span> *Brahma*-world, where he attained to *Arahant*-ship at the moment of his birth. He is mentioned with six others, all in like circumstances, by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ghaṭīkāra</span>, on the occasion of a visit he paid to the Buddha. SN.i.35, 60; Thīg­a.222.

# Khuramāla

## English source

Khuramāla, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuramālī</span>. A sea. Once, merchants travelling from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhārukaccha</span> lost their way in it and were rescued by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppāraka</span>. In the sea were fishes with bodies like men and sharp razor-like snouts. Ja.iv.139.

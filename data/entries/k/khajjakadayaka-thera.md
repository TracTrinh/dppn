# Khajjakadāyaka Thera

## English source

Khajjakadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he gave a ripe *panasa*-fruit, with a coconut, to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span>; and thirteen *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indasoma</span> (Ap.i.182). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Setuccha Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.206f.

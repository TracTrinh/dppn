# Khandhasaṁyutta

## English source

Khandhasaṁyutta. The twenty-second chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṁyuttanikāya</span> and the first chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khandhavagga</span>. It consists of one hundred and fifty <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span>, divided into three sections. The chapter deals mainly with the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span> or constituent elements. SN.iii.1-188.

# Kesakambalasutta

## English source

Kesakambalasutta. Just as the hair blanket is reckoned the meanest and lowest of all woven garments, even so, of all theories advanced by recluses, that of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Makkhali</span> is the meanest.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Makkhali</span> proclaims that there is no doing of a deed, there is nothing done and no energy to do it. AN.i.286f.

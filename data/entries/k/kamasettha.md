# Kāmaseṭṭha

## English source

Kāmaseṭṭha. One of the greater <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span> who should be invoked if any follower of the Buddha be molested by an evil spirit (DN.iii.204).

In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (DN.ii.258) he is mentioned among the vassals of the Four Great Kings – versed in craft, hoodwinking wizards, clever in feigning.

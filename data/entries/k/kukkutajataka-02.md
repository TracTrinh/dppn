# Kukkuṭajātaka 02

## English source

Kukkuṭajātaka 02. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuhajātaka</span>. (Ja 448). The Bodhisatta was once the chief of a large flock of fowls. A falcon, by means of engaging speech, tried to become friendly with him in order to eat him, but his attempts failed. There could be no friendship between fowl and falcon, said the Bodhisatta. The story was related in reference to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>’s attempts to kill the Buddha. Ja.iv.55ff.

# Kāḷavallimaṇḍapa

## English source

Kāḷavallimaṇḍapa. A *vihāra* in Ceylon, the residence of the Elder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span> (DN­a.i.190, 191; Snp-a.i.56; VbhA.352, 353; Ja.iv.490; Mhv­ṭ.606). It was near the village <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nakulanagara</span> (Dhs­a.339) and was situated in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> (AN­a.i.384).

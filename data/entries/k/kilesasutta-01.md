# Kilesasutta 01

## English source

Kilesasutta 01. The five kinds of alloy which are used with gold – iron, copper, tin, lead and silver – and the similar five corruptions of the heart – sensual desire, malevolence, sloth and torpor, excitement and worry, doubt and wavering (SN.v.92f).

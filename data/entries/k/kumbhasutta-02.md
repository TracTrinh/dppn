# Kumbhasutta 02

## English source

Kumbhasutta 02. A pot, if overset, empties out its water and cannot take it in again. A monk, cultivating the Eightfold Path, empties out <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span> and cannot take it in again. SN.v.48.

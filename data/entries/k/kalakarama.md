# Kāḷakārāma

## English source

Kāḷakārāma. The park presented to the Buddha by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷaka</span>, and the monastery he built therein for the Buddha. The Buddha stayed there on his visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāketa</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Subhaddā</span>’s request. AN.ii.24; AN­a.ii.482.

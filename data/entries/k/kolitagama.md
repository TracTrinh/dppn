# Kolitagāma

## English source

Kolitagāma. The village in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> was born.

It was near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissagāma</span> and not far from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. See, e.g., Snp­a.i.326; Dhp­a.i.73; Mvu.iii.56.

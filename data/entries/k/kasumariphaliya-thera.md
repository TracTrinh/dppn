# Kāsumāriphaliya Thera

## English source

Kāsumāriphaliya Thera. An *Arahant*. The verses attributed to him are the same as those of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāsumāriphaladāyaka</span>. He is probably to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotidāsa Thera</span> (Ap.ii.445).

# Kukkuṭagiripariveṇa

## English source

Kukkuṭagiripariveṇa. A row of cells built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaniṭṭhatissa</span> after he had demolished the boundaries of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> (Mhv.xxxvi.10).

They were later restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sena</span> (Mhv.xxxvii.15; Mhv­ṭ.678).

The place is also referred to as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuṭārāma</span> (e.g., Mhv.xxxvii.15).

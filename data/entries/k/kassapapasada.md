# Kassapapāsāda

## English source

Kassapapāsāda. A building attached to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span> and erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa IV</span>. A village was made over for its maintenance (Cv.lii.13). It is identified with the “<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Kasub-vad-mahapahā</span>” mentioned in an inscription of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda IV</span>. in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavanārāma</span>. Ep.Zey.i.216.

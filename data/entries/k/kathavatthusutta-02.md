# Kathāvatthusutta 02

## English source

Kathāvatthusutta 02. Monks should not indulge in idle talk, as of kings, robbers, great ministers, etc. There are, for monks, ten suitable topics of conversation, such as contentment, virtue, energy, wisdom, etc. AN.v.128f.

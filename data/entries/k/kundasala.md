# Kuṇḍasālā

## English source

Kuṇḍasālā. A suburb of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍhanapura</span> (Kandy), on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>. It was laid out by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narindasīha</span>, who made it his favourite residence (Cv.xcvii.34). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span> planned its garden and erected a *vihāra*. Cv.c.216f.

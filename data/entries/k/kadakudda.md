# Kaḍakuḍḍa

## English source

Kaḍakuḍḍa. A general of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span>, captured by the *Senāpati* *Deva* and sent to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxx.143) Later he seems to have been appointed general by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> himself and given the title of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span>, for we find him fighting on the side of the king and being entrusted with important campaigns. Cv.lxxii.39, 222, 272; lxxv.181.

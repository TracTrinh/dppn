# Kaṇhagaṅgā

## English source

Kaṇhagaṅgā. The name of one part of the river which flows from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anotatta</span>. This part is sixty leagues long and flows over a rocky bed. Snp-a.ii.439; AN­a.ii.760; Ud­a.302; MN­a.ii.586, etc.

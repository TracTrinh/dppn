# Kusinārāvihāra

## English source

Kusinārāvihāra. A monastery built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. in a suburb of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhapura</span>. It consisted of three image houses, each containing three storeys, six <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pāsādas</span>, etc. Cv.lxxiii.152; lxxviii.84; also Cv.Trs.ii.18, n.3.

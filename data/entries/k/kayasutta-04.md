# Kāyasutta 04

## English source

Kāyasutta 04. There are certain things that should be got rid of by action, others by speech, yet others by mind. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> explains what these things are and how to get rid of them. AN.v.39f.

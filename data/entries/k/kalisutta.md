# Kālisutta

## English source

Kālisutta. The Buddha, while staying at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigrodhārāma</span>, visits his kinswoman <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷigodhā</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapilavatthu</span>, and tells her of the four qualities to be found in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span> –

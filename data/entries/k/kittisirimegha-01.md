# Kittisirimegha 01

## English source

Kittisirimegha 01. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna II</span>. and king of Ceylon from 656 CE. The length of his reign is uncertain; he was slain by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span> (Cv.xli.65-92; see also Cv.Trs.i.61, n.2). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rūpavatī</span>, queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>., was probably a lineal descendant of this king. Cv.lxxiii.136, 142.

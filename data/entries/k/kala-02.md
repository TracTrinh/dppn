# Kāḷa 02

## English source

Kāḷa 02. An Elder. A certain woman ministered to him as though he were her son, but when she expressed her desire to see the Buddha, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷa</span> tried to dissuade her from doing so. One day she visited the Buddha without telling <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷa</span> of her intention, and when he learnt where she had gone he hurried to the Buddha and tried to prevent him from preaching to her, in case she should stop caring for him. Dhp­a.iii.155f.

# Kākadīpa

## English source

Kākadīpa. An island to the east of Ceylon. A part of the expeditionary force sent by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāmañña</span> landed on this island, captured some of the inhabitants and brought them to Ceylon, where they were presented to the king (Cv.lxxvi.57). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākadīpa</span> may have been the name of one of the Andaman Islands.

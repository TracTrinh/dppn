# Kuṇḍalī 03

## English source

Kuṇḍalī 03. The name of a *vimāna* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>. In this *vimāna* was born a man who once tended <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> and looked after them when they stayed in a *vihāra* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāsi</span>. Vv.vi.8; Vv-a.295f.

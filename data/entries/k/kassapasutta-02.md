# Kassapasutta 02

## English source

Kassapasutta 02. Contains the thoughts that came to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, before his Awakening, on the nature of becoming, of cessation, etc. (SN.ii.9; cf. DN.ii.30f). The same thoughts are also attributed to the other Buddhas, from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī</span> downwards. For details see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotamasutta</span>.

# Koṭalavāpigāma

## English source

Koṭalavāpigāma *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kālavāpigāma</span>. A village in Ceylon. A story is told of how the wife of the chief householder in the village was put in bonds by the king’s tax-gatherers, under the impression that she was a serving-woman. Vibh­a.441.

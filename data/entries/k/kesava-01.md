# Kesava 01

## English source

Kesava 01. An ascetic in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. His story is given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kesavajātaka</span>. He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bakabrahma</span> (Ja.iii.145; SN.i.144; SN­a.i.165; MN­a.i.555). He is sometimes addressed as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kesī</span>, e.g., Ja.iii.144, 362.

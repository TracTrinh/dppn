# Kammasutta 02

## English source

Kammasutta 02. Seven qualities which lead monks away from ruin (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">aparihāniyā dhammā</span>): abstention from delight in (1) worldly activity, (2) disputation, (3) sleep, (4) society, (3) evil desires, (6) evil friends, (7) being easily satisfied with regard to attainments. AN.iv.22.

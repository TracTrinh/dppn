# Kanakadattā

## English source

Kanakadattā. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇṭakānandā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kandanandā</span>. A nun, disciple of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana</span>, who brought a branch of the Buddha’s *udumbara*-*bodhi* to Ceylon. She, with her following, was left behind by the Buddha. Dpv.xvii.17.

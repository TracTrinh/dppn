# Kāsapabbata

## English source

Kāsapabbata. A mountain in Ceylon, once the headquarters of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍukābhaya</span> (Mhv.x.27). It lay on the way from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijitapura</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> encamped there and constructed a tank near by. Mhv.xxv.50; see also Mhv.Trs.70 n.

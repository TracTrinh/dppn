# Karoto Sutta

## English source

Karoto Sutta. Discussion of the view that there is neither merit nor demerit in any kind of action whatsoever, whether good or bad (SN.iii.208). The reference is evidently to the heresy of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pūraṇa Kassapa</span> (C.p. DN.i.52).

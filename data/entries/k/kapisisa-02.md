# Kapisīsa 02

## English source

Kapisīsa 02. A minister of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span>. One day when the king, accompanied by his queen, was going up the steps of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ākāsacetiya</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Acchagallavihāra</span>, he saw the minister, who had come down after the sweeping of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span>, sitting by the roadside. Because <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapisīsa</span> failed to prostrate himself before the king, the latter slew him (Mhv.xxxiii.68f).

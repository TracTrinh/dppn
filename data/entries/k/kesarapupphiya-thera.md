# Kesarapupphiya Thera

## English source

Kesarapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a *vijjādhara* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>, and having seen the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Vessabhū</span>, gave him three *kesara*-flowers. Ap.i.187.

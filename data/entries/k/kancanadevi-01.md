# Kañcanadevī 01

## English source

Kañcanadevī 01. Daughter of the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span> and sister of the Bodhisatta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kañcana</span>.

With her brothers she renounced the world and lived in a hermitage after her parents’ death.

Her story is told in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhisajātaka</span> (Ja.iv.305ff).

She was a previous birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uppalavaṇṇā</span> (Ja.iv.314).

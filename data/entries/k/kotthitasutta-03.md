# Koṭṭhitasutta 03

## English source

Koṭṭhitasutta 03. Three *Suttas*. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Koṭṭhita</span> visits the Buddha and asks for a brief statement of the Dhamma. The Buddha answers that desire should be put away for that which is (1) impermanent, (2) <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, (3) without a self. SN.iv.145f.

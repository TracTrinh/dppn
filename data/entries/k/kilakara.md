# Kīḷākāra

## English source

Kīḷākāra. One of the gardens laid out by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxxix.12). There was a sluice of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamasamudda</span> at the end of the garden, and from this sluice branched off the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Salaḷavatī</span> canal (Cv.lxxix.43).

# Kosikī

## English source

Kosikī. A river, probably a branch of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ganges</span>. It flowed from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>, and on its bank was a mango-grove three leagues in extent. Ja.v.2, 5, 6.

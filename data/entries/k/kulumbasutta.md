# Kulumbasutta

## English source

Kulumbasutta. A discourse, evidently well-known, but not included in the Three Rescensions (*tisso saṅgīti*) (Vin-a.iv.742, 743).

The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthasālinī</span> (Dhs-a.91) gives an extract from it and refers “to an infanticide of Kulumba.” The quotation is to show that a bodily action may arise also in the mind-door.

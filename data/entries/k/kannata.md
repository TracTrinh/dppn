# Kaṇṇāta

## English source

Kaṇṇāta. The name given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Vaṁsa</span> to the Kanarese of South India. They are often mentioned as mercenary soldiers in Ceylon, e.g., Cv.lv.12; lx.24, 26; lxx.230.

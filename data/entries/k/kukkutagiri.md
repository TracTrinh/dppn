# Kukkuṭagiri

## English source

Kukkuṭagiri. A place in Ceylon. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (SN­a.iii.50) that it was so-called because it was there that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhātissa</span>’s attendant, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> 43), refused to kill some pheasants and set them free at the risk of losing his own life.

# Kāḷatittha

## English source

Kāḷatittha. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. There <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vikkamapaṇḍu</span> had his seat of government (Cv.lvi.12). It is identified with the modern Kalutara, at the mouth of the Kaḷugaṅga.

# Kaddūravaḍḍhamāna

## English source

Kaddūravaḍḍhamāna. A tank in Ceylon.

It was joined up to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Giritālaka</span> tank by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāverī</span> canal, and to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Arimaddavijayaggāma</span> tank by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somavatī</span> canal.

It formed part of the irrigation schemes of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxxix.55f.

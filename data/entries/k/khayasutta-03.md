# Khayasutta 03

## English source

Khayasutta 03. The Buddha exhorts the monks to cultivate the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span>, which lead to the destruction of craving. In answer to a question of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udāyī</span> he goes on to explain how the cultivation is pursued. SN.v.86f.

# Kaṇha 02

## English source

Kaṇha 02. The name of the Bodhisatta; he was born in a Brahmin family and later became a sage. He is also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhatāpasa</span>, and is mentioned among those the memory of whose lives caused the Buddha to smile. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhajātaka</span> (2). Dhs­a.294, 426.

# Kāyasutta 02

## English source

Kāyasutta 02. Just as the body is sustained by material food so are the five hindrances maintained by their own foods. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> explains what these foods are. SN.v.64f.

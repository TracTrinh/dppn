# Kassapavihāra

## English source

Kassapavihāra. A monastery to which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhopatissa II</span>. gave the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senāmagāma</span> (Cv.xlv.27). This monastery is probably to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapagirivihāra</span>.

# Kusinārāsutta 01

## English source

Kusinārāsutta 01. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Baliharaṇa</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusinārā</span>. Offerings, made to a monk by the pious, bring him no real advantage if he is slothful; he should be strenuous and vigilant. AN.i.274f.

# Kusamba

## English source

Kusamba (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusumba</span>). A sage (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Isi</span>), on the site of whose hermitage was built the city which came, for that reason, to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosambī</span>. Snp­a.i.300; MN­a.ii.539; Ud­a.248; Paṭis-a.413; see also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Rāmāyaṇa</span> i.34.

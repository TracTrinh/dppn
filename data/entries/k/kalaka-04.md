# Kāḷaka 04

## English source

Kāḷaka 04. See AN.v.164, *Sutta* No. lxxxvii. Is <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷaka</span> here a proper name or a generic name (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷakabhikkhu</span>) meaning a wicked monk?

I am inclined to take it as the latter. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷakabhikkhusutta</span>.

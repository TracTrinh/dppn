# Kinnughaṇḍu

## English source

Kinnughaṇḍu. One of the great <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span>, mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āṭānāṭiyasutta</span>, to be invoked when disciples of the Buddha are worried by evil spirits (DN.iii.204). He is one of the vassals of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Four Regent Gods</span> (DN.ii.258).

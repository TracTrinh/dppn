# Khemārāma

## English source

Khemārāma. A locality in Ceylon. Here <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>, having vanquished eleven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> chiefs, distributed among his troops the booty rescued from them – hence the name (Mhv.xxv.10; Mhv­ṭ.474).

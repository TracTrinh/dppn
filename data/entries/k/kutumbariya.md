# Kuṭumbariya

## English source

Kuṭumbariya. A monastery in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. A novice living there was in the habit of getting two ladles of rice at the house of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇa</span> in the district. It was a time of scarcity, and one day, seeing a guest in the house, he took only one ladleful. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇa</span> was greatly pleased, and when he reported the matter to his friends and acquaintances, they gave the novice sixty meals in perpetuity. AN­a.ii.262.

# Kosalabimbavaṇṇanā

## English source

Kosalabimbavaṇṇanā. A book containing an account of an image built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>, and of the merit of building images. The work was probably written about the thirteenth or fourteenth century. Quotations from it are given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhammasaṅgaha</span>.

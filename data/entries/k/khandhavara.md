# Khandhāvara

## English source

Khandhāvara. The family name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āyasmanta</span> (Cv.lxxx.37). They were worshippers of the god Skanda, and were an offshoot of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moriyavaṁsa</span>. According to the colophon of the Sinhalese poem, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sālalihiṇisandesa</span>.

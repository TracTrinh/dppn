# Kyānagāma

## English source

Kyānagāma. A village in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Malaya</span> country, not far from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Laṅkādhikāri</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti</span> once encamped there (Cv.lxx.283, 300; lxxii.207), and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. went there disguised as a musician. Cv.lxxii.264.

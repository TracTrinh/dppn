# Kimatthasutta

## English source

Kimatthasutta. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paribbājakas</span>, holding other views, should ask why members of the Buddha’s Saṅgha live the holy life, the reply should be “for the full understanding of *dukkha*.” They should also be told of the way to such understanding. SN.v.6.

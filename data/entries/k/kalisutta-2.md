# Kāḷīsutta

## English source

Kāḷīsutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷī Kururaghara</span> visits <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> and asks him for a detailed exposition of one of the stanzas in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumāripañhas</span> (the stanza occurs at SN.i.126). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> explains, and his explanation deals with the ten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kasiṇas</span>. AN.v.46f.

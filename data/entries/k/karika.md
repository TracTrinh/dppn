# Kārikā

## English source

Kārikā. A grammatical work in Pall, written by the Elder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasenāpati</span> at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānandavihāra</span> in Pagan. A *ṭīkā* on the work is ascribed to the same author. Gv.p.63, 73; Bode, 16 and n.1.

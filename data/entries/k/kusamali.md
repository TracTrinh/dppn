# Kusamāli

## English source

Kusamāli. One of the seas through which the mariner <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppāraka</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>) piloted his ship. It was full of emeralds and looked like an expanse of dark <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kusa</span>-grass. Its full name was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nīlavaṇṇa Kusamāla</span>. Ja.iv.140.

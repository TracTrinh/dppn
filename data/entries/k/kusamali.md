# Kusamāli

## English source

Kusamāli. One of the seas through which the mariner <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppāraka</span> (*q.v.*) piloted his ship. It was full of emeralds and looked like an expanse of dark *kusa*-grass. Its full name was *Nīlavaṇṇa Kusamāla*. Ja.iv.140.

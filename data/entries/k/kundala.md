# Kuṇḍalā

## English source

Kuṇḍalā. Daughter of the king of *Devaputta*.

Once she was a bitch in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakubandhakandara</span> and a *Sāmaṇera*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>, had given her a little food. Later, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> was on his way to the Bodhi-tree (in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gayā</span>) she saw him, and, remembering her past existence, invited him to the palace and entertained him. Later she built a *vihāra* for him, where he attained *Arahant*-ship. Ras.i.103f.

# Kisalayapūjaka Thera

## English source

Kisalayapūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dvāravatī</span>. One day, seeing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> going through the air with his attendant monks, he threw up into the sky, as an offering, an *asoka* shoot covered with buds.

Twenty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekassara</span>. Ap.i.200.

# Kañcanamāṇava

## English source

Kañcanamāṇava. The name given to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> by his parents, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccāna</span> being the family name. He was called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcana</span> because his body was of a golden colour. Thag­a.483; AN­a.i.116.

# Kontaraṭṭhapabbatavihāra

## English source

Kontaraṭṭhapabbatavihāra. A monastery in Ceylon, the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span> Thera. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span> heard from a crow of the monk’s death he went there and held great celebrations. Ras.ii.64.

# Kusinārāsutta 03

## English source

Kusinārāsutta 03. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upavattana</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusinārā</span>, in the *Sāla*-grove of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallas</span>. The Buddha, just before his death, invites the monks to question him with regard to any doubts or misgivings they may have. They remain silent (AN.ii.79f). The *Sutta* occurs also as part of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Parinibbānasutta</span>. DN.ii.137, 154.

# Khīrarukkhasutta

## English source

Khīrarukkhasutta. Where lust, malice, and infatuation exist in a man, even trifling objects, cognisable by the senses, find their way into the mind, just as, in a sap-tree, sap flows out wherever man cuts it with an axe (SN.iv.159f).

# Kathāsutta 02

## English source

Kathāsutta 02. On the five qualities which help a monk who practises <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ānāpānasati</span> to attain to the immovable state (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">akuppaṁ</span>). These qualities include conversation which enlarges the mind. AN.iii.120f.

# Kokanadā 01

## English source

Kokanadā 01. Two daughters of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajjunna</span>, both called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kokanadā</span>, though the younger was sometimes called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Kokanadā</span>. They visited the Buddha at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭāgārasālā</span> and spoke verses in praise of the Buddha, the Dhamma and the Saṅgha. SN.i.29f.

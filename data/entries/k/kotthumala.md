# Kotthumala

## English source

Kotthumala. A hill in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māyāraṭṭha</span> in Ceylon. The Almsbowl and the Tooth Relic of the Buddha were once buried there by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vācissara</span> as a protection from enemies. Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu III</span>. had them removed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambuddoṇi</span>. Cv.lxxxi.18ff; see also Cv.Trs.ii.137, n.1.

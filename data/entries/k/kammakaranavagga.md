# Kammakāraṇavagga

## English source

Kammakāraṇavagga. A group of ten *Suttas*, forming the first section of the *Dukanipāta*, on various subjects, such as faults and their punishment, worldly and spiritual striving, the two things (fear and shame) which guard the world, etc. AN.ii.47ff.

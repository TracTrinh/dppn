# Kammakāraṇavagga

## English source

Kammakāraṇavagga. A group of ten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span>, forming the first section of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Dukanipāta</span>, on various subjects, such as faults and their punishment, worldly and spiritual striving, the two things (fear and shame) which guard the world, etc. AN.ii.47ff.

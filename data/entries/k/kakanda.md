# Kākanda

## English source

Kākanda, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākandī</span>. The commentaries speak of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākaṇḍa</span> as a sage of yore and mention him in the company of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Savattha</span> and Kusumba. His residence later came to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākandī</span>. Snp-a.i.300; cp. Khp­a.110; Ud­a.55.

# Khema 05

## English source

Khema 05. The deer-park near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> (Ja.vi.480; Bv.xx.4; AN­a.i.80, 169). There <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> was born, and there he preached his first sermon to his chief disciples, and later another sermon to their eighty-four thousand followers (Bv­a.196f).

This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khema</span> is identical with the present <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isipatana</span>. DN­a.ii.471.

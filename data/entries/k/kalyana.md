# Kalyāṇa

## English source

Kalyāṇa (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇaka</span>). A king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sammata</span> race. He was the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vararoja</span> and one of the ancestors of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span>. His son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Varakalyāṇa</span>. DN­a.i.258; Snp-a.i.352; Ja.ii.311; iii.464; Mhv.ii.2; Dpv.iii.4; Mvu.i.345.

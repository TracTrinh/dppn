# Kusaṭṭhakadāyaka Thera

## English source

Kusaṭṭhakadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span> he was a Brahmin and gave the Buddha *kusaṭṭhaka* (eight handfuls of *kusa*-grass?) (Ap.ii.416). He is evidently to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migasira</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.306.

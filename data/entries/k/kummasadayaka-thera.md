# Kummāsadāyaka Thera

## English source

Kummāsadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago, having nothing else to give, he put sour gruel (*kummāsa*) into the bowl of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> (Ap.ii.415). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīvaka</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.307.

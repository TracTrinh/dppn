# Kākasutta

## English source

Kākasutta. The wicked monk is like a crow in that he possesses the same ten qualities: offensiveness, recklessness, shamelessness, excessive greed, cruelty, gruesomeness, want of strength, “earthyness” (? *oravitā*), bewilderment and meanness. AN.v.149; see also G.SN.v.101.

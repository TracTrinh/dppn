# Kaṭṭhavāhana 02

## English source

Kaṭṭhavāhana 02. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. He was the Bodhisatta, son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmadatta</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, and of a faggot-gatherer, whom the king met in a grove, singing as she picked up the sticks. His story is related in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṭṭhahārijātaka</span>. Ja.i.133ff; Dhp­a.i.349; Ja.iv.148.

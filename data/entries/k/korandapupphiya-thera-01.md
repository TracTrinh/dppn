# Koraṇḍapupphiya Thera 01

## English source

Koraṇḍapupphiya Thera 01. An Arahant. Ninety-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he saw the footprint of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> and offered to it a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">koraṇḍa</span>-plant in bloom. Fifty-seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vītamala</span> (Ap.i.206). He is probably to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ramanīyavihārī</span>. Thag­a.i.116.

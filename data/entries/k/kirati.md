# Kirāṭi

## English source

Kirāṭi. A locality in Ceylon, near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aḷisāra</span>. There <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māyāgeha</span> once captured a fortification (Cv.lxx.165). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kirāṭi</span> may be the name of a tiny river. See Cv.Trs.i.301, n.1.

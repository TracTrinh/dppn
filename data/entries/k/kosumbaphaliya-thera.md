# Kosumbaphaliya Thera

## English source

Kosumbaphaliya Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he saw the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Sikhī</span> and gave him a *kosumba*-fruit (Ap.ii.449). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Usabha</span> Thera (Thag­a.i.320).

# Kāraliyagiri

## English source

Kāraliyagiri. A monastery in Ceylon, the residence of a Thera named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span>, who taught the monks the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātukathā</span> after having given up the study of the scriptures for eighteen years. Vsm.i.96.

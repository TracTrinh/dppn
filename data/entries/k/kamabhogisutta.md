# Kāmabhogīsutta

## English source

Kāmabhogīsutta. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span> on the ten classes of wealthy men (*kāmabhogī*), so far as they deserve praise or blame. Some of them obtain their wealth by evil means, enjoy it in evil ways, and do not share it with others; others are different in different degrees. AN.v.176-82.

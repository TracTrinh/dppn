# Khandhena Sutta 01

## English source

Khandhena Sutta 01. All the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span> are impermanent. He who has faith in the doctrine is a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">saddhānusāri</span>; he who has understood it moderately is a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dhammānusāri</span>; he who knows and sees the doctrine is a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span>. SN.iii.227f.

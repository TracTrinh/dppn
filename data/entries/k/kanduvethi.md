# Kaṇḍuveṭhi

## English source

Kaṇḍuveṭhi. A city in India, the capital of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narasīha</span>; perhaps a variant reading of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇḍuveṭṭi</span> (Cv.xlvii.7). Hultszch says (JRAS.1913, p.527) that both names are synonyms with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kādavarū</span>, a designation of the Pallava kings.

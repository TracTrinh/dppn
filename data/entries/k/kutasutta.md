# Kūṭasuttā

## English source

Kūṭasuttā 04. Two discourses of similar import, preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span>. When a peaked house is un-thatched, the peak, the roof-beams and the walls are all unprotected. When thought is unguarded all actions also are unguarded. AN.i.261f.

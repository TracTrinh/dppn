# Karaṇḍaka 01

## English source

Karaṇḍaka 01. A hermitage (*assamapada*) near the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span>. The Bodhisatta, when born as an elephant as related in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mātiposakajātaka</span> (*q.v.*), returned to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Karaṇḍaka</span> after the death of his mother. The hermitage was the residence of five hundred ascetics, and the king, out of regard for the Bodhisatta, looked after them. Ja.iv.95.

# Kaṇṭhapiṭṭhi

## English source

Kaṇṭhapiṭṭhi. An important village, among those given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IX</span>. to a number of small <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāras</span> in order that the monks of these <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāras</span> could obtain rice-gruel without going, as formerly, to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span>. Cv.xlix.89.

# Kittisirimegha 03

## English source

Kittisirimegha 03. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇābharaṇa</span> by his second queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabhāvatī</span>. His grandfather was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivallabha</span> (Cv.lxiv.24). His father, on his deathbed, repented of the harm he had done to the Buddha’s religion and asked his son to do reparation by joining <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. The latter, on hearing this, sent for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirimegha</span> and made him a member of his court (Cv.lxxii.303, 311).

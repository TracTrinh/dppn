# Kapallakkhaṇḍa

## English source

Kapallakkhaṇḍa. A locality in Ceylon on the field of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Haṅkārapiṭṭhi</span>. Here was fought a fierce battle between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Iḷanāga</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇas</span>, in which the latter were slain in large numbers (Mhv.xxxv.34).

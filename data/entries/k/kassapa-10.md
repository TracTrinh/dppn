# Kassapa 10

## English source

Kassapa 10. An ascetic, also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada</span>, who lived in a hermitage near Mt. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosika</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. He saw the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Padumuttara</span> in the forest, invited him into the hermitage, provided a seat and asked for words of advice. He was a former birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekāsanadāyaka</span> Thera. Ap.ii.381.

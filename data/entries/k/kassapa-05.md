# Kassapa 05

## English source

Kassapa 05. (called *Kassapamāṇava*). The Bodhisatta in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Buddha</span>. He was a Brahmin versed in the Vedas, and having heard the Buddha preach, built a monastery costing one thousand crores. Ja.i.38; Bv.xiv.9f; Bv­a.176.

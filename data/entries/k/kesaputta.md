# Kesaputta

## English source

Kesaputta. A township of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosalans</span> and the residence of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kālāmas</span>. The Buddha once stayed there, on which occasion he preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kesaputtiyasutta</span>. AN.i.188.

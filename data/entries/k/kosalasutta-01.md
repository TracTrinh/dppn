# Kosalasutta 01

## English source

Kosalasutta 01. While <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span> is visiting the Buddha, a messenger arrives and announces the death of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallikā</span>. The king is greatly grieved, but the Buddha consoles him by pointing out how it is impossible to escape old age, decay, disease, destruction. AN.iii.57.

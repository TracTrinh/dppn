# Kāmabhūsutta 03

## English source

Kāmabhūsutta 03. Records another visit of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Citta</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāmabhū</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Citta</span> asks a series of questions about the activities of the body, speech and mind and their cessation. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāmabhū</span> explains them to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Citta</span>’s satisfaction. SN.iv.293.

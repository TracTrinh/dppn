# Kaṇirapabbhāra

## English source

Kaṇirapabbhāra. A cave, probably in the side of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cetiyagiri</span>, into which King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kanirajānutissa</span> ordered sixty monks to be flung, they having been found guilty of high treason (Mhv.xxxv.11).

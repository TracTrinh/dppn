# Kattikā

## English source

Kattikā. Name of a constellation and also of a month (October to November), during which the full moon is near the constellation of Pleiades (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Kattikā</span>).

It is the last month of the rainy season (See, e.g., Netti.143. For details see PTSPED. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">s.v.</span>).

The full moon day of the month was observed as a festival and great rejoicings were held, particularly at night, e.g., Ja.i.433; 499, 508.

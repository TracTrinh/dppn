# Kattikā

## English source

Kattikā. Name of a constellation and also of a month (October to November), during which the full moon is near the constellation of Pleiades (*Kattikā*).

It is the last month of the rainy season (See, e.g., Netti.143. For details see PTSPED. *s.v.*).

The full moon day of the month was observed as a festival and great rejoicings were held, particularly at night, e.g., Ja.i.433; 499, 508.

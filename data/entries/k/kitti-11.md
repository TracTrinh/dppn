# Kitti 11

## English source

Kitti 11. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti Senāpati</span>. General of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līlāvatī</span>. He deposed Coḍagaṅga, placed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līlāvatī</span> on the throne, and carried on the government for three years (1197-1200) (Cv.lxxx.30). He was a patron of learning (PLC.207).

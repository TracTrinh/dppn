# Kuṭelitissa Mahā Vihāra

## English source

Kuṭelitissa Mahā Vihāra. A monastery in Ceylon. It was once the residence of a *Sāmaṇera* of great *iddhi*-power who later lost all these powers as a result of falling in love with a weaver’s daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kabupelanda</span> (Vibh­a.293; but see MN­a.ii.700).

It may perhaps be the same as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭālivihāra</span> (*q.v.*).

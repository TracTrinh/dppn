# Kuṭelitissa Mahā Vihāra

## English source

Kuṭelitissa Mahā Vihāra. A monastery in Ceylon. It was once the residence of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sāmaṇera</span> of great <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhi</span>-power who later lost all these powers as a result of falling in love with a weaver’s daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kabupelanda</span> (Vibh­a.293; but see MN­a.ii.700).

It may perhaps be the same as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭālivihāra</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>).

# Kappasutta 01

## English source

Kappasutta 01. Two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappa Thera</span> – see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappa</span> (3) – asks the Buddha how it is possible to get rid of thoughts of “I” and “mine,” and to have conceit regarding the body utterly destroyed. SN.iii.169f.; see also SN.ii.253f.

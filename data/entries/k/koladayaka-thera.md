# Koladāyaka Thera

## English source

Koladāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. He was a hermit in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, and, seeing the Buddha alone, gave him a *kola* (jujube) fruit (Ap.ii.397). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gayā Kassapa</span>. Thag­a.i.417.

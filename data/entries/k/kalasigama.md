# Kalasigāma

## English source

Kalasigāma. The birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milinda</span> in the Island of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Alasandā</span> (or Alexandria) in the Indus (Mil.83). Rhys Davids (*Milinda Questions*, i., p.xxiii) thinks that the name is identical with that of the Greek settlement Karisi.

# Kesivagga

## English source

Kesivagga, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosiyavagga</span>. The twelfth chapter of the *Catukkanipāta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span> (AN.ii.112-21). The first *Sutta* is that of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kesi</span>, the horse-trainer, and most of the *Suttas* deal with the idea of “training.” The Commentary calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosiyavagga</span>.

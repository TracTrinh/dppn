# Kalyāṇitissa

## English source

Kalyāṇitissa. A king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇī</span>, father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vihāramahādevī</span> (Mhv.xxii.12ff). He was great-grandson of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muṭasīva</span> and grandson of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span>. His younger brother was called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayyauttiya</span> (Mhv­ṭ.431).

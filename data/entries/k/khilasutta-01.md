# Khilasutta 01

## English source

Khilasutta 01. Fallowness of heart arises from doubt in the Buddha, the Dhamma, the Saṅgha, and the way of training (*sikkhā*), and from anger against one’s fellow-celibates. AN.iii.248; iv.460; DN.iii.237; MN.i.101; Vibh.377, etc.

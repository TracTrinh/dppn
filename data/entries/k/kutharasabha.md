# Kuṭhārasabhā

## English source

Kuṭhārasabhā. Some sort of council chamber, probably attached to the Court in Ceylon. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirimegha</span> is mentioned as employing the services of the head of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sabhā</span> to fetch the prince <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> from his retreat (Cv.lxvi.61).

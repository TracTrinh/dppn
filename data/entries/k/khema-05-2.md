# Khemā 05

## English source

Khemā 05. Chief queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmadatta</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. She dreamed of a golden deer preaching the Dhamma, and her wish to see her dream come true was fulfilled (Ja.iv.256). For details see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rurujātaka</span> (Ja 482).

# Ketumatī 01

## English source

Ketumatī 01. The future name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bārāṇasī</span>. It will be at the head of eighty-four thousand towns, the capital of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkha</span> and the birthplace of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya Buddha</span>. DN.iii.75f; Ja.vi.594; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāgatavaṁsa</span> vv.8, 30; according to v.8 it is the same as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusāvatī</span>.

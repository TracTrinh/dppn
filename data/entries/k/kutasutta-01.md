# Kūṭasutta 01

## English source

Kūṭasutta 01. In a peaked house (*kūṭāgāra*) all the rafters converge towards the peak; so are all wrong states fixed together in ignorance. SN.ii.262.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

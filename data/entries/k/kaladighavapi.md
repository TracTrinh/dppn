# Kāḷadīghavāpi

## English source

Kāḷadīghavāpi. A lake, evidently in Ceylon. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Piṇḍapātikatissa</span> saw an elephant’s corpse floating in the lake and made it his subject of meditation for *Arahant*-ship. Vsm.i.191.

There was evidently a *vihāra* attached to it. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhima Commentary</span> (Ma.i.353f) gives a story of a novice who lived there.

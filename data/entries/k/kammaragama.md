# Kammāragāma

## English source

Kammāragāma. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, identified with modern Kamburugamuva. It was one of the places passed by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcukināyaka</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> I, in his victorious progress (Cv.lxxv.47; Cv.Trs.ii.48, n.2).

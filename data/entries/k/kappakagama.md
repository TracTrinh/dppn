# Kappakagāma

## English source

Kappakagāma, (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappukagāma</span>). A *vihāra* in Ceylon, the residence of the Thera *Deva*. It was from him that King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vohārikatissa</span> heard the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Dhamma</span> and, as a mark of favour, the king restored five buildings belonging to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappakagāma</span> monastery (Mhv.xxxvi.29).

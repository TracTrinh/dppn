# Kimatthiyasutta 01

## English source

Kimatthiyasutta 01. On the purpose of good actions, how good conduct gradually rears a summit of righteousness, from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">avippaṭisāratā</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vimuttiñāṇadassana</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> is a conversation between the Buddha and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>.

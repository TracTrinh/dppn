# Kathāsutta 01

## English source

Kathāsutta 01. A monk should not indulge in childish talk – e.g., about kings, robbers, ministers, etc. – but should speak only of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span> and things connected therewith. SN.v.419f.

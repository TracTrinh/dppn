# Kaṭacchubhikkhadāyikā Therī

## English source

Kaṭacchubhikkhadāyikā Therī. An *Arahant*. Ninety-two *kappas* ago she gave a ladleful of food to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span>. As a result, she was thirty-six times chief queen of the king of heaven and wife of fifty kings of men (Ap.ii.516f).

She is evidently to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayamātā</span>. Thīg­a.39f.

# Khuddapārinda

## English source

Khuddapārinda. A Tamil usurper. He was the brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parinda</span> who had usurped <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātusena</span>’s throne. He reigned for sixteen years (between 430 and 460 CE.). Cv.xxxviii.30f.

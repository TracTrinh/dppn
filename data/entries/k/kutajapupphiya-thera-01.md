# Kuṭajapupphiya Thera 01

## English source

Kuṭajapupphiya Thera 01. An Arahant. Ninety-two *kappas* ago he offered a *kuṭaja*-flower to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Buddha</span>. Seventeen *kappas* ago he was king three times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pupphita</span>. Ap.i.191.

# Kulasekhara 01

## English source

Kulasekhara 01. A Cholian king of South India. He besieged the Paṇḍyan king, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkama</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span>, and the latter sent for help to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. of Ceylon. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> sent an expeditionary force to South India under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span>, but in the meantime the Paṇḍyan king had been slain and his capital taken. The Sinhalese force, however, landed and carried on a prolonged campaign against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span> and his allies, who seem to have been numerous and powerful. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span> was defeated, and the Paṇḍyan king’s son, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vīra Paṇḍu</span>, was installed in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Coḷa</span> prisoners taken in the war were brought to Ceylon and employed in the reconstruction of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. For details of this war see Cv.lxxvi, and lxxvii. For <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span>’s later history see Cv.Trs.ii.100, n.1.

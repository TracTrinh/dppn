# Kappāsikavanasaṇḍa

## English source

Kappāsikavanasaṇḍa (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappāsiyavanasaṇḍa</span>). A grove near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uruvelā</span>. There the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddavaggiyā</span> came across the Buddha while seeking for a woman who had run away with certain of their belongings. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> preached to them and they entered the Saṅgha, obtaining various Fruits of the Path, from the first to the third. Vin.i.23f; Ja.i.82; Dhp­a.i.72; Dpv.i.34; AN­a.i.57, 84; Thīg­a.3.

# Kassapa 18

## English source

Kassapa 18. Younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya II</span>. He was *Mahādipāda* or *Yuvarāja* under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya</span> (Cv.li.91), and later became king as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa IV</span>. (896-913 CE.) (Cv.lii.1ff). His daughter <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena</span> married <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa V</span>. (Cv.li.93)

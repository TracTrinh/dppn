# Kallavāla

## English source

Kallavāla, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kanavālamutta</span>. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magadha</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> lived there immediately after his ordination, and on the seventh day, while meditating there, he became an *Arahant*, being admonished by the Buddha, who warned him against sloth. AN.iv.85; Thag­a.ii.94; Dhp­a.i.79; AN­a.i.91.

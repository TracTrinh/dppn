# Kilañjadāyaka Thera

## English source

Kilañjadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was a worker in reeds in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tivarā</span> and gave a mat of rushes to be offered to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Seventy-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jutindhara</span> (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jalandhara</span>). Ap.i.219.

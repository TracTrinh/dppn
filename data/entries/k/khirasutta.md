# Khīrasutta

Khīrasutta. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. Incalculable is the beginning of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṁsāra</span>. The milk drunk by a being during his wanderings in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṁsāra</span> is more in quantity than the water of the four seas.

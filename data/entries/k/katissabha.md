# Kaṭissabha

## English source

Kaṭissabha <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">v.l.</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṭissaha</span>. A lay disciple of the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nādikā</span>, one of those, who, after their death, were declared by the Buddha, in answer to a question by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, to have destroyed the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">orambhāgiya</span> fetters and to have become inheritors of the highest heaven, thence never to return. DN.ii.92; SN.v.358f.

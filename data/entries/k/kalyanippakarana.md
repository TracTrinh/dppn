# Kalyāṇippakaraṇa

## English source

Kalyāṇippakaraṇa. A record of the famous <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span> inscriptions set up near Pegu by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammaceti</span>, giving details of the consecration of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇisīmā</span> in Pegu by the monks who received their ordination at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇivihāra</span> in Ceylon. Bode, 23, 38f.; PLC.257f.

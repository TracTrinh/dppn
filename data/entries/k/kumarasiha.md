# Kumārasīha

## English source

Kumārasīha. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya</span>; he was adopted by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senāratana</span> and given the province of Uva. He died young. Cv.xcv.22; also Cv.Trs.ii.233, n.3.

# Kakkārupupphiya Thera

## English source

Kakkārupupphiya Thera *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kekkārupupphiya Thera</span>. An *Arahant*.

# Kuṭumbiyaputtatissa

## English source

Kuṭumbiyaputtatissa. An *Arahant*. He lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgudīpa</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>, fleeing from the battle at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷaṅganiyapiṭṭhi</span>, wished to give a share of his food to a monk before sitting down to eat. When the meal-time was announced, the Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama</span>, hearing it with his divine ear, sent Kuṭumbiyaputta to receive the share set apart for the brotherhood (Mhv.xxiv.22ff; xxxii.31f). It transpired later that the food so received was divided by Kuṭumbiyaputta among twelve thousand monks in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgudīpa</span>. Mhv.xxxii.55; Mhv­ṭ.598.

See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bodhimātu Mahā Tissa</span>.

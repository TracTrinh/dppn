# Kakkārupūjaka Thera

## English source

Kakkārupūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a *Deva* and offered a *kakkāru*-flower to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Sikhī</span>. Nine *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sattuttama</span> (Ap.i.177). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jenta</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.219.

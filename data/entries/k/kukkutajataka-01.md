# Kukkuṭajātaka 01

## English source

Kukkuṭajātaka 01. (Ja 383). The story of a cat who tried to deceive a cock – with the idea of eating him – by offering to become his wife. Her efforts failed. The cock was the Bodhisatta.

The story was told to a monk who was tempted by the sight of a woman (Ja.iii.265f).

This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jātaka</span> is illustrated in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Bharhut Stūpa</span>. Cunningham: Pl.xlvii.5.

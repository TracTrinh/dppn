# Kapilasutta

## English source

Kapilasutta. Preached by the Buddha to the people assembled to hear his explanation of the golden colour of the fish, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapilamaccha</span> (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapila</span> 4) (Snp-a.i.305f; Dhp­a.iv.42: Ud­a.180; Thag­a.i.356). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttanipāta</span> calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammacariyasutta</span>.

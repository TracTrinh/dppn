# Kumārapetavatthu 02

## English source

Kumārapetavatthu 02. Two sons of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> king committed adultery and were born as *Petas*. One night they uttered loud lamentations and the inhabitants of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> sought the Buddha’s protection. The Buddha explained things to them, and the people gave alms and made over their merit so gained to the *Petas*. Pv.iv.6; Pv-a.261f.

# Kulantavāpi

## English source

Kulantavāpi (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulatthavāpi</span>). A tank to the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. In the battle between the forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Eḷāra</span> the water in the tank was dyed red with blood of the slain, hence the name. Mhv.xxv.66.

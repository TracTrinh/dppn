# Kukkuṭa 02

## English source

Kukkuṭa 02. A frontier town near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>; the capital of a kingdom three hundred leagues in extent, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kappina</span> once ruled. There were three rivers to cross on the way from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuṭa</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> (Thag­a.i.507f; Ap.ii.469). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuṭavatī</span>.

# Kāḷamattiya

## English source

Kāḷamattiya, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kālamattika</span>. A forest. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muṭṭhika</span>, after his death, was born as a goblin in this forest, and when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Baladeva</span> reached the spot during his flight, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muṭṭhika</span> challenged him to a wrestling match and ate him up “like a radish-bulb.” Ja.iv.82, 88.

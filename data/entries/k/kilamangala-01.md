# Kīḷamaṅgala 01

## English source

Kīḷamaṅgala 01. A district in South India which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span> subdued and gave over to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mālava</span> (Cv.lxxvi.209-11). Its chieftain (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nāḍālvāra</span>) was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span>’s ally (Cv.lxxvii.80). It was one of the two divisions of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṅgala</span>, the other being called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Melamaṅgala</span>.

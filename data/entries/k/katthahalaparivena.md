# Kaṭṭhahālapariveṇa

## English source

Kaṭṭhahālapariveṇa. A monastic residence in or near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. A monk from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgalla</span>, who was asked to participate in the building of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>, stayed in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pariveṇa</span> during his visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. Mhv.xxx.34.

# Kalambatitthavihāra

## English source

Kalambatitthavihāra. A *vihāra* in Ceylon. In it once lived fifty monks who agreed on the full-moon day of *Āsāḷha* not to talk to each other until they became *Arahants*. Their goal was reached within a month (DN­a.i.190; SN­a.iii.155; Snp-a.i.57; Vibh­a.353; MN­a.i.209f).

This *vihāra* is very probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Galambatittha</span> (*q.v.*).

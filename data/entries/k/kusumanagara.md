# Kusumanagara

## English source

Kusumanagara. The Pāli name for the city now known as Bassein, in Burma (Bode, 24). Near the city was the birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chapaṭa</span>. Sās.74; see also pp.41, 43, 147; Ind.An. 1893, xxi.17.

# Kāsumāriphalakadāyaka Thera

## English source

Kāsumāriphalakadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he gave a *kāsumāri*-fruit to the Buddha (Ap.i.294). He is probably to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīvaka</span> *Sāmaṇera* (Thag­a.i.61).

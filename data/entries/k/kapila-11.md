# Kapila 11

## English source

Kapila 11. An ancient teacher of philosophy, mentioned together with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kanāda</span> as having taught that the soul was limitless (*na antavā*) (Ud­a.339; see also *Śvetāśvatara Upaniṣad* v.2, and *Rāmāyaṇa* i.40). He is probably identical with the founder of the *Sāṅkhya* system.

# Kaṇṇāvaḍḍhamāna

## English source

Kaṇṇāvaḍḍhamāna. A mountain in Ceylon, the residence of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span>-king, father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷodara</span> and brother-in-law of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahodara</span>. Mhv.i.49; but see my edition of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā</span>, where I take the name of the mountain to be <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaḍḍhamāna</span>.

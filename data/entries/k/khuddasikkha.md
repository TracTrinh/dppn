# Khuddasikkhā

## English source

Khuddasikkhā. A Compendium of the Vinaya, composed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasiri</span> and ascribed to a period before <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> (JPTS 1883, pp.86f). To this Compendium there exists a Sinhalese paraphrase of the eleventh century, and based on the Compendium is one *Porāna­ṭīkā* by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revata</span> and another by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅgharakkhita</span>. For details see PLC., 77; Bode, 24; see also Sās.69; Svd. 1208f; Gv.62, 70; Sās-d.64.

# Cūḷa Gallakavihāra

## English source

Cūḷa Gallakavihāra, Cūla Gallakavihāra. A monastery built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷābhaya</span> on the bank of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṇakanadī</span> to the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> (Mhv.xxxv.13). Attached to it was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Padhānaghara</span>, built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi II</span>. Cv.xlii.49.

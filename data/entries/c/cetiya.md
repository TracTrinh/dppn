# Cetiya

## English source

Cetiya. A mythical king (Mhv.ii.3; Dpv.iii.5; Mvu.i.348). See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ceti</span>.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apacara</span> is also referred to as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cetiya</span> (Ja.iii.457, 460, etc.), shortened into <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cecca</span> (Ja.v.267).

## Vietnamese translation

Cetiya. Một vị vua huyền thoại (Mhv.ii.3; Dpv.iii.5; Mvu.i.348). Xem Ceti.

Apacara cũng được gọi là Cetiya (Ja.iii.457, 460, etc.), được rút gọn thành Cecca (Ja.v.267).

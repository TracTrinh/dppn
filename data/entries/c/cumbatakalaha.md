# Cumbaṭakalaha

## English source

Cumbaṭakalaha. The name given (e.g., Ja.i.208) to the quarrel between the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koḷiyans</span> about the water of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohiṇī</span> (*q.v.*).

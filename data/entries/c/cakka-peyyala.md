# Cakka Peyyāla

## English source

Cakka Peyyāla. The seventh chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saccasaṁyutta</span> (SN.v.456-8).

When the Buddha’s teaching disappears, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṁyuttanikāya</span>, from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakka Peyyāla</span> down to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Oghataraṇa</span>, will be among the earliest portions to disappear. Vibh­a.432.

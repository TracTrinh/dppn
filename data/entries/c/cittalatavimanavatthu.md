# Cittalatāvimānavatthu

## English source

Cittalatāvimānavatthu. The story of a poor man who looked after his parents, refusing to marry, and engaged in various acts of piety. After death he was born in a twelve-league *vimāna* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>. Vv.vii.1; Vv-a.299f.

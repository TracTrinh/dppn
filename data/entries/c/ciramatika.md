# Cīramātikā

## English source

Cīramātikā. An irrigation canal, the taxes from which King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span> gave to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> (Cv.xli.100). The canal probably led out of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cīravāpi</span>.

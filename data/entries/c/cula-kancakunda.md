# Cūḷa Kañcakunda

## English source

Cūḷa Kañcakunda, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Culla Kañcakunda</span>. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> chief of South India who fought against the forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> 1. but was later subdued (Cv.lxxvi.185, 217, 220, 305). The name is closely connected with that of the districts of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcakudiya</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcakudiyarājā</span> (Cv.lxxvi.124, 130).

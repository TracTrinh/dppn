# Catusāmaṇeravatthu

## English source

Catusāmaṇeravatthu. The story of the four *sāmaṇeras*: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkicca</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍita</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sopāka</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revata</span>. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcacchiddageha</span>.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ñāṇābhivaṁsa</span> (see below) wrote a fresh account of the story. For the story see Dhp­a.iv.176f.

## Vietnamese translation

Catusāmaṇeravatthu. Câu chuyện về bốn vị *sāmaṇera* (sa-di): Saṅkicca, Paṇḍita, Sopāka, Revata. Xem Pañcacchiddageha.

Ñāṇābhivaṁsa (xem bên dưới) đã viết một bản tường thuật mới về câu chuyện này. Để xem câu chuyện, xem Dhp­a.iv.176f.

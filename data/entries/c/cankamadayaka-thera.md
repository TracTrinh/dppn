# Caṅkamadāyaka Thera

## English source

Caṅkamadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Eighteen *kappas* ago he made a magnificent cloistered walk for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span>. For three *kappas* he was king of the gods and was three times <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span>. Ap.i.99.

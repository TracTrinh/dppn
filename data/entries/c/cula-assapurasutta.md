# Cūḷa Assapurasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Assapurasutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Assapurasutta</span>. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Assapura</span> to the assembled monks.

It is not the robe that makes the recluse, nor living under a tree, nor intoning texts, bathing three times a day, etc.

It is by putting away all the evil qualities that a man becomes a true *Bhikkhu*. MN.i.281-4.

# Cūḷa Puṇṇamasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Puṇṇamasutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Puṇṇamasutta</span>. Preached to the monks assembled on a full-moon night at the *Migārāmātupāsāda*. The *Sutta* teaches how it is possible to tell a bad man and a good man through their conduct. MN.iii.20ff.

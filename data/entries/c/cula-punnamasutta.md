# Cūḷa Puṇṇamasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Puṇṇamasutta, Cūla Puṇṇamasutta. Preached to the monks assembled on a full-moon night at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Migārāmātupāsāda</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> teaches how it is possible to tell a bad man and a good man through their conduct. MN.iii.20ff.

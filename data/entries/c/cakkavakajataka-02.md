# Cakkavākajātaka 02

## English source

Cakkavākajātaka 02. (Ja 451). Similar to the above. The reason given for the colour of the crow was that his heart was full of fear and sin and that he had done evil in past lives. The greedy monk always went about looking for invitations. Ja.iv.70-2.

# Cittāpokkharaṇī

## English source

Cittāpokkharaṇī. A bathing pond in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpuyyāna</span>, erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. It was adorned with gay pictures, hence, probably, the name. Cv.lxxiii.121.

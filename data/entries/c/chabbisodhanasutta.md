# Chabbisodhanasutta

## English source

Chabbisodhanasutta. On the six-fold scrutiny by which a monk can know whether he is justified in saying that for him rebirth is no more, that his heart has been absolutely delivered from the *āsavas*. MN.iii.29-37.

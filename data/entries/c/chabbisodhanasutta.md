# Chabbisodhanasutta

## English source

Chabbisodhanasutta. On the six-fold scrutiny by which a monk can know whether he is justified in saying that for him rebirth is no more, that his heart has been absolutely delivered from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">āsavas</span>. MN.iii.29-37.

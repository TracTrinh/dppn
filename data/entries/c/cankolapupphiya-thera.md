# Caṅkolapupphiya Thera

## English source

Caṅkolapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> he was known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada Kassapa</span> and offered the Buddha a *caṅkola* flower. Seventy-four *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Romasa</span>. Ap.i.215.

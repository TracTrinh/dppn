# Cūḷa Sīhanādasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Sīhanādasutta, Cūla Sīhanādasutta. Followers of the Buddha excel those of other teachers in faith in their teacher, faith in his Dhamma, strict observance of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sīlas</span> and in love towards their fellow-believers, laymen and monks.

Their goal is the goal of men without cravings, without attachments, without facetiousness, without combative spirit, without obsessions. Their Dhamma is perfect, rightly revealed, rightly preached. MN.i.63ff.

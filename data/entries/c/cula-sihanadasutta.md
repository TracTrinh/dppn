# Cūḷa Sīhanādasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Sīhanādasutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Sīhanādasutta</span>. Followers of the Buddha excel those of other teachers in faith in their teacher, faith in his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Dhamma</span>, strict observance of the *sīlas* and in love towards their fellow-believers, laymen and monks.

Their goal is the goal of men without cravings, without attachments, without facetiousness, without combative spirit, without obsessions. Their <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Dhamma</span> is perfect, rightly revealed, rightly preached. MN.i.63ff.

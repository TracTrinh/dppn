# Cūḷa Dhanuggaha 02

## English source

Cūḷa Dhanuggaha 02. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udiccabrahmin</span>. He was a clever archer, but being rather dwarfed in stature, he joined <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bimbisāra</span> as his page and took service under the Andhra king. For details see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhīmasenajātaka</span> (Ja 80). He is also referred to as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Dhanupaṭṭhāka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cullupaṭṭhāka</span>. Ja.i.357.

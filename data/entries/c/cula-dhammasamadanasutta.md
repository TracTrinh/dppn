# Cūḷa Dhammasamādānasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Dhammasamādānasutta, Cūla Dhammasamādānasutta. Preached to the monks assembled at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. There are four ways of professing a Dhamma – one temporarily pleasant but ripening to pain thereafter; another temporarily unpleasant but ripening to pleasure, etc. – The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> also describes the monks and recluses who follow these different ways. MN.i.305ff.

# Cūḷa Buddhaghosa

## English source

Cūḷa Buddhaghosa, Cūla Buddhaghosa. An author of Ceylon to whom the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhavaṁsa</span> (pp.63, 67; see PLC.126) ascribes a work entitled <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Jātattagīnidāna</span>, probably a Jātaka Commentary, and a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotattagīnidāna</span>.

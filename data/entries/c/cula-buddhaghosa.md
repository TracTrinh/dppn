# Cūḷa Buddhaghosa

## English source

Cūḷa Buddhaghosa, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Buddhaghosa</span>. An author of Ceylon to whom the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhavaṁsa</span> (pp.63, 67; see PLC.126) ascribes a work entitled *Jātattagīnidāna*, probably a Jātaka Commentary, and a *Sotattagīnidāna*.

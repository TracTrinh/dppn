# Cara 02

## English source

Cara 02. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> chieftain to be invoked by followers of the Buddha in times of need. DN.iii.205; the PTS edition calls him <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇicara</span> as does the PTS edition of the commentary (DN.iii.970); but the Sinhalese edition, both text and commentary, divides this name into two thus: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇi</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cara</span>.

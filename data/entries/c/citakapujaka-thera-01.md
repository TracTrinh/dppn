# Citakapūjaka Thera 01

## English source

Citakapūjaka Thera 01. An Arahant. Ninety-one *kappas* ago a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span>, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, died in the forest and the Thera*,* who was then a *Deva*, descended from the *Deva*-world and burned the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span>’s body on a pyre (Ap.i.227).

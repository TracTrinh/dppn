# Cetokhilasutta 01

## English source

Cetokhilasutta 01. While the five fallows of his heart (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetokhilā</span>) are left untilled – doubts about the Teacher, about the Dhamma, about the Saṅgha and the course of training, lack of ardour – and the five bondages are unshattered (attachment to sensual pleasures, to the body, to visible forms, over-eating, desire to be born among the gods) – no monk can possibly show growth and progress in the Dhamma and the Vinaya. MN.i.101ff.

# Candakumāra 02

## English source

Candakumāra 02. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmadatta</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>; and brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahiṁsaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suriyakumāra</span>. He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. For details see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadhammajātaka</span> (Ja 6). Ja.i.127ff; Dhp­a.iii.73ff.

## Vietnamese translation

Candakumāra 02. Con trai của Brahmadatta, vua xứ Benares; và là anh trai của Mahiṁsaka và Suriyakumāra. Ngài được nhận diện là Sāriputta. Để biết chi tiết, hãy xem *Devadhammajātaka* (Ja 6). Ja.i.127ff; Dhp­a.iii.73ff.

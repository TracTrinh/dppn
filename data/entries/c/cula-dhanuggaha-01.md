# Cūḷa Dhanuggaha 01

## English source

Cūḷa Dhanuggaha 01. A Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, a very skilled archer. See the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Dhanuggahajātaka</span> (Ja 374).

## Vietnamese translation

Cūḷa Dhanuggaha 01. Một vị Bà-la-môn ở Benares, một cung thủ rất điêu luyện. Xem Cūḷa Dhanuggahajātaka (Ja 374).

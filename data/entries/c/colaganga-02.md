# Coḷagaṅga 02

## English source

Coḷagaṅga 02. King of Ceylon (1196-7 CE.). He was the son of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nissanka</span>’s sister. He slew <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vikkamabāhu III</span>. and reigned for nine months. He was then deposed by the general <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti</span> and his eyes were put out. Cv.lxxx.29ff.

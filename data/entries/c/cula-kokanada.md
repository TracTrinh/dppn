# Cūḷa Kokanadā

## English source

Cūḷa Kokanadā, Culla Kokanadā. The younger of the two daughters of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajunna</span>, both of whom were called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kokanadā</span>. She visited the Buddha at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṭāgārasālā</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesālī</span> and questioned him. SN.i.30.

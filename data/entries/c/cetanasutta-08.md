# Cetanāsutta 08

## English source

Cetanāsutta 08. Where there is action of body, speech or mind, there arises pleasure or pain caused by intention (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetanā</span>). This is due to ignorance; when ignorance is destroyed, there is no field or base for such action. AN.ii.157f.

# Campakapupphiya Thera 01

## English source

Campakapupphiya Thera 01. An Arahant. Thirty-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he offered seven flowers in a basket to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span>. Two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> later he became a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vihatābhā</span> (Ap.i.167). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Belaṭṭhānika</span> (Thag­a.i.205).

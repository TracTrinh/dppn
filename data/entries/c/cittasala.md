# Cittasālā

## English source

Cittasālā. A hall in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> to the east of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpārāma</span>, within sight of the Bodhi-tree. The body of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghamittā</span>, as desired by her, was cremated near the hall and a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Thūpa</span> was erected over the remains. Mhv.xx.52.

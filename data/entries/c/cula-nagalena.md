# Cūḷa Nāgalena

## English source

Cūḷa Nāgalena, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Nāgalena</span>. A cave in Ceylon (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tambapaṇṇi</span>). It was once the abode of five hundred monks, all of whom won *Arahant*-ship, by meditating in that spot. Vsm.127.

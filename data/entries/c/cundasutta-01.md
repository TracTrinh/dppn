# Cundasutta 01

## English source

Cundasutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cunda Kammāraputta</span> visits the Buddha in his Mango-grove in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāvā</span> and questions him as to how many classes of recluses there are in the world. Four, answers the Buddha – *maggajina*, *maggadesaka*, *maggajīvī* and *maggadūsī* – and proceeds to explain them (Snp.vs.83-90; Snp­a.i.159ff). The circumstances in which the *Sutta* was preached are given *s.v.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cunda</span> (1).

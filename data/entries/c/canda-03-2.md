# Candā 03

## English source

Candā 03. A *kinnarī*, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Canda</span>, the Bodhisatta. See the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candakinnarajātaka</span> (Ja 485, Ja.iv.283ff). She is sometimes called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candī</span>, e.g., Ja.iv.284.

## Vietnamese translation

Candā 03. Một *kinnarī*, vợ của Canda, vị Bồ-tát. Xem *Candakinnarajātaka* (Ja 485, Ja.iv.283ff). Bà đôi khi được gọi là Candī, ví dụ, Ja.iv.284.

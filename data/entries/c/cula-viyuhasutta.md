# Cūḷa Viyūhasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Viyūhasutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t15" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t15">Cūla Viyūhasutta</span>. Preached in connection with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (Snp­a.ii.554). It describes how philosophers of different schools proclaim different truths; such disputations only lead to strife in the world. Snp., pp.171-4.

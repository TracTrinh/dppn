# Candikāputta Thera

## English source

Candikāputta Thera. A discourse on the teaching of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>, delivered by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candikāputta</span> to the monks, is recorded in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāyūpasutta</span> (AN.iv.402f) (*q.v.*).

The Commentary (AN­a.ii.808) says that his mother’s name was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candikā</span>, hence his own.

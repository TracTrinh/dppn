# Cūḷantevāsika

## English source

Cūḷantevāsika. A youth of good family who, as related in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷakaseṭṭhijātaka</span> (Ja 4), earned money by his wits, after having listened to the counsel of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Kaseṭṭhi</span>. He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Panthaka</span>. Ja.i.120f.

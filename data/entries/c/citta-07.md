# Citta 07

## English source

Citta 07. A herdsman, servant of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghagāmaṇi</span>. He was put to death by the brothers of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ummādacittā</span>, because he refused to promise to kill <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ummādacittā</span>’s child should it be a boy. He was reborn as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span>. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittarāja</span>. Mahā Vaṁsa.ix.22f.; Mahāvaṁsaṭīkā.278.

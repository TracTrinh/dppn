# Citta 08

## English source

Citta 08. A minister, spoken of as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Citta Mahāmatta</span>. He was very generous and kept in his house a gong, by sounding which monks could obtain the requisites at any time. Each day he spent sixty <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kahāpaṇas</span> for the supply of medicines alone. Vibh­a.341.

# Catukuṇḍikaniraya

## English source

Catukuṇḍikaniraya. A description of the sufferings undergone by a child while in its mother’s womb. The foetus has to lie bent in four (*catukuṇḍena*), hence the name. Ja.iii.243f.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

# Catukuṇḍikaniraya

## English source

Catukuṇḍikaniraya. A description of the sufferings undergone by a child while in its mother’s womb. The foetus has to lie bent in four (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">catukuṇḍena</span>), hence the name. Ja.iii.243f.

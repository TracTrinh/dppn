# Candanagāma

## English source

Candanagāma. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. The nobles of the village took part in the festival of the arrival of the Bodhi-tree in Ceylon, and in the village one of the eight Bodhi-saplings was planted. Mhv.xix.54, 62; Vin-a.i.100; Mbv.161.

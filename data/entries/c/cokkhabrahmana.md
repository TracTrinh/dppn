# Cokkhabrāhmaṇa

## English source

Cokkhabrāhmaṇa. A Brahmin, referred to in the Commentaries (MN­a.ii.875; AN­a.i.248); no explanation is given of him in the references: *Diṭṭhivippayuttena pana ariyasāvako pariḷābhibhūto pariḷāhavūpasamatthaṁ mattahatthiparittāsito viya Cokkhabrāhmaṇo gūthaṁ kañci saṅkhāraṁ sukhato upagacchati*.

# Cūḷaṅganiyapiṭṭhi

## English source

Cūḷaṅganiyapiṭṭhi. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. There a battle was fought between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> and his brother, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gāmaṇi</span> was defeated and forced to flee. Mhv.xxxiv.19; see also xxxii.31f.; and AN­a.i.365.

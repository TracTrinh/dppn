# Candakumāra 01

## English source

Candakumāra 01. The son and viceroy of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekarāja</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pupphavatī</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>). He was the Bodhisatta. For his story see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khaṇḍahālajātaka</span> (Ja 542, Ja.vi.131ff). It is also given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cariyāpiṭaka</span> (p.77) as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candakumāracariyā</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Canda</span> is sometimes referred to as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candaka</span> (Cp. p.144) and sometimes as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candiya</span>. Ibid., 137, 152, 154

## Vietnamese translation

Candakumāra 01. Người con trai và phó vương của Ekarāja, vua xứ Pupphavatī (Benares). Ngài chính là Bodhisatta. Để biết câu chuyện của ngài, hãy xem *Khaṇḍahālajātaka* (Ja 542, Ja.vi.131ff). Nó cũng được đưa ra trong *Cariyāpiṭaka* (p.77) với tên là *Candakumāracariyā*. Canda đôi khi được gọi là Candaka (Cp. p.144) và đôi khi là Candiya. Sđd., 137, 152, 154

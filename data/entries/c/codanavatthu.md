# Codanāvatthu

## English source

Codanāvatthu. A valley (?) near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>, visited by the Buddha in the course of his wanderings.

Here he laid down a rule, allowing monks to recite the *Pātimokkha* under a learned monk if the leader of their own company should be inefficient. Vin.i.116f.

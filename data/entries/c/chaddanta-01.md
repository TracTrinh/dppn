# Chaddanta 01

## English source

Chaddanta 01. A forest in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. In the forest was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mandākinī Lake</span>, on the banks of which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aññā Koṇḍañña</span> lived in retirement for twelve years, waited upon by eight thousand elephants who had once ministered to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddhas</span>.

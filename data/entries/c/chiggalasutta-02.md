# Chiggalasutta 02

## English source

Chiggalasutta 02. It is more probable that a blind turtle, rising to the surface only once in a hundred years, should put his neck through a yoke (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">chiggala</span>) with a single hole, floating about in the ocean, than that a fool who has gone to the Downfall should become a man again (SN.v.455; cp. MN.iii.169; Thīg.500).

# Cittupaṭṭhānapāsāda

## English source

Cittupaṭṭhānapāsāda. A hall within the precincts of the king’s palace in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, where the people waited on the monks with gifts. Here King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhātika</span> provided gifts for the monks. Mhv.xxxiv.65; Mhv­ṭ.633.

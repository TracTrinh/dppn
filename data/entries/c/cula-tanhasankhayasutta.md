# Cūḷa Taṇhāsaṅkhayasutta

## English source

Cūḷa Taṇhāsaṅkhayasutta, Cūla Taṇhāsaṅkhayasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> visits <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migārāmatupāsāda</span> and asks him how a monk can be delivered by the extirpation of cravings and become foremost among gods and men. The Buddha briefly explains, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> leaves him after expressing his satisfaction.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>, desiring to know if <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> has really understood the teaching, visits him and asks him the same question. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> evades this question and, accompanied by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessavaṇa</span>, conducts <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> through his palace, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vejayanta</span>, in order to demonstrate his power. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> causes the palace to quake and, seeing the agitation of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>, again asks him the same question; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> gives the Buddha’s explanation, word for word, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> is satisfied. MN.i.251ff.

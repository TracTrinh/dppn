# Chalaṅga

## English source

Chalaṅga. A Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Haṁsavatī</span>.

He had one thousand eight hundred pupils with whose assistance he built a bridge over the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhagīrathī</span> for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha Padumuttara</span> and his disciples. He was a previous birth of Dhotaka Thera. Ap.ii.344.

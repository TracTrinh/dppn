# Candanapāsāda

## English source

Candanapāsāda. A building in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maricavaṭṭivihāra</span> erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda IV</span>. It housed the Hair Relic of the Buddha in a jewelled reliquary. Cv.liv.40f.

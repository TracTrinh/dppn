# Chattavaḍḍhi

## English source

Chattavaḍḍhi. The spot in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna I</span>. presented his parasol to the monks as a mark of homage. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pariveṇa</span> called by the same name was built there. Mhv.xxxix.32.

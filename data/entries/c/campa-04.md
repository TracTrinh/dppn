# Campā 04

## English source

Campā 04. The river which flowed between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅga</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magadha</span> (now called Chāndan). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Campeyya</span> held sway over the river. Ja.iv.454f.

## Vietnamese translation

Campā 04. Con sông chảy giữa Aṅga và Magadha (nay gọi là Chāndan). Nāga vương Campeyya cai quản con sông này. Ja.iv.454f.

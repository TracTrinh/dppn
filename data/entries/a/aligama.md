# Āḷigāma

## English source

Āḷigāma. A stronghold in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āḷisāra</span> district on the banks of the modern Ambaṅgaṅga. Here <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span>’s forces fought a decisive battle with those of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span>. Cv.lxx.113ff, and Geiger’s note thereon in the Cv.Trs.i.296, n.4.

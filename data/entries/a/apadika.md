# Apadika

## English source

Apadika, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amarika</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aparika</span>. A river. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vasabha</span> Thera*,* in a previous birth as the *jaṭila* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada</span>, erected on its banks a *cetiya* in memory of the Buddha. Thag­a.i.258; Ap.ii.437. In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apadāna</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vasabha</span> Thera is referred to as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulinathūpiyatthera</span>, and the river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apadika</span> as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amarika</span> (var. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aparika</span> Ap.ii.p.437).

## Vietnamese translation

Apadika, Amarika, Aparika. Một con sông. Vasabha Thera, trong một đời trước là *jaṭila* Nārada, đã dựng trên bờ sông ấy một *cetiya* để tưởng niệm Đức Phật. Thag­a.i.258; Ap.ii.437. Trong Apadāna, Vasabha Thera được gọi là Pulinathūpiyatthera, và sông Apadika được gọi là Amarika (var. Aparika Ap.ii.p.437).

# Ambaṅganaṭṭhāna

## English source

Ambaṅganaṭṭhāna. The spot where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span> gave a mango to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span>.

The Elder ate the mango and had the stone planted in the ground. Immediately a tree grew from it and the earth trembled. The Elder declared that the spot would become a place of assembly for the Saṅgha of Ceylon and would be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambaṅgana</span>. Vin-a.i.101.

# Arahaṁsutta 03

## English source

Arahaṁsutta 03. That noble disciple is released by perfect insight (*sammadaññā*) who has really seen the satisfaction in, the misery of, the escape from, the five *indriyas*. SN.v.194.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

# Ajagara

## English source

Ajagara. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span> who lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>. He was seen there by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>, but not by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>’s companion <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakkhaṇa</span> Thera. Later, in answer to a question by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakkhaṇa</span> Thera<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">,</span> the Buddha revealed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span>’s past. He had been a bandit in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>’s time, and having been unintentionally offended by the treasurer <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumaṅgala</span>, who had built a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhakuṭi</span> for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, he sought to take revenge on him and to make him angry by committing various heinous crimes against him. But the latter showed no wrath, and once, after having given alms to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, he gave over the merit, so gained, to the bandit. He thereupon repented, but his evil <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kamma</span> was too great for him to be able to win any special attainment. Dhp­a.iii.60ff.

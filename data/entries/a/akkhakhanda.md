# Akkhakhaṇḍa

## English source

Akkhakhaṇḍa. A section of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vidhurajātaka</span> which deals with events leading up to the surrendering of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vidhura</span> by the king, when the latter lost his wager with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇaka</span>. Ja.vi.286.

# Añjanavasabha

## English source

Añjanavasabha. The state elephant of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span>, king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurus</span>.

It was credited with the power of bringing rain; the Brahmins of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantapura</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaliṅga</span>, therefore, begged for it during a severe drought.

But the elephant was of no avail, the rain did not come, and so it was returned to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span>. Ja.ii.368f.; Dhp­a.iv.88f.

## Vietnamese translation

Añjanavasabha. Voi chúa của <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span>, vua xứ <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurus</span>.

Con voi được cho là có năng lực kêu mưa; các Bà-la-môn xứ <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantapura</span> ở <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaliṅga</span> vì vậy đã xin mượn nó trong thời hạn hán khốc liệt.

Nhưng con voi cũng vô ích, mưa không rơi, và vì thế nó được trả lại cho <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span>. Ja.ii.368f.; Dhp­a.iv.88f.

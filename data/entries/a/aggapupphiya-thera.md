# Aggapupphiya Thera

## English source

Aggapupphiya Thera. One of the *Arahants*. In a previous birth he had offered flowers, from the top of a tree, to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, hence the name. In later birth he was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amita</span>. Ap.i.229.

## Vietnamese translation

Aggapupphiya Thera. Một trong các *Arahants*. Trong một đời trước, ông đã dâng hoa từ ngọn cây lên <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, do đó có tên này. Trong một đời sau, ông là một <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> tên là <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amita</span>.

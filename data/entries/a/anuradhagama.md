# Anurādhagāma

## English source

Anurādhagāma. The name given to the settlement founded by the two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādha</span>s. It was near the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kadambanadi</span> (Mhv.ix.9; x.76). The capital, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, was later founded near it.

## Vietnamese translation

Anurādhagāma. Tên được đặt cho khu định cư do hai Anurādha thành lập. Nó ở gần Kadambanadi (Mhv.ix.9; x.76). Kinh đô Anurādhapura về sau được thành lập gần nơi ấy.

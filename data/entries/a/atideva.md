# Atideva

## English source

Atideva. The Bodhisatta born as a Brahmin in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revata Buddha</span>. Having heard the Buddha preach he gave him his upper garment (Ja.i.35; Bv.vi.10; Mbv.10). He belonged to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rammavatī</span>. Bv­a.134.

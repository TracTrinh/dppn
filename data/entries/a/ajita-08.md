# Ajita 08

## English source

Ajita 08. (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajina</span>). A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> who lived ninety-one *kappas* ago. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāsaka Thera</span>*,* in a previous birth, gave him mangoes to eat).

# Asoka 06

## English source

Asoka 06. The chief disciple of the future <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Metteyya Buddha</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāgatavaṁsa</span>. v.97). According to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> (xxxii.81) he should be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Asoka 06. Vị đệ tử đứng đầu của Metteyya Buddha trong tương lai (Anāgatavaṁsa. v.97). Theo Mahā Vaṁsa (xxxii.81), vị này nên được đồng nhất với Duṭṭhagāmaṇī.

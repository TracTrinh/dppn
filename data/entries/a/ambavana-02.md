# Ambavana 02

## English source

Ambavana 02. A district in Ceylon, near the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khīravāpi</span>. It was not far from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthinagara</span>. The name is preserved in that of the Ambaṅgaṅga which flows through the valley of Matale. Cv.lxvi.85; lxix.9; lxx.98, 191-6. See also Cv. trans. i.260, n.1.

# Alasaka

## English source

Alasaka. The name of a disease, of which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Korakhattiya</span> died (DN.iii.7). Rhys Davids translates it as “epilepsy” and suggests that its name is a negative of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">lasikā</span>, the synovial fluid. Dial.iii.12, n.2.

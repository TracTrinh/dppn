# Aggimāla (Aggimāli

## English source

Aggimāla (Aggimāli). A mythological sea, which stands like a blazing bonfire and is filled with gold (Ja.iv.139-40). It is one of the seas crossed by the merchants mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppārakajātaka</span> (Ja 463).

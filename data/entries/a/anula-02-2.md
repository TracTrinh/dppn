# Anulā 02

## English source

Anulā 02. Widow of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khallāṭanāga</span>, King of Ceylon, and later wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> (Mhv.xxxiii.35, 36). When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> had to flee from his enemies, she was the only one of his wives whom he took with him, because she was with child (Mhv.xxxiii.45). Later, when they were hiding in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Malaya</span>, under the protection of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tanasiva</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anulā</span> quarrelled with the wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tanasiva</span> and, as a result, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> killed him. (Mhv.xxxiii.62ff).

## Vietnamese translation

Anulā 02. Góa phụ của Khallāṭanāga, vua Ceylon, và về sau là vợ của Vaṭṭagāmaṇī (Mhv.xxxiii.35, 36). Khi Vaṭṭagāmaṇī phải chạy trốn khỏi kẻ thù, bà là người vợ duy nhất mà ông đưa đi cùng, vì bà đang mang thai (Mhv.xxxiii.45). Về sau, khi họ ẩn náu ở Malaya dưới sự che chở của Tanasiva, Anulā cãi nhau với vợ của Tanasiva và, do đó, Vaṭṭagāmaṇī giết ông ta. (Mhv.xxxiii.62ff).

# Abhayanāga

## English source

Abhayanāga. Younger brother of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vohārikatissa</span>. With the help of his uncle <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhadeva</span> he conspired against the king and, obtaining the assistance of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷas</span>, he overthrew and killed him. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayanāga</span> reigned for eight years (CE 291-9). Mhv.xxvi.42-53.

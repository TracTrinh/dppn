# Apanthaka

## English source

Apanthaka. Given as a personal name in a passage where it is stated that names are mere designators, they signify nothing. Thus “<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Panthakas</span>” (Guides) too lose their way, so do “<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Apanthakas</span>.” Ja.i.403.

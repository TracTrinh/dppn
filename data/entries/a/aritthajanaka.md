# Ariṭṭhajanaka

## English source

Ariṭṭhajanaka. Son of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Janaka</span>, whom he succeeded as King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mithilā</span>. His brother was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Polajanaka</span>, the viceroy, who later killed him and captured his kingdom. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ariṭṭhajanaka</span>’s son was the Prince <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Janaka</span>, who was the Bodhisatta. Ja.vi.3042.

# Accuta 03

## English source

Accuta 03. A hermit, black-toothed and with matted hair, who lived in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅka</span> forest near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅkagiri</span>. He directed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jūjaka</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessantara</span>’s dwelling in the forest (Ja.vi.532). He was a previous incarnation of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> (Ja.vi.593).

## Vietnamese translation

Accuta 03. Một ẩn sĩ, răng đen và tóc bện, sống trong rừng <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅka</span> gần <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅkagiri</span>. Ông chỉ đường cho <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jūjaka</span> đến nơi cư trú của <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessantara</span> trong rừng (Ja.vi.532). Ông là một tiền thân của <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> (Ja.vi.593).

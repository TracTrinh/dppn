# Ambasakkharapetavatthu

## English source

Ambasakkharapetavatthu. The story of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambasakkhara</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span>, as given under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambasakkhara</span>. The Elder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kappiṭaka</span> related the story to the Buddha, and the Buddha made it an occasion for a discourse to the assembled multitude. Pv-a.243-4.

# Anattasutta 01

## English source

Anattasutta 01. Preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rādha</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> in answer to his question “What is not-self?” SN.iii.196.

## Vietnamese translation

Anattasutta 01. Được thuyết cho Rādha tại Sāvatthī để trả lời câu hỏi của vị ấy: "Cái gì là vô ngã?" SN.iii.196.

# Abbhasutta

## English source

Abbhasutta. Thunder clouds arise sometimes because the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abbhāvalāhaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> wish to give joy to their bodies. SN.iii.256.

## Vietnamese translation

Abbhasutta. Mây sấm đôi khi khởi lên vì chư thiên Abbhāvalāhaka muốn đem lại niềm vui cho thân họ. SN.iii.256.

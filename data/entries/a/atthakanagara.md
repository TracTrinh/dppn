# Aṭṭhakanagara

## English source

Aṭṭhakanagara. A city, from which came the householder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dasama</span> who, while on a visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span> on business, went to see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Beluvagāma</span> and questioned him (MN.i.349f.; AN.v.342-7). The conversation is recorded in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṭṭhakanāgarasutta</span>.

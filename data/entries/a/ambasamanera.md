# Ambasāmaṇera

## English source

Ambasāmaṇera. Name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silākāla</span>. When he was a novice in the Saṅgha, at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bodhimaṇḍa</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Vihāra</span>, he fulfilled his duties to the community with zeal and skill. Once he presented a mango-fruit to the Saṅgha, and the monks, being pleased, gave him this name. Cv.xxxix.48ff.

# Anatthatāyasutta

## English source

Anatthatāyasutta. Negligence (*pamāda*) conduces to great loss. AN.i.16.

## Vietnamese translation

Anatthatāyasutta. Sự phóng dật (*pamāda*) dẫn đến tổn thất lớn. AN.i.16.

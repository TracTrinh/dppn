# Anuruddha 06

## English source

Anuruddha 06. Author of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammatthasaṅgaha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paramatthavinicchaya</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāmarūpapariccheda</span> and, perhaps, of the *Anuruddhaśataka* (Gv.61, 67; SdS. 64; Sās.69). He was an incumbent of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūlasoma</span> *Vihāra* and probably lived in the eleventh or twelfth century. For details see PLC., *s.v.*

# Āvāsikavagga

## English source

Āvāsikavagga. The twenty-fourth chapter of the *Pañcakanipāta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>. It consists of ten *Suttas* dealing with the qualities of a resident monk which make him worthy of honour and agreeable, or otherwise. AN.iii.261-7.

# Āghātavinayasutta 02

## English source

Āghātavinayasutta 02. A sermon by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> to the monks on the way in which ill-will arises in men, and the methods by which it may be overcome. These methods are illustrated by various similes. AN.iii.186-90.

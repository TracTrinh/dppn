# Acchariyabbhutasutta

## English source

Acchariyabbhutasutta (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Acchariyadhammasutta</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Acchariyabbhutadhammasutta</span>). The wonders attendant on the nativity of a being destined to become <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span>, described from the time of his leaving the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span> heaven.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> gives them in detail with the Buddha listening and giving his approval. MN.iii.118ff.

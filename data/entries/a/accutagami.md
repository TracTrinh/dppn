# Accutagāmī

## English source

Accutagāmī. One of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijaya</span>’s companions in colonising Ceylon. He founded a settlement at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ujjenī 2</span> (Dpv.ix.32, 36). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> (Dpv.vii.45) mentions the founding of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ujjenī 2</span>, but does not give <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Accutagāmī</span>’s name.

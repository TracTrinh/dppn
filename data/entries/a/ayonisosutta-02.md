# Ayonisosutta 02

## English source

Ayonisosutta 02. In one who practices unsystematic attention, sensual desires arise afresh and grow; similarly malevolence, sloth and torpor. In him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhānā</span> fade away. The reverse happens in the case of the man who practises systematic attention. SN.v.84.

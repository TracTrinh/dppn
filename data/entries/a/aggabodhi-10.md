# Aggabodhi 10

## English source

Aggabodhi 10. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VII</span>. King of Ceylon (CE 766-72), son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda I</span>. He was first ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span> and, later, joint king with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VI</span>. He married <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghā</span>, daughter of the latter, and became king on his death. Cv.xlviii.39, 60, 61, 68, 80.

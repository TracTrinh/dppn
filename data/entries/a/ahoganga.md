# Ahogaṅgā

## English source

Ahogaṅgā. A mountain in North India, on the Upper <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ganges</span>. There, for some time, lived the Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambhūta</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāṇavāsi</span>, and it was there that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yasa Kākaṇḍakaputta</span> saw him. The meeting of *Arahants* to discuss what measures should be taken against the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesālī</span> monks was also held there, and at the meeting were present monks from the Western country and from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Avanti Dakkhiṇāpatha</span> (Vin.ii.298-9).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggaliputta</span> lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ahogaṅgā</span> all alone for seven years, prior to the Third Council for which he was awaiting the right time (Mhv.v.233; see also Vin. Texts, ii.146, n.1. The Mbv., p.106, says *upari Gaṅgāya*; see also Vin-a.i.57). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> describes it as being “further up the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ganges</span>” (*uddhaṁ Gaṅgāya*).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggaliputtatissa</span> came from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ahogaṅgā</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span> on a raft. Vin-a.i.57.

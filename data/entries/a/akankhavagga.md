# Ākaṅkhavagga

## English source

Ākaṅkhavagga. The eighth chapter of the *Dasakanipāta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>.

It consists of ten *Suttas* on such subjects as the “thornless” life, the obstacles to desired things, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migasālā</span>’s questions on the future life of individuals, the likeness of a bad monk to a crow, the qualities of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigaṇṭhas</span>, etc. AN.v.131-51.

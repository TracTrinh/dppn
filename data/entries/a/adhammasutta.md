# Adhammasutta

## English source

Adhammasutta. Three <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> describing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Dhamma</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">adhamma</span> and their different qualities (AN.v.222ff). In the last <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> explains in detail what the Buddha taught to the monks in brief.

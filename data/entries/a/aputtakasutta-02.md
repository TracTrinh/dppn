# Aputtakasutta 02

## English source

Aputtakasutta 02. Contains an account of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aputtaka</span>’s past; the wealth that a man stores here has to be left behind for others; hence let him make a good store for life elsewhere by using this wealth well. SN.i.91-3.

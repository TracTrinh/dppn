# Aggisutta 01

## English source

Aggisutta 01. A number of monks go to the *Paribbājakārāma* at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, and have a courteous discussion with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paribbājakas</span>, who claim that their teaching is the same as the Buddha’s. The monks are unable to refute their claim and seek the Buddha’s advice. He tells them that the *bojjhaṅgas* form the distinctive feature of the Dhamma and that the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paribbājakas</span>, if questioned about them, would not be able to answer. SN.v.112.

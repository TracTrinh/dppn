# Apheggusāradīpanī

## English source

Apheggusāradīpanī. A book composed at Haṁsavatī, probably by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Suvaṇṇadīpa</span>, teacher of Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīvalī</span>. In Nevill’s MS. Catalogue in the British Museum it is described as an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">anuṭīkā</span> dealing with matter in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammatthavibhāvanī</span>. Bode, 36, n.2.

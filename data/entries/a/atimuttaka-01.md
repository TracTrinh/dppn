# Atimuttaka 01

## English source

Atimuttaka 01. A cemetery near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, where robbers used to deposit their stolen goods. Two ascetics, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇḍavya</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpāyana</span>, lived there.

# Ādāsamaṇḍapa

## English source

Ādāsamaṇḍapa. One of the numerous buildings erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpuyyāna</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. It was so-called because its walls were made of mirrors. Cv.lxxiii.119.

## Vietnamese translation

Ādāsamaṇḍapa. Một trong nhiều tòa nhà do Parakkamabāhu I dựng lên trong Dīpuyyāna ở Pulatthipura. Nó được gọi như vậy vì các bức tường được làm bằng gương. Cv.lxxiii.119.

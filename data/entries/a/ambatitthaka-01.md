# Ambatitthaka 01

## English source

Ambatitthaka 01. A Tamil stronghold surrounded by a river and a moat; it was captured by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> after a siege of four months (Mhv.xxv.7-9). The crafty <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Titthamba</span> lived there, and it is said that, in the end, he was conquered by a conspiracy in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> offered to allow him to marry <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>’s mother (Mhv­ṭ.473f). Near here was a ford across the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>.

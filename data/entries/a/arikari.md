# Arikārī

## English source

Arikārī. A monastery in Ceylon. It is not recorded by whom it was first built. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya I</span>. found it in a dilapidated condition and had it repaired. He also built there a house for the distribution of food and added a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pāsāda</span> (Cv.xlix.32).

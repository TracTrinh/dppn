# Anulā 05

## English source

Anulā 05. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Seṭṭhi</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. She lived with her husband in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Andhakavinda</span>, and after her father’s death she fed Brahmins in his name, but this pious act was of no benefit to him (Pv-a.105ff).

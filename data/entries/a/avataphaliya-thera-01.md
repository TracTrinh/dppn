# Avaṭaphaliya Thera 01

## English source

Avaṭaphaliya Thera 01. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. In a previous birth, ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago, he gave an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">avaṭa</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tāla</span>?) fruit to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sataraṁsī</span> (Ap.ii.409). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambula Kaccāyana</span>. Thag­a.i.314.

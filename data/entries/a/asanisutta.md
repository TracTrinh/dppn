# Asanisutta

## English source

Asanisutta. What is the falling of a thunderbolt compared with the danger for a learner (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sekha</span>) arising from gains, favours and flattery? (SN.ii.229).

The Commentary explains that a thunderbolt destroys one life-span only, while gains, etc., bring a man to infinitely prolonged misery. SN­a.ii.154.

# Aggabodhi 09

## English source

Aggabodhi 09. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VI</span>. King of Ceylon (CE 727-66), son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span>, brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda I</span>., and, at one time, ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pācīnadesa</span> (Cv.xlviii.32). He became king under the name <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāmegha</span>. Cv.vv.42, 60, 61, 76, 90; see Geiger, Cv. trans. i.114, n.2.

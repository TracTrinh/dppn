# Atulamba

## English source

Atulamba. The mango tree produced by the juggler <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaṇḍukaṇṇa</span> to make Prince <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Panāda</span> laugh. The mango is known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessavaṇa</span>’s mango and it is impossible to approach it. Ja 489 Ja.iv.324; see also ii.397.

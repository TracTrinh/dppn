# Ādipādapunnāgakhaṇḍa

## English source

Ādipādapunnāgakhaṇḍa. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> in the south of Ceylon. It was in the district of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Guttasāla</span>. Here an encounter took place between the forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. and those of the rebels in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. Cv.lxxv.14.

Nơi này thuộc quận <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Guttasāla</span>.

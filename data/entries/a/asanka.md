# Āsaṅkā

## English source

Āsaṅkā. The adopted daughter of the Bodhisatta in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āsaṅkajātaka</span> (Ja 380). She was so-called because she came to him when he crossed the water owing to his doubt (*āsaṅkā*) as to what was in the lotus. Ja.iii.250.

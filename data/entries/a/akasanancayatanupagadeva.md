# Ākāsānañcāyatanūpagādevā

## English source

Ākāsānañcāyatanūpagādevā. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> born in the Realm of Infinite Space (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ākāsānañcāyatana</span>) (MN.iii.103).

They belong to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arūpa</span> world and their life term is twenty thousand <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> (AN.i.267; Abhidh-s., p.23).

Their mind arises and ceases moment by moment (Kv.i.207-8).

In the description of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arūpāvacarabhūmi</span>, these <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> represent the lowest limit, the highest being the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nevasaññānāsaññā</span>. Paṭis.i.84.

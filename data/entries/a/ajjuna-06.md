# Ajjuna 06

## English source

Ajjuna 06. The eldest of the five sons of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span>, all of whom were married to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhā</span>. On discovering her liason with a hunchbacked slave and her treachery towards themselves, they gave her up and retired to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span> (Ja.v.425f). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajjuna</span> was a previous birth of the bird-king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuṇāla</span> (Ja.v.427).

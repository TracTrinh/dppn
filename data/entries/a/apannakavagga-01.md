# Apaṇṇakavagga 01

## English source

Apaṇṇakavagga 01. The eighth chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Catukkanipāta</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>. It consists of ten <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> on various topics, including an extract from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Parinibbānasutta</span> and a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> containing reasons why women are excluded from public assemblies and serious business. AN.ii.76-53.

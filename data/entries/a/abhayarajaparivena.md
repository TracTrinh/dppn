# Abhayarājapariveṇa

## English source

Abhayarājapariveṇa. A building erected by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu IV</span>. in connection with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vanaggāmapāsāda</span> *Vihāra*.

He built it in order that he might give the merits arising there from to his father, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu II</span>. It was richly endowed (Cv.lxxxviii.51-2; Cv. trans. ii.186, n.4).

## Vietnamese translation

Abhayarājapariveṇa. Một công trình do vua Vijayabāhu IV. dựng lên liên quan đến Vanaggāmapāsāda *Vihāra*.

Ông xây nó để có thể hồi hướng công đức phát sinh từ đó cho cha mình, Parakkamabāhu II. Công trình được cấp dưỡng dồi dào (Cv.lxxxviii.51-2; Cv. trans. ii.186, n.4).

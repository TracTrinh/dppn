# Aggisama

## English source

Aggisama. The Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pupphathūpiya</span> was born sixteen times in succession as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> and ruled under this name.

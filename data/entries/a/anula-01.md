# Anula 01

## English source

Anula 01. A Thera<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">,</span> incumbent of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭipabbata</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Vihāra</span> in Ceylon. He evidently possessed the celestial eye and, seeing how <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumana</span>, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakuṇṭaka Atimbara</span>, had once been a pig, he expressed marvel that such things should happen. She heard his exclamation, and having learnt from him the story of that past life, she herself got the power of seeing her past lives. Dhp­a.iv.50-1.

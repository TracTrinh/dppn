# Araṇāsutta

## English source

Araṇāsutta. On the Undefiled. Preached in answer to a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span>’s questions as to who are undefiled and fit to receive homage from everyone. Monks, says the Buddha, are so worthy (SN.i.44-5).

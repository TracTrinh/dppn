# Aggivessana

## English source

Aggivessana. Probably the name of a Brahmin clan, the Agniveśyāyanas, and the Kṣatriyas who were so styled, took the name from their Brahmin *Purohitas* (*Further Dialogues*, i.162 n).

The name is used by the Buddha in addressing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saccaka Nigaṇṭhāputta</span> (MN.i.229f; 237f), and also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghanakha</span> *Paribbājaka* (MN.i.497f).

In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantabhūmisutta</span> (MN.iii.128f) the novice <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aciravata</span> is thus addressed by Prince <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayasena</span>, who visits him, and also by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Aggivessana. Có lẽ là tên của một dòng tộc Bà-la-môn, dòng Agniveśyāyana, và các Kṣatriya được gọi như vậy đã lấy tên từ các vị *Purohita* Bà-la-môn của họ (*Further Dialogues*, i.162 n).

Tên này được Đức Phật sử dụng khi xưng hô <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saccaka Nigaṇṭhāputta</span> (MN.i.229f; 237f), và cũng với <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghanakha</span> *Paribbājaka* (MN.i.497f).

Trong <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantabhūmisutta</span> (MN.iii.128f), sa-di <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aciravata</span> được hoàng tử <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayasena</span>, người đến thăm vị ấy, xưng hô như vậy, và cũng bởi <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span>.

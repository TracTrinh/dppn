# Akataññujātaka

## English source

Akataññujātaka (Ja 90). A merchant is befriended by a colleague in another country but refuses to return the service. The servants of the latter thereupon take revenge. The story is related to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span>, who experiences similar ingratitude at the hands of a fellow-merchant. Ja.i.377-9.

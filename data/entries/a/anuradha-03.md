# Anurādha 03

## English source

Anurādha 03. A Sākiya prince, brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddakaccānā</span>; a great uncle of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍukābhaya</span>. He founded a settlement at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhagāma</span> and constructed a tank, to the south of which he erected a house for himself. Later he handed this over to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍukābhaya</span>. Mhv.vii.43-4.

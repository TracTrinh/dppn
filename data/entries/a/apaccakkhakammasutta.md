# Apaccakkhakammasuttā

## English source

Apaccakkhakammasuttā. Five discourses in which the Buddha explains to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vacchagotta</span> how diverse opinions arise through want of clearness about the facts of body, feeling, perception, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">volitions</span> and consciousness. SN.iii.262.

# Accima

## English source

Accima. A king. One of the descendants of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sammata</span> (Dpv.iii.8; Mvu.ii.5ff.; see also Mvu.i.348. Mhv­ṭ. 126).

He had twenty-eight sons and grandsons, of immeasurably long life, who reigned in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kusāvatī</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mithilā</span>.

# Anuruddhasutta 05

## English source

Anuruddhasutta 05. On the eight thoughts of a great being (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mahāpurisa vitakka</span>). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span> had acquired seven of them and the Buddha paid him a special visit to teach him the eighth, which brought him <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship. Later the Buddha repeated the sermon to the monks. AN.iv.228ff.

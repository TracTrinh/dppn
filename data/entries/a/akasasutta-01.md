# Ākāsasutta 01

## English source

Ākāsasutta 01. A conversation between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> on the attainment of and dwelling in the sphere of the infinity of space (SN.iii.237). The full title of the *Sutta* should be <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ākāsānañcāyatana</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Ākāsasutta 01. Cuộc đàm thoại giữa <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> và <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> tại <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> về sự chứng đạt và an trú trong Không vô biên xứ (SN.iii.237). Tên đầy đủ của bài kinh nên là <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ākāsānañcāyatana</span>.

# Assakaṇṇa

## English source

Assakaṇṇa. One of the mountains round <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sineru</span> (Snp-a.ii.443; Vin-a.i.119). It is higher than <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinataka</span>, and between these two flows the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīdantara Samudda</span>. Ja.vi.125.

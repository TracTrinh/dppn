# Ariyamuni

## English source

Ariyamuni. One of the monks who were sent to Ceylon by the King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayojjhā</span>, at the request of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>, to re-establish the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upasampadā</span> Ordination in Ceylon. He is mentioned as second in order to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upāli</span>, the leader of the delegation of Siamese monks. Cv.c.95; also Cv.trans.ii.282, n.2.

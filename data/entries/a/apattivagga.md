# Āpattivagga

## English source

Āpattivagga. The twenty-fifth chapter of the *Catukkanipāta* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>, containing ten *Suttas* on various subjects. AN.ii.239-46.

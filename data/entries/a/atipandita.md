# Atipaṇḍita

## English source

Atipaṇḍita. The Bodhisatta was once born as the son of a merchant family in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span> and was named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍita</span>. He entered into partnership with another man, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atipaṇḍita</span>, who tried to deceive him but in vain. Ja.i.405f.

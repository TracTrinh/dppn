# Añjanapabbata

## English source

Añjanapabbata. One of the six peaks of the Himālaya from which rose the five great rivers and round which were the seven lakes (Ja.v.415). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pabbata</span>, one of the seven chief pupils of the Bodhisatta <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>, had his hermitage there. Ja.v.133.

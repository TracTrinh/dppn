# Ambayāgudāyaka Thera 02

Ambayāgudāyaka Thera 02. (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambayāgadāyaka Thera</span>?).

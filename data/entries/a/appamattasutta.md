# Appamattasutta

## English source

Appamattasutta. Also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asamattasutta</span>. Association with the worthy, listening to the Dhamma, systematic reflection and living according to the precepts of the Dhamma – these things, if cultivated, lead to infinite insight (SN.v.412). This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> should probably be called Appamatta; the text gives both names. </p>.

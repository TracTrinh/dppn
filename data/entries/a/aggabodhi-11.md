# Aggabodhi 11

## English source

Aggabodhi 11. Maternal cousin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghā</span>, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VII</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghā</span>, estranged from her husband, joined the nuns at her father’s suggestion, he ran away with her, but was later caught, and the family became reconciled. Cv.xlviii.50, 60-1.

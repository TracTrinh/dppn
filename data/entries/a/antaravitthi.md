# Antaraviṭṭhi

## English source

Antaraviṭṭhi, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Antaraviṭṭhika</span>. One of the villages given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lābhavāsī</span> monks. It was situated in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājaraṭṭha</span> not far from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> (Cv.lx.68), probably between that town and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhasāra</span>. It is mentioned in an account of battles which apparently took place in the neighbourhood of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. Ibid., lxi.46; lxx.322; see also Cv. trans. i.221, n.4, and 229. n.2.

## Vietnamese translation

Antaraviṭṭhi, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Antaraviṭṭhika</span>. Một trong các ngôi làng được <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. ban cho các vị tăng <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lābhavāsī</span>. Làng này nằm ở <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājaraṭṭha</span>, không xa <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> (Cv.lx.68), có lẽ giữa thành ấy và <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhasāra</span>. Nó được nhắc đến trong một tường thuật về các trận chiến dường như diễn ra ở vùng lân cận <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. Ibid., lxi.46; lxx.322; xem thêm Cv. trans. i.221, n.4, và 229. n.2.

# Ajita 04

## English source

Ajita 04. General of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Licchavīs</span> and follower of the Buddha. Immediately after his death he was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>; he visited the Buddha to refute a statement made about him by the naked ascetic <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭikaputta</span> to the effect that he had been born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahā Niraya</span> as a result of having followed the teaching of the Buddha. DN.iii.15-16; DN­a.iii.825.

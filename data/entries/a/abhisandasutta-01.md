# Abhisandasutta 01

## English source

Abhisandasutta 01. Unvarying loyalty to the Buddha, to the Dhamma, to the Saṅgha and possession of virtues dear to the Ariyan – these are the four floods of merit that bring happiness. SN.v.391-2.

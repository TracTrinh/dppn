# Aggabodhi 06

## English source

Aggabodhi 06. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span>, claiming descent from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Okkāka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghasivā</span>. He was independent ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. Cv.xlv.38-48.

## Vietnamese translation

Aggabodhi 06. Con trai của Mahā Tissa, tuyên bố có dòng dõi từ Okkāka và Saṅghasivā. Ông là vị cai trị độc lập của Rohaṇa. Cv.xlv.38-48.

# Anoma 07

## English source

Anoma 07. One of the two chief disciples of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anomadassī Buddha</span>. Ja.i.36; Bv­a.145; Dhp­a.i.88ff. The Bv.viii.22 calls him <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>. He preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sarada</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tāpasa</span> on the occasion when the latter made up his mind to become an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Aggasāvaka</span> himself.

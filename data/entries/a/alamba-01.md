# Ālamba 01

## English source

Ālamba 01. Probably the name of a divine musician, one of a large number who wait on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> and on his wives (Vv., pp.16, 47). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammapāla</span> (Vv-a.96) quotes this view and objects to it, saying that the name is not that of a musician but of a musical instrument. The opinion that the name denotes a celestial musician seems, however, to be the right one. For a discussion see Hardy: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimānavatthu</span> Commentary (PTS Ed.), 372-3.

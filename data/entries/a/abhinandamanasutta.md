# Abhinandamānasutta

## English source

Abhinandamānasutta. One who is enamoured of body, etc., becomes <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>’s bondsman; by not being enamoured one becomes free. SN.iii.75.

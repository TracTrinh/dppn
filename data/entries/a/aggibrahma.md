# Aggibrahmā

## English source

Aggibrahmā. Nephew of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> and husband of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghamittā</span>. He entered the Saṅgha on the same day as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissakumāra</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>’s brother. Mhv.v.169; Vin-a.i.51; Mbv.102.

# Aruṇavatī 01

## English source

Aruṇavatī 01. The city and the country of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavā</span>, and the birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span> (Bv.xxi.15). It was from there that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhibhū</span> went to the *Brahma*-world to preach to *Brahma* and his attendants (SN.i.155f). At that time <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Salaḷapupphiya Thera</span> was a confectioner in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavatī</span> (Ap.i.218). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇapura</span>.

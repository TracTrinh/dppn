# Ākāsasutta 04

## English source

Ākāsasutta 04. Just as divers winds blow in the sky, so when a monk cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path, the *satipaṭṭhāna*s, the *sammappadhānas*, the *iddhipādas*, the *indriyas*, the *bālas* and the *bojjhaṅgas* reach fulfilment. SN.v.49.

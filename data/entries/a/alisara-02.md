# Āḷisāra 02

## English source

Āḷisāra 02. A canal in Ceylon, probably leading from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambaṅgaṅga</span>. King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vasabha</span> gave a share of the water of the canal to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mucelavihāra</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissavaḍḍhamānaka</span>.

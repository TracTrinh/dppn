# Ayyamitta

## English source

Ayyamitta (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Mitta</span>). A monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassakalena</span>. A very poor woman provided him with food. When a tree deity pointed out this to him he put forth effort and became an *Arahant*. MN­a.i.237f; DN­a.iii.790.

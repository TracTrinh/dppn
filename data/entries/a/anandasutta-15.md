# Ānandasutta 15

## English source

Ānandasutta 15. Preached by the Buddha in reply to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>’s question as to how notions of “I” and “mine” and the tendency to vain conceit could be completely destroyed (AN.i.132f). This *Sutta* refers to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇakapañha</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇa</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

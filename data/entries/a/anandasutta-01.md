# Ānandasutta 01

## English source

Ānandasutta 01. Preached by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅgīsa</span>. Once as they were going together for alms to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅgīsa</span> confessed that he was disaffected. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> advised him on how to overcome the disaffection by proper cultivation of the senses. SN.i.188.

## Vietnamese translation

Ānandasutta 01. Do Ānanda thuyết cho Vaṅgīsa. Một lần, khi họ cùng đi khất thực đến Sāvatthī, Vaṅgīsa thú nhận rằng ông đang bất mãn. Ānanda khuyên ông về cách vượt qua sự bất mãn bằng sự tu tập đúng đắn các căn. SN.i.188.

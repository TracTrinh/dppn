# Adhammavādi

## English source

Adhammavādi. A monk who lived soon after the death of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. Having been guilty of various offences, he was charged by his colleague <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammavādi</span>; he persuaded certain *vinayadhara* monks to give an *ex parte* judgment in his favour (Snp-a.i.195-7). The two monks who were chiefly responsible for this judgment were later known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hemavata</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sātāgira</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Adhammavādi. Một vị tỳ-kheo sống không lâu sau khi <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span> nhập diệt. Vì đã phạm nhiều tội khác nhau, ông bị đồng sự là <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammavādi</span> buộc tội; ông thuyết phục một số tỳ-kheo *vinayadhara* đưa ra phán quyết đơn phương có lợi cho mình (Snp-a.i.195-7). Hai vị tỳ-kheo chịu trách nhiệm chính về phán quyết này về sau được biết đến là <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hemavata</span> và <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sātāgira</span>.

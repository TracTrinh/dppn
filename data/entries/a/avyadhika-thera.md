# Avyādhika Thera

## English source

Avyādhika Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he built an *aggisālā* for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> and a hospital and hot baths for the sick. Later, seven *kappas* ago, he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aparājita</span>. Ap.i.215.

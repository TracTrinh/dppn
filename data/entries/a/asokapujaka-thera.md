# Asokapūjaka Thera

## English source

Asokapūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was the king’s parkkeeper in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tivarā</span> and offered an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> flower to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paduma Buddha</span>.

Seventy *kappas* ago he became king sixteen times under the name <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇañjaha</span>. Ap.i.199.

# Abhidhammavibhānavā

## English source

Abhidhammavibhānavā. A *ṭīkā* on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammatthasaṅgaha</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumaṅgala</span>, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> (Nava <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimalabuddhi</span>) (Gv.p. 62; Svd.1227).

It is the most famous of the exegetical works on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammatthasaṅgaha</span>. *Compendium of Philosophy*, Preface ix.

## Vietnamese translation

Abhidhammavibhānavā. Một *ṭīkā* về Abhidhammatthasaṅgaha của Sumaṅgala, đệ tử của Sāriputta (Nava Vimalabuddhi) (Gv.p. 62; Svd.1227).

Đây là tác phẩm nổi tiếng nhất trong các tác phẩm chú giải về Abhidhammatthasaṅgaha. *Compendium of Philosophy*, Preface ix.

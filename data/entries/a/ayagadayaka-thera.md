# Āyāgadāyaka Thera

## English source

Āyāgadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he worshipped with gladsome heart the *Thūpa* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span> and gave carpenters money to build an *āyāga* (a long alms-hall, says Ap­a.). As a result he was born in *Deva* worlds and could bring even the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> into subjection. He could produce rain at will. Ap.i.89-90.

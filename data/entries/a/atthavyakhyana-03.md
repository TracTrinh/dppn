# Atthavyākhyāna 03

## English source

Atthavyākhyāna 03. By <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Vimalabuddhi</span>; written, says the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhavaṁsa</span>, independently, according to his own convictions. Gv.70.

## Vietnamese translation

Atthavyākhyāna 03. Do Cūḷa Vimalabuddhi sáng tác; Gandhavaṁsa nói rằng được viết độc lập, theo những niềm tin của riêng ông. Gv.70.

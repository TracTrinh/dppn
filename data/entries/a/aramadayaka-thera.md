# Ārāmadāyaka Thera

## English source

Ārāmadāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. In a past life he planted a garden with shady trees for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>, and gave the Buddha the fruits and flowers that grew there. Thirty-seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was born seven times as king, by name <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mudusītala</span>. Ap.i.251.

# Anopama

## English source

Anopama. Birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessabhū Buddha</span> and capital of his father, King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppatīta</span>. DN.ii.7; but Bv.xxii.18 gives it as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anoma</span>. The Bv­a. (p. 205) calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anūpama</span>.

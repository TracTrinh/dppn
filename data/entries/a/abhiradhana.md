# Abhirādhana

## English source

Abhirādhana. A friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambhūta Sītavaniya</span>. He went with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambhūta</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhūmija</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jeyyasena</span> to hear <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> preach. Thag­a.i.47.

## Vietnamese translation

Abhirādhana. Một người bạn của Sambhūta Sītavaniya. Ông cùng với Sambhūta, Bhūmija và Jeyyasena đi nghe Gotama Buddha thuyết pháp. Thag­a.i.47.

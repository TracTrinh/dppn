# Aruṇa 05

## English source

Aruṇa 05. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> present at the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span>. They were of diverse hue, of wondrous gifts, mighty powers, comely and with splendid following. DN.ii.260.

# Abhinandena Suttā

## English source

Abhinandena Suttā. 2 suttas. By taking delight in the eye, ear, etc., one takes delight in suffering; by not so doing one is released from suffering. Similarly with regard to sights, sounds, etc. SN.iv.13.

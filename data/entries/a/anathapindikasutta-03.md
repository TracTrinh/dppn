# Anāthapiṇḍikasutta 03

## English source

Anāthapiṇḍikasutta 03. Records a visit paid by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span> to the Buddha, who tells him of the five kinds of guilty dread (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pañca-bhayāni verāni</span>) which are allayed in the Ariyan disciple, and of the four limbs of the Stream-winner (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpattiyangāni</span>). SN.v.387-9.

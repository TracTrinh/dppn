# Andabharīgāmakūṭakasutta

## English source

Andabharīgāmakūṭakasutta. Story of a village cheat, born as a *Peta*.

His secret organs (*anda*) were huge in size.

He was among the *Petas* seen by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> on his way to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>, in company with the Elder <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakkhaṇa</span>.

He had been a corrupt judge in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> and had taken bribes and given unjust judgments. SN.ii.258, SN­a.ii.162.

# Arimaddavijayagāma

## English source

Arimaddavijayagāma. A village and a tank. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Somavatī</span> canal was built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. to connect the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Arimaddavijayagāma</span> with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaddūravaḍḍhamāna</span> tank. Cv.lxxix.56.

## Vietnamese translation

Arimaddavijayagāma. Một ngôi làng và một hồ chứa. Kênh Somavatī được Parakkamabāhu I. xây để nối Arimaddavijayagāma với hồ chứa Kaddūravaḍḍhamāna. Cv.lxxix.56.

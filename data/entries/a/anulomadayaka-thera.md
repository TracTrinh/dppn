# Anulomadāyaka Thera

## English source

Anulomadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. He built a railing round <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anomadassī</span>’s Bodhi-tree and the Buddha expressed delight with it. As a result, in a later birth he became a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabbagghana</span> (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sabbaghana</span>) (Ap.i.173). He is evidently to be identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mettaji Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.194.

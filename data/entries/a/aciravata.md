# Aciravata

## English source

Aciravata. A novice who had a conversation with Prince <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayasena</span> on the life of the bhikkhu. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aciravata</span> repeats this conversation to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> who thereupon preaches the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantabhūmisutta</span> (MN.iii.128ff).

The novice is throughout addressed as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggivessana</span>.

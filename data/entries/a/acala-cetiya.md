# Acala Cetiya

## English source

Acala Cetiya. The name given to the spot at the entrance to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkassa</span>, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> first placed his right foot on his descent from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>. Dhp­a.iii.227.

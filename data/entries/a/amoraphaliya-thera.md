# Amoraphaliya Thera

## English source

Amoraphaliya Thera *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āmodaphaliya Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave an *amora*-fruit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> (Ap.ii.447).

The verses ascribed to him in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Apadāna</span> are, in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span> Commentary, attributed to two monks, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isidatta</span> (Thag­a.i.238) and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama</span> (Ibid., i.256).

# Ānandasutta 09

## English source

Ānandasutta 09. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> is instructed as to how concentration on breathing (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ānāpānasati</span>) leads to the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s and how these, in turn, bring to completion the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span>. These last lead to complete knowledge and release (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vijjāvimutti</span>). The methods of their development are explained in detail. SN.v.328-33.

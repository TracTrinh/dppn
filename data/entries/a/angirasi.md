# Aṅgīrasī

## English source

Aṅgīrasī. A term of affection (Radiant One) used by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcasikha</span> in addressing <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suriyavaccasā</span> (DN.ii.265). The Commentary (DN­a.iii.701) explains that she was so-called because her limbs shone (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">aṅge rasmiyo assā ti Aṅgīrasī</span>.)

# Anoma 04

## English source

Anoma 04. An ascetic of great power, who lived in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Buddha</span>. He gave a jewelled chain to the Buddha and offered him a meal of fruit. In the present age he became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hemaka</span> Thera. Ap.ii.351-4.

# Abhaya 15

## English source

Abhaya 15. Called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ābhidhammika Abhaya</span>. A monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vālikapiṭṭhi</span> *Vihāra* (*q.v.*). For others named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> see under their titles, e.g. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Gatimba</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghabhāṇaka</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meghavaṇṇa</span>, etc.

## Vietnamese translation

Abhaya 15. Được gọi là Ābhidhammika Abhaya. Một vị tăng của Vālikapiṭṭhi *Vihāra* (*q.v.*). Về những người khác tên Abhaya, xem dưới các mục tên riêng của họ, ví dụ Mahā Gatimba, Dīghabhāṇaka, Meghavaṇṇa, v.v.

# Aṇḍhabharīgāmakūṭakasutta

## English source

Aṇḍhabharīgāmakūṭakasutta. Story of a village cheat, born as a Peta. His secret organs (*aṇḍa*) were huge in size. He was among the Petas seen by Mahā <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> on his way to Rājagaha from Gijjhakūṭa, in company with the Elder Lakkhaṇa. He had been a corrupt judge in Rājagaha and had taken bribes and given unjust judgements. SN.ii.258, SN-a.ii.162.

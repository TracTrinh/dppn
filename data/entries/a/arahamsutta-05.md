# Arahaṁsutta 05

## English source

Arahaṁsutta 05. (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhasutta</span>). It is by the cultivation of the four *iddhipadā* that the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tathāgata</span> is called *Arahant* or Fully Enlightened One (SN.v.257).

## Vietnamese translation

Arahaṃsutta 05. (hoặc Buddhasutta). Chính nhờ sự tu tập bốn *iddhipadā* mà Tathāgata được gọi là *Arahant* hay Bậc Toàn Giác (SN.v.257).

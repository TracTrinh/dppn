# Arahantasutta

## English source

Arahantasutta. Of all the forms of becoming, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span> have the best in all the worlds. They attain this by right insight with regard to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">saṅkhāras</span> (SN.iii.82-4).

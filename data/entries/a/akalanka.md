# Akalaṅka

## English source

Akalaṅka. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Coḷa</span> officer who fought against the Sinhalese army of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. during the latter’s invasion of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span> kingdom. Cv.lxxvii.17, 55, 80, 90.

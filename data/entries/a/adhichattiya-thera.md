# Adhichattiya Thera

## English source

Adhichattiya Thera (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chattādhichattiya</span>). An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he placed a parasol on the *Thūpa* containing the relics of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> (Ap.i.170). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāmidatta</span> (Thag­a.i.189).

## Vietnamese translation

Adhichattiya Thera (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Chattādhichattiya</span>). Một *Arahant*. Trong một đời trước, ngài đã đặt một lọng trên *Thūpa* chứa xá-lợi của <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> (Ap.i.170). Rõ ràng ngài đồng nhất với <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāmidatta</span> (Thag­a.i.189).

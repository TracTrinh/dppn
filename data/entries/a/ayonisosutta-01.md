# Ayonisosutta 01

## English source

Ayonisosutta 01. (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vitakkitasutta</span>). A certain monk staying in a forest tract in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> was occupied with evil and wrongful thoughts. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> of the forest, desiring his welfare, drew near and admonished him to give up his muddled ways and fix his thoughts on the Buddha, the Dhamma and the Saṅgha (SN.i.203).

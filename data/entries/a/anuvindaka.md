# Anuvindakā

## English source

Anuvindakā. Name of a people, mentioned with hosts of others, as seeking and finding hospitality in the house of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jatukaṇṇika</span>, when, in a previous birth, he was a banker in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Haṁsavatī</span>. Ap.ii.359.

# Ambariyavihara

## English source

Ambariyavihara. A monastery in Ceylon, the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piṅgala Buddharakkhita</span> Thera. It was near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Antaravaḍḍhamāna</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pabbata</span> (SN­a.ii.113; MN­a.i.165; Dhs­a.103). It was also the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piṇḍapātikatissa</span> Thera. AN­a.i.277.

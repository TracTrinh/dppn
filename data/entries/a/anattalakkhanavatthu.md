# Anattalakkhaṇavatthu

## English source

Anattalakkhaṇavatthu. The story of five hundred monks. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha</span>, knowing their past, advises them to reflect on the “selfishness” of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span> (Dhp­a.iii. 406-7).

These monks had devoted themselves to meditation on this topic for 20,000 years in the dispensation of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>.

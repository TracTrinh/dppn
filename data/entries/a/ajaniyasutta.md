# Ājāniyasutta

## English source

Ājāniyasutta. Three discourses identical, in the main, with the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ājaññasutta</span> (1), but the fourth quality (good proportions) is omitted. The *Suttas* differ from one another in the definition of “speed” in the case of the monk. AN.i.244.

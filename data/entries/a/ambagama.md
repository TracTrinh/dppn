# Ambagāma

## English source

Ambagāma. A village in Ceylon near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> identified with the modern Ambagamuva. A battle was fought there between the forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxx.321) <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu II</span>. built a bridge, thirty-four cubits in length, over the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khajjotanadī</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambagāma</span>. Ibid., lxxxvi.23.

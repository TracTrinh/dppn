# Anugāra

## English source

Anugāra. An eminent wandering ascetic. He is mentioned as living in the *Paribbājakārāma* in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moranivāpa</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veḷuvana</span> near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. He was probably one of the company who was with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakuludāyi</span> when the Buddha came to visit the latter. MN.ii.1.

## Vietnamese translation

Anugāra. Một du sĩ khổ hạnh lỗi lạc. Ông được nhắc đến là sống trong *Paribbājakārāma* tại <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moranivāpa</span> trong <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veḷuvana</span> gần <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. Có lẽ ông là một trong nhóm người đang ở cùng <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakuludāyi</span> khi Buddha đến thăm vị này. MN.ii.1.

# Ananta

Ananta. The serpent king referred to under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anantapokkharaṇī</span>, but not elsewhere mentioned in the old books. He is also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anantabhoga</span>. For details see Hopkins’ <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Epic Mythology</span> (pp. 23-4).

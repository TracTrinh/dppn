# Āpā

## English source

Āpā. A class of deities who were present at the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (DN.ii.259).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> (DN­a.ii.689) says they were born as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> because of their having practised *āpokasiṇa* in previous lives.

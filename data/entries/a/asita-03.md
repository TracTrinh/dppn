# Asita 03

## English source

Asita 03. A garland-maker in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>. While taking a garland to the palace, he saw the Buddha and offered it to him. As a result, twenty-five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he became a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dvebhāra</span>. In the present age he was known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sukatāveḷiya</span> Thera (Ap.i.217).

# Aṅgīrasa 07

## English source

Aṅgīrasa 07. An ascetic, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅgīrasa Gotama</span>, who was killed by the thousand­armed <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajjuna</span>. The ascetic disturbed the animals when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ajjuna</span> was waiting to hunt, and the king, in anger, shot at him with a poisoned arrow (Ja.v.135, 144 and 145; DN­a.i.266). This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅgīrasa</span> is probably to be identified with one of the foregoing.

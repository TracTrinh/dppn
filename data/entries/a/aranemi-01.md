# Araṇemi 01

## English source

Araṇemi 01. A Brahmin teacher of a past age, given in a list of six teachers, who were <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Purohitas</span>. AN.iii.373. The others being <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sunetta</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūgapakkha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddālaka</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hatthipāla</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>. In AN.iv.135f a seventh name is added, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Araka</span>; see also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Divy</span>, 632.

They practised <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ahiṁsā</span> and, abstaining from flesh, got rid of their lusts. As a result, they were born in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span>loka. They had many disciples.

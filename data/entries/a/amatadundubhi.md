# Amatadundubhi

## English source

Amatadundubhi. One of the names under which the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bahudhātukasutta</span> is known (MN.iii.67). Like soldiers in the field of battle, so the disciples in the path, developing insight after the method of this <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span>, raise aloft the standard of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship – hence the name. MN­a.ii.888.

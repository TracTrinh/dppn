# Aruṇapura

## English source

Aruṇapura. A city in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambapālī</span> was born there in a Brahmin family (Ap.ii.613; Thīg­a.i.213). It is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavatī</span>.

## Vietnamese translation

Aruṇapura. Một thành phố vào thời Sikhī Buddha. Ambapālī sinh tại đó trong một gia đình Bà-la-môn (Ap.ii.613; Thīg­a.i.213). Có lẽ nó đồng nhất với Aruṇavatī.

# Ājaññasutta 01

## English source

Ājaññasutta 01. Like a king’s thoroughbred horse possessed of beauty, strength, speed and good proportions, a monk worthy of offerings should have beauty (of life), strength (of character), speed (of insight) and good proportions (of necessaties). AN.ii.250-1.

# Aṅgīrasa 06

## English source

Aṅgīrasa 06. An ascetic. The name occurs in a list of eleven ascetics who, because of their holy lives, passed the *Peta* world and were born in *Brahma*’s heaven (Ja.vi.99; Ja.v.267). For the others see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Akitti</span>.

# Iṭṭhāsutta 01

## English source

Iṭṭhāsutta 01. Preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span> on five things in the world which are very desirable but are difficult to attain – longevity, beauty, happiness, fame, happy rebirth – and on the means of obtaining them (AN.iii.47-9).

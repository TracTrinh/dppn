# Icchānaṅgalaka

## English source

Icchānaṅgalaka. An *upāsaka* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Icchānaṅgala</span>. He was a devoted disciple of the Buddha and had been in the habit of visiting him often (Ud­a.115). Once he visited the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> after a long interval, and on being asked why he had been absent so long, he replied that he had been kept busy by various duties. Thereupon the Buddha sang the joy of the life free from ties. Ud.p.13.

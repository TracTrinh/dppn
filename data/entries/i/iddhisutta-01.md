# Iddhisutta 01

## English source

Iddhisutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span> tells the monks that by cultivating the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s, he enjoys psychic power in many ways, such as multiplying himself. He can reach even to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> world. SN.v.303.

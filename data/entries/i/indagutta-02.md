# Indagutta 02

## English source

Indagutta 02. The Thera appointed by the monks of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span> to superintend the work of building the eighty-four thousand <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāras</span> undertaken by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>. The Thera<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">,</span> by his power, made it possible for the dedication festivals of all the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāras</span> to be performed on the same day. Mhv.v.174; Vin-a.i.49.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indagutta</span> originally lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sīhakumbhavihāra</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devaputta</span>, at the head of a large congregation. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span>, having heard of his fame, invited him to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span>. He went with 60,000 monks and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> received them with great honour. At the sight of the honours paid to him <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indagutta</span> was filled with pride. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> noticing this, admonished him. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indagutta</span> benefiting by the advice, developed insight even as he stood and became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="15" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ras.i.80f.

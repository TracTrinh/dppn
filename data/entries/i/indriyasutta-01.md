# Indriyasutta 01

## English source

Indriyasutta 01. The monk possessed of six qualities – the five *indriyas* (*saddhā*, etc.), and the freedom of mind brought about by the destruction of the *āsavas* – is worthy of offerings, etc. AN.iii.281.

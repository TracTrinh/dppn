# Inda 01

## English source

Inda 01. Given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āṭānāṭiyasutta</span> as the name of the ninety-one sons of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhataraṭṭha</span>, king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhabbas</span>. They are represented as being of great strength and followers of the Buddha (DN.iii.197).

The name is also given as that of the ninety-one sons of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Virūḷha</span>, king of the *Kumbhaṇḍas* (DN.iii.198); of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Virūpakkha</span>, king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span> (p.199); and of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuvera</span>, king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span> (p.202). Further on in the same *Sutta*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Inda</span> is mentioned with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Soma</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Varuṇa</span> and others as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span>, to whom appeal should be made by disciples of the Buddha when needing protection (p.204).

In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (DN.ii.257f), also, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Inda</span> is mentioned as the name of the Sons of the Regent Gods of the Four Quarters.

# Giri 01

## English source

Giri 01. A *Nigaṇṭha* who occupied the *Nigaṇṭhārāma*, later destroyed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṭṭagāmaṇī</span> to make room for the construction of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagiri Vihāra</span>. The *Nigaṇṭha*’s name was included in that of the new *Vihāra*. Mhv.xxxiii.43f, 83; Dpv.xix.14.

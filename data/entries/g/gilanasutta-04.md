# Gilānasutta 04

## English source

Gilānasutta 04. The Buddha visits <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span> lying ill in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pippalīguhā</span>, and talks to him of the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span>. Delighted with the talk, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span> recovers. SN.v.79.

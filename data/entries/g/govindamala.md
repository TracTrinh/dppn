# Govindamala

## English source

Govindamala. A mountain in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. The *Ādipāda* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu</span> founded a town there and used it as a fortification for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māgha</span>’s forces overran the country. Cv.lxxxi.6; also Cv.Trs.ii.135, n.4.

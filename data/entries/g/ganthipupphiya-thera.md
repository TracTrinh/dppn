# Gaṇṭhipupphiya Thera

## English source

Gaṇṭhipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he gave a *ganthi*-flower to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. Forty-one *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Varaṇa</span> (Ap.i.162). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hatthārohaputta</span>. Thag­a.i.170.

# Gāḷhagaṅgā

## English source

Gāḷhagaṅgā. A river in South Ceylon which was once decreed to be the boundary of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. It is generally identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>. Cv.xlviii.132; Cv.Trs.i.122, n.4.

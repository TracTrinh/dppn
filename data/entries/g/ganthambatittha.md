# Ganthambatittha

## English source

Ganthambatittha. A ford in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>. There an *udakakkhepasīmā* was erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya I</span>. (Cv.xciv.17). The name is the Pāli equivalent for the Sinhalese Gātambé.

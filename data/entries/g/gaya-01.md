# Gayā 01

## English source

Gayā 01. A pond in which people bathed, that their sins might be washed away (Ja.v.388f). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (MN­a.i.145) it was a circular pond in which was a bathing ghat (*maṇḍavāpisaṇṭhānaṁ titthaṁ*). But see below, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gayā</span> (2).

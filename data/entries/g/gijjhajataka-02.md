# Gijjhajātaka 02

## English source

Gijjhajātaka 02. (Ja 399). Once the Bodhisatta was a vulture, and supported his blind parents who lived in a cave. One day, being caught in a trap, he was heard by a hunter lamenting for his parents; the hunter set him free.

The story was told in reference to a monk who supported his mother. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Channa</span> was the hunter. Ja.iii.330f.

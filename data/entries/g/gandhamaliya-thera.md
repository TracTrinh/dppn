# Gandhamāliya Thera

## English source

Gandhamāliya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he offered to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> a *gandha-thūpa* covered with *sumana*-flowers. Forty *kappas* ago he became king, sixteen times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devagandha</span>. Ap.i.135.

# Goṇḍigāma

## English source

Goṇḍigāma. A tank constructed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissa II</span>. (Cv.xxxvii.185). The village belonging to it was given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jeṭṭhatissa III</span>. to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavanavihāra</span> (Cv.xliv.97). The tank was restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi V</span>. Cv.xlviii.9.

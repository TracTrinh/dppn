# Giripunnāgiya Thera

## English source

Giripunnāgiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he offered a *giripunnāga*-fruit to the Buddha <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sobhita</span>, then staying at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittakūṭa</span> (Ap.ii.416). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhadinna</span>. Thag­a.i.304.

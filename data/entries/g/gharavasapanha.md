# Gharāvāsapañha

## English source

Gharāvāsapañha. The question asked by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span> and answered by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vidhura</span>, before he was taken away by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇaka</span>. The question dealt with how a householder could so live as to get the best out of his life, both for this world and for the next. Ja.iv.286f.

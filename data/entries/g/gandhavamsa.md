# Gandhavaṁsa

## English source

Gandhavaṁsa. A late Pāḷi work written in Burma.

It relates, in brief, the history of the Pāli Canon and gives accounts of post-canonical Pāḷi books written in Burma and Ceylon.

The colophon states that the work was composed by a forest-dwelling Elder named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandapañña</span>. At the end of each chapter the work is referred to as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Gandhavaṁsa</span>; perhaps what we have now is an abridged edition of a larger work.

The work is published in JPTS, 1886, pp.55-80.

# Girikumbhila

## English source

Girikumbhila. A *vihāra* in Ceylon built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lañjitissa</span> (Mhv.xxxiii.14).

# Gotamā

## English source

Gotamā. Mother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candakumāra</span> and chief queen of the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span> (Ja.vi.134). She is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Māyā</span> (Ja.vi.157). She is sometimes also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotamī</span>, e.g., Ja.vi.148, 151.

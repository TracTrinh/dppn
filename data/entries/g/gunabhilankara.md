# Guṇābhilaṅkāra

## English source

Guṇābhilaṅkāra. A Thera of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tunnagāma</span>. He was one of the originators of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Ekaṁsika</span> controversy (Sās.118). He was, later on, the incumbent of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jeyyabhūmivihāra</span>. (Sās.132, 163).

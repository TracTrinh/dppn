# Gutta

## English source

Gutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Guttaka</span>. A Tamil usurper. He was a horse-dealer and came to Ceylon with another Tamil named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena</span>. They killed King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūratissa</span> and reigned at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> for twenty-two years (177-155 B.C.). They were killed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asela</span>. Mhv.xxi.10f; Dpv.xviii.47f.

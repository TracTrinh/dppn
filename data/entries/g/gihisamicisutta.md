# Gihīsāmīcisutta

## English source

Gihīsāmīcisutta. The Buddha tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anāthapiṇḍika</span> of four things which constitute the householder’s path of duty – he waits upon the Saṅgha with offers of robes, food, lodgings, requisites and medicines for use in sickness. AN.ii.65.

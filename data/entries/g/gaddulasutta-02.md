# Gaddulasutta 02

## English source

Gaddulasutta 02. Just as a dog tied by a leash to a strong pillar cannot escape, so the untaught <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puthujjanas</span> cannot escape from the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span>. Mind is even more diverse than a show piece (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">caraṇacitta</span>).

As a painter fashions all kinds of likenesses, so the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puthujjana</span> creates and recreates the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span>. SN.iii.151f.

# Guttā 01

## English source

Guttā 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Guttā Therī</span>. She belonged to a Brahmin family of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>; when she reached adolescence household life became repugnant to her owing to her *upanissaya* (potential), and, with her parents’ consent, she entered the Saṅgha under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajāpatī Gotamī</span>. For a long time she could not concentrate her mind, but, encouraged by the Buddha, she attained *Arahant*-ship. Thīg.vv.163-8; Thīg­a.157f.

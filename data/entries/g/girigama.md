# Girigāma

## English source

Girigāma. A village in Ceylon. A young novice named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcaggalalena</span>, travelling through the air, saw the daughter of the smith of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Girigāma</span> bathing in a lotus pond with five of her friends, and heard them singing aloud. He was so fascinated by her that he lost his power of flight. Snp­a.i.70; Ap­a.i.128.

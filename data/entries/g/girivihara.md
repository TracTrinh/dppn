# Girivihāra

## English source

Girivihāra. A *vihāra* in Ceylon erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi</span> I; he gave one hundred fields for its support (Cv.xlii.12). It was the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Piṇḍapātiyatissa</span> (AN­a.i.367), and also of a novice, pupil of *Tepiṭaka*, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Summa Thera</span>. (DN­a.ii.514).

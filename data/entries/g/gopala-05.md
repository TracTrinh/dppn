# Gopāla 05

## English source

Gopāla 05. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Devaputta</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>. He was the teacher of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uracchadamālā</span> and gave alms to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span> and the monks; he heard the Dhamma but could get no special attainment. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> met him during a visit to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> and, in answer to the Elder’s questions, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gopāla</span> gave an account of himself. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> thereupon preached to him and he became a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span>. Vv.v.14; Vv-a.270ff.

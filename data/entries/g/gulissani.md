# Gulissāni

## English source

Gulissāni. A monk living in the wilds, who once came on some business to see the monks at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalandakanivāpa</span>. It was on his account that the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gulissānisutta</span> was preached. MN.i.469.

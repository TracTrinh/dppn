# Godhagattatissa Thera

## English source

Godhagattatissa Thera. He it was who brought about a reconciliation between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span> and his brother <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> (Mhv.xxiv.49-53). The Commentary (Mhv­ṭ.469) explains that he had a cutaneous complaint which made his skin scaly like that of a *godha* (iguana).

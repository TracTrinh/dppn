# Gandhodakiya Thera

## English source

Gandhodakiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he saw the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> and sprinkled perfumed water on him. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he became king, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sugandha</span> (Ap.i.157f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ātuma Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.160f.

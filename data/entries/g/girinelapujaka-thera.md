# Girinelapūjaka Thera

## English source

Girinelapūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a hunter, and having seen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, he offered him a *nela*-flower (Ap.ii.457). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gavampati</span>. Thag­a.i.104.

# Ghosasaññaka Thera

## English source

Ghosasaññaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a hunter, and hearing the Buddha preach was delighted by the sound of his voice (Ap.ii.451). His verses are in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span> Commentary attributed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gahavaratīriya</span> (Thag­a.i.91) and to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammika</span>. Ibid., p.398.

# Gaṇadevaputtā

## English source

Gaṇadevaputtā. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> mentions (MN­a.ii.737) the palaces of the *Gaṇadevaputtas* (*Gaṇadevaputtānañ-ca vimānāni*) among those seen by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nemi</span> as he was being conducted by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mātali</span> through the *Deva*-worlds. I cannot trace the name in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nimijātaka</span>. The word may be used as a collective noun.

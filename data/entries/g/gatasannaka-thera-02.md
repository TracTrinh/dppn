# Gatasaññaka Thera 02

## English source

Gatasaññaka Thera 02. An Arahant. He saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> going through the air, though only his robe was visible to him. The sight filled him with joy and he concentrated his mind thereon. Ap.i.253f.

# Girikālī

## English source

Girikālī (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Girikārī</span>). Daughter of the chaplain of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span>. She became a nun, won *Arahant*-ship, and became famous as an eminent teacher of the Vinaya. Dpv.xviii.20.

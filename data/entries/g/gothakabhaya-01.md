# Goṭhakābhaya 01

## English source

Goṭhakābhaya 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṭhābhaya</span>. A king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> dynasty; son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yaṭṭhālayakatissa</span> and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span> (Mhv.xv.170; xxii.11). He is sometimes called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span>, e.g., Mhv.xxiii.10, 56.

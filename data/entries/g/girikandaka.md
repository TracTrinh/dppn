# Girikaṇḍaka

## English source

Girikaṇḍaka. A *vihāra* in Ceylon restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lx.60).

The *vihāra*’s *gocaragāma* was a village called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vattakālaka</span> (*q.v.*), where lived a girl who, by reason of her great rapture, transplanted herself to the *vihāra* through the air. Vsm.143; Dhs-a.116.

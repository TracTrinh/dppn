# Gatasaññaka Thera 01

## English source

Gatasaññaka Thera 01. An Arahant. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span> he joined the Saṅgha at the age of seven, and threw up into the air seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">naṅgaliki</span>-flowers as an offering to the Buddha. Eight <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he became king, three times, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggisikha</span>. Ap.i.127.

# Gotapabbata

## English source

Gotapabbata *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭapabbata</span>. A *vihāra* in South Ceylon built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahallaka Nāga</span> (Mhv.xxxv.124). It may be that it is identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭapabbata</span> *vihāra* and that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahallaka</span> merely restored it.

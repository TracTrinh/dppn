# Gaṅgalatittha

## English source

Gaṅgalatittha. A ford on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kadambanadī</span> near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. It was the starting-point of the boundary line which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span> laid down for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span>. Mhv­ṭ.361; cf. Mbv.136.

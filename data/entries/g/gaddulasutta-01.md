# Gaddulasutta 01

## English source

Gaddulasutta 01. Incalculable is the beginning of *Saṁsāra* and the untaught *puthujjana*, having wrong notions of self, revolve from birth to birth, like a dog tied by a leash to a pillar or stake round which it unceasingly chases. SN.iii.149.

# Gaddulasutta 01

## English source

Gaddulasutta 01. Incalculable is the beginning of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṁsāra</span> and the untaught <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puthujjana</span>, having wrong notions of self, revolve from birth to birth, like a dog tied by a leash to a pillar or stake round which it unceasingly chases. SN.iii.149.

# Gāthā

## English source

Gāthā. A portion of the *Tipiṭaka* classified according to the matter (*aṅga*) of each one.

It includes the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammapada</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Therīgāthā</span>, and those *Suttas*, composed of stanzas only, found in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttanipāta</span> and not included under the term *Sutta*. DN­a.i.23f; Vin.iii.8.

# Giñjakāvasathasuttā 02

## English source

Giñjakāvasathasuttā 02. A group of *Suttas* preached at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Giñjakāvasatha</span> in answer to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>’s questions regarding the destinies of various disciples, men and women, who had died at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nādikā</span>. The first *Sutta* of the group includes the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammādāsa</span> (*q.v.*). SN.v.356ff; cf. DN.191ff.

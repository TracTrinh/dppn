# Gonisavihāra

## English source

Gonisavihāra. A *vihāra* in Ceylon where the young <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhatusena</span> (*q.v.*) was brought up by his uncle, while he remained in disguise as a monk (Cv.xxxviii.21). Geiger thinks it was to the south of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> (Cv.Trs.i.30, n.1).

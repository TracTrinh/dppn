# Govindiya

## English source

Govindiya. Evidently the title given to the High Treasurer. It occurs in the phrase <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Govindiye abhisiñcissāmi</span>, when <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Disampati</span> proposes to appoint <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span> to the rank of Treasurer. DN.ii.232, cp. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jāṇussoṇī</span>.

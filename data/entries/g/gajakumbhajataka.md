# Gajakumbhajātaka

## English source

Gajakumbhajātaka (Ja 345). The Bodhisatta was once a minister of the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. Noticing that the king was slothful, the Bodhisatta took a tortoise as an object lesson, showing him how the indolent came to misery.

The story was related in reference to a monk who was slothful regarding his duties. Ja.iii.139f.

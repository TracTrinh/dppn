# Gosīsanikkhepa Thera

## English source

Gosīsanikkhepa Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he had spread <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">gosīsa</span> (sandalwood paste) outside a monastery.

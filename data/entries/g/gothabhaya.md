# Goṭhābhaya

## English source

Goṭhābhaya. A monk living in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghapāla</span> *pariveṇa*. He was the maternal uncle of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṭhakābhaya</span> II, and tried, without success, to win the king over from the influence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghamitta</span>. Mhv.xxxvi.115f.

# Giridīpā

## English source

Giridīpā. An island near Ceylon. When the Buddha visited Ceylon he drew <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Giridīpā</span> up to Ceylon, told the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span> to go on to it, and then restored it to its former place (Mhv.i.30; Dpv.i.67f). Geiger thinks that the reference is not to an island, but to the highlands (*giri*) in the interior of Ceylon (Mhv.Trs., p.4, n.4).

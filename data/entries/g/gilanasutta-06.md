# Gilānasutta 06

## English source

Gilānasutta 06. The Buddha lies ill in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalandakanivāpa</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veḷuvana</span>; <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Cunda</span> visits him, and they talk of the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span>. The Buddha immediately recovers. SN.v.81.

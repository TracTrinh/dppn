# Gagga 02

## English source

Gagga 02. A Brahmin, father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅgulimāla</span> (MN.ii.102). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gagga</span>, whose wife was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mantāṇī</span>, was chaplain to the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> (MN­a.ii.743). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gagga</span> may have been a *gotta*-name. Thus when, after his ordination, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅgulimāla</span> is introduced to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span>, the latter addresses him as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gagga Mantāṇiputta</span>. MN.ii.102.

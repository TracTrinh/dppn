# Gavapāna

## English source

Gavapāna. The name of a special almsgiving held in honour of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṅgala Buddha</span> by the Bodhisatta, when he was born as the Brahmin <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suruci</span>. The chief item of food was a kind of pudding made of milk, rice and honey. Bv.iv.11; Bv­a.122.

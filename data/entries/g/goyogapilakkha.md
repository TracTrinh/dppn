# Goyogapilakkha

## English source

Goyogapilakkha. A spot near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, visited by the Buddha on his begging rounds (AN.i.280). The Commentary (AN­a.i.460) explains that it was near a fig tree (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">pilakkha</span>) set up at the spot where cows were.

# Gaṅgeyya 01

## English source

Gaṅgeyya 01. Adjective formed from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṅga</span> (Ja.ii.151). The description “<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Gaṅgeyya Nāgaraja</span>” in Ja.iii.362 probably means a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king “dwelling in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṅga</span>” and not “named Gaṅgeyya.”

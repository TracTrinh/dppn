# Gavaghāṭakasutta

## English source

Gavaghāṭakasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> reports to the Buddha that, while descending <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gijjhakūṭa</span>, he saw a vast lump of flesh flying through the air. The Buddha says it was a cattle butcher of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>, born as a *Peta*. SN.ii.256.

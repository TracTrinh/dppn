# Gaṅgāsiripura

## English source

Gaṅgāsiripura. The Pāli name for the town of Gampola in Ceylon (Cv.xxxxvi.18). In it was an ancient *vihāra*, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigamaggāmapāsāda</span> (Cv.lxxxviii.48). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu IV</span>. made it his capital. Cv.xc.107.

# Gaṇaka Moggallāna

## English source

Gaṇaka Moggallāna. A Brahmin teacher of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. He visited the Buddha at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pubbārāma</span>, and the Buddha preached to him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṇaka Moggallānasutta</span>, after which, it is said, he became the Buddha’s follower (MN.iii.1ff). His name and his teaching seem to indicate that he was an accountant.

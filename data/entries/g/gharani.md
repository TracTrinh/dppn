# Gharaṇī

## English source

Gharaṇī. A woman, lay-disciple of the Buddha. She had attained the Third Fruit of the Path and when the Buddha was about to perform the Twin Miracle, she offered to perform a miracle herself – to transform the earth into water and dive about in it like a water-bird. Dhp­a.iii.209.

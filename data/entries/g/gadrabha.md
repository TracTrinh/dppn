# Gadrabha

## English source

Gadrabha. Doorkeeper of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āḷavaka</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span>. He warned the Buddha of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span>’s evil nature and requested him to go away, but, on finding the Buddha determined to stay, he informed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> of the Buddha’s arrival. Snp­a.i.220; AN­a i.211.

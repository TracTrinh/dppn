# Gokulaṅkavihāra

## English source

Gokulaṅkavihāra. A monastery built by cowherds for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhaniya</span> and his wife after their ordination. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says that it existed even in his day.

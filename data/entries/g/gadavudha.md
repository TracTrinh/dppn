# Gadāvudha

Gadāvudha. The club wielded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vessavaṇa</span> when he was yet a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puthujjana</span>.

# Govinda

## English source

Govinda. The steward or treasurer of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Disampati</span>.

He had a son, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>, who succeeded him after his death and came to be known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Govinda</span> (DN.ii.230f; Mvu.iii.204).

“<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Govinda</span>” was evidently a title and not a name. See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Govindiya</span>.

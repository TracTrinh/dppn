# Gandhapūjaka Thera

## English source

Gandhapūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the past he put a handful of perfume on the funeral pyre of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> (Ap.ii.406). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hārita</span>. Thag­a.i.376.

# Geyya

## English source

Geyya. The second section of the *Tipiṭaka* arranged according to matter (*aṅgavasena*). It includes all the *Suttas* composed in verse, especially the whole <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sagāthakavagga</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṁyuttanikāya</span>. DN­a.i.23f.

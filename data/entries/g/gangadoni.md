# Gaṅgādoṇi

## English source

Gaṅgādoṇi. A hill in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇimekhala</span> district in Ceylon. The general <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkha</span> founded a city there during <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māgha</span>’s invasion. The hill was only two *yojanas* away from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māgha</span>’s capital, but provided quite a safe retreat. Cv.lxxxi.7f.

# Haliddavasana

## English source

Haliddavasana. A township of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koḷiyans</span>. The Buddha, when staying there, preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kukkuravatikasutta</span> to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṇṇakoliyaputta</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Seniya Kukkuravatika</span>. MN.i.387; see also SN.v.115f., where another Buddha is mentioned as having preached a sermon there.

# Hemā 01

## English source

Hemā 01. A woman who lived in a village near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. She married a man near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Titthapaṭṭana</span>. Once, wishing to see her husband, she went along the sea and was seized by a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span>. But when he discovered that she knew the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammacakkappavattanasutta</span> by heart he paid her great honour. Ras.ii.135f.

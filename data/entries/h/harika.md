# Hārika

## English source

Hārika. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hārita</span>. A bandit of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. After death he was born as a *Peta* with a headless trunk, and was seen by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>. His mouth and his eyes were on his chest. SN.ii.260.

# Hārita 02

## English source

Hārita 02. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Brahma</span> who was present at the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span>, at the head of one hundred thousand other *Brahmas* (DN.ii.261; DN­a.ii.693; cf. DN­a.i.40). He was one of the chief *Brahmas*. See, e.g., DN­a.ii.693; MN­a.ii.576.

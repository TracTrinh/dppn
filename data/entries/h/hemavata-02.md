# Hemavatā 02

## English source

Hemavatā 02. A heretical sect in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambudīpa</span> (Mhv.v.12; Dpv.v.54). They were so-called because they lived on Mount Himavata (Rockhill, p.184). They held that a Bodhisatta was not an ordinary mortal, that even a *tīrthaka* could have the five *abhiññā*, that the *puggala* is separate from the *khandhas*. Ibid., 190.

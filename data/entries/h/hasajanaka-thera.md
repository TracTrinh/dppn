# Hāsajanaka Thera

## English source

Hāsajanaka Thera. An *Arahant*, Ninety-one *kappas* ago he saw the rag robe of a Buddha hanging from the branch of a tree. Pleased with the sight, he did obeisance to it. Ap.i.259.

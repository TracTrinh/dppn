# Hemavata

## English source

Hemavata. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkha</span> chief, to be invoked by followers of the Buddha in time of need (DN.iii.204). He was present at the preaching of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Samayasutta</span> (DN.ii.256). He was the friend of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sātāgira</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span> for his story).

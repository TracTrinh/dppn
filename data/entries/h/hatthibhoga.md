# Hatthibhoga

## English source

Hatthibhoga. The district given for the maintenance of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Iḷanāga</span>’s state elephant, who saved the king from the prison into which he was cast by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇas</span> (Mhv.xxv.20f., 44). It was in the south of Ceylon and in the village was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṅguravihāra</span>. MN­a.i.530.

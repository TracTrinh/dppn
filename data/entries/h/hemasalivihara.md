# Hemasālīvihāra

## English source

Hemasālīvihāra. A monastery in Ceylon. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda II</span>. there was a Thera in the *vihāra*, expert in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhamma</span>, and the king had the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhamma</span> recited by him, and built a bathing tank for the Thera*’*s use. Cv.xlviii.142.

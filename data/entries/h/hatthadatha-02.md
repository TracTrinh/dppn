# Hatthadāṭha 02

## English source

Hatthadāṭha 02. A native of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uṇhanagara</span>. He was summoned to Ceylon by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Potthakuṭṭha</span> and consecrated king (676 CE.). He built the *Padhānaghara* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷadīghāvika</span> and died after a reign of only six months, killed by the soldiers of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇavamma</span>. Cv.xlvi.45; xlvii.57; see also Cv.Trs.i.102, n.3.

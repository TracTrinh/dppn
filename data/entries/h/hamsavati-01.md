# Haṁsavatī 01

## English source

Haṁsavatī 01. The city of birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> (Ja.i.37; Bv.xi.19, etc.). It existed in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Buddha</span> also, for he preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Brahmadeva</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udayana</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Haṁsavatī</span> (Bv­a.189). The river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhagīrathī</span> flowed by the city. Ap.ii.343.

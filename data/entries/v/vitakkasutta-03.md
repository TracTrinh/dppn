# Vitakkasutta 03

## English source

Vitakkasutta 03. Thoughts of lust, hatred, and delusion are unprofitable. Thoughts of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, its cause, etc., are concerned with profit. SN.v.417.

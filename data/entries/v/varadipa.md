# Varadīpa

## English source

Varadīpa. The name given to Ceylon in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana Buddha</span>. Its capital was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaḍḍhamāna</span> and its king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddha</span>. Vin-a.i.86; Mhv.xv.93; Dpv.i.73; ix. 20; xv. 45, etc.

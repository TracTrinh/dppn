# Vāhadīpa

## English source

Vāhadīpa, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāhadīpavihāra</span>. A monastery in Ceylon, to which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VI</span>. added a *Pāsāda* (Cv.xlviii.65), and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya I</span>. another, called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senaggabodhipabbata</span> *pāsāda*, (Cv.xlix.33) which was later repaired by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dappula II</span>. Cv.xlix.76.

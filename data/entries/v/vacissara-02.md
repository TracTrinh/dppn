# Vācissara 02

## English source

Vācissara 02. A monk of Ceylon, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. Numerous works are assigned to him, among them commentaries on the various books of grammar: the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambandhacintā</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subodhālaṅkāra</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vuttodaya</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vivaraṇa</span>. He also wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sumaṅgalapasādanī</span> on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuddasikkhā</span> and a commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vyākaraṇa</span> (PLC. 204). He seems also to have written the Pāli <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpavaṁsa</span> and several books in Sinhalese. PLC.217; also Gv.62, 71.

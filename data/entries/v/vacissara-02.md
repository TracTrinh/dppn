# Vācissara 02

## English source

Vācissara 02. A monk of Ceylon, pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>. Numerous works are assigned to him, among them commentaries on the various books of grammar: the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambandhacintā</span> *ṭīkā*, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subodhālaṅkāra</span> *ṭīkā*, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vuttodaya</span> *vivaraṇa*. He also wrote the *Sumaṅgalapasādanī* on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuddasikkhā</span> and a commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> *vyākaraṇa* (PLC. 204). He seems also to have written the Pāli <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpavaṁsa</span> and several books in Sinhalese. PLC.217; also Gv.62, 71.

# Vibhaṅgappakaraṇa

## English source

Vibhaṅgappakaraṇa. One of the seven books of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span>; it is generally placed second in the list (e.g., Mil. 12). It deals in a general way with the different categories and formulas given in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasaṅgaṇi</span>, though different methods of treatment are used. The book is divided into eighteen chapters, each of which is called a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vibhaṅga</span>. Each chapter has three portions: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttantabhājaniya</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Abhidhammabhājaniya</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Paññāpucchaka</span> or list of questions. The Commentary to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vibhaṅga</span> is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sammohavinodanī</span>.

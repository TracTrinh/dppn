# Vimaladhammasūriya 02

## English source

Vimaladhammasūriya 02. King of Ceylon (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya II</span>., 1687-1707 CE.). He was the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha II</span>. He held festivals in honour of the Tooth Relic and the Footprint at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumanakūṭa</span> and, sending an embassy to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rakkhaṅga</span>, obtained thirty-three monks, headed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Santāna Thera</span>*,* to reorganize the Saṅgha in Ceylon. He was succeeded by his son, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narindasīha</span>. Cv.xcvii.1ff.

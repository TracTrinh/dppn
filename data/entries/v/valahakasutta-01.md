# Valāhakasutta 01

## English source

Valāhakasutta 01. There are four kinds of rain clouds: those which produce thunder but no rain; those which produce rain but no thunder; those which produce neither; those which produce both. There are four similar kinds of persons: those that speak but do not act; those that act but do not speak; those that do neither; and those that do both. AN.ii.102.

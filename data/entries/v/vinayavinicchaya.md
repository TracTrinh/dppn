# Vinayavinicchaya

## English source

Vinayavinicchaya. A Vinaya treatise by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span>, written at the request of his pupil <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhasīha</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttaravinicchaya</span> is a supplement to this work. Two *ṭīkās* on it are found; one by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revata</span> and the other by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vācissara</span>. PLC. 108f.

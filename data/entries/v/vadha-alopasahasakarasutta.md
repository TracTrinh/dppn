# Vadha-ālopasāhasakārasutta

## English source

Vadha-ālopasāhasakārasutta. Few are they who abstain from torture, highway robbery and violent deeds; it is because they do not see the Four Noble Truths. SN.v.473.

## Vietnamese translation

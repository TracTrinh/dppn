# Vasālanagara

## English source

Vasālanagara. A village in Ceylon, probably near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittalapabbata</span>. It was the residence of two brothers, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Nāga</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga</span>, who later became monks. SN­a.ii.125.

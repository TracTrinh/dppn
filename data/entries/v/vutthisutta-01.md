# Vuṭṭhisutta 01

## English source

Vuṭṭhisutta 01. A conversation between two <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> and the Buddha’s comments thereon. Of things making for progress, knowledge is the best; ignorance is the greatest worsener; among beings that walk, the Saṅgha is the best; among declarants, the Buddha is the best. SN.i.42.

# Vādīsutta

## English source

Vādīsutta. There are four kinds of expounders (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vādī</span>): those that know the meaning of a passage but not the letter, those that know the letter but not the meaning, those that know neither, those that know both. AN.ii.138.

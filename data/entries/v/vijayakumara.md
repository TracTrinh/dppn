# Vijayakumāra

## English source

Vijayakumāra. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirināga II</span>., and king of Ceylon for one year (302-3 CE.). He was killed in his palace by the three <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇas</span>: <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghatissa</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghabodhi</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Goṭhābhaya</span>. Mhv.xxxvi.57f; Dpv.xxii.51.

# Vīrabāhu 01

## English source

Vīrabāhu 01. Younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. He was made *uparājā* and put in charge of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span>. He married <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhaddā</span>. He helped the king in the conquest of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> when the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Velakkāra</span> troops revolted. Cv.lix. 11, 43; lx. 40.

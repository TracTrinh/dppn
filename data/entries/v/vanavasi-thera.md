# Vanavāsī Thera

## English source

Vanavāsī Thera. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theragāthā</span> Commentary (i.440) mentions a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vanavāsī Thera</span> as the teacher of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tekicchakāni</span>. This is probably not a proper name but only a descriptive epithet.

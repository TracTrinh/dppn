# Vijayuttara

## English source

Vijayuttara. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>’s conch trumpet, which he blew at the moment of the Buddha’s Awakening. It was one hundred and twenty hands in length (Ja.i.72; Bv­a. 239). He also blew it on the occasion of the enshrinement of relics in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span> (Mhv.xxx.74) and on the day of the coronation of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candakumāra</span>. Ja.vi.157.

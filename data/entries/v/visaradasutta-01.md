# Visāradasutta 01

## English source

Visāradasutta 01. A man who takes life takes what is not given, is given over to lust, lies, and takes spirituous liquors, such a man lives as a layman without confidence. Following the opposite course, he lives with confidence. AN.iii.203; cf. No. 3.

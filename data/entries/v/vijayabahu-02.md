# Vijayabāhu 02

## English source

Vijayabāhu 02. Sister’s son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. and king of Ceylon (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu II</span>., 1186-87 CE.). He succeeded his uncle. Among his acts was the grant of an amnesty to all those imprisoned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>., and the dispatch of an embassy to the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Arimaddana</span> with a letter in Pāli, composed by himself. He was a good king, but was slain at the end of one year’s reign by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> (afterwards <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda VI</span>.). His viceroy was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittinissaṅka</span>. Cv.lxxx.1-18.

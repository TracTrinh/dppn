# Valliggāma

## English source

Valliggāma. A village in South Ceylon. In the reign of Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇavatī</span> (1202-8), the regent, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āyasmanta</span>, sent the *Ādhikārī* *Deva* to the village, where a *vihāra* was erected (Cv.lxxx.38) by him.

Later, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu IV</span>. built the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu</span> *pāsāda* attached to the *vihāra* and gave for its maintenance the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāligiri</span> (Cv.xc.96).

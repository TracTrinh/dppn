# Vihārabīja

## English source

Vihārabīja. A village in Ceylon from which five hundred young men entered the Saṅgha on the occasion of the enshrinement of the Buddha’s collar-bone in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thūpārāma</span>. Mhv.xvii.59.

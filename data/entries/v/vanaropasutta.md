# Vanaropasutta

## English source

Vanaropasutta (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vacanasutta</span>). The Buddha says, in answer to a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span>’s question, that those who plant groves and fruitful trees and build causeways, dams and wells, and give shelter to the homeless, increase in merit every day. SN.i.33.

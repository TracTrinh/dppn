# Vassasutta 02

## English source

Vassasutta 02. Just as rain, falling on mountain tops, flows into gullies, pools, great lakes and rivers and from there into the ocean, so do the virtues of the Ariyan disciple flow onwards and lead to the destruction of the *āsavas*. SN.v.396.

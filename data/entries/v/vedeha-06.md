# Vedeha 06

## English source

Vedeha 06. A Thera of Ceylon, who belonged to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Araññavāsīnikāya</span>. He wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rasavāhinī</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantakūṭavaṇṇanā</span>, and also, probably, the Sinhalese grammar known as the Sidatsaṅgarā. He lived in the thirteenth century and was a pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda Vanaratna</span>. PLC. 222f.; Svd.1263.

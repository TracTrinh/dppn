# Vācissara 03

## English source

Vācissara 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vācissara Thera</span>. Probably identical with (2) above. He was at the head of the Saṅgha in the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu III</span>., and had hidden the Alms bowl and Tooth relic of the Buddha in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kotthumala</span>, in order to preserve them. After that, he went to South India for protection. Later, he was sent for by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu</span>, whom he helped in the reformation of the Saṅgha. Cv.lxxxi.18ff.

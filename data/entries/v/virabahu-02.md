# Vīrabāhu 02

## English source

Vīrabāhu 02. The surname assumed by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇābharaṇa I</span>. when he became governor of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span>, with his headquarters at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Puṅkhagāma</span>. He also bore the title of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Mahādipāda</span>. This <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇābharaṇa</span> was the father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxi.26; lxii.4, 62.

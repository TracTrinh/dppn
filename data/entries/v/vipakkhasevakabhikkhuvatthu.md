# Vipakkhasevakabhikkhuvatthu

## English source

Vipakkhasevakabhikkhuvatthu. The story of a monk who joined <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>’s followers because of the rich offerings which he received, afterwards returning to his companions. He was reported to the Buddha, who related the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahilāmukhajātaka</span> (Ja 26) and identified the elephant of the story with the monk. Dhp­a.iv.95-7.

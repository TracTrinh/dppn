# Vaṇṇanāsutta

## English source

Vaṇṇanāsutta. A nun who, without test or scrutiny, praises the unworthy and blames the worthy, shows faith in things unbelievable and disbelief in things believable and rejects the gift of faith – such a one goes to purgatory. AN.iii.139.

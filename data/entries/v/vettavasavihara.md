# Vettavāsavihāra

## English source

Vettavāsavihāra. A monastery in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pācīnakaṇḍakarāji</span> in Ceylon. It was given by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi II</span>. to the minister of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāliṅga</span> king who came over to Ceylon during his reign and entered the Saṅgha under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>; the minister gave it back to the Saṅgha. Cv.xlii.48; see also Cv.Trs.i.71, n.2.

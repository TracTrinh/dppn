# Vādino Sutta

## English source

Vādino Sutta. No dogmatists, no matter where from, can make a monk who understands suffering, its cause, etc., quake or waver. He is like a stone column sixteen cubits long, half of its length buried under the earth. No wind can make it tremble. SN.v.445.

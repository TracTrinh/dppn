# Visamaloma

## English source

Visamaloma. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammāsoka</span> and his chief queen, so-called because of his coarse hair. Once he crossed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candabhāgā</span> in flood, killing with his hands 120 crocodiles that attacked him. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Asoka</span> was frightened and had him put in chains, but later made him viceroy. He had given alms to a monk in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. Ras.i.32f.

# Varañāṇamuni

## English source

Varañāṇamuni. A Thera. One of the two Theras appointed to lead the delegation of monks who left Ayojjhā (in Siam) to go to Ceylon, for the furtherance of the Saṅgha in Ceylon, in the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>. He was expert in the Dhamma and the Vinaya and taught them to the monks of Ceylon. Cv.c.138, 174.

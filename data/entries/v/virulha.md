# Virūḷha

## English source

Virūḷha, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Virūḷhaka</span>. One of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cātummahārājikā</span>. He is the king of the south, and, in the assembly of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>, sits facing north (DN.ii.207, 221). He is lord of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Kumbhaṇḍas</span>. DN.iii.198; Divy.126, 148.

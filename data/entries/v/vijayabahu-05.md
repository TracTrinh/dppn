# Vijayabāhu 05

## English source

Vijayabāhu 05. King of Ceylon (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu V</span>.). He succeeded <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vannibhuvanekabāhu</span>, and was himself succeeded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu IV</span>. Cv.xc.105; he was among the successors of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu IV</span>., and reigned somewhere between 1302 and 1346 CE.

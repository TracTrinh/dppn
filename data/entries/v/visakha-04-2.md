# Visākhā 04

## English source

Visākhā 04. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Visākhā Therī</span>. She belonged to the harem of the Bodhisatta and left the world with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Pajāpatī Gotamī</span>. She received a topic of meditation from the Buddha and in due course won *Arahant*-ship. A verse uttered by her, admonishing her companions, is included in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Therīgāthā</span>. Thīg.vs.13; Thīg­a.20.

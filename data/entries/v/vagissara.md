# Vāgissara

## English source

Vāgissara. One of the Sinhalese envoys sent by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāmañña</span>. His companion was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammakitti</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāmañña</span> king put them into a leaky vessel and sent them home. Cv.lxxvi.32.

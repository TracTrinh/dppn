# Vidhūra 01

## English source

Vidhūra 01. One of the two chief disciples of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span> (DN.ii.4; SN.ii.191; Bv.xxiii.20. Ja.i.42).

He received his name because he was a peerless preacher of the Dhamma (MN.i.333; quoted at Paṭis-a.p. 496).

# Vālikapiṭṭhivihāra

## English source

Vālikapiṭṭhivihāra. A monastery, probably in Ceylon, the residence of Ābhidhammika <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya Thera</span>. At the commencement of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vassa</span> he, with a large number of his colleagues, recited the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Suññatāsutta</span>, and, as a result, they lived apart from each other during the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vassa</span>, attaining <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship, before the end of the season. MN­a.ii.907.

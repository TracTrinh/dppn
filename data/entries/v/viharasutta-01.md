# Vihārasutta 01

## English source

Vihārasutta 01. On the nine kinds of abiding (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihārā</span>) in the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span> and in the spheres of infinite space, infinite consciousness, nothingness, neither-perception-nor-non-perception, and in the sphere where feeling and perception have ended.

# Vihārasutta 01

## English source

Vihārasutta 01. On the nine kinds of abiding (*vihārā*) in the four *jhānas* and in the spheres of infinite space, infinite consciousness, nothingness, neither-perception-nor-non-perception, and in the sphere where feeling and perception have ended. AN.iv.410.

# Vīmaṁsakasutta

## English source

Vīmaṁsakasutta. The Buddha tells the monks at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> that the enquiring monk, who searches the heart of others, should study the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tathāgata</span>. He then proceeds to give details as to how the study should be undertaken. MN.i.317ff.

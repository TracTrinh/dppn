# Vatthugāthā

## English source

Vatthugāthā. The introductory stanzas (976-1031) of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇavagga</span> which give the story of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span>, the circumstances which led to his sending his students to the Buddha and their journey to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> (Snp., pp. 190-7).

The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Niddesa</span>, which comments on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pārāyaṇavagga</span> (p.6ff), does not comment on these stanzas.

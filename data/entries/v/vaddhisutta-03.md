# Vaḍḍhīsutta 03

## English source

Vaḍḍhīsutta 03. The Ariyan disciple grows in ten ways: in lands and fields, wealth and possessions, wife and family, servitors and retinue, beasts of burden, faith, virtue, learning, generosity and wisdom. AN.v.137.

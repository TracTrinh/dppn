# Viggāhitasutta

## English source

Viggāhitasutta. The Buddha exhorts the monks not to engage in wordy warfare, such talk being neither profitable nor conducive to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Nibbāna</span>. They should converse about <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>, its cause, etc. SN.v.419.

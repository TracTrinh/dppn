# Viggāhitasutta

## English source

Viggāhitasutta. The Buddha exhorts the monks not to engage in wordy warfare, such talk being neither profitable nor conducive to *Nibbāna*. They should converse about *dukkha*, its cause, etc. SN.v.419.

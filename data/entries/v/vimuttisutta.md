# Vimuttisutta

## English source

Vimuttisutta. On the five spheres of release experienced when one teaches the Dhamma to another, when yet another listens, when one learns it oneself, when one ponders and reflects on it, and when one has rightly penetrated into it. AN.iii.21f.

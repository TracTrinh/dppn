# Vattasutta

## English source

Vattasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> addresses the monks at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> on the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅga</span> and of his ability to abide in any of these according to his desire; just as a nobleman possessed of many robes can don whichever he desires.

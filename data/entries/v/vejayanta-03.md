# Vejayanta 03

## English source

Vejayanta 03. The chief of the eighty-four thousand chariots owned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassana</span> (SN.iii.145; DN.ii.187). The navel of its wheels was made of sapphire, the spokes of seven kinds of precious things, the rim of coral, the axle of silver, etc. SN­a.ii.237.

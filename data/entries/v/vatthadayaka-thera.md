# Vatthadāyaka Thera

## English source

Vatthadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span>, he was a *Garuḷa*, and, seeing the Buddha on his way to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhamādana</span>, he offered him a garment. Thirty-six *kappas* ago he was king seven times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇaka</span>. Ap.i.116.

# Vivekasutta 02

## English source

Vivekasutta 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> tells <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, in answer to his questions that the clearness of his senses and the agreeable colour of his face are due to the fact that he had passed the siesta in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Andhavana</span>, aloof from passions and from evil things.

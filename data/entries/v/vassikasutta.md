# Vassikasutta

## English source

Vassikasutta. Just as of all scented flowers the jasmine (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vassika</span>) is the chief, so of all profitable conditions earnestness is the chief. SN.v.44.

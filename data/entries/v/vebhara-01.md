# Vebhāra 01

## English source

Vebhāra 01. One of the five hills surrounding <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> (e.g., MN.iii.68). At its foot was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sattapaṇṇiguhā</span>, where the first Convocation was held (Vin.ii.76; W. 159; Vin-a.i.10, etc.). The river <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tapodā</span> (*q.v.*) rose in a lake at the foot of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vebhāra</span>. SN­a.i.30f.

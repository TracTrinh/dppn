# Vikkamarājasīha

## English source

Vikkamarājasīha. The last king of Ceylon. He was the son of the sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājādhirājasīha</span>, whom he succeeded. He ruled for eighteen years (1798-1815 CE.), but the people rebelled against him, and he was obliged to abdicate in favour of the *Ingirisī* (English). Cv.ci.19ff.

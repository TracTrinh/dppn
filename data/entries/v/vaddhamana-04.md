# Vaḍḍhamāna 04

## English source

Vaḍḍhamāna 04. The name of a Bodhi-tree in Ceylon. Attached to it was a temple, restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IV</span>. (Cv.xlviii.5) and again by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya V</span>. (Cv.xlix.15).

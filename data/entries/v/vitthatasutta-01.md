# Vitthatasutta 01

## English source

Vitthatasutta 01. A detailed account of the four kinds of progress: painful progress with sluggish intuition, the painful mode with swift intuition, the pleasant mode with sluggish intuition, the pleasant mode with swift intuition. AN.ii.149f.

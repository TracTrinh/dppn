# Valāhaka 01

## English source

Valāhaka 01. A family of horses from which the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Assaratana</span> of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> is supplied (Khp-a. 172; MN.iii.174). He is best among animals, because he takes his rider away from all danger (MN­a.ii.616). Noble chargers come from the Valāhaka stock. Dhp­a.iii.248.

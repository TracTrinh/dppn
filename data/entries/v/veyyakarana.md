# Veyyākaraṇa

## English source

Veyyākaraṇa. A portion of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Tipiṭaka</span> in its arrangement according to matter (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">aṅga</span>). According to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> it includes the whole of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> not composed in verse. DN­a.i.24; Pug.iv.9, 28.

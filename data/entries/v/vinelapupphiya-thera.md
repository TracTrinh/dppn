# Vinelapupphiya Thera

## English source

Vinelapupphiya Thera. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Minelapupphiya</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he met <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span> and offered him a *vinela*-flower. Twenty-nine *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumeghaghana</span> (Ap.i.203f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāmaṇeyya Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.121.

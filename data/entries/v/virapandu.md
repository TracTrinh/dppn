# Vīrapaṇḍu

## English source

Vīrapaṇḍu. The youngest son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkama</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span>. When his father was murdered he fled, but <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span> took him under his protection and restored his kingdom. He was crowned at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇas</span> officiating. Cv.lxxvi.193f.; lxxvii.5, 25, 103; see also Cv.Trs.ii.100, n.1.

# Vaṇijjāsutta 01

## English source

Vaṇijjāsutta 01. The Buddha explains to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>, in answer to his question, why it is that some people succeed in their trade and others do not, while in the case of yet others they prosper even beyond their hopes. AN.ii.81f.

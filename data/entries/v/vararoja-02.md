# Vararoja 02

## English source

Vararoja 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> says (DN­a.ii.656) that the heretics once paid <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vararoja</span> one thousand to speak ill of the Buddha’s person. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vararoja</span> went to see the Buddha and was struck by the perfection of every feature; he spoke the Buddha’s praises in a verse of over one thousand lines.

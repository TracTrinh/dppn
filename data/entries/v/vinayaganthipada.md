# Vinayagaṇṭhipada

## English source

Vinayagaṇṭhipada. A Vinaya treatise ascribed to a Thera named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> of Ceylon and written in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. This authority was quoted by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Ekaṁsikas</span> in support of their views. Bode, 75 f; see also 75, n.2, where the author is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Joti</span>; also PLC. 189f.

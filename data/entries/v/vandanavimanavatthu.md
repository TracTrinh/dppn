# Vandanavimānavatthu

## English source

Vandanavimānavatthu. The story of a woman who, seeing monks on their way to visit the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, worshipped them with great devotion, watching them pass out of sight. She was later reborn in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> saw her and heard her story. Vv.iv.11; Vv-a.205f.

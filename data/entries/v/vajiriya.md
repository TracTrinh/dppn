# Vājirīya

## English source

Vājirīya. A heretical sect of Buddhists, one of the seventeen schools which branched off one hundred years after the Buddha’s death. Mhv.v.13; Mhv. p.97; Dpv.v.54 calls them *Apararājagirikā*.

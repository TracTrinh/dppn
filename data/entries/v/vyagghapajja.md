# Vyagghapajja

## English source

Vyagghapajja. The name given to the city of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koḷiyans</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>), because it was built on a tiger’s track.

The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koḷiyans</span> themselves thus came to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vyagghapajjā</span>. AN­a.ii.558, 778; Snp­a.i.356; DN­a.i.262; cf. Mvu.i.355.

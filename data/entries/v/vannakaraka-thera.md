# Vaṇṇakāraka Thera

## English source

Vaṇṇakāraka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Thirty-one <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was a painter (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vaṇṇakāra</span>) in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavatī</span> and painted the drapery of the Buddha’s <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span>. Twenty-three <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candūpama</span>.

# Vaṇṇakāraka Thera

## English source

Vaṇṇakāraka Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he was a painter (*vaṇṇakāra*) in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavatī</span> and painted the drapery of the Buddha’s *cetiya*. Twenty-three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candūpama</span>. Ap.i.220.

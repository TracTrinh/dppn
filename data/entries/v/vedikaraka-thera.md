# Vedikāraka Thera

## English source

Vedikāraka Thera. An *Arahant*. He built a railing round the *Thūpa* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyadassī Buddha</span>. Sixteen *kappas* ago he was king thirty-two times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇippabhāsa</span> (Ap.i.171). He is evidently identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijaya</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.192.

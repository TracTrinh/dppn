# Visuddhācariya Thera

## English source

Visuddhācariya Thera. A monk sent by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammika</span>, ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayojjhā</span>, to Ceylon, at the head of a group of monks, and at the request of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>, to reinstate the Saṅgha in that Island. He remained in Ceylon, admitting many monks into the Saṅgha, to whom he taught the doctrine. Cv.c.131, 171f.

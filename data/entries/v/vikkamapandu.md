# Vikkamapaṇḍu

## English source

Vikkamapaṇḍu. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Lānakitti</span>. He was staying in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duḷu</span> country when he heard of the events in Ceylon, and, going to the province of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, he carried on the government at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷatittha</span> for one year (1046 CE.), till he was slain by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jagatīpāla</span>. Cv.lvi.11ff.

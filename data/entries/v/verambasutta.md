# Verambāsutta

## English source

Verambāsutta. A monk whose heart is possessed by gains and flattery, and whose senses are unguarded in the presence of women – he is like a bird caught in a hurricane (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">verambavāta</span>). SN.ii.231.

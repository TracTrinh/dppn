# Verambāsutta

## English source

Verambāsutta. A monk whose heart is possessed by gains and flattery, and whose senses are unguarded in the presence of women – he is like a bird caught in a hurricane (*verambavāta*). SN.ii.231.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

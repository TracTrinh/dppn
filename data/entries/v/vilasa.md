# Vilasa

## English source

Vilasa. A very rich man of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kandalisālagāma</span>. His wealth was fabulous, and the king, wishing to test its extent, asked him to supply various luxuries. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muggagāmavihāra</span> was built on the spot where his carts, bringing green peas to the king, stopped outside the city. Ras.ii.130f.

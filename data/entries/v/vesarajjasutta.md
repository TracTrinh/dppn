# Vesārajjasutta

## English source

Vesārajjasutta. The four confidences of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tathāgata</span>: he must be perfectly enlightened, have destroyed the *āsavas*, the hindrances declared by him must really be hindrances, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Dhamma</span> preached by him must never fail in its aim. AN.ii.8.

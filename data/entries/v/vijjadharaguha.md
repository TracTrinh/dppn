# Vijjādharaguhā

## English source

Vijjādharaguhā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijjādharalena</span>. A cave in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>, forming part of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttarārāma</span> (Cv.lxxviii.73). The boundary of the *Baddhasīmāpāsāda* grounds passed fifty staves (375 ft.) to the north of this cave. Ibid., vs. 66. See Cv.Trs.ii.111, n.2.

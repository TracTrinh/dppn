# Vesālīsutta 03

## English source

Vesālīsutta 03. The Buddha once addressed the monks on the great benefits of meditating on *asubha*, and then retired into solitude in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vana</span> for a fortnight. The monks, filled with the idea of *asubha*, felt loathing for their bodies and many committed suicide. The Buddha hearing of this, summoned the monks to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭāgārasālā</span> and taught them the great merits of concentration on breathing. SN.v.320f.

# Veḷuvana 02

## English source

Veḷuvana 02. A bamboo grove in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kajaṅgalā</span>, where the Buddha once stayed. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upāsakas</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kajaṅgalā</span>, having questioned the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kajaṅgalā</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Bhikkhunī</span>, went to the Buddha there and asked him to verify her answers. AN.v.54f.

# Velamikā

## English source

Velamikā. Chief of the eighty-four thousand women who waited on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassana</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sammata</span>. She was also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khattiyānī</span>. SN.iii.146; but at DN.ii.187 the chief queen is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhaddā</span>.

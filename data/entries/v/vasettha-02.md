# Vāseṭṭha 02

## English source

Vāseṭṭha 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāsiṭṭha</span>. The name of an old <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṛṣi</span> held in high esteem for his knowledge. He was one of the originators of the Vedic runes. Vin.i.245; DN.i.104; MN.ii.164, 200; Mil.162, etc.; cf. Vasiṣṭha in Vedic Index.

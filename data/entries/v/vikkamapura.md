# Vikkamapura

## English source

Vikkamapura. A town in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span>, once used as headquarters by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>., before his capture of the throne. It was near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kyānagāma</span>. Cv.lxxii.147, 263. Geiger suggests (Cv.Trs.i.333, n.3) that it was the town attached to the fortress of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhagiri</span>.

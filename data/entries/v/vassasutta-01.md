# Vassasutta 01

## English source

Vassasutta 01. A monk asks the Buddha why it sometimes rains. It rains when the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vassavalākaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> wish to revel their bodies answers the Buddha. SN.iii.257.

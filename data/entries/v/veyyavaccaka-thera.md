# Veyyāvaccaka Thera

## English source

Veyyāvaccaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a servant to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, and, having nothing to give, worshipped his feet. Eight *kappas* ago he was a king called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sucintita</span> (Ap.i.138). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sañjaya</span> Thera. Thag­a.i.120.

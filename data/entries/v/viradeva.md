# Vīradeva

## English source

Vīradeva. Ruler of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Palandīpa</span>, who invaded Ceylon in the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayabāhu I</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vikkamabāhu</span> marched against him, but was defeated at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mannāra</span> and had to retreat to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhasāra</span>. Thither he was pursued by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vīradeva</span>, who, however, was slain in a battle at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Antaraviṭṭhika</span>. Cv.lxi.36ff.

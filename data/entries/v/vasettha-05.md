# Vāseṭṭha 05

## English source

Vāseṭṭha 05. A lay disciple, evidently distinct from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāseṭṭha</span> (3). He visited the Buddha at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭāgārasālā</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesālī</span> and the Buddha preached to him (AN.iv.258). See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāseṭṭhasutta</span> (2). He is mentioned among the Buddha’s eminent lay disciples. AN.iii.451.

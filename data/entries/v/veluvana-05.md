# Veḷuvana 05

## English source

Veḷuvana 05. A monastery erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. in the suburb of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijita</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>. It consisted of three image houses, each three storeys high, a *Thūpa*, a cloister, a two-storeyed *pāsāda*, four gateways, four long *pāsādas*, eight small ones, one refectory, one sermon hall, seven fire hoses and twelve privies. Cv.lxxiii.152, lxxviii.87f.; see also Cv.Trs.ii.113, n.1.

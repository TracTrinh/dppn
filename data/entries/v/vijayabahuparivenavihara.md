# Vijayabāhupariveṇavihāra

## English source

Vijayabāhupariveṇavihāra. A monastery built by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu III</span>. in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vattalagāma</span> (Cv.lxxxi.58). A monk, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāyasatti</span>, was its chief incumbent in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu IV</span>., Cv.xc.91.

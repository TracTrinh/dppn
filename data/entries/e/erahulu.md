# Erāhuḷu

## English source

Erāhuḷu. A locality in Ceylon, near which an engagement took place between the forces of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>., and his foes (Cv.lxxiv.91). It is identified with the present district Eravur, north-west of Batticaloa. Cv.Trs.ii.30, n.3.

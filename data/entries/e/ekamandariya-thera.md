# Ekamandāriya Thera

## English source

Ekamandāriya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he was a youth in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> and, seeing the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> in *samādhi*, brought a *mandārava*-flower and held it above the Buddha’s head for seven days. Ap.i.286.

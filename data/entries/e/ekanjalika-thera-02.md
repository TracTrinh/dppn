# Ekañjalika Thera 02

## English source

Ekañjalika Thera 02. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he saw the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddha</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Romasa</span>, on the banks of a river and, being pleased with his radiant appearance, paid homage to him with clasped hands. Ap.i.281.

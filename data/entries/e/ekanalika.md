# Ekanāḷika

## English source

Ekanāḷika. A famine that broke out in Ceylon during the time of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuñcanāga</span>. The people were reduced to very little food, but the king maintained, without interruption, a great alms-giving (*mahāpeḷā*) appointed for five hundred monks. Mhv.xxxvi.20.

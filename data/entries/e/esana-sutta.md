# Esanā Sutta

## English source

Esanā Sutta. A group of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Suttas</span> on the three kinds of longing (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">esanā</span>) the longing for sensual delights, for becoming, for the holy life. These are spoken of in relation to the Noble Eightfold Path (SN.v.54f). The same is repeated for (1) the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span> (SN.v.136), (2) the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s (SN.v.191), (3) the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">indriyas</span> (SN.v.240, 242), (4) the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">balas</span> (SN.v.250, 252), (5) the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhipādas</span> (SN.v.291), and (6) the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span> (SN.v.309).

# Esanā Sutta

## English source

Esanā Sutta. A group of *Suttas* on the three kinds of longing (*esanā*) the longing for sensual delights, for becoming, for the holy life. These are spoken of in relation to the Noble Eightfold Path (SN.v.54f). The same is repeated for (1) the seven *bojjhaṅgas* (SN.v.136), (2) the four *satipaṭṭhāna*s (SN.v.191), (3) the *indriyas* (SN.v.240, 242), (4) the five *balas* (SN.v.250, 252), (5) the *iddhipādas* (SN.v.291), and (6) the *jhānas* (SN.v.309).

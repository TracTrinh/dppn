# Eṇiphassā

## English source

Eṇiphassā. A name, either of some kind of musical instrument or, more probably, of a class of celestial musicians who waited on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> and his queens. Vv.xviii.11; i.26; Vv-a.94, 211; for explanation see 372.

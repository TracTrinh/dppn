# Eṇijaṅghasutta

## English source

Eṇijaṅghasutta. One of the *Suttas* in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devatāsaṁyutta</span>. A *Deva* asks the Buddha how it is possible to wander indifferent to the calls of sense, limbed like the antelope (*eṇi*) or the lion. The Buddha answers, by getting rid of the desires of sense. SN.i.16.

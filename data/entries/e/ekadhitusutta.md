# Ekadhītusutta

## English source

Ekadhītusutta. A devout lay-sister should admonish her only daughter to be like <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khujjuttarā</span> or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Veḷukaṇṭakiyā Nandamātā</span>, or, if she goes to homelessness, like <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khemā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uppalavaṇṇā</span>. SN.ii.236.

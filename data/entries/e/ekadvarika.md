# Ekadvārika

## English source

Ekadvārika also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekadvāra</span>. A *vihāra* built by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subha</span> to the east of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, at the foot of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekadvārika</span> *pabbata*. Mhv.xxxv.58; Mhv­ṭ.648. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekadvārika</span> *pabbata* was also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaṅganta</span> *pabbata*. Mhv­ṭ. 424.

## Vietnamese translation

Ekadvārika cũng là Ekadvāra. Một *vihāra* do Vua Subha xây ở phía đông Anurādhapura, dưới chân *pabbata* Ekadvārika. Mhv.xxxv.58; Mhv­ṭ.648. *Pabbata* Ekadvārika cũng được gọi là *pabbata* Vaṅganta. Mhv­ṭ. 424.

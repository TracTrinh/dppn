# Ekatthambhapāsāda

## English source

Ekatthambhapāsāda. One of the buildings erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. It rose sheer from the ground and was crowned with a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">maṅkara</span> (dolphin). In it was a golden chamber on a golden column, resembling a cave, for the use of the king. Cv.lxxiii.92ff; see also Cv.Trs.ii.11, n.4.

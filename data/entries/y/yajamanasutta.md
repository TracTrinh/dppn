# Yajamānasutta

## English source

Yajamānasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span> asks the Buddha how best to offer gifts, so as to gain great reward. The Buddha replies that the gifts should be offered to the Saṅgha of monks. SN.i.233.

# Yadaniccasutta 01

## English source

Yadaniccasutta 01. The *khandhas* are impermanent, what is impermanent is suffering, and what is suffering is void of self. Thus does the Noble Disciple comprehend things. SN.iii.22.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

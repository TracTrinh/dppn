# Yadaniccasutta 01

## English source

Yadaniccasutta 01. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span> are impermanent, what is impermanent is suffering, and what is suffering is void of self. Thus does the Noble Disciple comprehend things. SN.iii.22.

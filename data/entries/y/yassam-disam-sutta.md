# Yassaṁ disaṁ Sutta

## English source

Yassaṁ disaṁ Sutta. Five qualities, the possession of which makes a king honored wherever he rules – pure descent, great wealth, strong armies, a wise minister and great glory; and five similar qualities – virtue, learning, active energy, insight and emancipation which enable a monk to live free in heart wherever he resides. AN.iii.151ff.

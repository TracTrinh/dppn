# Yudhiṭṭhila 03

## English source

Yudhiṭṭhila 03. The name of a *gotta* to which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya</span>, king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurus</span>, belonged (Ja.iii.400; v. 59, etc.). Mention is made also of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koravyarājā</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yudhiṭṭhila</span> *gotta* (Ja.iv.361). Probably the kings of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurus</span> (*q.v.*) belonged mostly to a dynasty that claimed its descent from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yudhiṭṭhila</span> (Yudhiṣṭhira?).

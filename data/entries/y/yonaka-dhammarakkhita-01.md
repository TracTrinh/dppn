# Yonaka Dhammarakkhita 01

## English source

Yonaka Dhammarakkhita 01. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Thera</span>. He was sent to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aparantaka</span> at the conclusion of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggaliputtatissa</span>’s Council (Mhv.xii.4). There he preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggikkhandhopamasutta</span> and converted thirty-seven thousand beings.

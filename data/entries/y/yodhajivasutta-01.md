# Yodhājīvasutta 01

## English source

Yodhājīvasutta 01. On five kinds of warriors: those who are frightened by a cloud of dust, by the sight of a flag, by tumult, by conflict, and those who fight victoriously; and on five similar kinds of monks. AN.iii.87f.

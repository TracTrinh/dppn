# Yavakalāpiya Thera

## English source

Yavakalāpiya Thera. An *Arahant*. He was a barley reaper (? *yavasika*) in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>, and one day, seeing the Buddha on the road, he spread a sheaf of barley for his seat. Ap.i.282.

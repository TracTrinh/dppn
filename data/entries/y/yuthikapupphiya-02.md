# Yūthikapupphiya 02

## English source

Yūthikapupphiya 02. An *Arahant* Thera. In a previous birth he saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> and offered him a *yūthika* (jasmine) flower. Fifteen *kappas* ago he was a king name <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samitananda</span>. Ap.i.202.

# Yūthikapupphiya 01

## English source

Yūthikapupphiya 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yūthikapupphiya Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he saw the Buddha (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha</span>) on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candabhāgā</span>, and offered him a *yūthika* (jasmine) flower. Sixty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samuddhara</span>. Ap.i.184.

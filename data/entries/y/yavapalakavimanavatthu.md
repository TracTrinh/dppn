# Yavapālakavimānavatthu

## English source

Yavapālakavimānavatthu. The story of a *yavapālaka* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> who gave a meal of sour gruel (*kummāsa*) to an *Arahant*, and, as a result, was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> met him and learnt his story. Vv.iv.7; Vv-a.294.

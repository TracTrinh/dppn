# Yavamajjhaka

## English source

Yavamajjhaka. A village near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mithilā</span>, the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amarādevī</span>, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahosadha</span>. Ja.vi.365, 366; 330 says there were villages of this name on the four sides of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mithilā</span>.

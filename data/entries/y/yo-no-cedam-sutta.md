# Yo no cedaṁ Sutta

## English source

Yo no cedaṁ Sutta. If there were no satisfaction from the earth element, beings would not lust after it; if there were no misery in it, they would not be repelled by it; if there were no escape from it, beings would not so escape; when all these things are fully known, beings will live aloof from the world. SN.ii.172.

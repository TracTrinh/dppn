# Yamelutekulā

## English source

Yamelutekulā. Two Brahmins, probably named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yamelu</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tekula</span>, who proposed to the Buddha that the Dhamma should be put into Sanskrit (*chandasi*, the word seems never to mean Sanskrit, but rather into metrical form). The Buddha refused their request. Vin.ii.139.

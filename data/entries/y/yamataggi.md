# Yamataggi

## English source

Yamataggi (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yāmataggi</span>). A sage of old who led a good life and persuaded his followers to do likewise. He was among the originators of the Vedic hymns. Vin.i.245; DN.i.238, 239; MN.ii.169, 200; AN.iii.224, 229; iv. 61; also Ja.vi.251, where he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yāmataggi</span> and is described as a king of old. Cp. Vedic Index, see *Jamadagni*.

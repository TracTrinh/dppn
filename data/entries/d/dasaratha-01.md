# Dasaratha 01

## English source

Dasaratha 01. Another name for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Janasandha</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span> (see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Janasandha</span> 1). The scholiast (Ja.ii.299) explains that he was so-called because he did with his one chariot what others did with ten chariots (*dasahi rathehi kattabbaṁ attano ekeneva rathena karaṇato Dasaratho ti*).

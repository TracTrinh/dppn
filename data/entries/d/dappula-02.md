# Dappula 02

## English source

Dappula 02. Nephew of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VI</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda II</span>. succeeded <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi VII</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dappula</span> rose against him, but, in spite of several attempts, failed to overcome him. He thereupon retired to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span> where he ruled, having formed a treaty with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span>. Some time later, he made another attempt to win the throne and fought a fierce battle at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Ummāra</span>, where he was defeated and forced to flee (CV.xlviii.90, 98, 109, 122, 125, 131, 155f).

# Dhanapāla 02

## English source

Dhanapāla 02. A *seṭṭhi* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Erakaccha</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dasanna</span>. He was a miser and, after death, was born as a *Peta*. Some merchants, travelling to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttarāpatha</span>, saw his sufferings and, at his request, gave alms to the Buddha on his behalf. Pv.ii.7; Pv-a.99ff.

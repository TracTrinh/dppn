# Dakkhiṇāgirivihāra 02

## English source

Dakkhiṇāgirivihāra 02. A monastery built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saddhātissa</span> in Ceylon (Mhv.xxxiii.7). It was restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhātusena</span> (Cv.xxxviii.46), and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa V</span>. granted a village for its maintenance (Cv.lii.60). It is probably identical with the *Dakkhiṇāgiridaḷhavihāra*, in which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. erected an assembly-hall (Cv.xlii.27). It has sometimes been identified with the present Mulkirigalavihāra (Cv.Trs.i.33, n.3).

It was once the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Appihā</span> *Sāmaṇera* (Mhv­ṭ.552) and of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷa Buddharakkhita</span> (MN­a.i.469).

# Devāsutta 03

## English source

Devāsutta 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahāli</span> visits the Buddha at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kūṭāgārasālā</span> and asks if he has seen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>. The Buddha answers that he has and that he knows many things about <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sakka</span>. He then repeats what is given in Nos. 1 and 2 above. SN.i.229.

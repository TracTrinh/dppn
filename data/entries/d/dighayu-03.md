# Dīghāyu 03

## English source

Dīghāyu 03. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyan</span> prince, grandson of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amitodana</span> and brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddakaccānā</span>. He went to Ceylon and there founded a settlement which he named after himself. His son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghagāmaṇī</span>. Mhv.ix.10f; 18; Dpv.x.6, 8.

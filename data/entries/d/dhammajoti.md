# Dhammajoti

## English source

Dhammajoti. A Sinhalese monk of the eighteenth century who wrote a Sinhalese paraphrase (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sanne</span>) to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bālavatāra</span>, called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Okandapolasanne</span>, because it was written in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Okandapolavihāra</span>.

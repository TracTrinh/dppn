# Dhammajoti

## English source

Dhammajoti. A Sinhalese monk of the eighteenth century who wrote a Sinhalese paraphrase (*sanne*) to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bālavatāra</span>, called the *Okandapolasanne*, because it was written in *Okandapolavihāra*.

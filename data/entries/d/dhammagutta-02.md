# Dhammagutta 02

## English source

Dhammagutta 02. A brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vedisadevī</span>. He was one of the nobles who escorted the Bodhi-tree.

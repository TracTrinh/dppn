# Dhammasangaṇigeha

## English source

Dhammasangaṇigeha. A building in the centre of the citadel of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. It was evidently built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa V</span>., who placed therein a copy of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasangaṇi</span> adorned with manifold jewels, and caused a festal procession to be held in its honour (Cv.lii.50f).

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda IV</span>. restored the building. Cv.liv.45.

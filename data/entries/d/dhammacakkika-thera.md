# Dhammacakkika Thera

## English source

Dhammacakkika Thera (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammacakkadāyaka Thera</span>). An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he placed a *Dhammacakka* in front of the seat of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Eleven *kappas* ago he became king eight times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sahassarāja</span>. Ap.i.90.

# Dhanapālī

## English source

Dhanapālī. A slave-girl who, in spite of her name meaning, Wealth-protector, was ill-treated by her master and mistress. The incident is mentioned as illustrating the small importance of a name. Ja.i.402.

## Vietnamese translation

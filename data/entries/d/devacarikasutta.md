# Devacārikasuttā

## English source

Devacārikasuttā. A group of three *Suttas* describing how <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> visits the *Deva*-worlds and learns from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> how they attained happiness through following the Buddha’s teaching (SN.v.366f).

# Devadattasutta 02

## English source

Devadattasutta 02. Preached in the same circumstances as the above. It gives eight reasons for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>’s downfall, and urges upon the monks the necessity for reflection on the good and bad fortune which overtake oneself and others from time to time. AN.iv.160f; cp. Vin.ii.202.

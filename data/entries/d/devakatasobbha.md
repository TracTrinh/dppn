# Devakaṭasobbha

## English source

Devakaṭasobbha. A pool near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosambī</span>. Close by was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pilakkhaguhā</span> and the *Paribbājakārāma*, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span> once visited <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sandaka</span> and held a discussion with him. MN.i.513; MN­a.ii.687.

# Dhammasattha

## English source

Dhammasattha. Name given to the codes of law drawn up from time to time in Burma, with the assistance of the monks. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammavilāsa</span> (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>) was the author of the oldest of these known by name.

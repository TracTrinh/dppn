# Disāpāmokkha

## English source

Disāpāmokkha. A monk of Burma. He joined the Saṅgha in his old age and studied hard, till he astonished the chief Theras by his learning, and was appointed by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Narapati</span> as his teacher. Sās., p.77.

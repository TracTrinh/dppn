# Dvayatānupassanāsutta

## English source

Dvayatānupassanāsutta. A dissertation delivered by the Buddha, outside the *Migārāmātupāsāda*, on a full-moon day. He tells of the twofold insight – the insight of *dukkha* and its cause, *nirodha* and the way thereto. He then proceeds to explain the origin of *dukkha*, from *upadhi*, *avijjā*, etc. (Snp. p.139ff).

# Dāmali

## English source

Dāmali. A *Devaputta* who visits the Buddha at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span> and tells him that an *Arahant* has to work hard for nothing. The Buddha points out to him that there is nothing left for an *Arahant* to do. SN.i.47.

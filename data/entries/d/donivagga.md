# Doṇivagga

## English source

Doṇivagga. A village mentioned in the campaigns of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxxv.69, 72). It stood in a depression twelve miles from the modern <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ratnapura</span>, and the name is preserved in a stream flowing through it, the Denavaka. Cv.Trs.ii.50, n.3.

# Dhammasavaṇiya

## English source

Dhammasavaṇiya. A Thera who attained *Arahant*-ship at the age of seven, having heard the Buddha preach the stanza beginning with “*aniccā vata saṅkhārā*.”

In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> he was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jaṭila</span> who, while flying through the air, was forced to descend where the Buddha sat preaching, it being impossible to fly over him. He was afterwards born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>. Ap.i.273f.

# Dāṭhāppabhuti 03

## English source

Dāṭhāppabhuti 03. An *Ādipāda* (royal prince), contemporary of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span> defeated the *Vetullavādins*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāppabhuti</span> was so incensed that he raised his arm to strike the Thera*,* but an ulcer immediately appeared on it. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāppabhuti</span> refused to ask <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>’s forgiveness and died soon after. Cv.xlii.36f.

# Dukūla

## English source

Dukūla (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dukūlaka</span>). A hunter’s son, father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suvaṇṇasāma</span>. He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kassapa</span>. For his story see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāmajātaka</span> (Ja 540). See also Mil.123; Vin-a.i.214.

# Daṇḍissara

## English source

Daṇḍissara. A special grant given by kings to mendicant artists. It is first heard of in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa IV</span>. (Cv.lii.3), and seems to have been kept up by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena III</span>. (Cv.liii.30) and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lx.22).

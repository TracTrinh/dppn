# Dhammasaṅgāhaka Therā

## English source

Dhammasaṅgāhaka Therā. The name given to the Elders who took part in the Three Councils.

These Elders sometimes inserted in the Canon additional information by way of explanation or of summarising what has gone before, e.g., DN.ii.128, 134, 135, 167; as explained by DN­a.ii.568, etc.

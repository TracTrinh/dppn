# Devakūṭa 01

## English source

Devakūṭa 01. The name of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cetiyapabbata</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. The Buddha visited it, and all the people of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ojadīpa</span> (as Ceylon was then called) paid him homage. Mhv.xv.63; Vin-a.i.86; Dpv.xv.38; xvii.14, 32.

# Dhammasaṅgaha

## English source

Dhammasaṅgaha. Evidently another and earlier name for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasangaṇi</span>.

The name occurs in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumaṅgalavilāsini</span> (DN­a.i.17) in the classification of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhamma</span> books.

Its Commentary is also referred to as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasaṅgahaṭṭhakathā</span>, e.g., Vibh­a.391, 43, 105, 518; Paṭis-a.405.

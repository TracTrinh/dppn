# Dhanañjaya 04

## English source

Dhanañjaya 04. King of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kurus</span>, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koravyarājā</span>. He reigned in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indapatta</span> and belonged to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yudhiṭṭhila</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">gotta</span>. For his story see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sambhavajātaka</span> (Ja 515, Ja.v.57ff). He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>.

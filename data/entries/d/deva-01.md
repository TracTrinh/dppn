# Deva 01

## English source

Deva 01. *Aggasāvaka* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sujāta Buddha</span>. He was a chaplain’s son, and the Buddha’s first sermon was addressed to him and his friend <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudassana</span> (Ja.i.38; Bv­a.168, 170). He is also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudeva</span> (Bv.xiii.25).

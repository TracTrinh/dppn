# Dhammānanda

## English source

Dhammānanda. A monk who wrote several Pāli grammatical works. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhavaṁsa</span> (p.74, also Svd.1250; but see under these names) assigns to him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccāyanasāra</span> together with its <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkā</span>, and also the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccāyanabheda</span>.

# Daḷhika

## English source

Daḷhika. A monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāgala</span>.

A pupil of his once stole a turban from a shopkeeper and confessed his fault to Daḷhika, thinking that he would, on that account, become a *pārājika*.

But the value of the turban was less than five *māsakas* and the pupil was saved from a *pārājika*-offence. Vin.iii.67.

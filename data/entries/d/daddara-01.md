# Daddara 01

## English source

Daddara 01. (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Daddarapabbata</span>). A mountain in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span> (Ja.ii.8, 67; iii.16; Ap.ii.536); it is the same as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rajatapabbata</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>), which was called Daddara on account of the thunder playing round it.

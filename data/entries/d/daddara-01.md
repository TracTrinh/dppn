# Daddara 01

## English source

Daddara 01. (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Daddarapabbata</span>). A mountain in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span> (Ja.ii.8, 67; iii.16; Ap.ii.536); it is the same as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rajatapabbata</span> (*q.v.*), which was called Daddara on account of the thunder playing round it.

## Vietnamese translation

Daddara 01. (Daddarapabbata). Một ngọn núi ở Himavā (Ja.ii.8, 67; iii.16; Ap.ii.536); núi này cũng chính là Rajatapabbata (*q.v.*), được gọi là Daddara vì sấm vang quanh nó.

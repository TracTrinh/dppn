# Devala 04

## English source

Devala 04. An ascetic in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. He lived before the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>, who was yet in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tusita</span>, but realizing in his mind the qualities of previous Buddhas, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devala</span> built a *cetiya* on the bank of a river and made offerings to it in the name of the Buddha. Later, he was born in the *Brahma*-world. He was a previous birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirima</span> Thera (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulinuppādaka</span>), Thag­a.i.280; Ap.ii.426.

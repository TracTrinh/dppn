# Dhaññavatī 02

## English source

Dhaññavatī 02. A city in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paduma Buddha</span>. It was the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudhaññaseṭṭhi</span>, whose daughter, also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhaññavatī</span>, offered a meal of milk-rice to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paduma</span>, just before his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>. Bv­a.147.

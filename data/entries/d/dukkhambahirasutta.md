# Dukkhambāhirasutta

## English source

Dukkhambāhirasutta. Forms seen by the eye are <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, so are the things perceived by other senses. They are void of self. SN.iv.4.

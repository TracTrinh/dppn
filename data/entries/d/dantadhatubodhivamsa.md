# Dantadhātubodhivaṁsa

## English source

Dantadhātubodhivaṁsa. A work ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span>, author of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jinālaṅkāra</span> (*Buddhaghosuppati*, pp.49-51). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhavaṁsa</span> (pp.62, 65, 72, 75) mentions a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dantadhātuppakaraṇa</span> as the work of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammakitti</span>. The reference is evidently to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāvaṁsa</span> by that author.

# Duccaritasutta 03

## English source

Duccaritasutta 03. A monk once came to the Buddha and asked for a brief teaching. The Buddha asked him to abandon wrong conduct with regard to body, feelings, mind and mind-states, and to cultivate the four *satipaṭṭhāna*s. SN.v.188.

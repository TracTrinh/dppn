# Duccaritasutta 03

## English source

Duccaritasutta 03. A monk once came to the Buddha and asked for a brief teaching. The Buddha asked him to abandon wrong conduct with regard to body, feelings, mind and mind-states, and to cultivate the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s. SN.v.188.

# Devadahakkhaṇasutta

## English source

Devadahakkhaṇasutta. Arahants need not strive earnestly in respect of the six-fold sphere of sense, but those who are yet students (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sekhā</span>) must do so. The reasons for this are given (SN.iv.124).

# Dhātusutta 05

## English source

Dhātusutta 05. The appearing of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dhātus</span> is the appearing of decay and death. Their cessation is also simultaneous. SN.iii.231.

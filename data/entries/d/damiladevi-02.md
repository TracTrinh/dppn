# Damiḷādevī 02

## English source

Damiḷādevī 02. Queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Dāṭhika Mahā Nāga</span>. She was young and beautiful and when she visited <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambatthala</span>, an old monk named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Citta</span> fell in love with her and refused to be consoled even though she died soon after.

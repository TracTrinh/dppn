# Devasutta

## English source

Devasutta. The struggle of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">asuras</span> is typical of that of the monks with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>; victory is sometimes on one side, sometimes on the other, until the enemy is completely crushed and rendered ineffective.

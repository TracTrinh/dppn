# Dīgha 03

## English source

Dīgha 03. A Brahmin. He found he could not satisfy other Brahmins, even though he gave five bowls of food to each. One day he went to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>, and, in order to test the monks, served them with only one vessel of rice. Thirty monks partook of it and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīgha</span> was pleased with their great moderation. AN­a.i.262f.

# Dhajadāyaka Thera 01

## English source

Dhajadāyaka Thera 01. An Arahant. He set up a standard at the foot of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara</span>’s Bodhi-tree and swept the ground around it. He was once a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uggata</span>, and again a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Megha</span> (Ap.i.108f).

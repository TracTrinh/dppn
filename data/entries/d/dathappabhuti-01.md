# Dāṭhāppabhuti 01

## English source

Dāṭhāppabhuti 01. Father of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silākāla</span>. He was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇa</span> and served under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa I</span>. Later he quarrelled with the king and retired to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mereliya</span> district. Cv.xxxix.44.

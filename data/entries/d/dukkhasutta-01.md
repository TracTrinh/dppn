# Dukkhasutta 01

## English source

Dukkhasutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Dukkha</span> arises from the contact of the senses and the objects proper to the senses, resulting in feeling, which, in turn, produces craving. By destroying this process <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span> is destroyed. SN.ii.71; cf. SN.iv.86.

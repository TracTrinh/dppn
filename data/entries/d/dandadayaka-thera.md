# Daṇḍadāyaka Thera

## English source

Daṇḍadāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Ninety-four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">kappas</span> ago he presented to the Saṅgha a walking-stick (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ālambana</span>) made from a forest bamboo-tree (Ap.i.283; repeated at ii.456). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumāputtasahāya</span>. Thag­a.i.103.

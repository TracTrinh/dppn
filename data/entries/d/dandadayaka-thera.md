# Daṇḍadāyaka Thera

## English source

Daṇḍadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he presented to the Saṅgha a walking-stick (*ālambana*) made from a forest bamboo-tree (Ap.i.283; repeated at ii.456). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kumāputtasahāya</span>. Thag­a.i.103.

# Deva 10

## English source

Deva 10. A general of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>., called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkāpura</span>, probably identical with 9. He fought against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūkarabhātu</span>, and later took part in the Sinhalese expedition to South India and fought in fierce battles at Tirippāḷuru and Rājinā, capturing the latter place. Cv.lxxv.130; lxxvi.250, 310, 324, 326.

# Dvedhāvitakkasutta

## English source

Dvedhāvitakkasutta. Preached at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. The Buddha tells the monks how, before the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, he divided his thoughts into two groups – the first being of pleasures of sense, of harm and of hurt, and the other thoughts of renunciation. He then proceeds to explain how this ultimately led to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>. MN.i.114ff.

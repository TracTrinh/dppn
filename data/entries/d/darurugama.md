# Dārūrugāma

## English source

Dārūrugāma. A village near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span> in Ceylon. Near it was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayavaḍḍhanakoṭṭa</span> (Cv.xci.6). The name may have been <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dārugāma</span>, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">uru</span> being a descriptive adjective meaning <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mahā</span> (Cv.Trs.ii.213, n.2).

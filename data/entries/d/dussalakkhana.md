# Dussalakkhaṇa

## English source

Dussalakkhaṇa. A Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> who claimed to be able to prognosticate by looking at pieces of cloth.

# Dhammakitti 01

## English source

Dhammakitti 01. One of the Sinhalese envoys of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. to the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāmañña</span>. The king insulted him and sent him, together with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāgissara</span>, back to Ceylon in a leaky ship.

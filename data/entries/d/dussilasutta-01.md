# Dussīlasutta 01

## English source

Dussīlasutta 01. For the wicked who lack virtue, right concentration is perforce destroyed, and this ultimately leads to the destruction of emancipated knowledge and insight, just as a tree devoid of branches and leaves is ultimately destroyed.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

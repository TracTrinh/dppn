# Dīghasumma

## English source

Dīghasumma. A Thera of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span>. A fisherman, living at the mouth of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span> River, gave him alms on several occasions and remembered him at the moment of his death.

# Devadahasutta 02

## English source

Devadahasutta 02. Preached to the monks at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadaha</span>. Some monks, going to reside in the western districts, come to the Buddha to take leave of him. He advises them, before starting, to visit <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span>, whom he extols. This they do, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> explains to them the fundamental teachings of the Buddha, in order that they may be ready to answer any questions which may be put to them. SN.iii.6ff.

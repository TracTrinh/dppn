# Duyyodhana 01

## English source

Duyyodhana 01. The Bodhisatta, born as king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Magadha</span>. He was later born as king of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span>, under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkhapāla</span>. For his story see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkhapālajātaka</span> (Ja 524), SN.v.162ff.

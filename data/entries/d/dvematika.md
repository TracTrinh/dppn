# Dvemātikā

## English source

Dvemātikā. A late compilation, made in Burma, from the Pāli texts. It contains the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhikkhupātimokkha</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhikkhunīpātimokkha</span>, and extracts from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parivāra</span> and other Vinaya texts. Bode, 6, n.2.

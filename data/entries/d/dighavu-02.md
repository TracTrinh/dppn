# Dīghāvu 02

## English source

Dīghāvu 02. A householder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> and son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotika</span> *gahapati*. He was a *Sotāpanna* and, when he lay ill, he sent his father to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> requesting the Buddha to visit him. The Buddha accepted the request, preached to him and consoled him. Soon afterwards <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīghāvu</span> died and was born as an *Anāgāmī*. SN.v.344f.

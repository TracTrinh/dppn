# Dundubhissara Thera

## English source

Dundubhissara Thera, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Durabhisāra Thera</span>. An *Arahant*. After the Third Council he accompanied the Thera <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Majjhima</span> to the region at the foot of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span> (Vin-a.i.68; Mhv­ṭ.317; Mbv.115). In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpavaṁsa</span> (viii.10) he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Durabhisāra</span>.

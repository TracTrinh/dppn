# Dukkhalakkhaṇavatthu

## English source

Dukkhalakkhaṇavatthu. The story of five hundred monks who, in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>, had practised meditation on the characteristics of suffering. In the present age they became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span> immediately on hearing a stanza on suffering. Dhp­a.iii.406.

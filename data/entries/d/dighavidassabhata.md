# Dīghavidassabhātā

## English source

Dīghavidassabhātā. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khuddakapāṭha</span> Commentary (Khp­a.126f) contains a reference to a “Dīghavidassabhātā” to the effect that, for the space of four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddha</span>-intervals, he burnt in hell, going up and down, his body sixty leagues in height, and five hundred families who accepted his views suffered with him.

# Devatāsutta 02

## English source

Devatāsutta 02. The Buddha tells the monks how, on various occasions, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> had visited him and told him that they had felt remorse after their death because they had not honoured recluses during their life as human beings. Others who had honoured recluses had obtained happiness. AN.iv.390f.

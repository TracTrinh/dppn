# Daṇḍasutta

## English source

Daṇḍasutta. Incalculable is the beginning of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Saṁsāra</span>, not revealed; just as no one knows how a stick thrown up into the air will fall, whether on its side, its tip, its butt-end, etc. SN.ii.184.

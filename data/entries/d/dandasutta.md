# Daṇḍasutta

## English source

Daṇḍasutta. Incalculable is the beginning of *Saṁsāra*, not revealed; just as no one knows how a stick thrown up into the air will fall, whether on its side, its tip, its butt-end, etc. SN.ii.184.

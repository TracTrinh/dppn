# Dhammahadayavibhaṅgasutta

## English source

Dhammahadayavibhaṅgasutta. In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suttasaṅgaha</span>, a portion of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammahadayavibhaṅga</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vibhaṅgappakaraṇa</span> (Vibh.422f) – dealing with birth in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāmāvacara</span> worlds, the good deeds that lead to birth therein, and the span of life in each world – has been taken as a separate <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> (No.14) and called by this name.

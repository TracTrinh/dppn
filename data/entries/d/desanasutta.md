# Desanāsutta

## English source

Desanāsutta or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhāvanāsutta</span>. On the psychic power, its basis, and the practice which leads to its cultivation (SN.v.276). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sutta</span> corresponds, word for word, with a passage in Aśvaghoṣa’s <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sūtrālaṅkāra</span>.

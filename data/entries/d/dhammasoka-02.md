# Dhammāsoka 02

## English source

Dhammāsoka 02. A king of Ceylon (1208-9 CE.). He was of royal blood, and was three months old on his accession. He was killed by the *Mahādipāda* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anīkaṅga</span>. Cv.lxxx.42, 44; also Cv.Trs.ii.131, n.1.

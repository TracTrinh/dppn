# Dukkhadhammasutta

## English source

Dukkhadhammasutta. When a monk knows the arising and the destruction of all states of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span>, he realizes the nature of sensual pleasures and has no longing for them. This is explained by various similes. SN.iv.188ff.

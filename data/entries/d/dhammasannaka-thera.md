# Dhammasaññaka Thera

## English source

Dhammasaññaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Once, during a festival in honour of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>’s *bodhi*-tree, he heard the Buddha preach and paid him homage. Thirty-three *kappas* ago he was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sutavā</span>. Ap.i.249.

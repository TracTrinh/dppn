# Dverataniya Thera

## English source

Dverataniya Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> he was a hunter, and, seeing the Buddha in a forest, gave him a piece of flesh. Four *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Rohita</span>. Ap.i.214.

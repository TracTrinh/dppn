# Dhammasenāpati 02

## English source

Dhammasenāpati 02. A monk of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānandavihāra</span> in Pagan. He probably started as a nobleman and later joined the Saṅgha. He wrote a Pāli grammatical work called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kārikā</span>. Bode, 15f; Gv.63, 73.

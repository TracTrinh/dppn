# Dhanapiṭṭhi

## English source

Dhanapiṭṭhi. A locality in Ceylon. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IV</span>. its chief was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Datta</span>. He erected there a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> called by his name. Cv.xlvi.41, 43.

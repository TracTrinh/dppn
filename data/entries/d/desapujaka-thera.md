# Desapūjaka Thera

## English source

Desapūjaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. He once saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> passing through the air and, much pleased, offered homage in his direction. In another birth he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gosujāta</span> (Ap.i.183).

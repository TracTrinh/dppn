# Dhātubhājaniyakathā

## English source

Dhātubhājaniyakathā. The last chapter of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhavaṁsa</span>. It contains details of the distribution of the relics of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gotama Buddha</span> (Bv.xxviii). The Commentary makes no comments on this.

# Duppasaha

## English source

Duppasaha. A king of long ago, descendant of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sammata</span>. He was the last of fifty kings who ruled in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayujjha</span>. Sixty of his descendants reigned in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. Dpv.iii.16; Mhv­ṭ.127.

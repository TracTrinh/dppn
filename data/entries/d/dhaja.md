# Dhaja

## English source

Dhaja. One of the eight Brahmins who recognised the signs at the Buddha’s birth (Ja.i.56). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milindapañha</span> (p.236) speaks of him as one of the Buddha’s first teachers.

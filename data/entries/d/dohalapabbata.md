# Dohaḷapabbata

## English source

Dohaḷapabbata. A mountain in Ceylon, probably in the district of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Janapada</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Silāmeghavaṇṇa</span> once occupied it (Cv.xliv.56; Cv.Trs.i.79, n.4). Near to it was an image house of the Buddha, called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūkara</span>. Cv. c.294.

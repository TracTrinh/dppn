# Dāṭhā

## English source

Dāṭhā. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. She was given to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Malayarājā</span>, the sister’s son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. (Cv.xlii.6, 10), who afterwards became <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi II</span>. (Cv.xlii.64). She seems to have been also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅghabhaddā</span>. (Cv.xlii.41).

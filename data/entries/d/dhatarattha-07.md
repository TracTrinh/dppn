# Dhataraṭṭha 07

## English source

Dhataraṭṭha 07. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgas</span> (DN.ii.259), descendants of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhataraṭṭha</span> and of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samuddajā</span> (Ja.vi.219), and possessed great power. They dwell in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sattasidantarasamudda</span> (SN­a.ii.254).

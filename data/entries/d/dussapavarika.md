# Dussapāvārika

## English source

Dussapāvārika. A *seṭṭhi* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nālandā</span> who owned a mango-grove near the city. Having heard the Buddha preach, he became his follower and built for him and his Saṅgha a monastery in his mango-grove, which came to be called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāvārikambavana</span>. DN­a.iii.873; MN­a.ii.594; SN­a.iii.169.

# Dhammacaryageha

## English source

Dhammacaryageha. A building in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>, erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span>. It was attached to the royal palace, and when the Tooth Relic was brought to Ceylon it was deposited in this building. Cv.xxxvii.95.

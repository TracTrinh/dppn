# Dakkhiṇāsutta

## English source

Dakkhiṇāsutta. The four purities in gifts (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dakkhiṇāvisuddhi</span>), depending on whether giver and receiver are both virtuous, or whether only one of them (AN.ii.80f; cp. MN.iii.256f). It was probably also called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇāvisuddhisutta</span>. Khp-a.222.

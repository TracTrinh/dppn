# Dolāmaṇḍapa

## English source

Dolāmaṇḍapa. A building erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpuyyāna</span>. It was so-called because it contained a swing hung with minute golden bells. Cv.lxxiii.116.

# Devamantiya

## English source

Devamantiya. One of the nobles of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milinda</span>.

He it was who took the king to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgasena</span> and who was asked to invite <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāgasena</span> to the palace (Mil.22f.29).

The name is considered to be a corruption of the Greek Demetrius (Mil. Trans.vol.i.p.xix, etc.).

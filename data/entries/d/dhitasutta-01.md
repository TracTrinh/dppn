# Dhītāsutta 01

## English source

Dhītāsutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pasenadi</span> visits the Buddha and grumbles because <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallikā</span> has given birth to a daughter. The Buddha points out to him that a woman may prove a better offspring than a male. SN.i.86.

# Labujagāma

## English source

Labujagāma. A village in Ceylon, in the province of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saparagamu</span>. Once, for a short period, the Tooth Relic of the Buddha was placed in the monastery there, after being taken from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayavaḍḍhanapura</span> (Cp. Cv.xci.17f), and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya</span> removed it from there to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍanapura</span>. Cv.xciv.11f.

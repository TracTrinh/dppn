# Lāla

## English source

Lāla. A country in India, the capital of which was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhapura</span>; it was the birthplace of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijaya</span>, founder of the Sinhalese dynasty (Dpv.ix.5; Mhv.vi.5, 36). The country is now generally identified with the modern Gujarat, the Larika of Ptolemy.

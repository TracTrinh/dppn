# Lakkhaṇa 02

## English source

Lakkhaṇa 02. One of the eight Brahmins who recognized the auspicious signs at the birth of the Bodhisatta (Ja.i.56). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milinda</span> (Mil. 236) speaks of him as one of the Buddha’s first teachers.

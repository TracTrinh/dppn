# Lomasanāga

## English source

Lomasanāga. A monk of Ceylon who lived in the *Padhānaghara* in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅguguhā</span> on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cetiyapabbata</span>. He is given as an example of a monk who did not abandon his meditations in spite of extreme cold or heat. MN­a.i.65.

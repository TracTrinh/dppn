# Lokasutta 09

## English source

Lokasutta 09. The world and its arising are fully known by a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tathāgata</span> and he is released from both; he also knows the ending of it and the way thereto. He speaks as he does; he is unconquered in the world. AN.ii.23.

# Lokāyatikasutta

## English source

Lokāyatikasutta. A Brahmin, well versed in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lokāyata</span> (*q.v.*), asks the Buddha a series of questions regarding the world and existence. The Buddha ignores them and teaches him the *paṭiccasamuppāda*, which he accepts. SN.ii.77f.

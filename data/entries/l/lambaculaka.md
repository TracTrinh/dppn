# Lambacūḷaka

## English source

Lambacūḷaka. A town in the domain of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pajaka</span> (Ja.iii.463), and, therefore, in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Avanti</span>. Elsewhere (Ja.v.133) it is mentioned as having been in the domain of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Caṇḍappajjota</span>, probably again referring to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Avanti</span>.

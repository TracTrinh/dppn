# Laṅkādhināyaka

## English source

Laṅkādhināyaka, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkādhinātha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Laṅkānātha</span>. Cv.lxx. 24, 205.

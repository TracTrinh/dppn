# Lābugāmaka

## English source

Lābugāmaka. A village in Ceylon where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍukābhaya</span> vanquished his uncles. Their heads were collected and lay “like a heap of gourds,” hence the name of the village (Mhv.x.72; see also Mhv.Trs.73, n.2.). Its original name was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nagaragāma</span>. Mhv­ṭ. 292.

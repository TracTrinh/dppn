# Laṅkāgiripabbata

## English source

Laṅkāgiripabbata. A hill in the mountainous central province of Ceylon, in the district once known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bodhigāmavara</span>. It is mentioned in the account of the campaigns of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxvi.90; lxx.88; for identification with modern Laggala, see Cv.Trs.i.259, n.3.

# Lohakūṭapabbatavihāra

## English source

Lohakūṭapabbatavihāra. A monastery in a very remote place in India. It could be reached only by hanging on to the branch of a tree when the wind bent it. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhāsena</span> attained *Arahant*-ship there. Ras.ii.110f.

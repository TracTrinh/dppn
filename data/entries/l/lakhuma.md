# Lakhumā

## English source

Lakhumā. A woman who lived near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kevaṭṭadvāra</span> *gāma*, near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>. She was pious and gave alms to several holy monks, listened to their preaching, and became a *Sotāpanna*. After death she was born in a *vimāna* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> and there had a conversation with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>, at the end of which she became a *Sakadāgāmī*. Vv.ii.2; Vv-a.97f.

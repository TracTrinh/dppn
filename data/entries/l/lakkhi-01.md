# Lakkhī 01

## English source

Lakkhī 01. The Pāli equivalent of Lakṣmī, goddess of beauty and fortune. The name is found usually only in later works, e.g., Cv.lxxii.101; lxxvi.233; Ja.ii.413; iii.306 (explained as being equal to *paññā*); v.113; the word Lacchī, however, probably equivalent to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lakkhī</span> (1), is found in Thīg.vs.419, but the passage is obscure. See *Sisters* 159, n.2.

# Lokissara

## English source

Lokissara. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> chief who came from India with a spear wound on his shoulder. He defeated <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līlāvatī</span> in Ceylon and reigned there for nine months (1210-11 CE.), till he was defeated by the general <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkama</span>.

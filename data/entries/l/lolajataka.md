# Lolajātaka

## English source

Lolajātaka (Ja 274). The story of the Bodhisatta born as a pigeon and of his friendship with a greedy crow. The story is practically the same as that of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapotajātaka</span> (Ja 42) (*q.v.*), and was related in reference to a greedy monk who was reported to the Buddha. The crow is identified with the monk. Ja.ii.361ff.

# Liṅgatthavivaraṇa

## English source

Liṅgatthavivaraṇa. A Pāli grammatical work by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhūtacandana</span>, a monk of Pagan. Two Commentaries on it were written, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Liṅgatthavivaraṇappakāsaka</span> by Ñāṇasāgara and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Liṅgatthavivaraṇaṭīkā</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttama</span>. A work called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Liṅgatthavivaraṇavinicchaya</span>, by an unknown author, is also mentioned. Gv.63, 65, 67, 72, 73, 75; Bode, 22, n.1.

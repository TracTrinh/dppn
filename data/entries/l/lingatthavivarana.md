# Liṅgatthavivaraṇa

## English source

Liṅgatthavivaraṇa. A Pāli grammatical work by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhūtacandana</span>, a monk of Pagan. Two Commentaries on it were written, the *Liṅgatthavivaraṇappakāsaka* by Ñāṇasāgara and the *Liṅgatthavivaraṇaṭīkā* by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttama</span>. A work called *Liṅgatthavivaraṇavinicchaya*, by an unknown author, is also mentioned. Gv.63, 65, 67, 72, 73, 75; Bode, 22, n.1.

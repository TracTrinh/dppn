# Laṅkāvihāra

## English source

Laṅkāvihāra. A monastery near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Gāma</span>; it was near there that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span> found <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vihāradevī</span> when she landed from the sea (Mhv.xxii.22). But this is probably a wrong reading. See Mhv­ṭ. 432, where the place is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tolaka</span> *vihāra*.

# Megha 01

## English source

Megha 01. A youth in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpaṅkara Buddha</span>. Hearing the Buddha prophesy the future of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Megha</span> entered the Saṅgha with him. He was a former birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammaruci</span> Thera. Ap.ii.430.

# Mahā Summa Thera 03

## English source

Mahā Summa Thera 03. Pupil of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissa</span>. After reading the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayapiṭaka</span> nine times, he went to live beyond the river (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>) (Vin-a.i.263). His views are quoted in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samantapāsādikā</span>, e.g., ii.368; iii.535, 538, 556, 588, 596, 609, 644, 646, 647, 651, 683, 698, 715, 719, etc.

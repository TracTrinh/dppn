# Miñjavataṁsakiya Thera

## English source

Miñjavataṁsakiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Thirty-one *kappas* ago he made offerings at the Bodhi-tree of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>. Twenty-six *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meghabbha</span>. Ap.i.216f.

# Mālava

## English source

Mālava. The name of various <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Damiḷa</span> chiefs, allies of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kulasekhara</span> (Cv.lxxvi. 132, 137, 210, 235, 265ff., 284). Two of them were called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lambakaṇṇas</span>. Cv.lxxvii.27.

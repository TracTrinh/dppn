# Matta 03

## English source

Matta 03. A hunter who discovered four marvellous gems near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pelavāpikagāma</span>, seven leagues to the north of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. He reported his discovery to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>, and the gems were used for the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. Mhv.xxviii.39; Mhv­ṭ. 512.

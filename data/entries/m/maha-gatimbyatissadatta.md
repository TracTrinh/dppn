# Mahā Gatimbyatissadatta

## English source

Mahā Gatimbyatissadatta (*v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Gatigamiyatissadatta</span>). A Thera. He once went over from Ceylon to India to worship the Bodhi-tree. While crossing, seeing only the waters round him, he fell to wondering which was the more extraordinary, the sound of the ocean waves or the method of the twenty-four divisions of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṭṭhāna</span>. The limits of the great ocean then became apparent to him. Dhs-a. p.11.

# Maṇḍadīpa

## English source

Maṇḍadīpa. The name of Ceylon in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>; its capital was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Visāla</span> and its king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayanta</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span> was called Mahā Sāgara. Mhv.xv.127; Dpv.i.73; ix.20; xv.57, etc.

# Moranivāpasutta 02

## English source

Moranivāpasutta 02. Preached at the *Paribbājakārāma* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moranivāpa</span>. It enumerates several groups of qualities the possession of which make a monk fully proficient in the higher life. Three of the groups are triads and the fourth a dyad. A stanza, attributed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sanaṅkumāra</span>, is also quoted. AN.v.326ff.

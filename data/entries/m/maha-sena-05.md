# Mahā Sena 05

## English source

Mahā Sena 05. A king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span>. He and his sister worked with their own hands and gave alms to 500 monks from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgudīpa</span>, among whom was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sīva</span> (8). The monk wished that they should see their alms being eaten by the monks in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgudīpa</span>. Ras.i.72f.

# Mahā Nāmasutta 02

## English source

Mahā Nāmasutta 02. The circumstances are similar to those of the above *Sutta*. The noble disciple, possessed of unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, the Dhamma and the Saṅgha, and who cultivates virtues dear to the Ariyans, such a one is destined to *Nibbāna*. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāma</span> is such a one. SN.v.371; 404.

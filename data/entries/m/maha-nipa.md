# Mahā Nīpa

## English source

Mahā Nīpa. A place in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> through which the *simā* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> passed (Mbv. 134,135). It probably received its name from a *nīpa* tree growing there.

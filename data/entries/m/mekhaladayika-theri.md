# Mekhaladāyikā Therī

## English source

Mekhaladāyikā Therī. An *Arahant* Therī. Ninety-four *kappas* ago she offered her *mekhalā* a girdle for the restoration of the *Thūpa* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span> (Ap.ii.513f). She is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mettikā Therī</span>. Thīg­a.35.

# Mahā Tissa 01

## English source

Mahā Tissa 01. A Thera who lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaggari</span> in Ceylon. He was an *Arahant*, and is mentioned as being among the last of those who took part in various assemblies that followed the departure of the Bodhisatta during different births, such as the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddālaka</span> *Samāgama*, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūgapakkha</span>, etc. Ja.vi.30.

# Mahī 01

## English source

Mahī 01. One of the five great rivers of India, all of which have a common origin (Vin.ii.237; AN.iv.101; v. 22; SN.ii.135; v. 38; Mil. 20, 104; Vsm.10, etc.). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttarāpa</span> was to the north of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahī</span> (Snp­a.ii.437ff). It is also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Mahī</span>.

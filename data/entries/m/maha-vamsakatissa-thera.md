# Mahā Vaṁsakatissa Thera

## English source

Mahā Vaṁsakatissa Thera. Of Ceylon. Mentioned among the last of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahants</span>. He was among those who took part in various “assemblies” the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuddālaka</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūgapakkha</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ayoghara</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hatthipāla</span>. Ja.vi.30.

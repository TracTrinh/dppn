# Mahā Nettappāsāda

## English source

Mahā Nettappāsāda. A monastery in Ceylon, for the incumbent of which <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. built a *vihāra* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vātagiri</span> (Cv.lxxxviii.46). Among the incumbents of Mahā Nettappāsāda was an Elder known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vīdāgama</span> Thera*,* author of several Sinhalese works. PLC. 253.

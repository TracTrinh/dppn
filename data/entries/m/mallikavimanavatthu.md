# Mallikāvimānavatthu

## English source

Mallikāvimānavatthu. The story of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mallikā</span>, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhula</span>, who, after death, was born in a wonderful palace in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, surrounded by all manner of luxuries. This was chiefly as a result of her having offered her *Mahā Latāpasādhana* in honour of the Buddha, after his death. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span> met her in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span> and heard her story. VV.iii.8; Vv-a.165ff.

# Madhubhaṇḍapūjā

## English source

Madhubhaṇḍapūjā. A ceremony held by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhātikābhaya</span> in honour of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. It consisted of offerings of vessels filled with honey, and of lotus flowers strewn ankle-deep in the courtyard, of lighted lamps filled with fragrant oil, etc. Mhv.xxxiv.52ff.; Mhv­ṭ.631.

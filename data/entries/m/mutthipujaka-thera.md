# Muṭṭhipūjaka Thera

## English source

Muṭṭhipūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha Buddha</span>, while the Buddha was practicing austerities, he gave him a handful of *girinela*-flowers. Twenty-three *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sunela</span>. Ap.i.201.

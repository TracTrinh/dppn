# Maṅgalacetiya

## English source

Maṅgalacetiya. A religious building, probably in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissa II</span>. erected a *Thūpa* to the north of it and also an image house containing an image (Cv.xxxvii.183). It is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bahumaṅgalacetiya</span> (*q.v.*). Cv.Trs.i.36; n.1.

# Mahā Saṅgharakkhita 03

## English source

Mahā Saṅgharakkhita 03. Called *Malayavāsī* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Saṅgharakkhita</span> or *Uparimaṇḍalakamalayavāsī*. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissabhūti</span> went to him, on finding his mind corrupted by sinful thoughts, and having received from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅgharakkhita</span> a topic of meditation, he attained to *Arahant*-ship (AN­a.i.23f.; MN­a.i.55). He was one of the last of the *Arahants*. J. L. Makārakkhira. Ja.iv.490; vi.30.

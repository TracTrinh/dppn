# Makkarakaṭa

## English source

Makkarakaṭa. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Avanti</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kaccāna</span> once stayed there in a forest hut and was visited by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lohicca</span> and his pupils (SN.iv.116f). The Commentary (SN­a.iii.29) calls Makkarakaṭa a town (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nagara</span>).

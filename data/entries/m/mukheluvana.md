# Mukheluvana

## English source

Mukheluvana. A grove in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kajaṅgalā</span>. It was there that the Buddha preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indriyabhāvanāsutta</span> (MN.iii.298). The Commentary explains (MN­a.ii.1028) that the grove consisted of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mukhelu</span>-trees.

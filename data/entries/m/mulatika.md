# Mūlaṭīkā

## English source

Mūlaṭīkā. A sub-commentary on the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span> written by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda Thera</span> of Ceylon (Gv.60, 69; Svd. 1217). It was so-called because it was the first of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">ṭīkās</span> (Sās.33). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">anuṭīkā</span> on this is called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Līnatthavaṇṇanā</span>. Gv.60.

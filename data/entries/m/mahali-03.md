# Mahāli 03

## English source

Mahāli 03. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyan</span> prince, one of seven grandsons of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Amitodana</span>. They were brothers of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddakaccānā</span>, wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍuvāsadeva</span>, and came to Ceylon, where they settled. Dpv.x.6. See Mhv.ix.6, 9.

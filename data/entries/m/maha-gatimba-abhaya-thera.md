# Mahā Gatimba Abhaya Thera

## English source

Mahā Gatimba Abhaya Thera. Mentioned among those who could remember early incidents in their lives. When he was five days old he saw a crow pecking at some milk-rice prepared for a ceremony and made a sound to drive it away. This was the earliest recollection of the Thera. He had a beautiful complexion. DN­a.ii.530; MNid-a.234. AN­a.ii.596.

# Muṇḍagaṅgā

## English source

Muṇḍagaṅgā. A village in Ceylon, near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māliya-unna</span> *vihāra*. It was the residence of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāliya</span>, in his previous birth as artisan. Mhv­ṭ. 605.

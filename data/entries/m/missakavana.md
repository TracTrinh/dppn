# Missakavana

## English source

Missakavana. A park in Tāvatiṁsa (Ja.vi.278; Divy.194,195; Mvu.ii.451). It is generally mentioned together with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandana</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phārusaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cittalatāvana</span>, e.g., Vin-a.i.164; Vibh-a.439; Vsm.425.

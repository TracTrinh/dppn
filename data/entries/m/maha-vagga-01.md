# Mahā Vagga 01

## English source

Mahā Vagga 01. A section of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayapiṭaka</span>, divided into chapters called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Khandhakas</span>. The introductory chapters give an account of the incidents immediately following the Buddha’s Awakening, leading up to the foundation of the Saṅgha. It then gives various rules for members of the Saṅgha, together with the circumstances which led to the formulation of each rule.

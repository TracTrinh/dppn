# Mahā Diṭṭhena Sutta

## English source

Mahā Diṭṭhena Sutta. The great heresy: that the four elements and weal and woe are stable and permanent, that weal and woe are allotted to each person and do not wax and wane (SN.iii.211f). Some of these views are elsewhere attributed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pakudha Kaccāyana</span> and others to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Makkhali Gosāla</span>.

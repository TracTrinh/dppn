# Medhaṅkara 06

## English source

Medhaṅkara 06. A Burmese author of the fourteenth century. He was the royal preceptor of Queen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddā</span>, mother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Setibhinda</span>, king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muttima</span> *nagara*. He studied for a time in Ceylon and afterwards wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lokadīpasāra</span>. Sās. p. 42; Bode, 35f.

# Mahā Vyaggha Thera

## English source

Mahā Vyaggha Thera. An *Arahant* of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ukkanagara</span> *vihara*. He received a portion of sour millet gruel given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>, and distributed his share among seven hundred monks.

# Manāpāmanāpā Sutta

## English source

Manāpāmanāpā Sutta. Five qualities that make a woman attractive to a man: she is beauteous in form, possessed of wealth, moral, vigorous, and has offspring. Absence of these qualities robs her of this claim. Likewise for a man (SN.iv.238f.).

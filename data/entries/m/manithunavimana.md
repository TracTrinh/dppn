# Maṇithūnavimāna

## English source

Maṇithūnavimāna. A palace in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, the abode of a *Deva* who had been a resident of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span> and cleared a path leading from the forest to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, in order to make it easy for the many monks who went to and fro. (Vv.vii.3; Vv-a.301f.)

# Mahā Datta 04

## English source

Mahā Datta 04. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">senāpati</span>. He was a follower of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brāhmaṇas</span>, and at the moment of his death he saw before him a picture of hell. The Brahmins who were round him asked him what he saw, and he said that he saw a house blood red in colour. They assured him that that was the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Brahma</span> world, but after death he was born in hell. MN­a.ii.803.

# Malānī Sutta

## English source

Malānī Sutta. On eight stains – non-repetition in the case of a mantra, late rising, sluggishness in beauty, carelessness in a guard, misconduct in a woman, etc., stinginess in one who gives, unskilful things stain this world and the next, and ignorance, which is the greatest stain.

# Mañjaripūjaka Thera

## English source

Mañjaripūjaka Thera. An *Arahant*.

Ninety-two *kappas* ago, while walking in the street with a spray of flowers in his hand, he saw the Buddha (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissā</span>) and offered him the flowers.

Seventy-three *kappas* ago he was a king, named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotiya</span>. Ap.i.228.

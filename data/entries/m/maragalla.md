# Māragalla

## English source

Māragalla. A village in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, the birthplace of the minister Buddha (Cv.lv.26). It is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mārapabbata</span> mentioned in the account of the campaigns of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda II</span>. Cv.xlviii.129.

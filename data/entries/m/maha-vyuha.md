# Mahā Vyūha

## English source

Mahā Vyūha. A gabled chamber erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassana</span> into which he could retire during the heat of the day. It was made of silver. DN.ii.182; DN­a.ii.632; see Dial.ii.214, n.1.

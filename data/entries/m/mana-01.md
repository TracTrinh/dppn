# Māna 01

## English source

Māna 01. Youngest brother and viceroy of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi III</span>. (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirisaṅghabodhi</span>). He was governor of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dakkhiṇadesa</span>. He was later found guilty of an offence in the women’s apartments of the palace and was treacherously killed by the court officers. Cv.xliv.84,123f.

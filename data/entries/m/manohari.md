# Manoharī

## English source

Manoharī. A king of Thaton who later became a vassal of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anorata</span> of Burma. It is said that flames issued from his mouth when he spoke, till <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anorata</span> made him eat food taken from a holy shrine. Manoharī, confounded by the loss of his power, sold a gem and erected two great images of the Buddha (Sās.64; Bode, 13, 14, 15).

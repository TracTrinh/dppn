# Migajālena Sutta

## English source

Migajālena Sutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migajāla</span> visits the Buddha and asks what is meant by an *ekavihārī* (lone dweller) and what by a *sadutiyavihārī* (living with a mate). The Buddha answers that he who is enamored of objects of sense is a *sadutiyavihāri*, while the lone dweller is he who has cast off craving. SN.iv.35f.

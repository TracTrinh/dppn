# Mahā Raṭṭha 01

## English source

Mahā Raṭṭha 01. A country where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Dhammarakkhita</span> went after the Third Council. It is generally identified with the country of the Marāthī at the source of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Godhāvarī</span>. Mhv.xii.5, 37; Dpv.viii.8; Vin-a.i.64,67.

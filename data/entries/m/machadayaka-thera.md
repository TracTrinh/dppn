# Machadāyaka Thera

## English source

Machadāyaka Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-four *kappas* ago he was a hawk on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Candabhāgā</span> and once gave a large fish to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Siddhattha Buddha</span>. Ap.i.232.

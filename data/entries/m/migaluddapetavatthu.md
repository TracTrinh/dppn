# Migaluddapetavatthu

## English source

Migaluddapetavatthu. The story of a hunter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span> who, acting on the advice of a holy friend, refrained from hunting at night. He was reborn as a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vemānikapeta</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada</span> came across him in the course of his wanderings and learnt his story. Pv.iii.7; Pv-a.204ff.

# Māyādhanu 01

## English source

Māyādhanu 01. The youngest brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhuvanekabāhu VII</span>. and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha I</span>. He reigned over a part of Ceylon with his capital at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sītāvaka</span> (1521-81 CE.). His reign was marked by a series of severe and fluctuating struggles against his brother and against the Portuguese then in Colombo. He is said to have been succeeded by his son, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha</span>. Cv.xciii.3ff.; Cv.Trs.ii.224, n.1; 225, n.3.

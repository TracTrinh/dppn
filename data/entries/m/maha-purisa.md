# Mahā Purisa

## English source

Mahā Purisa. The name given to a Great Being, destined to become either a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span> or a Buddha.

# Mahallarāja

Mahallarāja. A *padhānaghara* erected by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi III</span>. and his viceroy <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māna</span>.

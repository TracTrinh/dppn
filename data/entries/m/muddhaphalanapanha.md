# Muddhaphālanapañha

## English source

Muddhaphālanapañha. The name given to the questions formulated by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span> (*q.v.*) and given to his disciples to be put to the Buddha. The questions were so named evidently because they were suggested by the curse uttered upon <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bāvarī</span> by the Brahmin, whose request for money he had to refuse. AN­a, i.183.

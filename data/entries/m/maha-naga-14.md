# Mahā Nāga 14

## English source

Mahā Nāga 14. A king of Ceylon, mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammasangaṇi</span> Commentary (Dhs-a.399). While travelling to India from Ceylon he won the favour of an Elder, and on his return became king. Out of gratitude he established gifts of medicine in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Setambaṅgaṇa</span> for as long as he lived. (Dhs-a.399).

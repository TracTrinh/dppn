# Maggadattika Thera

## English source

Maggadattika Thera. An *Arahant*. He once saw <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Atthadassī Buddha</span> wandering about and scattered flowers in his path. Twenty thousand *kappas* ago he was king five times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pupphachadaniya</span>. Ap.i.189.

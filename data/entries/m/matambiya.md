# Māṭambiya

## English source

Māṭambiya. A *padhānaghara* built by the *Damiḷa* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Potthakuṭṭha</span>. He gave for its maintenance the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambavāpi</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Būkakalla</span> and the villages of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tantavāyikacāṭikā</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Niṭṭhilaveṭṭhi</span>, together with slaves. Cv.xlvi.19f.; Cv.Trs.i.100, n.1.

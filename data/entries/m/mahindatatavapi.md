# Mahindataṭavāpi

## English source

Mahindataṭavāpi. A tank built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi I</span>. The image of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> Thera (*q.v.*) was taken there by the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Taracchas</span> and set up on its dyke at the time of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> festival. Cv.xlii.29.

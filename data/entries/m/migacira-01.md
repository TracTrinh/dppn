# Migācira 01

## English source

Migācira 01. A park in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span> (Ja.v.68, 476, 506). This seems to have been an old name for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Isipatana</span>, for it was the scene of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sikhī Buddha</span>’s first sermon (Bv­a. 205), and all Buddhas preach their first sermon in the same place. See Buddha.

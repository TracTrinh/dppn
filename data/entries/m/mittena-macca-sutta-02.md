# Mittena maccā Sutta 02

## English source

Mittena maccā Sutta 02. It is impossible that a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span> should be born in hell, or as an animal or a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Peta</span>. Therefore, all those dear to one should be advised on and established in the four limbs of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpatti</span>. SN.v.365.

# Mittena maccā Sutta 02

## English source

Mittena maccā Sutta 02. It is impossible that a *Sotāpanna* should be born in hell, or as an animal or a *Peta*. Therefore, all those dear to one should be advised on and established in the four limbs of *Sotāpatti*. SN.v.365.

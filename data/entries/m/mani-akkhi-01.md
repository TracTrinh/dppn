# Maṇi-akkhi 01

## English source

Maṇi-akkhi 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇi­akkhika</span>. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span>, maternal uncle of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahodara</span>.

He came to take part in the battle between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahodara</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷodara</span>, and having heard the Buddha preach on that occasion, begged him to visit his kingdom. The Buddha agreed, and, three years later went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span> in the eighth year after the Awakening on the full moon day of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vesākha</span>. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> entertained him and his five hundred monks on the spot where, later, the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kalyāṇi</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t18">cetiya</span> was built. (Mhv.i.63ff.; xv.162; Dpv.ii.42, 52; it is said that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇi-akkhika</span> went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambudīpa</span> to bring the Buddha to Ceylon (Mhv­ṭ.111). According to the Extended <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> (i.700) he was the uncle (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="15" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mātula</span>) of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="16" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahodara</span>.

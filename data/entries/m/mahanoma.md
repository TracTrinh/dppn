# Mahānoma

## English source

Mahānoma. The name of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana Buddha</span>. The capital, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vaḍḍhamāna</span>, lay to the south of it, and the park was given by King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samiddha</span> to the Buddha. Mhv.xv. 92,107ff.

# Mahā Nāga 13

## English source

Mahā Nāga 13. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāga Thera</span>. He once went to his mother’s house for alms and while sitting there entered into *jhāna*. The house caught fire and all the others fled. When the fire was put out the Thera was discovered unhurt, and the villagers did him great honour. Finding his attainments discovered, he rose into the air and went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Piyaṅgudīpa</span>. Vsm.706.

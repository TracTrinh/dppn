# Mahā Paduma 05

## English source

Mahā Paduma 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Paduma Thera</span>. Preacher of Jātakas (*Jātakabhāṇaka*). When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Iḷanāga</span> was in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>, after fleeing from the capital, he heard the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapijātaka</span> (Ja 250) from Mahā Paduma, who lived in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tulādhāra</span> *vihāra*, and was greatly pleased. Mhv.xxxv.30.

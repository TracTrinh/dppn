# Māyādhanu 02

## English source

Māyādhanu 02. The name of a district in Ceylon, between the coast and the mountains. Its capital was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sītāvaka</span>, founded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu IV</span>. There was in it a village called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udakagāma</span>. Cv.xc. 100; c. 213; Cv.Trs.ii.209, n.8.

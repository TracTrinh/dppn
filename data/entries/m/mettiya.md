# Mettiyā

## English source

Mettiyā. A nun who, at the instigation of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mettiyabhummajakā</span>, charged <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dabba Mallaputta</span> with having violated her chastity. She was expelled from the Saṅgha for this offence.

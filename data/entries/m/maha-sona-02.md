# Mahā Soṇa 02

## English source

Mahā Soṇa 02. One of the ten chief warriors of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Duṭṭhagāmaṇī</span>. He was born in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Hunadarīvāpī</span> in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kuḷumbarī</span> district as the eighth son of a man named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span>. He was as strong as ten elephants, and took a prominent part in the attack on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijitapura</span>. Mhv.xxiii.2, 46ff.; xxv. 27f.; 44f. See also Ras.ii.86f.

# Majjhapallivihāra

## English source

Majjhapallivihāra. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span> in Ceylon restored by the monk <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅgharakkhita</span> in the reign of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span>. The king showed the monk great honour and gave the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mālāgāma</span> for the maintenance of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>. Cv.c.234. (Sinhalese <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Medapola</span>.)

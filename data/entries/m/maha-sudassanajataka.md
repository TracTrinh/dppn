# Mahā Sudassanajātaka

## English source

Mahā Sudassanajātaka (Ja 95). Relates the story of the death of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassana</span>. For the rest of the king’s story, reference is suggested to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassanasutta</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sudassana</span>’s queen is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Subhaddā</span> and is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāhulamātā</span>. Ja.i.391-3.

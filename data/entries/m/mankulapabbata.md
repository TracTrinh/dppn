# Maṅkulapabbata

## English source

Maṅkulapabbata. A locality where the Buddha spent his sixth *vassa* (Bv­a.3). The reference is perhaps to the Maṅkulakārāma (*q.v.*), but there the Buddha is said to have stayed only seven days of the rainy season.

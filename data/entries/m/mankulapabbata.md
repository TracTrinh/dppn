# Maṅkulapabbata

## English source

Maṅkulapabbata. A locality where the Buddha spent his sixth <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vassa</span> (Bv­a.3). The reference is perhaps to the Maṅkulakārāma (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>), but there the Buddha is said to have stayed only seven days of the rainy season.

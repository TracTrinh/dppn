# Moriya

## English source

Moriya. A very pious Brahmin of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Macala</span>. He and his wife <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senā</span> gave alms till all their wealth was exhausted, but a deity gave him wealth again. Ras.i.86f.

# Mandāra

## English source

Mandāra. A mountain in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>, mentioned together with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meru</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Daddara</span>. Ap.ii.536, 86; according to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhānappadīpikā</span> vs.606, it is the western mountain, behind which the sun sets.

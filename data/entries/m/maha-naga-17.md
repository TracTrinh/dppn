# Mahā Nāga 17

## English source

Mahā Nāga 17. A monk of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kontaraṭṭhakapabbata</span> *vihāra*. He died seated in mid­air, and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kākavaṇṇatissa</span>, having heard of it from a crow, paid him great honour. Ras.ii.64.

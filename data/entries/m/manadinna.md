# Mānadinna

## English source

Mānadinna. A householder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājagaha</span>. When he lay ill he was visited by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ānanda</span>, to whom he confessed that even in his illness he practiced the four *satipaṭṭhāna*. He was quite free from the five *orambhāgiyasaṁyojanā*. SN.v.178.

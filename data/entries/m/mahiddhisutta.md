# Mahiddhisutta

## English source

Mahiddhisutta, or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Samaṇabrāhmaṇasutta</span>. All recluses or Brahmins possessed of *iddhi* power, whether of the past, present or future, must obtain it through the development of the four *satipaṭṭhāna*. SN.v.273f.

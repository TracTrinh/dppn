# Mahā Bodhivaṁsa

## English source

Mahā Bodhivaṁsa. A Pāli translation of a Sinhalese original, giving the history of the arrival of the Bodhi-tree in Ceylon. It was written about the tenth century and is ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Upatissa</span> (Svd.vs.1262), who wrote it at the request of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dāṭhā Nāga</span>.

<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saraṇaṅkara</span> *Saṅgharāja* wrote a paraphrase on it, the *Madhurārthaprakāśinī*. For details see PLC.156ff.

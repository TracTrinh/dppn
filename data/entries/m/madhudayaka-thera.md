# Madhudāyaka Thera

## English source

Madhudāyaka Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Once in the past he was a hermit living on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sindhu</span>, and, having seen <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha Buddha</span>, he gave him some honey. It is said that on the day of his birth there was a shower of honey (Ap.i.325). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Nāma Thera</span>. Thag­a.i.228.

# Majjhantika

## English source

Majjhantika or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sanikasutta</span>. Once a monk dwelt in a forest tract in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> and was told by a *Deva* of the forest how the noonday silence frightened him. But the monk replied that to him it was enchanting. SN.i.203.

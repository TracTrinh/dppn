# Manosattā

## English source

Manosattā. A class of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span>. Beings who die devoted to some idea are born in their world – e.g., a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nigaṇṭha</span> who will take only warm water and would rather die than take it cold (MN.i.376; MN­a.ii.597).

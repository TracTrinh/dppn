# Mātuputtikasutta

## English source

Mātuputtikasutta. Once a mother and her son – a monk and a nun – spent the rainy season at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>. They saw much of each other and, in course of time, became guilty of incest. When this was reported to the Buddha, he declared that nothing ensnares the heart of a man as does a woman; she is indeed a noose of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span>. AN.iii.67f.

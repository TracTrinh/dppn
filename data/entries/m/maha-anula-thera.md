# Mahā Anula Thera

## English source

Mahā Anula Thera. He once saw a number of monks, who had obtained only dry food, eating it on the banks of a river. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anula</span> resolved that the river water should turn into butter cream and made a sign to the novices, who then took the cream in cups and gave it to the monks. Vsm.404.

# Mahā Aṅgana

## English source

Mahā Aṅgana. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> through which the *sīmā* of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> passed; it was so-called because of a large *aṅgaṇa*-tree which stood there. Mbv.135,136.

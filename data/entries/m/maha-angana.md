# Mahā Aṅgana

## English source

Mahā Aṅgana. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> through which the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sīmā</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> passed; it was so-called because of a large <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">aṅgaṇa</span>-tree which stood there.

# Mucelapaṭṭana

## English source

Mucelapaṭṭana. Perhaps a place in Ceylon, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vohārikatissa</span> instituted alms (Mhv.xxxvi.30). The Mhv­ṭ. p.661f, however, says that Mucelapaṭṭana was a metal boat in which various gifts were kept for distribution among the monks.

# Mahinda 11

## English source

Mahinda 11. Younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena I</span>. and his viceroy. He quelled the rising of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya</span> against the king, his brother. When the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍu</span> king invaded Ceylon, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> led an army against him, and, on the defeat of his forces, he cut his own throat. Cv.l.6, 10, 21ff.

# Mahā Mucalamālaka

## English source

Mahā Mucalamālaka. A locality in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span>, where stood the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">uposatha</span> hall for monks (Mhv.xv.36). It was outside the enclosure of the Bodhi-tree. Mhv­ṭ. 346.

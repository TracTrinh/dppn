# Mahā Kāḷa 04

## English source

Mahā Kāḷa 04. A householder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bandhumatī</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>. He was a previous birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aññā Koṇḍañña</span>. He and his brother <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Kāḷa</span> gave the first fruits of their harvest, in nine stages of its growth, to the Buddha. AN­a.i.79ff.; Thag­a.ii.1f.

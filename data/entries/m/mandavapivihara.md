# Maṇḍavāpivihāra

Maṇḍavāpivihāra. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">A monastery built by</span> Mahā Cullitissa (Mhv.xxxiv.8). <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Dāṭhika Mahā Nāga</span> gave land for the monks of this *vihāra* out of gratitude to a *Sāmaṇera* who lived there (Mhv.xxxiv.93).

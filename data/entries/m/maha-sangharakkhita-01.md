# Mahā Saṅgharakkhita 01

## English source

Mahā Saṅgharakkhita 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Saṅgharakkhita Thera</span>. An *Arahant*.

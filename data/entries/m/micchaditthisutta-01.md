# Micchādiṭṭhisutta 01

## English source

Micchādiṭṭhisutta 01. Wrong view is abandoned by realizing that eye, objects, seeing, etc., are all impermanent. SN.iv.147.

## Vietnamese translation

Chưa dịch.

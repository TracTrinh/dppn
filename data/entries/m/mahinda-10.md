# Mahinda 10

## English source

Mahinda 10. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda III</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggabodhi IX</span>. came to the throne, contrary to the laws of succession, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda</span> fled to India (Cv.xlix.84f). He was afterwards slain by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena I</span>. (Cv.l.4).

## English source

Mārasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rādha</span> asks the Buddha as to what is meant by “*Māra*”. Anything that perishes, says the Buddha, such as body, feeling, perceptions, etc. SN.iii.188.

# Mahā Sīva 03

## English source

Mahā Sīva 03. A famous Commentator, sometimes called *Dīghabhāṇaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sīva</span>.

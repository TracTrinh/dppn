# Maghavapupphiya Thera

## English source

Maghavapupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. Ninety-one *kappas* ago he saw the Buddha (? <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī</span>) seated in meditation on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nammadā</span>, and honoured him by placing a *maghava*-flower near him. Ap.i.240f.

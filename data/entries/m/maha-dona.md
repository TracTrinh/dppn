# Mahā Doṇa

## English source

Mahā Doṇa. A <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nāga</span> king of the city of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Doṇa</span> on the bank of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gaṅgārahada</span>. He used to destroy the districts of those who did not pay him tribute. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nārada Buddha</span> preached to him and vanquished him. Bv.x.7; Bv­a.153.

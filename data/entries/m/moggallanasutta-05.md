# Moggallānasutta 05

## English source

Moggallānasutta 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Moggallāna</span> wonders how many <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> have become *Sotāpannas* and are assured of *Nibbāna*. In order to discover this, he visits <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> *Brahma*, who had once been a monk. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa</span> welcomes him and tells him that only those <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devas</span> who have faith in the Buddha, the Dhamma, and the Saṅgha have such assurance. AN.iii.331f.

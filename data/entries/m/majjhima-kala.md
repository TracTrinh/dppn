# Majjhima Kāḷa

## English source

Majjhima Kāḷa. The second of three brothers, all named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷa</span>, of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Setavyā</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Kāḷa</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Kāḷa</span>, the youngest and the oldest, used to travel about with caravans and procure goods, which were sold for them by Majjhima Kāḷa.

He had four crores. Dhp­a.i.66, 73.

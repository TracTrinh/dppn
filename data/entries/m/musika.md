# Mūsikā

## English source

Mūsikā. A slave woman of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yava</span> (see the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūsikajātaka</span>, Ja 373). One day, on going to prepare the king’s bath, she saw his son, sword in hand, waiting to kill him. When the prince found he was discovered, he cut Mūsikā in two and threw her into the lake. Ja.iii.217.

# Milakkhā

## English source

Milakkhā (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Milakkhukā</span>). The name given to the people of non-Ariyan origin, the *Mlecchas*, e.g., DN.iii.264; AN.i.35, etc. Their language is called *Milakkhabhāsā*.

# Mahā Purisasutta

## English source

Mahā Purisasutta. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> asks the Buddha who is a “<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">mahāpurisa</span>.” The Buddha answers that it is one who has won emancipation of mind, which can be attained by practising the four <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">satipaṭṭhāna</span>s. SN.v.158.

# Migājina

## English source

Migājina. An ascetic in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>. When <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Janaka</span> renounced the world he was followed by a great crowd, and there was danger that he might be turned from his noble purpose. Migājina, who had just risen from an ecstatic jhāna, saw this and, appearing before him, exhorted him to be earnest and determined. (Ja.vi.58ff) <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migājina</span> is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Moggallāna</span>. Ja.vi.68.

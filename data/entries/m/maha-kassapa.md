# Mahā Kassapa

## English source

Mahā Kassapa. An eminent Thera of Ceylon, incumbent of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udumbaragiri</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>, who, as the most senior monk, was in charge of the reform of the Saṅgha carried out by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. Cv.lxxviii.6, 16, 57; Cv.Trs.ii.102, n.2.

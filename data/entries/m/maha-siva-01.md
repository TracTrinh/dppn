# Mahā Sīva 01

## English source

Mahā Sīva 01. King of Ceylon; he was the son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Muṭasīva</span> and the younger brother of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttiya</span>, whom he succeeded. He reigned for ten years (197-87 B.C.) and built the *Nagaraṅgaṇavihāra* for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhaddasāla</span> Thera. He was succeeded by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sūratissa</span>. Mhv.xxi.1ff.

# Meghalatā

## English source

Meghalatā. Among the decorations of the Relic Chamber of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>, are mentioned “*Meghalatā vijjukumārī*,” which is explained in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā</span> (p. 549) as “*Meghalatā nāma vijjukumāriyo*.”

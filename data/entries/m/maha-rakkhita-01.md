# Mahā Rakkhita 01

## English source

Mahā Rakkhita 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Rakkhita Thera</span>. He went after the Third Council to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yona</span> country, and there preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāḷakārāmasutta</span> One hundred and seventy thousand people adopted the Buddha’s faith and ten thousand entered the Saṅgha. Mhv.xii.5, 39; Dpv.viii.9; Vin-a.i.64, 67.

# Maṇimekhalapāsāda

## English source

Maṇimekhalapāsāda. A monastic building in Ceylon, probably belonging to the Mahāyānists. It held statues of the Bodhisattas, which were restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sena II</span>. (Cv.li.77.)

# Meghavaṇṇābhaya

## English source

Meghavaṇṇābhaya 03. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Meghavaṇṇābhayavihāra</span>.

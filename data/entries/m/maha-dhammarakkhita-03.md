# Mahā Dhammarakkhita 03

## English source

Mahā Dhammarakkhita 03. An Elder of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tulādhāra</span> *vihāra* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rohaṇa</span>. *Tipiṭaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷābhaya</span> went to him from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span> in order to learn from him the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Dhamma</span>. At the end of the teaching, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammarakkhita</span> asked <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> to give him a subject for meditation, for, he said, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> had a greater knowledge than he of that matter. The subject was given, and soon after <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammarakkhita</span> attained *Nibbāna*, and died while preaching at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Lohapāsāda</span>. Vsm.96f.

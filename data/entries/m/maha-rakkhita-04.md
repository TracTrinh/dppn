# Mahā Rakkhita 04

## English source

Mahā Rakkhita 04. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Rakkhita Thera</span>. When told by his patron that the latter had given a robe to a certain monk, he praised him; when the man offered to give him one, he praised that likewise. MN­a.ii.666.

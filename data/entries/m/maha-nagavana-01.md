# Mahā Nāgavana 01

## English source

Mahā Nāgavana 01. An open space in Ceylon, on the banks of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vālukagaṅgā</span>. It was three *yojanas* long and one wide and was the meeting place of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yakkhas</span>. The Buddha went there on his first visit to Ceylon, and in it was later built the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahiyaṅgaṇa Thūpa</span>. Mhv.i.22f.

# Migācira 02

## English source

Migācira 02. A park near <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Indapatta</span>, which once belonged to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhanañjaya Korabba</span> (Ja.vi.256). It existed even in the time of the Buddha, for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Raṭṭhapāla</span> Thera is mentioned as having stayed there. MN.ii.65; MN­a.ii.725, 730; but Thag­a.ii.34 calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Migājina</span>.

# Mahā Sākyamunigotamasutta

## English source

Mahā Sākyamunigotamasutta. The Buddha describes how, before reaching <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, he traced back, step by step, the cause of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">suffering</span> in the world and the Way of escape there from. SN.ii.10f.

# Mahā Tissa 15

## English source

Mahā Tissa 15. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa Thera</span>. Even when sixty years old he did not realize that he was a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">puthujjana</span> Then, one day, his pupil <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadinna</span> of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Talaṅgara</span> came to him, asking for his company in a journey to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tissa Mahā Vihāra</span>, where he had been asked to preach. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span> greeted him, and in the course of conversation, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadinna</span> discovered that his teacher was under the false impression that he was an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Wishing to show him his error, he persuaded <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="9" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span>, by his <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="10" data-dppn-term-class="t18">iddhi</span> power, to create a pond, and in the pond a lotus, which a young girl was picking. At the sight of the girl, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="11" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span> was possessed by lust, and realized that he was no <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="12" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="13" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammadinna</span> withdrew, and that same day <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="14" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Tissa</span> put forth effort and attained <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="15" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship. AN­a.i.25.

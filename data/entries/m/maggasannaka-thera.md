# Maggasaññaka Thera

## English source

Maggasaññaka Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> he was a *Devaputta* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himavā</span>, and, coming across some monks who had lost their way in the forest, he entertained them and set them on the right road. One hundred and five *kappas* ago he was king twelve times under the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sacakkhu</span> (Ap.i.152f). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ekadhammasavaniya</span>. Thag­a.i.151f.

# Mahā Patāpa 01

## English source

Mahā Patāpa 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Patāpana</span>. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Benares</span>, father of the Bodhisatta in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷa Dhammapālajātaka</span> (Ja 358, *q.v.*). He is identified with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devadatta</span>. He was swallowed up by the earth. Ja.iii.182. Dhp­a.i.129.

# Methunasutta

## English source

Methunasutta. Preached to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jāṇussoṇī</span> in answer to a question as to what constitutes <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">brahmacariyā</span> (chastity). There are seven “sex-bonds” mentioned, subjection to any of which is violation of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">brahmacariyā</span>. The Buddha claims that he has destroyed them all. AN.iv.54f.

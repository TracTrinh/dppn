# Mahā Tittha 02

## English source

Mahā Tittha 02. The name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Meghavana</span> in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. It was presented to the Buddha by king <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span>, who planted in it a branch of the Bodhi-tree brought by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rucānandā</span>. Mhv.xv. 58, 73f., 83.

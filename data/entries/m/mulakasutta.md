# Mūlakasutta

## English source

Mūlakasutta. The Buddha tells the monks that, should they be questioned by followers of other religions, they should answer that all things have desire (*chanda*) as their root. They originate in attention, they are caused by contact, their confluence is feeling, concentration is their chief state of all things, emancipation is the most precious. AN.v.106f. = AN.iv.338, where it is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mūlasutta</span>.

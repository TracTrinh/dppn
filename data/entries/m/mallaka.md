# Mallaka

## English source

Mallaka. An Elder. As he stood one day looking at a ploughed field a sign arose in him of the size of the field. He enlarged it, induced the five *jhānas*, and, developing insight, became an *Arahant* (Vsm.123). He is mentioned as consulting *Dīghabhāṇaka* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> on some problem connected with *jhāna*. Vsm.265f.

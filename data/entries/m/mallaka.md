# Mallaka

## English source

Mallaka. An Elder. As he stood one day looking at a ploughed field a sign arose in him of the size of the field. He enlarged it, induced the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhānas</span>, and, developing insight, became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> (Vsm.123). He is mentioned as consulting <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Dīghabhāṇaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span> on some problem connected with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jhāna</span>. Vsm.265f.

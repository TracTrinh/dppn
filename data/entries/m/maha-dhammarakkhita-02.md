# Mahā Dhammarakkhita 02

## English source

Mahā Dhammarakkhita 02. An ancient Commentator, generally called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Tipiṭaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Dhammarakkhita</span>, who is quoted several times in the Commentaries. He was a contemporary of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Dīghabhāṇaka</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhaya</span>, e.g., Dhs-a.267, 278, 286f.; Vibh-a.81; Paṭis-a.405.

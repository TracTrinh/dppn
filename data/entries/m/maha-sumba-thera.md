# Mahā Sumba Thera

## English source

Mahā Sumba Thera. A disciple of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṇāgamana Buddha</span>; he came to Ceylon at the Buddha’s request. He, with one thousand others, was left behind to look after the new converts. Mhv.xv.123.

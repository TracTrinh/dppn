# Mahā Sena 04

## English source

Mahā Sena 04. A king of India who ruled in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pāṭaliputta</span>. He fed one thousand monks daily; but, not satisfied with that, he went to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttaramadhurā</span>, where he labored in disguise, giving alms with the wages so earned. Cv.xcii.23ff.

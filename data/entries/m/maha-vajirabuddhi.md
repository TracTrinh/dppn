# Mahā Vajirabuddhi

## English source

Mahā Vajirabuddhi. A monk of Ceylon, author of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayagaṇṭhi</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vinayagandhi</span>) or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vajirabuddhiṭīkā</span> on the Vinaya Commentaries (GV. 60, 66). He was a contemporary of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammaceti</span> of Burma, and presented him with a copy of his work. Bode, 39f.

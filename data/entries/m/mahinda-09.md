# Mahinda 09

## English source

Mahinda 09. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Udaya I</span>.; he was, however, known by the name of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhammikasilāmegha</span> and was a very pious man. He gave the income from the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Geṭṭhumba</span> Canal to be used in repairs of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ratanapāsāda</span>. He became king as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda III</span>. and reigned for four years (797-801 CE.). Cv.xlix.38ff.

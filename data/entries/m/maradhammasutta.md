# Māradhammasutta

## English source

Māradhammasutta. The Buddha admonishes <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rādha</span> and says that desire for whatever is perishable, such as the body, etc., must be put away. SN.iii.195, 198, 200.

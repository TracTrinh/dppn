# Orambhāgiyasutta 01

## English source

Orambhāgiyasutta 01. The five fetters concerned with the lower stages of existence: *sakkāyadiṭṭhi*, *vicikicchā*, etc. They could be destroyed by developing the Noble Eightfold Way. SN.v.61.

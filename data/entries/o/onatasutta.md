# Oṇatasutta

## English source

Oṇatasutta. On four classes of people in the world: the low and low, the high and high, the high and low, and the low and high (AN.ii.86; also found at Pug.52 and Pug.7).

The Commentary explains that each person is such and such but may, or will, become such and such.

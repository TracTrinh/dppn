# Orimasutta

## English source

Orimasutta. On the hither and the further shores – e.g., false belief (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">micchādiṭṭhi</span>) – is the hither shore and its opposite (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">sammādiṭṭhi</span>), the further shore. AN.v.233.

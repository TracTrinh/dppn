# Jālinī 02

## English source

Jālinī 02. A goddess of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, a former wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span>. Once seeing him old and feeble, she appeared before him in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span> and bade him aspire to rebirth among the gods. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anuruddha</span> told her there would be no rebirth for him. SN.i.200; Thag.vs.908; Thag­a.ii.73; SN­a.i.226.

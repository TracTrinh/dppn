# Jotipāsāṇa

## English source

Jotipāsāṇa. The name given to the crystals brought from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Uttarakuru</span> by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotika</span>’s wife. When anything requiring cooking was placed on them they gleamed hot, and went out of themselves when the cooking was complete. Dhp­a.iv.209; DN­a.iii.965.

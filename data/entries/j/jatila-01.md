# Jaṭila 01

## English source

Jaṭila 01. A class of ascetics, so-called on account of their matted hair (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jaṭilā ti tāpasā, te hi jaṭādhāritāya idha jaṭilā ti vuttā</span>) (Ud­a.74; see also 330). These ascetics are sometimes classed under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Isi</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Cūḷa Niddesa</span>.149) and also under <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">muni</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Cūḷa Niddesa</span>.513).

# Jaṭila 01

## English source

Jaṭila 01. A class of ascetics, so-called on account of their matted hair (*jaṭilā ti tāpasā, te hi jaṭādhāritāya idha jaṭilā ti vuttā*) (Ud­a.74; see also 330). These ascetics are sometimes classed under *Isi* (*Cūḷa Niddesa*.149) and also under *muni* (*Cūḷa Niddesa*.513).

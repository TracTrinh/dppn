# Jātipupphiya Thera

## English source

Jātipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In a previous birth he placed a bouquet of flowers on the dead body of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span>. As a result he was born among the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nimmānaratī</span> gods. Ap.i.405f.

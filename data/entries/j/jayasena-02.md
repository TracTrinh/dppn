# Jayasena 02

## English source

Jayasena 02. Father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Phussa Buddha</span> (Bv.xix.14; Ja.i.41). The Buddha preached to him and he became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span> (Bv­a.193). He was king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kāsī</span> and his wife was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirimā</span> (Pv-a.19). See also <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tirokuḍḍapetavatthu</span>.

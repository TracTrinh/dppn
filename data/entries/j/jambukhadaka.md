# Jambukhādaka

## English source

Jambukhādaka. A *Paribbājaka*. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṁyuttanikāya</span> records visits paid by him to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nālakagāma</span> and discussions between them on various topics, such as *Nibbāna*, *Arahant*-ship, the *āsavas*, *sakkāya*, ignorance, the Noble Eightfold Path, etc. (SN.iv.251-60)

The Commentary (SN­a.iii.91) says that he was a nephew of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāriputta</span> and a *channaparibbājaka*.

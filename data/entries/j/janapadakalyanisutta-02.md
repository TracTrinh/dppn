# Janapadakaḷyānīsutta 02

## English source

Janapadakaḷyānīsutta 02. Not even a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">janapadakaḷyānī</span> (a city belle) can continuously possess the heart of a man whose mind is won over by gains, favours and flattery. SN.ii.233.

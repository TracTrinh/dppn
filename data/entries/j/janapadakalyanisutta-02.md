# Janapadakaḷyānīsutta 02

## English source

Janapadakaḷyānīsutta 02. Not even a *janapadakaḷyānī* (a city belle) can continuously possess the heart of a man whose mind is won over by gains, favours and flattery. SN.ii.233.

# Jeṭṭhārāma

The villages of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pattapāsāṇa</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhabhelagāma</span> were given for its maintenance and one hundred attendants were provided for its service.

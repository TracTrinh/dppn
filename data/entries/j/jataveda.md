# Jātaveda

## English source

Jātaveda. The god of fire. The Jātakas contain references to his worship (e.g., Ja.i.214, 494; iii.17; v.452; vi.201, etc.). See <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggi</span>. He is also called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aggideva</span>.

# Jāṇussoṇīsutta 01

## English source

Jāṇussoṇīsutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jāṇussoṇī</span> visits the Buddha and tells him that if anyone has gifts to distribute he should give them to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tevijja</span> Brahmins. At the Buddha’s request, he describes these Brahmins, and the Buddha, in his turn, tells him what is considered the threefold-lore (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">tevijjā</span>) by the Ariyans. AN.i.166.

# Jāgarasutta

## English source

Jāgarasutta. A riddle set by a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> and answered by the Buddha, regarding the Five Spiritual Powers (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bala</span>) which respectively soil or cleanse, according to the spiritual health of the individual SN.i.3).

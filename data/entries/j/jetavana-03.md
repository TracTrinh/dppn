# Jetavana 03

## English source

Jetavana 03. A monastery in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>, built by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. It included the building which housed the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tivaṅka</span> image (Cv.lxxviii.32, 47). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nammadā</span> Canal flowed through the grounds of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jetavana</span>. Ibid., lxxix.48. See also Cv.Trs.ii.105, n.5.

## Vietnamese translation

Jetavana 03. Một tu viện tại Pulatthipura, do Parakkamabāhu I. xây dựng. Tu viện bao gồm công trình lưu giữ pho tượng Tivaṅka (Cv.lxxviii.32, 47). Kênh Nammadā chảy qua khuôn viên Jetavana. Ibid., lxxix.48. Xem thêm Cv.Trs.ii.105, n.5.

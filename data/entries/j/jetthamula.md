# Jeṭṭhamūla

## English source

Jeṭṭhamūla. Name of a month (May-June). It came in the hot season (e.g., Ja.v.412). On the fifth day of the waxing moon in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jeṭṭhamūla</span> the Buddha’s relics were divided (DN­a.i.6). On the full-moon day of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jeṭṭhamūla</span> the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aruṇavatīsutta</span> was preached. AN­a.i.438.

# Jaṭāsutta

## English source

Jaṭāsutta. A *Deva* asks the Buddha how sentient beings can escape from their tangles. By the destruction of *rāga*, *dosa*, *moha*, answers the Buddha.

This *Sutta* forms the basis of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Visuddhimagga</span>. SN.i.13; repeated at i.165.

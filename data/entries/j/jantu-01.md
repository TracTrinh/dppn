# Jantu 01

## English source

Jantu 01. A *Devaputta*. He saw a number of monks in a forest-lodge on the slopes of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span>, muddled in mind, loose of speech and heedless. He appeared before them on an *uposatha* day and reminded them of their duties. SN.i.61f.

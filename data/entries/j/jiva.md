# Jīvā

## English source

Jīvā. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jīvanti</span>. Daughter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ubbirī</span> and the king of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kosala</span>. When she died, it was her death which made <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ubbirī</span> attain to *Arahant*-ship. Thīg.vs.51; Thīg­a.53f.

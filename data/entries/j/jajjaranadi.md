# Jajjaranadī

## English source

Jajjaranadī. A river in Ceylon, the present Deduru-Oya. On the river was the famous causeway known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭṭhabaddha</span>, restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lxviii.16), who also built a dam across the river at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dorādattika</span>. Ibid., vs.37; see also lxxix.67.

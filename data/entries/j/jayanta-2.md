# Jayanta

## English source

Jayanta. King of Ceylon (then known as <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṇḍadīpa</span>) at the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa Buddha</span>. His capital was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Visāla</span>. It was a devastating war between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jayanta</span> and his younger brother which brought <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa</span> to Ceylon. Mhv.xv.127ff; Dpv.xv.60; xvii.7; Vin-a.i.87, etc.

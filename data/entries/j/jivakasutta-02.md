# Jīvakasutta 02

## English source

Jīvakasutta 02. Questioned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jīvaka</span>, the Buddha explains that an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">upāsaka</span> is one who has taken the Three Refuges and the Five Precepts, and that such a man, by reason of his qualities, works the welfare both of himself and others. AN.iv.222f.

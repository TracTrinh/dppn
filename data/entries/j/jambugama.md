# Jambugāma

## English source

Jambugāma. A village, probably a suburb of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Campā</span> (see below), which the Buddha visited during his last tour. It lay between <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambagāma</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhoganagara</span>. DN.ii.194.

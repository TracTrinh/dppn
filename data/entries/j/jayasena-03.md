# Jayasena 03

## English source

Jayasena 03. King of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kapilavatthu</span>. His son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhahanu</span> and his daughter <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Yasodharā</span>. His grandson was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suddhodana</span>. Mhv.ii.15.ff; Dpv.iii.44; Mhv­ṭ.134; but see Mvu.i.352, where he is called Hastikaśīrṣa. The Tibetan sources call him Dhanvadurga (Rockhill, p.13.)

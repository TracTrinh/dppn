# Jotiya 03

## English source

Jotiya 03. A *Nigaṇṭha*, for whom <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍukābhaya</span> built a house to the east of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nīcasusāna</span> at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> (Mhv.x.97). The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā</span> (p.296) calls him a *Nagaravaḍḍhakī*. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhayagirivihāra</span> was later erected on the spot occupied by Jotiya’s residence. Mhv­ṭ.620.

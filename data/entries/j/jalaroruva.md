# Jālaroruva

## English source

Jālaroruva. A *Niraya*, one of the divisions of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Roruva</span>, the other being <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dhūmaroruva</span>. It is filled with blood-red flowers, which enter the body of its inhabitants through the nine openings. Ja.v.271.

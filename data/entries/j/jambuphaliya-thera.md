# Jambuphaliya Thera

## English source

Jambuphaliya Thera. An <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. He once gave <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Padumuttara Buddha</span> the first fruits of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">jambu</span>-tree (Ap.ii.395). He is probably identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nadī Kassapa</span>. Thag­a.i.415.

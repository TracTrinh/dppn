# Jinālaṅkāra

## English source

Jinālaṅkāra. A Pāli poem of two hundred and fifty verses, containing a history of the Buddha’s life.

Its authorship is uncertain; some attribute it to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhadatta</span>, author of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhuratthavilāsinī</span>, others to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddharakkhita</span>.

There exists a *Ṭīkā* on it. Gv.69, 72; see PLC.110f.

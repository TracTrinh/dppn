# Jambāvatī

## English source

Jambāvatī. A *caṇḍālī*, mother of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sivi</span> and wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāsudeva</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaṇhāyanagotta</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vāsudeva</span> saw her on his way to the park from <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dvāravatī</span>, and, in spite of her birth, married her and made her his chief queen. Ja.vi.421.

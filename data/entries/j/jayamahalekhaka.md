# Jayamahālekhaka

## English source

Jayamahālekhaka. A rank conferred by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Devānampiyatissa</span> on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumitta</span>, who accompanied the Sacred Bodhi-tree to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Anurādhapura</span> (Mbv.165). The rank was evidently held by his descendants in perpetuity, e.g., Cv.lxix.12.

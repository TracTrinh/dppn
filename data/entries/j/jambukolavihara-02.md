# Jambukolavihāra 02

## English source

Jambukolavihāra 02. Another <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>, with its celebrated rock-temple called the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jambukolalena</span>, in the centre of Ceylon, twenty-six miles north of modern Matale. It was restored by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vijayabāhu I</span>. (Cv.lx.60) and rebuilt by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittinissaṅka</span>, who placed in it seventy-three golden statues of the Buddha. Cv.lxxx.23; see also Cv.Trs.ii.128, n.3.

# Jotipāla 04

## English source

Jotipāla 04. A monk at whose request <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> wrote the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāraṭṭhappakāsinī</span> and the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Manoraṭṭhapūraṇī</span>. He seems to have been a colleague of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhaghosa</span> and lived with him in several places, including <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kañcīpura</span>. Gv.68; SN­a.iii.235; AN­a.ii.874.

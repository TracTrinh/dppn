# Romamukkharaṭṭha

## English source

Romamukkharaṭṭha. A country mentioned in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vaṁsa</span> Commentary as a place rich in coral; it was from there that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bhātikābhaya</span> (*q.v.*) obtained the coral for the net which he threw over the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Thūpa</span>. Mhv­ṭ. 630.

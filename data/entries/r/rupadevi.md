# Rūpadevī

## English source

Rūpadevī. A pious woman of *Devaputta*. Because of alms given in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span>, she got food whenever she desired. One day <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Saṅgharakkhita</span>, who visited her house, revealed to her the reason for this and she became a *Sotāpanna*. Ras.i.24f.

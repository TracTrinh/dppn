# Rādhasutta 01

## English source

Rādhasutta 01. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rādha</span> asks the Buddha if ideas of “I” and “mine” are completely absent in him who knows and sees, regarding the body, consciousness and external objects. The Buddha answers in the affirmative. SN.iii.79.

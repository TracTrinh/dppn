# Ratosutta

## English source

Ratosutta. A monk who keeps guard over the door of his faculties, is moderate in eating and given to watchfulness, dwells in happiness in this life and has strong help in the destruction of the *āsavas*. The *Sutta* gives details of these faculties. SN.iv.175ff.

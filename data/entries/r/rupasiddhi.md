# Rūpasiddhi

## English source

Rūpasiddhi. A Pāli grammar by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhappiya</span> (or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpaṅkara</span>) Thera (*q.v.*). It is based on <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kaccāyana</span>’s grammar, in its general outlines, and its full name is *Padarūpasiddhi*. There is a *ṭīkā* on it ascribed to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhappiya</span> himself. PLC., p. 220f.

# Ramma 02

## English source

Ramma 02. Son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paduma Buddha</span> in his last lay life. He joined the Saṅgha and later became an <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>. Eighty crores of beings realized the Truth when the Buddha preached to him. Bv.ix.5,18; Bv­a.147.

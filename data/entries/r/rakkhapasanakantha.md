# Rakkhapāsāṇakaṇṭha

## English source

Rakkhapāsāṇakaṇṭha. A place in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājaraṭṭha</span>. It was the limit of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Coḷa</span> territory in Ceylon in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahinda V</span>. (Cv.lv.22). It was evidently a frontier post. Cv.lvii.67.

# Raṁsisaññaka 03

## English source

Raṁsisaññaka 03. An *Arahant* Thera. Thirty thousand *kappas* ago he saw a Buddha seated on a rock suffusing all the place with his aura, and was gladdened by the sight. Fifty-seven *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sujāta</span>. Ap.i.210.

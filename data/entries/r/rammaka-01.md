# Rammaka 01

## English source

Rammaka 01. A Brahmin whose hermitage was in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sāvatthī</span>, near the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pubbakoṭṭhaka</span>. It was a great resort of the monks, and there the Buddha preached the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ariyapariyesanasutta</span>. MN.i.160.

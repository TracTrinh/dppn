# Rajjasutta

## English source

Rajjasutta. Once the Buddha was thinking compassionately of those suffering from the cruelty of rulers and wondering if it were possible to rule without causing suffering. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māra</span> approaches and tries to make him absorbed in the fascination of exercising power, suggesting that if the Buddha wished he could change even the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Himālaya</span> into a mass of gold. But, says the Buddha, he has seen suffering and its source and will not, therefore, incline to sense desires. SN.i.116.

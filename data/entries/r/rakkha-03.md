# Rakkha 03

## English source

Rakkha 03. called *Mahā Lekha*. He was an officer of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. and took part in the campaigns against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Māṇābharaṇa</span>, being stationed at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Maṅgalabegāma</span>, at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pillaviṭṭhi</span>. At this place he fought a battle, which lasted for eight days, against <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Buddhanāyaka</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Māladeva</span>, and brought the district of *Kālavāpi* under his power. Cv.lxxii.161, 170ff., 182, 206.

# Ratanadoṇi

## English source

Ratanadoṇi. A village in Ceylon, given by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirirājasīha</span> for the maintenance of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dutiyaselavihāra</span>. The village had originally belonged to the *vihāra* but had been taken away. Cv.c.232.

# Revatī 03

## English source

Revatī 03. Another name, according to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpavaṁsa</span> (xxi.40f.; cp. Mhv.xxxv.14f), for <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīvalī</span>, daughter of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Āmaṇḍagāmaṇī Abhaya</span>. She was the sister of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cūḷābhaya</span> and succeeded him for a period of four months, when she was dethroned by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Iḷanāga</span>.

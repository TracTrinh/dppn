# Rūpavatī 02

## English source

Rūpavatī 02. Queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. She was a descendant of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirimegha</span>. She is mentioned as having erected a “golden *Thūpa* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>.” Cv.lxxiii.137, 142ff. The *Thūpa* has been identified with the modern *Pabuḷu Vehera* (*Archeological Survey of Ceylon*, vi.1014, p. 6).

# Rūpavatī 02

## English source

Rūpavatī 02. Queen of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Parakkamabāhu I</span>. She was a descendant of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kittisirimegha</span>. She is mentioned as having erected a “golden <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Thūpa</span> in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>.” Cv.lxxiii.137, 142ff. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Thūpa</span> has been identified with the modern <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Pabuḷu Vehera</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Archeological Survey of Ceylon</span>, vi.1014, p. 6).

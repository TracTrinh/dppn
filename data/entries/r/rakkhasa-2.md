# Rakkhasā

## English source

Rakkhasā, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rakkhasas</span>. A class of demons, chiefly nocturnal and harmful. They usually have their haunt in the water and devour men when bathing there. Some of them live in the sea, e.g., Thag.v.931; Snp. vs. 310; Ja.i.127; vi. 469; Dhp­a.i.367; iii.74; Mhv.xii.45, etc.

# Rukkhasutta 04

## English source

Rukkhasutta 04. Mighty trees, grown from tiny seeds, overspread other trees and kill them; so are householders destroyed by their lusts. There are five hindrances (*nīvaraṇa*) that overspread the heart; the seven *bojjhaṅgas* are not like them. SN.v.96f.

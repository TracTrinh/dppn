# Rukkhasutta 04

## English source

Rukkhasutta 04. Mighty trees, grown from tiny seeds, overspread other trees and kill them; so are householders destroyed by their lusts. There are five hindrances (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">nīvaraṇa</span>) that overspread the heart; the seven <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">bojjhaṅgas</span> are not like them. SN.v.96f.

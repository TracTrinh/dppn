# Revata 04

## English source

Revata 04. The DhpA.iv.176f mentions a novice Revata, with three others – <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Saṅkicca</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Paṇḍita</span> and <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sopāka</span> – all four of whom became *Arahants* at the age of seven. The Revata referred to is, very probably, Revata (3). For their story see <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pañcachiddageha</span>.

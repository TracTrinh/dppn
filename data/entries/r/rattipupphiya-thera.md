# Rattipupphiya Thera

## English source

Rattipupphiya Thera. An *Arahant*. In the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vipassī Buddha</span> he was a hunter, and, seeing the Buddha in the forest, he offered him some flowers which bloom by night (*rattikaṁ pupphaṁ*). Eight *kappas* ago he was a king named <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Suppasanna</span>. Ap.i.188.

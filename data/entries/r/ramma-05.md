# Ramma 05

## English source

Ramma 05. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rammavatī</span>. The city of birth of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpaṅkara Buddha</span>. It was while <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīpaṅkara</span> was on a visit to this city that <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sumedha</span> met him and was declared by him to be a Bodhisatta. At that time the Buddha was living in a monastery called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudassana</span> *Mahāvihāra*. Ja.i.11, 13, 29; iv.119; Dhp­a.i.69; Bv.ii.207; Bv­a. 65 calls it <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rammavatī</span>.

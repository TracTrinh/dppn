# Rājasīha 02

## English source

Rājasīha 02. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rājasīha II</span>. Youngest son of King <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Senāratana</span>. He dispossessed his brother and became king; many stories of his prowess are related (e.g., Cv.xcvi.7ff). He reigned for fifty-two years (1635-87 CE.), and his capital was at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sirivaḍḍhanapura</span>. He obtained wives from the royal family at <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Madhurā</span>. In his time, the Dutch came to Ceylon and exacted tribute. His son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vimaladhammasūriya</span>. Cv.xcv.23; xcvi.3ff.; xcix.109.

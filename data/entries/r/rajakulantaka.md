# Rājakulantaka

## English source

Rājakulantaka. The name given to one of the suburbs (*sākhānagarā*) of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span> (Cv.lxxiii.153). Geiger (Cv.Trs.ii.18, n.3) thinks that this is identical with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sīhapura</span>, mentioned elsewhere (Cv.lxxviii.79ff) as a suburb of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pulatthipura</span>.

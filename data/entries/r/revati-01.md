# Revatī 01

## English source

Revatī 01. Wife of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandiya</span> (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t18">q.v.</span>). Her story is given in Dhp­a.iii.290ff. and also at Vv-a.220ff, also referred to in Pv-a.257. According to the Vv-a version, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandiya</span> was born after death in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Tāvatiṁsa</span>, but <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Revatī</span>, on the death of her husband, stopped the gift of alms which he had instituted, abused the monks, and was cast alive into hell.

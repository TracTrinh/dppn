# Ratanasutta 02

## English source

Ratanasutta 02. The <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Dīgha Commentary</span> (DN­a.i.250) refers the reader to a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ratanasutta</span> of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta</span> for details of the seven gems of a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavatti</span>. The reference is evidently to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cakkavattisutta</span> (*q.v.*). SN.v.98.

# Rājagiriyā

## English source

Rājagiriyā. One of the heterodox Buddhist sects which branched off in the second century after the death of the Buddha (Dpv.v.54; Mhv.v.12). They formed a part of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Andhaka</span> sect. *Points of Controversy*, p.104.

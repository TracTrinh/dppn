# Rūpī Attā Sutta

## English source

Rūpī Attā Sutta, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Arūpī Atta suttā</span>, <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rūpī ca Arūpī ca Attā Sutta</span>. It is owing to the presence of the five <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">khandhas</span> that the view arises that the self has a form, is without sickness after death, or that it is formless, or that it both has form and is formless. SN.iii.218f.

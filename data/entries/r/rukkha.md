# Rukkha

## English source

Rukkha. *v.l.* <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rakkha</span>. An officer of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kassapa IV</span>. He built a *vihāra* in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Savāraka</span> which he handed over to the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Vihāra</span>. He also laid down rules for the guidance of the monks (Cv.lii.31).

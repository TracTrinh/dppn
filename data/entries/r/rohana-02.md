# Rohaṇa 02

## English source

Rohaṇa 02. A Brahmin, grandson of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pekkhuṇiya</span>. He was a friend of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Licchavī Sāḷha</span>, and a visit paid by both of them to <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nandaka</span> is recorded in the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Aṅguttaranikāya</span>.

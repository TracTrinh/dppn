# Ratanagharacetiya

## English source

Ratanagharacetiya. A shrine erected on the site of the Jewelled Hall (<span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ratanaghara</span>), which was created by the gods to the north-west of the Bodhi-tree. There the Buddha sat during the fourth week after the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, revolving in his mind the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Abhidhammapiṭaka</span>. Ja.i.78; Bv­a.8, 241.

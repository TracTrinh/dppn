# Rājasutta 02

## English source

Rājasutta 02. On the eighth day of the lunar fortnight, the ministers of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Cātummahārājikā</span> visit the earth, on the fourteenth day their sons, on the fifteenth day the kings themselves. They report what they find at the assembly of the gods in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sudhammā</span> hall and rejoice if men have done good, sorrowing if they have done evil. AN.i.142f.

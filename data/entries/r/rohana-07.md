# Rohaṇa 07

## English source

Rohaṇa 07. Headman of the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti</span> and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theraputtābhaya</span>. Rohaṇa was a supporter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Summa Thera</span><span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t18">,</span> and, having heard him preach at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭapabbata</span> <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t18">vihāra</span>, he became a <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="7" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Sotāpanna</span> and joined the Saṅgha, later attaining <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="8" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Arahant</span>-ship. Mhv.xxiii.55ff.

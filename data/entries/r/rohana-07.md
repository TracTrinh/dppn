# Rohaṇa 07

## English source

Rohaṇa 07. Headman of the village of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kitti</span> and father of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Theraputtābhaya</span>. Rohaṇa was a supporter of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Summa Thera</span>*,* and, having heard him preach at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Koṭapabbata</span> *vihāra*, he became a *Sotāpanna* and joined the Saṅgha, later attaining *Arahant*-ship. Mhv.xxiii.55ff.

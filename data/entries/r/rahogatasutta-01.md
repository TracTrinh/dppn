# Rahogatasutta 01

## English source

Rahogatasutta 01. While in solitude a monk thinks of the three kinds of feelings, and, visiting the Buddha, questions him. The Buddha tells him that the statement, “Whatsoever is experienced is joined with <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t18">dukkha</span>” is made concerning the impermanence of compounded things. The ceasing of activities is gradual, so is their mastery. SN.iv.216f.

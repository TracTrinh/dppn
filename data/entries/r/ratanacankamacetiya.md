# Ratanacaṅkamacetiya

## English source

Ratanacaṅkamacetiya or <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ratnacetiya</span>. A shrine erected near the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Animisacetiya</span> close to the Bodhi-tree, to mark the spot on which the Buddha spent the third week after the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t20" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t20">Awakening</span>, walking to and fro in the Jewelled Walk (*Ratanacaṅkama*). Ja.i.78; Bv­a.8, 241.

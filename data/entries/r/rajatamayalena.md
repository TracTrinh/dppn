# Rajatamayalena

## English source

Rajatamayalena. A cave in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ambilajanapada</span> in which lived five hundred *Arahants*. One day one of these was suffering from stomach trouble and ten thousand of his colleagues came by air to minister to him. They all found room in the cave because of their *iddhi* power. Mhv­ṭ. 552.

# Rucānandā

## English source

Rucānandā. A nun in the time of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Kakusandha Buddha</span>. She came to Ceylon at the Buddha’s wish with five hundred other nuns, bringing a branch of the Bodhi-tree. Mhv.xv.78; Dpv.xvii.16, 51ff.

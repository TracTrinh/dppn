# Roruka 01

## English source

Roruka 01. A city, capital of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sovīras</span>, established by <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Jotipāla</span>, chaplain of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Reṇu</span>. Its king was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Bharata</span>. (DN.ii.235; cp. Mvu.iii.208; see also Divy.544ff). In the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Ādittajātaka</span> (Ja 424, Ja.iii.470) it is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Roruva</span>.

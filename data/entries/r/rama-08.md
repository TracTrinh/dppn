# Rāma 08

## English source

Rāma 08. One of the generals of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gajabāhu</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāma</span> was once defeated by the general <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t18" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t18">Deva</span> (Cv.lxx.137, 142), but later won a victory at the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Rakkha</span> ford. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rāma</span> received the title of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="6" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Nīlagiri</span>, which was evidently the name of his district. Cv.lxxii.12; Cv.Trs.i.299, n.1; 320, n.2.

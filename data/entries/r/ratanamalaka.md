# Ratanamālaka

## English source

Ratanamālaka. A platform in <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Gandhamādana</span>, at the foot of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mañjūsa</span> tree, where <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Pacceka Buddhas</span> assemble on special occasions such as the *uposatha* (Snp­a.i.52). It seems also to have been called *Sabbaratnamāla*, e.g., Snp­a.i.66.

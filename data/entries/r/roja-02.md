Roja 02. A primeval king, son of <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="1" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Mahā Sammata</span>, and, therefore, an ancestor of the <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="2" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Sākiyans</span>. <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="3" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Roja</span>’s son was <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="4" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Vararoja</span>. Ja.ii.311; iii.454; Snp­a.i.353; Dpv.iii.4; Mhv.ii.2; Mhv­ṭ. 124; cp. Mvu.i.384 where he is called <span class="dppn-term dppn-term-t14" data-dppn-term-pos="5" data-dppn-term-class="t14">Rava</span>.

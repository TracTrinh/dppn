01. Mūsila 01 untranslated

Mūsila 01. (v.l. Musīla, Musila). Devadatta born as the chief musician of Ujjenī. For his story see the Guttilajātaka, Ja 243.

02. Mūsila 02 untranslated

Mūsila 02. Thera. Mūsila. A monk. A conversation is recorded in the Saṁyuttanikāya (SN.ii.115f) between him and Saviṭṭha, which is said to have taken place in Ghositārāma in Kosambī, regarding the paṭiccasamuppāda.

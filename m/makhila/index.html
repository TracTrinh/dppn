Makhilā

01. Makhilā 01

Makhilā 01. A city near the Deer Park at Isipatana close to where Kakusandha Buddha preached his first sermon. Bv­a. 210.

02. Makhilā 02

Makhilā 02. One of the two chief women disciples of Sikhī Buddha. Ja.i.41; Bv­a.204; see also Akhilā.

03. Makhilā 03

Makhilā 03. One of the chief female lay patrons of Atthadassī Buddha. Bv.xv.21.

04. Makhilā 04

Makhilā 04. Wife of Sobhita Buddha in his last lay life; she gave him a meal of milk-rice just before his Awakening. Bv­a.137; but Bv.vii.18 calls her Samaṅgī.

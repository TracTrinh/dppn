Moranivāpasutta untranslated

01. Moranivāpasutta 01 untranslated

Moranivāpasutta 01. Preached at Moranivāpa on the qualities which make a monk fully proficient in the holy life the aggregate of the virtues, concentration and insight, belonging to the adept. AN.i.291.

Chưa dịch.

02. Moranivāpasutta 02 untranslated

Moranivāpasutta 02. Preached at the Paribbājakārāma in Moranivāpa. It enumerates several groups of qualities the possession of which make a monk fully proficient in the higher life. Three of the groups are triads and the fourth a dyad. A stanza, attributed to Sanaṅkumāra, is also quoted. AN.v.326ff.

Chưa dịch.

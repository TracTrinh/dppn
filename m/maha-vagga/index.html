Mahā Vagga

01. Mahā Vagga 01

Mahā Vagga 01. A section of the Vinayapiṭaka, divided into chapters called Khandhakas. The introductory chapters give an account of the incidents immediately following the Buddha’s Awakening, leading up to the foundation of the Saṅgha. It then gives various rules for members of the Saṅgha, together with the circumstances which led to the formulation of each rule.

02. Mahā Vagga 02

Mahā Vagga 02. The second section of the Dīghanikāya, containing Suttas XIV.-XXIII.

03. Mahā Vagga 03

Mahā Vagga 03. The third section of the Suttanipāta, containing twelve Suttas.

04. Mahā Vagga 04

Mahā Vagga 04. The first section of the Paṭisambhidāmagga.

05. Mahā Vagga 05

Mahā Vagga 05. The fifth section of the Saṁyuttanikāya.

6. Mahā Vagga

Mahā Vagga 06. The seventh section of the Tikanipāta, the twentieth of the Catukka, the sixth of the Chakka, the seventh of the Sattaka, the second of the Aṭṭhaka, and the third of the Dasakanipāta of the Aṅguttaranikāya.

Mahā Sāmi

01. Mahā Sāmi 01

Mahā Sāmi 01. A name given by his people to Dappula, governor of Rohaṇa and father of Māṇavamma. Cv.xlv.50.

02. Mahā Sāmi 02

Mahā Sāmi 02. A title given to Moggallāna, ruler of Rohaṇa, and father of Kitti (afterwards Vijayabhāhu I.). Cv.lvii.30, 49.

03. Mahā Sāmi 03

Mahā Sāmi 03. A Thera of Ceylon, to whom tradition ascribes the authorship of the Mūlasikhā. PLC. 77.

04. Mahā Sāmi 04

Mahā Sāmi 04. Mahā Sāmi Thera. Author of the new ṭīkā on the Nāmarūpapariccheda Sās. 69.

05. Mahā Sāmi 05

Mahā Sāmi 05. Mahā Sāmi Thera. Author of a ṭīkā on the Subodhālaṅkāra. Gv.62.

6. Mahā Sāmi

Mahā Sāmi 06. The title of the head of the Saṅgha in Sri Lanka in the middle ages.

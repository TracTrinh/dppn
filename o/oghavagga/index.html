Oghavagga untranslated

Oghavagga. Several chapters in the Saṁyuttanikāya are called by this name; thus, in the Maggasaṁyutta (SN.v.59), the Bojjhaṅga (SN.v.136, 139), the Satipaṭṭhāna (SN.v.191), the Indriya (SN.v.241-242), the Sammappadhāna (SN.v.247), the Bala (SN.v.251, 253), the Iddhipāda (SN.v.292) and the Jhāna (SN.v.309).

Chưa dịch.

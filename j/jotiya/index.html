02. Jotiya 02 untranslated

Jotiya 02. A king of seventy-three kappas ago, a previous birth of Mañjaripūjaka. Ap.i.228.

Chưa dịch.

03. Jotiya 03 untranslated

Jotiya 03. A Nigaṇṭha, for whom Paṇḍukābhaya built a house to the east of the Nīcasusāna at Anurādhapura (Mhv.x.97). The Mahā Vaṁsaṭīkā (p.296) calls him a Nagaravaḍḍhakī. The Abhayagirivihāra was later erected on the spot occupied by Jotiya’s residence. Mhv­ṭ.620.

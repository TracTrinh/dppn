01. Juṇha 01 untranslated

Juṇha 01. An Elder of Kosala. For his story see the Mālutajātaka (Ja 17, Ja.i.165f).

Chưa dịch.

02. Juṇha 02 untranslated

Juṇha 02. Son of Brahmadatta, king of Benares. He was the Bodhisatta. See the Juṇhajātaka (Ja 456) Ja.iv.95ff.

Chưa dịch.

03. Juṇha 03 untranslated

Juṇha 03. A minister of Pasenadi. When Pasenadi held his Asadisadāna, Juṇha rejoiced in the king’s generosity, but his friend Kāḷa was displeased at what he considered as waste. The king, hearing of this from the Buddha, asked Juṇha to use the royal revenue to give alms on his own account during seven days. This Juṇha did. He became a Sotāpanna after hearing the Buddha preach. Dhp­a.iii.186ff.

Chưa dịch.

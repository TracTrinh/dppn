Yudhiṭṭhila

01. Yudhiṭṭhila 01

Yudhiṭṭhila 01. Son of the Paṇḍu king and one of the five husbands of Kaṇhā. Ja.v.424, 426.

02. Yudhiṭṭhila 02

Yudhiṭṭhila 02. Son of Sabbadatta, king of Ramma, and younger brother of the Bodhisatta, born as Yuvañjaya. He is identified with Ānanda. See the Yuvañjayajātaka.

03. Yudhiṭṭhila 03

Yudhiṭṭhila 03. The name of a gotta to which Dhanañjaya, king of the Kurus, belonged (Ja.iii.400; v. 59, etc.). Mention is made also of a Koravyarājā of the Yudhiṭṭhila gotta (Ja.iv.361). Probably the kings of the Kurus (q.v.) belonged mostly to a dynasty that claimed its descent from Yudhiṭṭhila (Yudhiṣṭhira?).

01. Yodhājīvasutta 01 untranslated

Yodhājīvasutta 01. On five kinds of warriors: those who are frightened by a cloud of dust, by the sight of a flag, by tumult, by conflict, and those who fight victoriously; and on five similar kinds of monks. AN.iii.87f.

Chưa dịch.

02. Yodhājīvasutta 02 untranslated

Yodhājīvasutta 02. On five kinds of warriors: those who go down into the thick of the fight where they are overpowered, those who are wounded and die on the way to their home, those who survive for some time but die of their wounds, those who are cured of their wounds, those that are victorious in battle and continue to fight. There are five corresponding kinds of monks. AN.iii.94ff.

Chưa dịch.

03. Yodhājīvasutta 03 untranslated

Yodhājīvasutta 03. Records the visit of the headman Yodhājīva to the Buddha. SN.iv.308.

Chưa dịch.

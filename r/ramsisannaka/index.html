Raṁsisaññaka

01. Raṁsisaññaka 01

Raṁsisaññaka 01. Raṁsisaññaka Thera. An Arahant. He was an ascetic in Himavā, and seeing Vipassī Buddha radiating light, he paid him homage. Ap.i.129.

02. Raṁsisaññaka 02

Raṁsisaññaka 02. An Arahant Thera. Ninety-two kappas ago he was an ascetic, and seeing Phussa Buddha in jhāna, was overjoyed at the sight. Ap.i.130.

03. Raṁsisaññaka 03

Raṁsisaññaka 03. An Arahant Thera. Thirty thousand kappas ago he saw a Buddha seated on a rock suffusing all the place with his aura, and was gladdened by the sight. Fifty-seven kappas ago he was a king named Sujāta. Ap.i.210.

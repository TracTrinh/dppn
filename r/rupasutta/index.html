Rūpasutta

01. Rūpasutta 01

Rūpasutta 01. Two of a group of Suttas preached to Rāhula, to show him that all things are fleeting, unhappy and changeable. SN.ii.245, 251.

02. Rūpasutta 02

Rūpasutta 02. He who realizes the impermanence of the body and the other khandhas becomes a Sotāpanna. SN.iii.225.

03. Rūpasutta 03

Rūpasutta 03. The cessation of suffering, disease, decay and death, is identical with the cessation of the five sense objects forms, sounds, etc. SN.iii.229.

04. Rūpasutta 04

Rūpasutta 04. Desire and lust, which arise from forms, sounds, etc., are corruption of the heart; the getting rid of them leads to higher knowledge. SN.iii.232.

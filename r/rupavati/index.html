Rūpavatī

01. Rūpavatī 01

Rūpavatī 01. Daughter of Vijayabāhu I. and Tilokasundarī. She had four sisters, and a brother called Vikkamabāhu (Cv.lix.31). She died young and unmarried (Cv.lix.45).

02. Rūpavatī 02

Rūpavatī 02. Queen of Parakkamabāhu I. She was a descendant of King Kittisirimegha. She is mentioned as having erected a “golden Thūpa in Pulatthipura.” Cv.lxxiii.137, 142ff. The Thūpa has been identified with the modern Pabuḷu Vehera (Archeological Survey of Ceylon, vi.1014, p. 6).

Khemappakaraṇa

Khemappakaraṇa. A work written by Khema, a Thera, probably of Ceylon.

The work is on the Abhidhamma and forms one of the Let-than (“Little-finger” manuals) studied in Burma.

It is also known as the Nāmarūpasamāsa.

A Commentary on it was written by Vācissara (Gv.61, 71; Sās-d.63; for details see PLC.156).

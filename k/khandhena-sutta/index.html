Khandhena Sutta untranslated

01. Khandhena Sutta 01 untranslated

Khandhena Sutta 01. All the khandhas are impermanent. He who has faith in the doctrine is a saddhānusāri; he who has understood it moderately is a dhammānusāri; he who knows and sees the doctrine is a Sotāpanna. SN.iii.227f.

Chưa dịch.

02. Khandhena Sutta 02 untranslated

Khandhena Sutta 02. The arising of suffering is due to the arising of the body, etc.; its cessation is brought about by their cessation (SN.iii.231).

Chưa dịch.

03. Khandhena Sutta 03 untranslated

Khandhena Sutta 03. Desire and lust (chandarāga) for body, etc., brings about corruption of the mind. SN.iii.234.

Chưa dịch.

Kakkaṭa untranslated

01. Kakkaṭa 01 untranslated

Kakkaṭa 01. An eminent monk mentioned, with Cāla, Upacāla, Kalimbha, Nikaṭa and Kaṭissaha, as staying with the Buddha at the Kūṭāgārasālā in Vesālī. When the Licchavīs started coming there to pay their respects to the Buddha, the monks, desiring solitude, went into the woodlands near by, such as the Gosiṅgasālavana. AN.v.133f.

Chưa dịch.

02. Kakkaṭa 02 untranslated

Kakkaṭa 02. A lay disciple of Nādikā (Ñātikā) mentioned with several others (SN.v.138). He is evidently identical with Kakudha (1).

Kūṭasutta untranslated

01. Kūṭasutta 01 untranslated

Kūṭasutta 01. In a peaked house (kūṭāgāra) all the rafters converge towards the peak; so are all wrong states fixed together in ignorance. SN.ii.262.

Chưa dịch.

02. Kūṭasutta 02 untranslated

Kūṭasutta 02. Similar to the above; all profitable conditions have earnestness as their peak. SN.v.43.

Chưa dịch.

03. Kūṭasutta 03 untranslated

Kūṭasutta 03. Just as all the rafters slope to the peak, so do the seven bojjhaṅgas tend towards Nibbāna. SN.v.75.

Chưa dịch.

4. Kūṭasuttā untranslated

Kūṭasuttā 04. Two discourses of similar import, preached to Anāthapiṇḍika. When a peaked house is un-thatched, the peak, the roof-beams and the walls are all unprotected. When thought is unguarded all actions also are unguarded. AN.i.261f.

Chưa dịch.

05. Kūṭasutta 05 untranslated

Kūṭasutta 05. Of the five powers (saddhā, hiri, ottappa, viriya, paññā) the last is the peak (AN.iii.10).

Chưa dịch.

06. Kūṭasutta 06 untranslated

Kūṭasutta 06. Of the five powers, (saddhā, viriya, sati, samādhi, paññā) the last is the peak. AN.iii.12.

Chưa dịch.

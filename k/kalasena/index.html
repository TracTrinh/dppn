01. Kāḷasena 01 untranslated

Kāḷasena 01. King of Ayojjhā. The Andhakaveṇhuputtā besieged his city and took the king prisoner. Ja.iv.82.

02. Kāḷasena 02 untranslated

Kāḷasena 02. v.l. Mahā Kāḷasena. One of the leaders of the Yakkhas in Ceylon. He was ruler of the Yakkha-city of Sirīsavatthu. He married Polamittā, and it was at the wedding-feast of these two that Vijaya attacked the city with Kuveni’s help and destroyed the Yakkhas. Mhv.vii.32ff Mhv­ṭ.259.

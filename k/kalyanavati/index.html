Kalyāṇavatī

Kalyāṇavatī. The first queen-consort of Kittinissaṅka.

After the death of Sāhasamalla she carried on the government of Ceylon for six months (according to some six years, 1202-1208 CE.) with the help of her general, Āyasmanta.

She built a vihāra called the Kalyāṇavatīvihāra in the village of Paṇṇasālaka. Cv.lxxx.34ff; also Cv.Trs.ii.130, n.3.

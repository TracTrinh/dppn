Kimatthisutta

01. Kimatthisutta 01

Kimatthisutta 01. The first part is identical with Kimatthasutta. If further questioned as to what is dukkha, monks should answer, “eye is dukkha, rūpa objects are dukkha, eye-contact, etc.” SN.iv.138

02. Kimatthisutta 02

Kimatthisutta 02. A discussion between Sāriputta and Jambukhādaka at Nālaka. “What is the purpose of the good life?” “The comprehension of suffering.” “Is there a way thereto?” “Yes, the Noble Eightfold Way.” SN.iv.253.

03. Kimatthisutta 03

Kimatthisutta 03. See also Sāmaṇḍakasaṁyutta.

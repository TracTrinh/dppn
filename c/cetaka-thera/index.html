Cetaka Thera untranslated

Cetaka Thera. He was the companion of Ānanda soon after the Buddha’s death and accompanied him to Subha’s house (DN.i.204).

The Commentary says he was so-called because he came from the Cetiya country (DN­a.ii.386; also DN­a.i.7 and Khp-a.94).

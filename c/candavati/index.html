Candavatī

01. Candavatī 01

Candavatī 01. Wife of Assalāyana and mother of Mahā Koṭṭhita. Ap.ii.480.

02. Candavatī 02

Candavatī 02. The city wherein, at the Silārāma, Sujāta Buddha died. Bv­a.171.

03. Candavatī 03

Candavatī 03. The birthplace of Anomadassī Buddha (Ja.i.36; Bv.viii.17; Dhp­a.i.88; AN­a.i.85; see also Ap.i.76). There Koṇḍañña Buddha spent his first vassa (Bv­a.110). It existed also in the time of Sumedha Buddha (Ap.ii.422). It was the capital of King Vijitāvī (Bv­a.111).

04. Candavatī 04

Candavatī 04. Daughter of Brahmadatta, king of Benares. The king offered her to Lomasa Kassapa on condition that he should perform a sacrifice of beasts. Kassapa agreed but later withdrew his consent. See the Lomasa Kassapajātaka (Ja 433). Ja.iii.515ff; Mil.220.

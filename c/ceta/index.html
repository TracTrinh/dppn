Ceta

1. Ceta

Ceta. A kingdom through which Vessantara passed on his way from Jetuttara.

Vessantara’s uncle ruled in Ceta, and it was ten leagues from Dunniviṭṭha (Ja.vi.514ff; Cp.i.9, vs.38f).

The women of Ceta are called Cetiyā (Ja.vi.514).

Ceta is probably another name for Cetiya.

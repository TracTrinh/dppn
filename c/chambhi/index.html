Chambhī untranslated

Chambhī. A Brahmin, chaplain of Mahā Cūḷanī. He conspired with queen Talatā and, having poisoned Mahā Cūḷanī, became king in his place. Later, fearing for his life, he wished to kill the king’s son, Cūḷanī, but Talatā (q.v.), by means of a ruse, saved the boy’s life. Ja.vi.470f.

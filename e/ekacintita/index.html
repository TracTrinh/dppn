Ekacintita

01. Ekacintita 01

Ekacintita 01. Sixteen kappas ago there were eighteen kings of this name. They were previous births of Sumaṅgala Thera. Ap.i.148; Thag­a.i.111.

02. Ekacintita 02

Ekacintita 02. Ekacintita Thera. An Arahant. When in the Deva-world, his term of life having come to an end, he was filled with anxiety as to where he should seek rebirth. A disciple of Padumuttara, named Sumana, seeing his plight, advised him to seek refuge in the Buddha. He followed the advice and escaped sorrow. Ap.i.194f.

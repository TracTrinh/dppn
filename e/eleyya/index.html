Eḷeyya untranslated

Eḷeyya. A rājā, probably of Magadha. He was a devout follower of Uddaka Rāmaputta. In his retinue were Yamaka, Moggalla, Ugga, Nāvindaki, Gandhabba, and Aggivessa, all of whom were also followers of the same teacher (AN.ii.180f).

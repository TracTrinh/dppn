01. Buddhappiya 01 untranslated

Buddhappiya 01. Buddhappiya Thera. He was a native of Coḷa, but studied in Ceylon under Ānanda Vanaratana. He was the head of Bālādiccavihāra and wrote two books, the Rūpasiddhi and the Pajjamadhu. He is also known as Coḷiya Dīpaṅkara. PLC.220 f.; Svd.1239, 1260; Sās-d.65.

Chưa dịch.

02. Buddhappiya 02 untranslated

Buddhappiya 02. A monk, one of those who requested Buddhaghosa to write the Jātaka Commentary. Gv.68; but see Ja.i.1, where his name does not occur.

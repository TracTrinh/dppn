Bhoja

01. Bhoja 01

Bhoja 01. A Brahmin, one of the eight who read the auspicious marks on the Buddha’s body on the fifth day after his birth. Ja.i.56; in the Milinda (p.236) he is called Subhoja.

02. Bhoja 02

Bhoja 02. A physician of old. Ja.iv. 496, 498.

03. Bhoja 03

Bhoja 03. A country. See Bhojaputta.

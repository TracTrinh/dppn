Bhāvitatta

01. Bhāvitatta 01

Bhāvitatta 01. One of the two chief disciples of Sumana Buddha (Ja.i.34; Bv.v.26). He was the Purohita in Sumana’s city of birth, and was one of those to whom Sumana preached his first sermon. Bv­a. 126.

02. Bhāvitatta 02

Bhāvitatta 02. A king who heard Paduma Buddha preach, and became a monk with a retinue of one hundred thousand crores. Bv­a.148.

03. Bhāvitatta 03

Bhāvitatta 03. The name of two Pacceka Buddhas. MN.iii.69.

Nibbānasutta untranslated

01. Nibbānasutta 01 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 01. The Buddha explains to Jāṇussoṇī the meaning of seeing Nibbāna in this life. AN.i.158.

Chưa dịch.

02. Nibbānasutta 02 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 02. Sāriputta explains to Ānanda why some beings do not attain Nibbāna in this very life. AN.ii.167.

Chưa dịch.

03. Nibbānasutta 03 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 03. It is impossible that one who sees sorrow in Nibbāna shall live in harmony and patience. AN.iii.442.

Chưa dịch.

04. Nibbānasutta 04 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 04. Sāriputta explains to Udāyī (Lāḷudāyi, according to the Commentary: AN­a.ii.810) how Nibbāna is happiness, though in it there is no experiencing (vedayitaṁ). AN.iv.414f.

Chưa dịch.

05. Nibbānasutta 05 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 05. Preached at Nālaka. Sāriputta explains to Jambukhādaka the meaning of Nibbāna and the way thereto. SN.iv.251.

Chưa dịch.

06. Nibbānasutta 06 untranslated

Nibbānasutta 06. Preached at Ukkācelā. Sāriputta explains to Sāmaṇḍaka the meaning of Nibbāna and the way thereto. SN.iv.261f.

Chưa dịch.

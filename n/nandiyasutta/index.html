Nandiyasutta untranslated

01. Nandiyasutta 01 untranslated

Nandiyasutta 01. The Paribbājaka Nandiya (see Nandiya 4) visits the Buddha and is instructed in the Noble Eightfold Path. SN.v.11.

Chưa dịch.

02. Nandiyasutta 02 untranslated

Nandiyasutta 02. The Sākiyan Nandiya (Nandiya 2) visits the Buddha and learns the difference between the Ariyan disciple who is remiss and the one who is earnest. SN.v.397ff.

Chưa dịch.

03. Nandiyasutta 03 untranslated

Nandiyasutta 03. Nandiya, the Sākiyan, is taught by the Buddha that the Ariyan disciple who is possessed of unwavering loyalty to the Buddha, the Dhamma and the Saṅgha, and who has the Ariyan qualities, is bound for enlightenment. SN.v.403.

Chưa dịch.

04. Nandiyasutta 04 untranslated

Nandiyasutta 04. Relates the visit of Nandiya, the Sākyan (Nandiya 2) to Sāvatthī, to be near the Buddha, and the instruction he receives from the Buddha at the end of the rainy season. AN.v.334ff.

Chưa dịch.

Nandarāmā

1. Nandarāmā

Nandarāmā. One of the chief women supporters of Paduma Buddha. Bv.ix.23.

01. Nandārāma 01

Nandārāma 01. v.l. Sunandārāma. The pleasances in which Dīpaṅkara Buddha preached his first sermon. It was also the scene of his death Bv.ii.212, 220.

02. Nandārāma 02

Nandārāma 02. The place where Padumuttara Buddha died. Bv.xi.31.

03. Nandārāma 03

Nandārāma 03. A pleasance in Sunandavatī where Tissa Buddha died (Bv.xviii.28; Bv­a.192). v.1. Sunandārāma.

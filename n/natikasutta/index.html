Ñātikasutta untranslated

Ñātikasutta. Once the Buddha, while meditating in the Giñjakāvasatha at Nātika, uttered a discourse regarding suffering, its arising and its cessation. A certain monk stood listening, and the Buddha asked him to learn the Dhamma as he had heard it. SN.ii.71; repeated at SN.iv.90.

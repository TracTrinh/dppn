Nikaṭa

01. Nikaṭa 01

Nikaṭa 01. An upāsaka of Ñātikā. After death he was born in the Suddhāvāsā, there to pass away. SN.v.358f.;DN.ii.91f.

02. Nikaṭa 02

Nikaṭa 02. One of several eminent Theras mentioned as staying in the Kūṭāgārasāla in Vesālī. When the Buddha came there, Licchavīs crowded out the place with all their retinues, and Nikaṭa and his colleagues, desiring solitude, retired to the Gosiṅgasālavana. AN.v.133f.

Nandisena

01. Nandisena 01

Nandisena 01. Minister of Assaka, king of Potali. Nandisena is identified with Sāriputta. For details see the Cūḷa Kāliṅgajātaka. (Ja.iii.1ff.)

02. Nandisena 02

Nandisena 02. Father of Suppatiṭṭhitabrahmā, and minister of Duṭṭhagāmaṇī. His wife was Sumanā. Dpv. xix. 9; Mhv­ṭ. 528.

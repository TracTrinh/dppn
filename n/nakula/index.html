Nakula

01. Nakula 01

Nakula 01. Second of the five sons of King Paṇḍu, the others being Ajjuna, Bhimasena, Yudhiṭṭhila and Sahadeva. All of them became husbands of Kaṇhā (q.v.). Ja. v. 424, 426.

02. Nakula 02

Nakula 02. A Damiḷa chief, ally of Kulasekhara. Cv. lxxvi. 139

03. Nakula 03

Nakula 03. One of the chief lay supporters of Atthadassi Buddha. Bv.xv. 21.

04. Nakula 04

Nakula 04. Son of Nakulapitā and Nakulamātā. There is nothing further recorded of him. SN­a. ii. 181.

01. Nakulā 01

Nakulā 01. The chief woman disciple of Sobhita Buddha. Bv.vii.22; Ja.i.35.

02. Nakulā 02

Nakulā 02. Chief of the lay women who supported Sobhita Buddha. Bv.vii.23.

03. Nakulā 03

Nakulā 03. Daughter of the seṭṭhi of Nakulanigama. She gave a meal of milk-rice to Sumedha Buddha just before his Awakening. Bv­a. 163.

Pabbata untranslated

01. Pabbata 01 untranslated

Pabbata 01. A Pacceka Buddha, mentioned in a nominal list. MN.iii.70.

Chưa dịch.

02. Pabbata 02 untranslated

Pabbata 02. The name of the Bodhisatta in the time of Koṇāgamana Buddha. He was king of Mithilā and entertained the Buddha and his monks. Ja.i.43; Bv­a.9; Bv.xxiv.215.

Chưa dịch.

03. Pabbata 03 untranslated

Pabbata 03. A sage, the chief disciple of Sarabhaṅga. For details see the Indriyajātaka (Ja 423, Ja.iii.463ff.; see also Ja.v.133, 151). Pabbata is identified with Anuruddha.

Chưa dịch.

04. Pabbata 04 untranslated

Pabbata 04. A minister of Vaṭṭagāmaṇī, who built a monastery called Pabbatārāma, which he presented to Kupikkala Mahā Tissa. Mhv.xxxiii.91.

Chưa dịch.

05. Pabbata 05 untranslated

Pabbata 05. A Laṅkāpura who fought against Parakkamabāhu I. and was captured alive. Cv.lxxv.180, 184.

Chưa dịch.

06. Pabbata 06 untranslated

Pabbata 06. A class of gods (Pabbatā) mentioned with the Nārada (Snp.vs.543). The Commentary says (Snp­a.ii.435) that they were wise (paññavanto).

Chưa dịch.

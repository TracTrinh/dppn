Pañcāliputta

Pañcāliputta. See Visākha Pañcāliputta.

According to Buddhaghosa (AN­a.ii.511), Visākha’s mother was a Brahmin called Pañcālī; but the Theragāthā Commentary says (Thīg­a.i.331f) that Visākha’s mother was the daughter of the rājā of Pañcāla.

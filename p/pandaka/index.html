Paṇḍaka untranslated

01. Paṇḍaka 01 untranslated

Paṇḍaka 01. A monk, friend of Kapila, who lived in a village near Kosambī. He was found guilty of having taken what did not belong to him and also of unchastity. Vin.iii.67.

02. Paṇḍaka 02 untranslated

Paṇḍaka 02. A Yakkha of the Himālaya region. He, his wife Hāritā, and his five hundred sons, became Sotāpannas when Majjhantika preached to Aravāḷa. Mhv.xii.21.

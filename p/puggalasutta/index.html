Puggalasutta

01. Puggalasutta 01

Puggalasutta 01. Seven kinds of persons who are worthy of gifts and homage. AN.iv.10f.

02. Puggalasutta 02

Puggalasutta 02. Nine kinds of persons who exist in the world: those who have attained the Four Fruits of the Path, these who are on the way thereto, and puthujjanas. AN.iv.372.

03. Puggalasutta 03

Puggalasutta 03. Preached at Sāvatthī, to Pasenadi, on the four kinds of persons: those that are joined to darkness and fare to light, those that are joined to darkness and fare to darkness, those that are joined to light and fare to darkness, those that are joined to light and fare to light. SN.i.93ff.; cp. DN.iii.233; AN.ii.85; Pp.iv.19.

04. Puggalasutta 04

Puggalasutta 04. Incalculable is the beginning of Saṁsāra. The bones of a single person faring on for an aeon, if collected, would be a pile as great as Vepulla. SN.ii.185.

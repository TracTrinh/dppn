Pariññāsutta

01. Pariññāsutta 01

Pariññāsutta 01. The five khandhas are things to be understood (pariññeyyā dhammā). SN.iii.26.

02. Pariññāsutta 02

Pariññāsutta 02. The teaching for the comprehension of all attachment: from eye and object arises eye-consciousness, the union of these is contact; from contact comes feeling; similarly with regard to the other senses. SN.iv.32f.

03. Pariññāsutta 03

Pariññāsutta 03. (or Addhānasutta). The five indriyas, if developed, conduce to comprehension of the way out. SN.v.236.

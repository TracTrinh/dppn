Paṭipadāsutta

01. Paṭipadāsutta 01

Paṭipadāsutta 01. The Buddha teaches both the wrong way and the right way. SN.ii.4.

02. Paṭipadāsutta 02

Paṭipadāsutta 02. The Buddha teaches both the way that leads to the arising of the condition of the body and also the way leading to the cessation of such a condition. SN.iii.43.

03. Paṭipadāsutta 03

Paṭipadāsutta 03. The Buddha teaches both wrong practice and right practice. SN.v.18.

04. Paṭipadāsutta 04

Paṭipadāsutta 04. The Buddha does not praise wrong practice, but he praises right practice. SN.v.18f.

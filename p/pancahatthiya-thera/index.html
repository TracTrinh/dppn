Pañcahatthiya Thera 01. Pañcahatthiya Thera. An Arahant. He once saw Sumedha Buddha, to whom he offered five handfuls of lilies, which formed a canopy over the Buddha’s head. Later, he was king five times under the name of Hatthiya. Ap.i.97f.

Paṇḍurājā untranslated

01. Paṇḍurājā 01 untranslated

Paṇḍurājā 01. The ancestor of the Paṇḍavas. Ajjuna, Nakula, Bhīmasena, Yudhiṭṭhila and Sahadeva are called the sons of Paṇḍurājā and are said to belong to the Paṇḍurājāgotta. Ja.v.426.

Chưa dịch.

02. Paṇḍurājā 02 untranslated

Paṇḍurājā 02. A king of Madhurā (Mhv.vii.50, 69, 72). This is probably a descriptive name and means that he belonged to the Pāṇḍyan dynasty.

Chưa dịch.

03. Paṇḍurājā 03 untranslated

Paṇḍurājā 03. A Damiḷa chief, ally of Kulasekhara. Cv.lxxvi.126.

Chưa dịch.

Pakkantasutta

Pakkantasutta. The Buddha addresses the monks at Gijjhakūṭa, soon after Devadatta had seceded from the order, and tells them that Devadatta’s gain was his ruin, in the same way as the flowering of the plaintain, the bamboo and the rush. SN.ii.241.

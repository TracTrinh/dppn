Pāṭikārāma untranslated

Pāṭikārāma. A park near Vesālī, where the Buddha was staying, when Sunakkhatta, having failed to impress him as to the greatness of Korakkhattiya, left the Saṅgha and went about abusing the Buddha. Ja.i.389; cp. Ja.i.77.

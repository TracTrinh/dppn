Udumbara

01. Udumbara 01

Udumbara 01. A Thera of Makuva, author of a ṭīkā on the Peṭakopadesa. Gv.75, 65.

02. Udumbara 02

Udumbara 02. A village. Revata went there from Kaṇṇakujja and stopped there before proceeding to Aggaḷapura and Sahajāti. Thither the Elders followed him to ask his opinion on the Vajjī heresy. Vin.ii.299.

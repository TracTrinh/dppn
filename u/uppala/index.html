Uppala

01. Uppala 01

Uppala 01. A Pacceka Buddha mentioned in the Isigilisutta. MN.iii.70.

02. Uppala 02

Uppala 02. One of the Yakkhas who guarded Jotiya’s palace. He had two thousand followers. Dhp­a.iv.209.

03. Uppala 03

Uppala 03. Father of the warrior Ummādaphussadeva. Mhv.xxiii.82.

04. Uppala 04

Uppala 04. (Uppalaka). A Niraya (SN.i.152; Sn.p.126). Buddhaghosa says (Snp-a.ii.476f; see also AN.v.173), however, that it is not a special hell, but the name of a period of suffering in hell.

05. Uppala 05

Uppala 05. One of the treasure-troves that rose up from the earth on the day of the Bodhisatta’s birth. It was three gāvutas in extent. DN­a.i.284.

6. Uppalā

Uppalā. One of the chief women supporters of Anomadassī Buddha. Bv.viii.24.

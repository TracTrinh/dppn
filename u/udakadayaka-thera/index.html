01. Udakadāyaka Thera 01 untranslated

Udakadāyaka Thera 01. An Arahant. In a former birth he saw the Buddha Siddhattha having his meal and brought him a pot of water. Sixty-one kappas ago he became a king named Vimala (Ap.i.205). He is probably identical with Sānu Thera. Thag­a.i.115.

Chưa dịch.

02. Udakadāyaka Thera 02 untranslated

Udakadāyaka Thera 02. An Arahant. In a previous birth he filled a vessel of water for Padumuttara Buddha. As a result, he could find water in any spot he wished (Ap.ii.437). His Apadāna verses are found in the Theragāthā Commentary under the names of two Theras: Mahā Gavaccha (i.57) and Gaṅgātīriya (i.249).

Chưa dịch.

02. Upavāṇasutta 02 untranslated

Upavāṇasutta 02. The Buddha explains to Upavāṇa the arising of sorrow (dukkha-samuppāda). SN.ii.41f.

03. Upavāṇasutta 03 untranslated

Upavāṇasutta 03. The Buddha explains, in answer to a question of Upavāṇa, how the Dhamma is immediate in its results (sandiṭṭhika). SN.iv.41f.

Upavāṇasutta 04. The conversation referred to elsewhere, between Sāriputta and Upavāṇa, where the latter explains how the bojjhaṅgas conduce to a happy life. SN.v.76f.

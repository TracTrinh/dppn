Vararoja untranslated

01. Vararoja 01 untranslated

Vararoja 01. Vararoja. A primeval king, son of Roja; his son was Kalyāṇa. Dpv.iii.4; Mhv.ii.2; Ja.ii.311, etc.

Chưa dịch.

02. Vararoja 02 untranslated

Vararoja 02. Buddhaghosa says (DN­a.ii.656) that the heretics once paid Vararoja one thousand to speak ill of the Buddha’s person. Vararoja went to see the Buddha and was struck by the perfection of every feature; he spoke the Buddha’s praises in a verse of over one thousand lines.

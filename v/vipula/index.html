Vipula

01. Vipula 01

Vipula 01. A Khattiya, father of Revata Buddha. Ja.i.35; Bv.vi.16.

02. Vipula 02

Vipula 02. One of the five peaks near Rājagaha, the highest of them. See Vepulla. SN.i.67; Ja.vi.518; Mil.242.

3. Vipulā

Vipulā. Mother of Revata Buddha. Ja.i.35; Bv.vi.6.

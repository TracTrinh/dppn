Vebhāra untranslated

01. Vebhāra 01 untranslated

Vebhāra 01. One of the five hills surrounding Rājagaha (e.g., MN.iii.68). At its foot was the Sattapaṇṇiguhā, where the first Convocation was held (Vin.ii.76; W. 159; Vin-a.i.10, etc.). The river Tapodā (q.v.) rose in a lake at the foot of Vebhāra. SN­a.i.30f.

Chưa dịch.

02. Vebhāra 02 untranslated

Vebhāra 02. A city in which Padumuttara Buddha preached and ordained ninety crores of men. Bv.xi.9.

Chưa dịch.

03. Vebhāra 03 untranslated

Vebhāra 03. The birthplace of Siddhattha Buddha, where, later, he preached the Buddhavaṁsa, when ninety crores of beings realized the Truth. Bv.xvii.5, 13; Bv­a. p. 186; Ja.i.40.

Chưa dịch.

04. Vebhāra 04 untranslated

Vebhāra 04. A city built by Vissakamma, where Valliya Thera (Candanamāliya) lived in a previous birth. Thag­a.i.294; Ap.ii.424.

Chưa dịch.

05. Vebhāra 05 untranslated

Vebhāra 05. v.l. for Dvebhāra (q.v.).

Chưa dịch.

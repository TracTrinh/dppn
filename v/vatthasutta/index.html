Vatthasutta

01. Vatthasutta 01

Vatthasutta 01. Benares cloth is of good colour, pleasant to handle and of great worth when new or of middling wear, or even when worn out. So is a good monk, whether he be a novice, of middle standing, or a senior. AN.i.247; cp. Pp. 34.

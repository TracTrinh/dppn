Virāgasutta

01. Virāgasutta 01

Virāgasutta 01. The Buddha teaches non-attachment and the path thereto. SN.iv.371.

02. Virāgasutta 02

Virāgasutta 02. Dispassion is the aim of the holy life as taught by the Buddha, and the way thereto is the Noble Eightfold Path. SN.v.27.

03. Virāgasutta 03

Virāgasutta 03. The idea of dispassion, if cultivated, leads to great profit. SN.v.133.

04. Virāgasutta 04

Virāgasutta 04. The four satipaṭṭhānas, if cultivated, lead to dispassion. SN.v.179.

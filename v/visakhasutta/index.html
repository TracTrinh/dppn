Visākhasutta untranslated

1. Visākhasutta untranslated

Visākhasutta. The Buddha listens to a sermon by Visākha Pañcāliputta and praises his skill in the assembly of monks. The Sutta also contains a teaching as to how the Dhamma should be preached. AN.ii.51f.; SN.ii.280f.

01. Visākhāsutta 01 untranslated

Visākhāsutta 01. The Buddha tells Visākhā (5) of the advantages of keeping the uposatha day. AN.iv.255.

Chưa dịch.

02. Visākhāsutta 02 untranslated

Visākhāsutta 02. The Buddha tells Visākhā (5) of eight qualities in a woman which will secure for her birth among the Manāpakāyikā Devā. AN.iv.267.

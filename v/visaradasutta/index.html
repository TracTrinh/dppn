Visāradasutta untranslated

01. Visāradasutta 01 untranslated

Visāradasutta 01. A man who takes life takes what is not given, is given over to lust, lies, and takes spirituous liquors, such a man lives as a layman without confidence. Following the opposite course, he lives with confidence. AN.iii.203; cf. No. 3.

Chưa dịch.

02. Visāradasutta 02 untranslated

Visāradasutta 02. Five things that give confidence to a woman: beauty, wealth, kindred, sons and virtue. SN.iv.246.

Chưa dịch.

03. Visāradasutta 03 untranslated

Visāradasutta 03. A woman who abstains from taking life, theft, sensual lusts, lying and intoxicants, lives in confidence. SN.iv.250; cf. No. 1 above.

Chưa dịch.

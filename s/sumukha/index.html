Sumukha

01. Sumukha 01

Sumukha 01. A Yakkha chief, to be invoked in time of need by followers of the Buddha. DN.iii.205.

02. Sumukha 02

Sumukha 02. A crow, general of Supatta. See the Supattajātaka (Ja 292). He is identified with Sāriputta. Ja.ii.436.

03. Sumukha 03

Sumukha 03. A goose, general of a flock of geese whose king was Dhataraṭṭha. See the Haṁsajātaka (Ja 502) and Mahā Haṁsajātaka (Ja 534). He is identified with Ānanda. Ja.iv.430; v.382.

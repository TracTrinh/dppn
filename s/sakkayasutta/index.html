Sakkāyasutta untranslated

01. Sakkāyasutta 01 untranslated

Sakkāyasutta 01. The Buddha teaches the monks about sakkāya, its arising, its cessation, and the way thereto. SN.iii.159.

Chưa dịch.

02. Sakkāyasutta 02 untranslated

Sakkāyasutta 02. It is by the realization of impermanence in all things that sakkāyadiṭṭhi can be abandoned. SN.iv.147.

Chưa dịch.

03. Sakkāyasutta 03 untranslated

Sakkāyasutta 03. Sāriputta tells Jambukhādaka that sakkāya is the five factors of grasping; the Noble Eightfold Path leads to its comprehension. SN.iv.259.

Chưa dịch.

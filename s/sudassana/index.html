17. Sudassana 17 untranslated

24. Sudassana 24 untranslated

25. Sudassana 25 untranslated

Sudassana 25. A Nāgarāja, son of Dhataraṭṭha. He was brother to Bhūridatta. See the Bhūridattajātaka (Ja 543). He is identified with Sāriputta. Ja.vi.219; see also Ja.iv.182.

28. Sudassana 28 untranslated

Sudassana 28. Nephew of Pasenadi. The Buddha taught him a stanza to recite whenever Pasenadi sat down to a meal, in order that the king might observe moderation in eating. For this service Pasenadi paid him one hundred kahāpaṇas a day. SN.i.82; Dhp­a.iii.264f. This story is also given at Dhp­a.iv.15f., but there the nephew is called Uttara.

Chưa dịch.

29. Sudassana 29 untranslated

Sudassana 29. called Sudassanakūṭa, Sudassanagiri, Sudassanasiluccaya. The first of the five mountain ranges surrounding Anotatta. It is of a golden colour, two hundred leagues in height, and bent inwards like a crow’s beak (Snp­a.ii.437; cf. 443; AN­a.ii.759; Ja.vi.125). Dīpaṅkara Buddha held an assembly of his monks there. Bv.ii.200.

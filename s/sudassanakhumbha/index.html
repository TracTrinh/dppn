Sudassanakhumbha untranslated

Sudassanakhumbha. A water pot, set on Erāvaṇa, for the use of Sakka. It is thirty leagues in circumference, and above it is a canopy twelve leagues wide made of precious stones. Surrounding it are thirty-two other pots. Dhp­a.i.273; Snp­a.i.369.

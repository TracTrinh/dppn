Samudayadhammasutta untranslated

01. Samudayadhammasutta 01 untranslated

Samudayadhammasutta 01. Ignorance means ignorance that the nature of the body, feeling, etc., is to come to be and then pass away. SN.iii.170.

Chưa dịch.

02. Samudayadhammasutta 02 untranslated

Samudayadhammasutta 02. The same as Sutta (1), but the explanation is given by Sāriputta to Mahā Koṭṭhita. SN.iii.171.

Chưa dịch.

03. Samudayadhammasutta 03 untranslated

Samudayadhammasutta 03. The Buddha teaches the monks about arising and the ending of the four satipaṭṭhānas. SN.v.184.

Chưa dịch.

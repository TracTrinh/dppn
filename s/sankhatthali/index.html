Saṅkhatthalī

Saṅkhatthalī, Saṅkhanāyakatthalī, Saṅkhanāthatthalī. An important place in the Dakkhiṇadesa of Ceylon, where Kittisirimegha had his capital. It was near Badalatthalī, and is mentioned several times in the account of the campaigns of Parakkamabāhu I. Cv.lxiii.43; lxiv.22; lxvi.9; lxvii.78, 82; also Cv.Trs.i.241, n.2.

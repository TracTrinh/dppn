2. Saccā untranslated

Saccā. A Licchavī maiden, daughter of a Nigaṇṭha and a Nigaṇṭhī.

She was sister to Saccaka. She was a great disputant, and, one day she and her sisters, Paṭācārā, Lolā and Avavādakā, engaged in a dispute with Sāriputta. Having been defeated, she joined the Saṅgha and became an Arahant. Ja.iii.1f.

Chưa dịch.

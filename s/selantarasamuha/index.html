Selantarasamūha

Selantarasamūha. The name of a monastic building provided by the king of Ceylon (probably Māṇavamma, Cv.lvii.37f.; Cv.Trs.i.196, n.2) for Dāṭhopatissa after his ordination. Later Yasodharā, daughter of Vijayabāhu I., erected there a building called the Pasādapāsāda. Cv.lx.84.

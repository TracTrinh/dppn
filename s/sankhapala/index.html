Saṅkhapāla untranslated

01. Saṅkhapāla 01 untranslated

Saṅkhapāla 01. The Bodhisatta born as a Nāga king. See the Saṅkhapālajātaka (Ja 521). Saṅkhapāla is evidently a generic name for the Nāgas of that world.

Chưa dịch.

02. Saṅkhapāla 02 untranslated

Saṅkhapāla 02. A king of Ekabala. Mahosadha (q.v.), hearing that he was collecting arms and assembling an army, sent a parrot to find out about it. The parrot reported that there was no reason to fear Saṅkhapāla. Ja.vi.390.

Chưa dịch.

03. Saṅkhapāla 03 untranslated

Saṅkhapāla 03. A lake in the Mahiṁsakaraṭṭha. It was the residence of the Nāga king, Saṅkhapāla. From the lake rose the river Kaṇṇapeṇṇā. Ja.v.162.

Chưa dịch.

04. Saṅkhapāla 04 untranslated

Saṅkhapāla 04. Saṅkhapāla Thera. A pupil of Buddhadatta, to whom he dedicated his Vinayavinicchaya Gv.40; PLC. 109.

Chưa dịch.

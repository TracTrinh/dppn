Sutana

1. Sutana

Sutana. The Bodhisatta born as a poor householder. See the Sutanojātaka (Ja 398).

01. Sutanā 01

Sutanā 01. Sutanī. A gazelle, sister of the Bodhisatta. See the Rohantamigajātaka (Ja 501). She is identified with Khemā. Ja.iv.423.

02. Sutanā 02

Sutanā 02. v.l. Sudhanā. An eminent upāsikā. AN.iv.347.

03. Sutanā 03

Sutanā 03. One of the Aggasāvikā of Maṅgala Buddha. Bv.iv.25.

Siṁsapāvana untranslated

01. Siṁsapāvana 01 untranslated

Siṁsapāvana 01. A grove in Āḷavi, where the Buddha stayed in the Gomagga and was visited by Hatthaka of Āḷavi. AN.i.136.

Chưa dịch.

02. Siṁsapāvana 02 untranslated

Siṁsapāvana 02. A grove in Kosambī, where the Buddha once stayed. See Siṁsapasutta. SN.v.437.

Chưa dịch.

03. Siṁsapāvana 03 untranslated

Siṁsapāvana 03. A grove to the north of Setavyā, where Kumāra Kassapa once stayed. Gotama Buddha also once stayed there during a journey (Dhp­a.i.59). It was the scene of the preaching of the Pāyāsisutta. DN.ii.316.

Chưa dịch.

Sīlasutta untranslated

01. Sīlasutta 01 untranslated

Sīlasutta 01. The Buddha exhorts the monks to live perfect in virtue; then will they be ardent, scrupulous and resolute. AN.ii.14.

Chưa dịch.

02. Sīlasutta 02 untranslated

Sīlasutta 02. On four persons, as regards their completeness in virtue, concentration and wisdom. AN.ii.136.

Chưa dịch.

03. Sīlasutta 03 untranslated

Sīlasutta 03. On four persons, as regards their respect for virtue, concentration and wisdom. AN.ii.136.

Chưa dịch.

04. Sīlasutta 04 untranslated

Sīlasutta 04. A monk who is virtuous, learned, has a pleasant and smooth speech, is able to develop the four jhānas at will, and has attained the destruction of the āsavas – such a one has achieved his goal. AN.iii.113.

Chưa dịch.

05. Sīlasutta 05 untranslated

Sīlasutta 05. A monk who has achieved virtue, concentration, insight, emancipation and the vision of emancipation such a one is worthy of offerings and homage. AN.iii.134.

Chưa dịch.

06. Sīlasutta 06 untranslated

Sīlasutta 06. A man, wanting in morals, loses wealth through neglect; evil rumour spreads about him; he has no confidence in gatherings; he is muddled in thought; and goes, after death, to a place of ill. AN.iii.252.

Chưa dịch.

07. Sīlasutta 07 untranslated

Sīlasutta 07. Sāriputta tells Mahā Koṭṭhita, in answer to a question, that the virtuous monk should methodically ponder on the five upādānakkhandhā. SN.iii.167.

Chưa dịch.

08. Sīlasutta 08 untranslated

Sīlasutta 08. Even as the dawn is the forerunner of the sun, so is virtue the forerunner of the Noble Eightfold Path. SN.v.30.

Chưa dịch.

09. Sīlasutta 09 untranslated

Sīlasutta 09. The benefits which come through monks being possessed of virtue, concentration, insight, release – release by knowledge and insight. SN.v.67f.

Chưa dịch.

10. Sīlasutta 10 untranslated

Sīlasutta 10. Ānanda tells Bhadda, in the Kukkuṭārāma in Pāṭaliputta, that the virtuous habits, spoken of by the Buddha, are those which come by cultivation of the four satipaṭṭhānas. SN.v.171.

Chưa dịch.

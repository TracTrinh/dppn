Gambhīra

01. Gambhīra 01

Gambhīra 01. A seaport village, visited by Mittavindaka in the course of his flight from Benares. Ja.i.239.

02. Gambhīra 02

Gambhīra 02. A channel branching off from the Parakkamasamudda. It started at the point of the flood-escape known as Makara. Cv.lxxix.40.

02. Gahapativagga 02 untranslated

Gahapativagga 02. The third section of the Aṭṭhakanipāta of the Aṅguttaranikāya. It contains ten Suttas, including the Buddha’s praises of Ugga of Vesālī, Ugga of Hatthigāma, and Hatthaka Āḷavaka. AN.iv.208-35.

Chưa dịch.

03. Gahapativagga 03 untranslated

Gahapativagga 03. The fifth chapter of the Nidānasaṁyutta. SN.ii.68-80.

Chưa dịch.

04. Gahapativagga 04 untranslated

Gahapativagga 04. The thirteenth chapter of the Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta. It contains conversations between various eminent householders on the one side, and the Buddha and the monks on the other. SN.iv.109-24.

Chưa dịch.

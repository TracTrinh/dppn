Gāmaṇī

01. Gāmaṇī 01

Gāmaṇī 01. A Tamil general and his stronghold, subdued by Duṭṭhagāmaṇī. Mhv.xxv.13.

02. Gāmaṇī 02

Gāmaṇī 02. A prince, youngest of one hundred brothers. His story is given in the Saṁvarajātaka. Ja.iv.130ff; see also Ja.i.136f.

03. Gāmaṇī 03

Gāmaṇī 03. One of the six brothers of Bhaddakaccānā; he remained behind when the others left for Ceylon. Mhv­ṭ.275.

04. Gāmaṇī 04

Gāmaṇī 04. See also Dīghagāmaṇī, Duṭṭhagāmaṇī and Āmaṇḍagāmaṇī.

Dīghāvu untranslated

01. Dīghāvu 01 untranslated

Dīghāvu 01. Eldest son of King Arindama I. He is identified with Rāhula. Ja.v.490.

Chưa dịch.

02. Dīghāvu 02 untranslated

Dīghāvu 02. A householder of Rājagaha and son of Jotika gahapati. He was a Sotāpanna and, when he lay ill, he sent his father to Gotama Buddha requesting the Buddha to visit him. The Buddha accepted the request, preached to him and consoled him. Soon afterwards Dīghāvu died and was born as an Anāgāmī. SN.v.344f.

Chưa dịch.

03. Dīghāvu 03 untranslated

Dīghāvu 03. Son of King Mahā Janaka and his queen Sīvalī. He became king when Mahā Janaka left the world. He is identified with Rāhula. Ja.vi.44, 61, 62, 68.

Chưa dịch.

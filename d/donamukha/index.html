Doṇamukha untranslated

Doṇamukha. The elephant sent by Prince Mahā Pāduma of Kumudanagara, at Soṇa’s request, to kill Piyadassī Buddha. The Buddha spoke to the elephant and so won him over. Cf. Nālāgiri. Bv.xiv.6; Bv­a.174f.

Devī untranslated

Devī. Mother of Mahinda and Saṅghamittā and wife of Asoka.

She was the daughter of Deva of Vedisagiri.

Asoka met and married her while on his way to Ujjenī, there to become Viceroy.

When he became king, Devī continued to live at Vedisagiri (Mhv.xiii.6ff; Dpv.vi.16; Vin-a.i.70).

She is sometimes called Vedisadevī (Mhv­ṭ.324).

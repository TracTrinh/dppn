Dhammarakkhita

01. Dhammarakkhita 01

Dhammarakkhita 01. A Yona Thera sent by the Third Council to Aparantaka. There he preached the Aggikkhandhopamasutta and converted thirty-seven thousand persons. Mhv.xii.4, 34f.

02. Dhammarakkhita 02

Dhammarakkhita 02. See Mahā Dhammarakkhita and Yonaka Mahā Dhammarakkhita.

03. Dhammarakkhita 03

Dhammarakkhita 03. A Thera in Ceylon in the time of Kittisirirājasīha. Cv.c.299.

04. Dhammarakkhita 04

Dhammarakkhita 04. A Thera at whose request Ācariya Dhammapāla wrote the Commentary on the Netti. Gv.69.

05. Dhammarakkhita 05

Dhammarakkhita 05. Mentioned as a high-class name, e.g., Vin.iv.8; Vin-a.ii.448, 480.

06. Dhammarakkhita 06

Dhammarakkhita 06. A monk of Asokārāma in Pāṭaliputta, under whom Nāgasena studied the Tipiṭaka. Mil.16, 18.

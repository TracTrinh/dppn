Dunniviṭṭha

Dunniviṭṭha. A Brahmin village in the Kāliṅga country. It lay on the road (along which Vessantara journeyed to Vaṅkagiri) from Jetuttara to the Ceti kingdom, five leagues from Arañjaragiri and ten from the Ceti country. It was the residence of Jūjaka and Amittatāpanā. Ja.vi.521, 541.

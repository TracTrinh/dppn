Index / D / Dūratissakavāpi

Dūratissakavāpi untranslated

Dūratissakavāpi. A tank in Rohaṇa, near Mahā Gāma. Meghavaṇṇābhaya, minister of Mahā Sena, leading a revolt against the king, once encamped on its bank (Mhv.xxxvii.18). Saddhātissa built a vihāra near by (Mhv.xxxiii.9). Mahā Nāga gave to the ascetics one thousand fields watered by the tank (Cv.xli.99; see Cv.Trs.i.62, n.1), and Parakkamabāhu I, repaired the tank (Cv.lxxix.32). Geiger thinks (Mhv.Trs.248, n.5) that Dūratissavāpi is probably identical with Dūravāpi.

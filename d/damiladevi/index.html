01. Damiḷādevī 01 untranslated

02. Damiḷādevī 02 untranslated

Damiḷādevī 02. Queen of Mahā Dāṭhika Mahā Nāga. She was young and beautiful and when she visited Ambatthala, an old monk named Citta fell in love with her and refused to be consoled even though she died soon after. AN­a.i.13.
